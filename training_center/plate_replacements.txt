## Summary

Use this guide when a plate was lost in transit, mailed incorrectly, or the wrong plate was transferred to the customer.

Form **HSMV 83416** is used for this workflow and may be signed by POA.

## When This Applies

* Plate was mailed out but lost in transit.
* Plate was mailed to the wrong address.
* Wrong plate was transferred to the customer.
* A transfer plate was processed but should have been a new plate.
* A new plate was issued but the customer did not receive it.

## Our Office Workflow

1. Confirm the VIN matches the deal.
2. Confirm the customer is the owner of record.
3. Confirm VIN-specific insurance.
4. Confirm the customer’s correct mailing address.
5. Confirm all required signatures are complete. 
6. Complete APPLICATION FOR REPLACEMENT LICENSE PLATE Form 83416, may be signed by POA.
	- Section 1 REPLACEMENT TYPE -Check box for License Plate & Decal
	- Check a box for REPLACEMENT REASON
	- Section 2 OWNER / CUSTOMER IDENTIFICATION
	- Section 3 VEHICLE INFORMATION
	- Section 4, sign and date 
7. Submit through the appropriate tag agency.
8. Save support documents and confirmation.

## Common Mistakes

* VIN does not match the deal.
* VIN-specific insurance was not verified.
* Mailing address was not confirmed.
* Wrong plate was transferred.
* Transfer plate was processed when a new plate was needed.
* New plate was processed when a transfer plate should have been used.
* Form 83416 is missing signatures.
* POA is missing or incomplete when used.

## When To Escalate

* No VIN-specific insurance.
* Customer is not the owner of record.
* VIN does not match.
* Correct mailing address cannot be confirmed.
* Plate status is unclear.
* It is unclear whether the transaction should be a replacement plate, new plate, or corrected transfer.
* POA is unclear, missing, or incomplete.
* There is a stop, registration issue, insurance issue, fraud concern, or unusual record status.

## Quick Checklist

* VIN checked.
* Ownership checked.
* VIN-specific insurance checked.
* Mailing address confirmed.
* Form 83416 completed.
* Signatures checked.
* POA checked, if used.
* Fees confirmed.
* Support documents saved.
* Rejection risk reviewed.
