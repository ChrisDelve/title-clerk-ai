# How to Fill Out Titles

## Summary
Use this guide when filling out blank title.

## When This Applies
- Trade customer or street purchase title was provided not signed. 

## What To Check First
1. VIN matches.
2. Confirm the customer is the owner of record.
3. Confirm if POA is needed to sign.
4. Confirm dates and miles.

## Our Office Workflow
1. Verify ownership.
2. Verify all documents are properly signed.
3. Confirm miles are equal to or less than previous odometer reading.
4. Verify most recent title issue date.
5. Verify no lienholder on title.

## Common Mistakes
- Wrong owner signed trade documents.
- Miles on documents are higher than what being resold at.
- Needing lien release if lienholder on title.

## When To Escalate
- Lien is not satisfied. 
- No documents signed by correct title owner.
- Title owner is deceased.

## Quick Checklist
- Ownership checked.
- Miles checked.
- Signed documents checked.
- Forms complete.
- Fees confirmed.
- Rejection risk reviewed.
