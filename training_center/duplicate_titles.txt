## Summary
Use this guide when a customer, dealership, lender, or internal department needs a replacement title because the original title is lost, or not provided.

## When This Applies
- Customer lost the Florida paper title.
- Dealer needs the title for a trade, wholesale, resale, or correction.
- A title is needed but the original paper title is not available.



## Our Office Workflow
1. Confirm the vehicle is titled in Florida.
2. Confirm the customer is the owner of record.
3. Confirm required forms have signatures.
4. Confirm miles are equal to or less than new sale.
5. Confirm no lien on record and/or notarized lien release provided.
6. Prepare the duplicate title form. (Can be signed by POA)
7. Submit through appropriate tag agency.
8. Save support documents and confirmation.

## Common Mistakes
- Miles are more than new sale.
- Missing lien release.
- Wrong owner signing.
- Name mismatch.
- Ordering duplicate paper title when actually electronic.

## When To Escalate
- Owner does not match.
- Active lien is unresolved.
- Customer is deceased.
- Vehicle is not Florida-titled.
- There is a stop, brand, fraud concern, or unusual title status.

## Quick Checklist
- Ownership checked.
- Signatures checked.
- Lien checked.
- Title status checked.
- Miles checked.
- Forms complete.
- Fees confirmed.
- Rejection risk reviewed.
