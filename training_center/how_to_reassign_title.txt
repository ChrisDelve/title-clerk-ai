## What Is Needed

Use these items as quick access for this workflow:

* Original paper title
* Verify VIN number matches title and documents
* Trade or gift documents
* Notarized lien release, if needed
* Odometer statement
* Power of Attorney, if used
* Audit trail, if signed by Power of Attorney
* Dealer information:

  * Dealership name
  * Dealership address
  * VF number / dealer number
  * Sales tax number

## Summary

Use this guide when the dealership is taking in a vehicle and reassigning the paper title correctly from the title owner to the dealership, and then from the dealership to the purchaser.

The main goal is to make sure the title owner, signatures, mileage, dates, purchaser information, and dealership reassignment information are completed correctly before the vehicle is resold.

The numbered steps in this guide are designed to match the numbered areas on the title training image.

---

## Our Office Workflow

### 1. Verify the title owner / signer

a. Verify the person listed as the owner on the title is the same person signing the trade or gift documents.

b. If there is more than one owner, verify the required owner signatures are present.

c. If the person signing does not match the title owner, stop and escalate before continuing.

---

### 2. Verify Branded Mileage and Dates

a. Make sure the title is not branded with more miles than what the trade-in/gift documents reads or resale paperwork.

b. Verify the date on the title does not conflict with the dates on the trade-in/gift documents or resale paperwork.

c. If mileage or dates do not line up, stop and escalate before continuing.

---

### 3. Signing the owner-to-dealer assignment

a. Have the customer sign in the correct seller signature line.

b. If the customer signed but did not print their name, print the customer’s name on the printed seller name line if needed.

c. If the title is signed by Power of Attorney, verify “POA” is written next to the signature.

d. If signed by Power of Attorney, the odometer statement and audit trail must be submitted with the title.

e. Print your name on the purchaser printed name line.

f. Sign your name on the purchaser signature line.

---

### 4. Complete the dealer-to-customer reassignment

a. Enter the purchaser’s name.

b. Enter the purchaser’s address.

c. Enter the mileage.

d. Enter the mileage read date.

e. Enter the date sold.

---

### 5. Complete the first reassignment on the back of the title

a. Enter the dealership name as the seller.

b. Enter the dealership address.

c. Enter the VF number / dealer number on the dealer number line.

d. Enter the sales tax number.

e. Print your name on the printed seller name line.

f. Sign your name on the seller signature line.

---

## Common Mistakes

* Title owner does not match the person signing the trade or gift documents.
* Required owner signature is missing.
* Customer signs in the wrong seller signature line.
* Printed names are missing where needed.
* “POA” is not written next to the signature when Power of Attorney is used.
* Odometer statement or audit trail is missing when signed by Power of Attorney.
* Mileage on the title conflicts with the odometer statement.
* Mileage or dates conflict with the resale paperwork.
* Purchaser name is incomplete or incorrect.
* Purchaser address is incomplete.
* Dealership information is incomplete.
* VF number / dealer number is missing.
* Sales tax number is missing.
* Seller signature line is not signed.
* Title appears altered, unclear, or incomplete.

## When To Escalate

Hold and escalate if:

* The title owner does not match the signer.
* Required owner signatures are missing.
* The title shows more miles than the odometer statement.
* Mileage is inconsistent.
* Dates are inconsistent.
* The title was signed incorrectly.
* Power of Attorney is unclear, missing, or incomplete.
* Audit trail is missing when Power of Attorney is used.
* Required dealership information is missing.
* There is a lien showing and no notarized lien release is provided.
* Anything on the title appears altered, unclear, or incomplete.

## Quick Checklist

Before finalizing, confirm:

* Title owner matches signed trade or gift documents.
* Required owner signatures are present.
* Mileage is correct.
* Dates are correct.
* Customer signed the correct seller line.
* Printed seller name is present if needed.
* POA is written next to signature, if used.
* Odometer statement is included, if needed.
* Audit trail is included, if signed by POA.
* Dealership is entered correctly as purchaser.
* Purchaser printed name is complete.
* Purchaser signature is complete.
* Purchaser name and address are completed.
* Mileage read date is completed.
* Date sold is completed.
* Dealership seller section is completed.
* Dealership address is entered.
* VF number / dealer number is entered.
* Sales tax number is entered.
* Seller printed name and signature are completed.
* Rejection risk reviewed.
