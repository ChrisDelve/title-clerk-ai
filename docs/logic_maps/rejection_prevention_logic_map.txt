COMMON CLERK ANSWER:
For vehicle registration, a customer must provide a valid Florida/out-of-state driver license or ID, or a valid passport. An unexpired U.S. passport or U.S. passport card may qualify. A foreign passport must be unexpired and supported by lawful-presence documentation such as a DHS stamp/mark, unexpired I-94, current permanent resident card, or unexpired immigrant visa. A foreign passport alone is not enough for registration. This rule applies to registration/plate issuance, not title-only applications. Hold the registration if required ID/passport documentation is missing or expired.
