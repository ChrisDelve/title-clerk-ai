COMMON CLERK ANSWER:
A spouse’s plate should not be treated as automatically transferable just because the parties are married. First verify the registered owner of the plate, the titled owner/applicant of the new vehicle, and who will be listed as the registrant. If the plate is registered to the spouse but the spouse is not an owner or registrant on the new vehicle, do not process it as a normal transfer tag. Process as a new plate and review IRF unless a valid statutory exception or tax collector guidance applies. If the spouse will be added as an owner or co-registrant, verify signatures, ID, insurance, ownership documents, and registration eligibility before proceeding.
