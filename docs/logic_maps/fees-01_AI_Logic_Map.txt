COMMON FEE ANSWER:
For dealership processing, a duplicate Florida motor vehicle/mobile home title should be quoted using the Fast Title amount by default. Duplicate Title - No Lien is $85.25 Fast Title total. Duplicate Title - 1 Lien is $87.25 Fast Title total. Agency/service fees may be added on top depending on the agency or processing office. Do not answer "$2.00" as the duplicate title fee; that is only a lien fee component. Do not answer "$75.25" as the dealership default unless clearly explaining it is the electronic record only / no lien amount, not the normal dealership fast-title amount. If the title is electronic or there is an active lien/ELT issue, do not use the normal owner duplicate-title workflow until title/lien authority is verified.
