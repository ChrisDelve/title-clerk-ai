COMMON FEE ANSWER:
For dealership processing, a duplicate Florida motor vehicle/mobile home title should be quoted using the Fast Title amount by default. Duplicate Title - No Lien is $85.25 Fast Title total. Duplicate Title - 1 Lien is $87.25 Fast Title total. Agency/service fees may be added on top depending on the agency or processing office. Do not answer "$2.00" as the duplicate title fee; that is only a lien fee component. Do not answer "$75.25" as the dealership default unless clearly explaining it is the electronic record only / no lien amount, not the normal dealership fast-title amount. If the title is electronic or there is an active lien/ELT issue, do not use the normal owner duplicate-title workflow until title/lien authority is verified.

COMMON OUT-OF-STATE ORIGINAL TITLE FEE ANSWER:
For dealership processing, an original Florida title for a vehicle previously titled or registered in another state or country should be quoted using the Fast Title amount by default. Original Title - Previously Titled or Registered in Another State or Country - No Lien is $95.25 Fast Title total. With 1 Lien, the Fast Title total is $97.25. Agency/service fees may be added on top depending on the agency or processing office. Do not answer "$85.25" as the out-of-state original title fee; $85.25 is the Fast Title amount for a transfer title or duplicate title with no lien, not the out-of-state original title row. Odometer disclosure/statement is required if applicable based on the vehicle’s model year and exemption status.
