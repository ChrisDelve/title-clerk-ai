==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Delinquent registration fee:
Applies:
Beginning:
11th calendar day:
Of month following:
Registration expiration month.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST FUND RULE
==================================================

Delinquent fee:
Applies ONLY:
To registration tax.

Does NOT apply:
To Trust Fund Fees.

==================================================
DELINQUENT FEE SCHEDULE
==================================================

License tax:
More than $5 but not more than $25:
Delinquent fee:
$5.

--------------------------------------------------

License tax:
Over $25 but not more than $50:
Delinquent fee:
$10.

--------------------------------------------------

License tax:
Over $50 but not more than $100:
Delinquent fee:
$15.

--------------------------------------------------

License tax:
Over $100 but not more than $400:
Delinquent fee:
$50.

--------------------------------------------------

License tax:
Over $400 but not more than $600:
Delinquent fee:
$100.

--------------------------------------------------

License tax:
Over $600:
Delinquent fee:
$250.

==================================================
NON-USE AFFIDAVIT EXEMPTION
==================================================

Delinquent fee does NOT apply:
If:
Vehicle continuously maintained:
In dead storage:
Entire previous registration period.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEAD STORAGE RULE
==================================================

Require:
- notarized affidavit
OR
- perjury clause affidavit

Stating:
Vehicle:
- not operated
- continuously stored

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Dead storage exemption:
Does NOT apply:
To:
- mobile homes
- park trailers
- travel trailers registered as mobile homes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CURRENT PERIOD RULE
==================================================

If non-use affidavit:
Applies only to portion:
Of current registration period:
Delinquent fee STILL required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXAMPLE RULE
==================================================

If registration:
December 1 through November 30
AND vehicle stored entire period:
No delinquent fee due:
When renewing in December.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PARTIAL STORAGE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle stored:
Only partial current period:
Delinquent fee:
AND back tax:
Still due.

==================================================
MAIL-IN EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Delinquent fee does NOT apply:
If mail-in registration:
Postmarked:
Before 11th calendar day:
Following expiration month.

==================================================
INSURANCE SURRENDER EXEMPTION
==================================================

Delinquent fee does NOT apply:
If:
- valid plate surrendered for insurance purposes
AND
- replacement plate applied for:
On same vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Insurance surrender exemption:
Requires:
Replacement plate:
On SAME vehicle.

==================================================
EXPIRED REGISTRATION CITATION EXEMPTION
==================================================

Delinquent fee does NOT apply:
If customer:
- assessed expired registration penalty
AND
- obtains valid registration:
Within 10 business days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DOT RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

Require:
Official Department of Transportation receipt:
Showing penalty paid.

==================================================
MILITARY EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Active-duty service member:
No delinquent fee due:
If registration expired:
While stationed:
35 miles or more:
From home of record.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MILITARY DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Require:
- official military orders
OR
- written commanding officer verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACTIVE DUTY RULE
==================================================

Military exemption applies:
To:
- motor vehicles
- mobile homes.

==================================================
BACK TAX RULES
==================================================

Back taxes may still apply:
Depending on:
Registration period
And affidavit coverage.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida delinquent registration fee procedures:
Impose escalating penalties
for late registration renewals,
while allowing strict statutory exemptions
for:
- dead storage
- military deployment
- insurance surrender situations
- timely mailed renewals
and certain expired registration citation corrections.
