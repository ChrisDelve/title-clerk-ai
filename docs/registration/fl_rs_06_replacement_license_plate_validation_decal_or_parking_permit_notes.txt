==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Replacement plates,
validation decals,
and parking permits:
Require proper application,
identity verification,
and replacement reason documentation.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT APPLICATION RULE
==================================================

Require:
HSMV 83146.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER SIGNATURE RULE
==================================================

If multiple owners:
Only one owner:
Must sign application.

==================================================
IDENTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- Driver license
- Identification card
- Passport
- Passport card

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CANADA RULE
==================================================

Identification card:
May be issued by:
Any state or Canada.

==================================================
POWER OF ATTORNEY RULE
==================================================

If applicant not owner:
Require:
POA per TL-02.

==================================================
BUSINESS AUTHORIZATION RULE
==================================================

Business replacement:
Requires:
Proof applicant authorized.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUNBIZ RULE
==================================================

Business authorization:
May verify through:
SunBiz.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LETTERHEAD RULE
==================================================

If SunBiz unavailable:
Require:
Original business letterhead authorization.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT FEE RULE
==================================================

Standard replacement fee:
$28 plus applicable service charges.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT POLICE REPORT RULE
==================================================

If police report provided:
Replacement:
No fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LOST IN MAIL RULE
==================================================

Items lost in mail:
Replaced:
No fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LOST IN MAIL REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- Audit number
- Date issued
- Statement item lost in mail

==================================================
PARKING PERMIT REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Permanent disabled parking permit replacement:
Requires:
- HSMV 83146
- Disability certification within 12 months

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VETERAN RULE
==================================================

Veteran may provide:
VA Summary of Benefits Letter:
Instead of disability certificate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO FEE PERMANENT PARKING RULE
==================================================

Permanent disabled parking permit replacement:
No fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY PERMIT RULE
==================================================

Temporary parking permit:
Replacement:
May require:
New HSMV 83039.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY VALID PERMIT RULE
==================================================

Valid temporary permit:
May replace:
Without new physician certification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY FEE RULE
==================================================

Replacement valid temporary permit:
Fee required.

==================================================
CANCELLED / SURRENDERED RULE
==================================================

Previously surrendered plate:
Now requesting replacement:
Fees required.

==================================================
CUSTOMER REQUEST RULE
==================================================

Customer may voluntarily replace:
Plate or decal:
At any time.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OPTIONAL FORM RULE
==================================================

HSMV 83146 optional:
For customer-initiated replacements:
At tax collector office:
Or convenience method.

==================================================
DAMAGED RULE
==================================================

Physically damaged plate/decal:
Requires:
HSMV 83146 and fees.

==================================================
DE-AUTHORIZED RULE
==================================================

If FLHSMV de-authorizes plate:
Customer may:
- Keep until 10-year replacement
OR
- Replace immediately.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ARF CREDIT RULE
==================================================

Advanced Replacement Fund credit:
Applied:
Toward new plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IMMEDIATE CANCELLATION RULE
==================================================

If de-authorization requires immediate cancellation:
Replacement required:
At next renewal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO FORM RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV-required de-authorization replacement:
Does NOT require:
HSMV 83146.

==================================================
DEFACED RULE
==================================================

Illegible plate/decal:
Requires replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUN DAMAGE RULE
==================================================

Sun fading:
Qualifies:
As defaced replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFACED FEE RULE
==================================================

Defaced replacement:
Requires:
Fees and HSMV 83146.

==================================================
DEFECTIVE DECAL RULE
==================================================

Defective decals:
Covered:
27 months after issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFECTIVE RETURN RULE
==================================================

Defective decal:
Must be returned:
For free replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSE PLATE EXCLUSION RULE
==================================================

Defective decal rule:
Does NOT include:
License plate.

==================================================
LOST OR DESTROYED RULE
==================================================

Lost/destroyed plate or decal:
Requires:
Replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LOST POLICE REPORT RULE
==================================================

Police report:
Allows:
Free replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ATTACHMENT RULE
==================================================

Police report copy:
Must attach:
To HSMV 83146.

==================================================
LOST IN TRANSIT RULE
==================================================

Plate/decal mailed:
And verified issued:
May replace free.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 180 DAY RULE
==================================================

Lost in transit:
Free replacement:
Only if:
Issued within 180 calendar days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OVER 180 DAY RULE
==================================================

If issuance exceeds 180 days:
Fees required.

==================================================
OBJECTIONABLE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Customer may replace:
Objectionable plate:
No fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AD CODE RULE
==================================================

Use replacement reason:
AD

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT RULE
==================================================

Comments must state:
"No fee, customer found plate to be objectionable."

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

Objectionable plate:
Must be surrendered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INVENTORY CONTROL RULE
==================================================

County objectionable plate ranges:
Require:
Inventory Control Unit contact.

==================================================
STOLEN RULES
==================================================

Stolen plate/decal:
With police report:
No fee replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LAW ENFORCEMENT CARD RULE
==================================================

Law enforcement case card:
Acceptable:
Instead of full report.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN COMMENT RULE
==================================================

Comments must include:
- Law enforcement agency
- Case number

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SP CODE RULE
==================================================

Replacement reason:
SP
Meaning:
Stolen with police report.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Police report or agency card:
Must attach:
To HSMV 83146.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO POLICE REPORT RULE
==================================================

If no police report:
Fees required.

==================================================
SEIZED PLATE RULE
==================================================

Seized plate:
Replacement:
Requires fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE SUSPENSION RULE
==================================================

Plate seized:
Due to insurance suspension:
Requires payment:
Regardless of insurance status later.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STATUTORY PAYMENT RULE
==================================================

Statute does not allow:
Free replacement:
For seized plate.

==================================================
TEMPORARY LICENSE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Temporary plate:
May replace:
Only personalized plate:
Pending new personalized issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PERSONALIZED RULE
==================================================

Temporary replacement:
Restricted:
To personalized plate replacement.

==================================================
DISABLED PARKING PERMIT RULES
==================================================

Permanent parking permits:
Require:
Current disability documentation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VA LETTER RULE
==================================================

VA Summary of Benefits Letter:
Must be:
Issued within 12 months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY PERMIT EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Expired temporary permit:
Requires:
New physician certification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SIX MONTH RULE
==================================================

Temporary placard:
May issue:
Additional six months:
If original certification supports longer duration.

==================================================
DOCUMENT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

All HSMV 83146 forms:
Must retain:
Minimum one calendar year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRONIC STORAGE RULE
==================================================

Retention may be:
Printed or electronic.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ATTACHED DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Additional required documents:
Must attach:
To HSMV 83146.

- Incorrect defective decal processing
- Missing retained documentation
- Not attaching police report
- Incorrect lost-in-transit calculation
