==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Fleet Registration Program:
Allows qualified companies:
To manage large vehicle groups:
Under consolidated fleet registration procedures.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLEET PLATE RULE
==================================================

Fleet plates:
Display:
- "FLORIDA" at top
- "FLEET" at bottom.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO DECAL RULE
==================================================

Fleet registrations:
Do NOT require:
- annual validation decals
OR
- individual registration certificates.

==================================================
SPECIALTY FLEET PLATE RULES
==================================================

Fleet companies:
May purchase:
Specialty license plates:
For fleet vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPONSOR APPROVAL RULE
==================================================

Specialty fleet plates:
Require:
Approval letter:
From sponsoring specialty plate organization.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLR RULE
==================================================

Fleet specialty plates:
Must include:
Letters:
"FLR"
On right side of plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIRECT ORDER RULE
==================================================

Fleet specialty plates:
Must be ordered:
Directly from FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-22 RULE
==================================================

Specialty plate processing:
Governed additionally by:
RS-22.

==================================================
ELIGIBILITY RULES
==================================================

Fleet companies:
Must submit:
Complete list of fleet vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MINIMUM VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Fleet participation requires:
Minimum:
- 100 vehicles
OR
- 25 trailers/semitrailers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APPORTIONED RULE
==================================================

Apportioned motor vehicles:
CANNOT participate:
In fleet program.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IDENTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

All fleet vehicles:
Must display:
- company name or logo
AND
- unit number.

==================================================
VEHICLE LIST REQUEST RULE
==================================================

Fleet company:
May request vehicle list:
From MV Public Records Unit:
For fee.

- number/type of vehicles
- FEID
OR

==================================================

==================================================

==================================================
REGISTRATION RULES
==================================================

Initial application:
Must be submitted:
90 days before:
Current registration expiration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Renewal information:
Should be submitted:
60 days before expiration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Fleet registrations expire:
In:
- June
OR
- December

Depending on:
Vehicle weight.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Fleet registration includes:
- annual registration taxes
- annual registration fees
- fleet management fee
- manufacturing fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLEET MANAGEMENT FEE RULE
==================================================

Additional annual fleet management fee:
$2.00:
Per vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MANUFACTURING FEE RULE
==================================================

One-time manufacturing fee:
$1.50:
Per vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLEET INCREASE RULE
==================================================

When fleet size increases:
$10 issuance fee:
Charged:
Per added vehicle.

==================================================
DELINQUENT FEE RULES
==================================================

Late fleet renewals:
Subject to delinquent fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DELINQUENT START RULE
==================================================

Delinquent fees begin:
11th calendar day:
Of month:
Following renewal period.

==================================================
DELINQUENT FEE SCHEDULE
==================================================

License tax:
$5-$25:
$5 delinquent fee.

License tax:
Over $25-$50:
$10 delinquent fee.

License tax:
Over $50-$100:
$15 delinquent fee.

License tax:
Over $100-$400:
$50 delinquent fee.

License tax:
Over $400-$600:
$100 delinquent fee.

License tax:
Over $600:
$250 delinquent fee.

==================================================
RECONCILIATION RULES
==================================================

Fleet companies:
Must submit:
Annual reconciliation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

All unassigned fleet plates:
Must be surrendered annually.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SANCTIONS RULE
==================================================

Failure to comply:
May result in:
- fines
- suspension
- termination
- registration stops
- plate cancellation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT $1000 RULE
==================================================

Fines:
Up to:
$1,000:
Per occurrence.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ERROR REPORT RULE
==================================================

Before renewal:
License plate agency:
Runs:
Error report:
For:
- administrative stops
- exceptions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INTERNAL CONTROL RULE
==================================================

Fleet companies:
Must maintain:
Internal vehicle control procedures.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RANDOM CHECK RULE
==================================================

License plate agencies:
May conduct:
Random fleet checks.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO RENEWAL NOTICE RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
Does NOT provide:
Fleet renewal notifications.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INVESTIGATION RULE
==================================================

Detailed investigations:
May occur:
For just cause.

==================================================
GENERAL INFORMATION RULES
==================================================

Fleet plates:
Use:
Light green background:
With darker green alphanumeric characters.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INVENTORY CODE RULE
==================================================

Inventory codes:
FLR and DUR:
Expire:
12/31/2099.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

System:
Will NOT automatically indicate:
Fleet plate replacement required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT CONDITIONS RULE
==================================================

Fleet plates replaced ONLY:
For:
- damage
- loss
- theft
OR
company request.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME TAX COLLECTOR RULE
==================================================

Fleet company:
Must continue using:
Original tax collector office:
For fleet processing.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OFFICE CHANGE RULE
==================================================

To switch tax collector office:
Fleet company must submit:
- new application
- printed vehicle list
- proof of insurance.

==================================================
DATA LISTING UNIT RULE
==================================================

Fleet companies:
May request:
Fleet vehicle lists:
Using fleet number.

9. Are renewal timelines met?
10. Are fleet management/manufacturing fees correct?
11. Are delinquent fees applicable?
12. Was annual reconciliation completed?
13. Were unassigned plates surrendered?
14. Does company maintain internal controls?
15. Are sanctions required?

17. Is office transfer documentation complete?

20. Does inventory code handling apply?
