==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

$225 Initial Registration Fee:
Required:
When owner:
Does NOT have:
Florida license plate:
To transfer:
From previously owned vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IRF AMOUNT RULE
==================================================

IRF amount:
$225.

==================================================
CLASS CODE EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

IRF exempt class codes include:
09, 14, 36, 37, 39, 41, 42 (Motor Coach),
46, 50, 51, 53, 54, 56, 62, 65, 69,
70, 71, 76, 77, 78, 80, 83,
91, 92, 93, 94, 95, 96, 97.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CLASS CODE 91/95 RULE
==================================================

Class code 91 or 95:
IRF exempt ONLY IF:
Vehicle:
30 years old or older.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIVATE MOTOR COACH RULE
==================================================

Private motor coach:
Class code 42:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTORCYCLE RULE
==================================================

IRF NOT required:
On:
- Motorcycles
- Mopeds
- Disability access vehicles

==================================================
HSMV 82002 RULE
==================================================

Most IRF exemptions require:
HSMV 82002:
IRF Exemption Affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82002:
And supporting documents:
Must retain:
Minimum one year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-62 RULE
==================================================

Retention procedures:
Governed by:
TL-62.

==================================================
PRIOR REGISTRATION EXEMPTION
==================================================

IRF exempt:
If customer provides:
Proof of prior Florida registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIOR REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Issuance system:
Must use:
Exemption reason:
"prior registration"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSE PLATE ENTRY RULE
==================================================

Prior plate number:
Must enter:
In issuance system prior registration field.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OVERRIDE RULE
==================================================

If override required:
Contact:
Field Support Center.

==================================================
TRANSFER BACK RULE
==================================================

If plate transferred:
Between exempt and non-exempt categories:
Customer may still qualify:
When transferring back.

==================================================
OPERATION OF LAW EXEMPTION
==================================================

Operation of law examples:
- Inheritance
- Bankruptcy
- Insolvency
- Replevin
- Divorce decree
- Judicial sale
- Repossession
- Storage lien sale

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIVORCE RULE
==================================================

If divorce decree awards vehicle:
IRF exempt:
Even if recipient:
Not previously on registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-AWARDED DIVORCE RULE
==================================================

If decree does NOT award vehicle:
And title transferred:
To person not on registration:
IRF due.

==================================================
IMMEDIATE FAMILY EXEMPTION
==================================================

Immediate family transfers:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IMMEDIATE FAMILY DEFINITION RULE
==================================================

Immediate family includes:
- Parents
- Children
- Spouse
- Surviving spouse
- Stepchildren
- Blood relatives
- Relatives by marriage
- Adopted relatives

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME HOUSEHOLD RULE
==================================================

Immediate family exemption:
Requires:
Same household address:
In issuance system.

==================================================
MILITARY EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

Military exemptions apply to:
- Nonresident active-duty military
- Spouses
- Dependent children
- Certain former military members
- Certain survivors

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONRESIDENT MILITARY RULE
==================================================

Nonresident military stationed in Florida:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MILITARY DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Require:
- Military orders
AND
- Driver license

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA RESIDENT MILITARY RULE
==================================================

Florida resident military:
Stationed outside Florida:
May qualify for IRF exemption.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DD214 RULE
==================================================

Former military member exemption:
Requires:
DD214 discharge document.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SIX MONTH RULE
==================================================

Former service member:
Must register vehicle:
Within 6 months after discharge.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MIA/DEATH RULE
==================================================

Spouse/dependent:
Of deceased or MIA service member:
May qualify:
Within one year.

==================================================
COURT ORDER EXEMPTION
==================================================

Court order ownership awards:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COURT ORDER RULE
==================================================

Require:
Copy of court order retained:
Minimum one year.

==================================================
FICTITIOUS NAME EXEMPTION
==================================================

Name changes:
Using fictitious/corporate name filings:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADMINISTRATIVE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Issuance system exemption reason:
"administrative"

==================================================
EXEMPTIONS NOT REQUIRING HSMV 82002
==================================================

No affidavit required for:
- Registration renewals
- Co-owner title transfers
- Manufacturer demonstrators
- 90-day temporary employed plates
- Non-operational transferred registrations
- Government-owned vehicles
- Goat vehicles
- Certain exempt plate owners

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CO-OWNER RULE
==================================================

Joint ownership transfer:
To original co-owner:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-OPERATIONAL RULE
==================================================

IRF exempt:
If transferred plate from vehicle:
- In storage
- Non-operational
OR
Not used on public roads.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOVERNMENT RULE
==================================================

Government-owned/operated vehicles:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
GOAT VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

"Goat" vehicle:
Agricultural citrus/farm vehicle:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXEMPT PLATE RULE
==================================================

IRF exempt license plate owners include:
- Disabled Veteran (100%)
- Ex-POW
- Medal of Honor
- Miccosukee Indian
- Seminole Indian
- Taiwanese Official
- X-Series

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABLED VETERAN LIMIT RULE
==================================================

Disabled veteran:
Only one tax-free plate allowed.

==================================================
EXCEPTIONS TO EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

Some scenarios:
Still require IRF:
Even with prior registration.

==================================================
TRUST RULES
==================================================

Transfer:
Individual to Trust:
Normally requires IRF.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST FLAG RULE
==================================================

Trust customer:
Must be created:
As business customer:
With TRUST flag marked.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BENEFICIAL USE RULE
==================================================

If trustee provides:
Beneficial use affidavit:
Individual may become registrant:
IRF exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82040 RULE
==================================================

Beneficial use statement:
May be entered:
On reverse side:
Of HSMV 82040.

==================================================
BUSINESS TO INDIVIDUAL RULE
==================================================

Company/entity:
To individual transfer:
IRF due.

==================================================
CUSTOMER STOP RULE
==================================================

If customer stop exists:
And old plate expired:
IRF due.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-50 RULE
==================================================

Customer stop procedures:
Governed by:
RS-50.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 90-DAY REFUND RULE
==================================================

If customer stop cleared:
Within 90 days:
IRF refund may apply.

==================================================
IRF REFUND RULES
==================================================

IRF refund possible:
If owner disposes of vehicle:
Within 3 months:
After paying IRF.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELIGIBLE VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Refund applies to:
- Private passenger automobiles
- Trucks 5,000 lbs or less
- Motor homes

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-65 RULE
==================================================

Refund procedures:
Governed by:
RS-65.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82050 RULE
==================================================

Disposed vehicle:
May require:
HSMV 82050:
Notice of Sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida's Initial Registration Fee system:
Requires a $225 fee
for first-time Florida registrations
unless the owner qualifies
through:
- transferable prior registration
- exempt class code
- military status
- family transfer
- operation of law
or other statutory exemptions,
all supported by strict affidavit
and documentation requirements.
