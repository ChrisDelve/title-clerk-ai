==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Certified copies of Florida motor vehicle records:
May be issued:
For motor vehicles being exported:
Outside the United States.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECORD RETENTION RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
Retains:
Titles, MSOs, applications, supporting documents,
and ownership records:
For minimum:
10 years.

==================================================
COMMON EXPORT USE RULE
==================================================

Certified copies commonly used:
For:
- leased vehicles
- vehicles with lienholder-held titles
- export ownership verification
- vehicle shipping documentation.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Customer must submit:
- request letter
OR
- HSMV 90510.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

Request must include:
Vehicle Identification Number (VIN).

==================================================
REQUEST MAILING ADDRESS
==================================================

Florida Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles
Division of Motorist Services
Bureau of Records Mail Stop #57
2900 Apalachee Parkway
Tallahassee FL 32399-0500

==================================================
REQUEST LETTER RULE
==================================================

Request letter:
Must specify:
Customer request details.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 90510 RULE
==================================================

Customer may alternatively submit:
HSMV 90510:
Motor Vehicle, Vessel and Mobile Home Records Request.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Certified copy of original title:
$3.00.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPUTER PRINTOUT RULE
==================================================

Certified computer-generated printout:
Fee:
$0.50:
For printout
PLUS
$3.00:
Certification fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MISSING FEE RULE
==================================================

If request submitted:
Without proper fees:
FLHSMV mails notice:
Requesting correct fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WRONG OFFICE RULE
==================================================

Requests received:
In wrong office:
Will be forwarded:
To correct office.

Processing may be delayed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PHONE CALL RULE
==================================================

Department phone inquiries:
Forwarded:
To appropriate office.

==================================================
PROCESSING TIME RULE
==================================================

Certified computer-generated printout:
Processed:
Within approximately:
Two weeks.

==================================================
APOSTILLE RULES
==================================================

Customers requiring apostille:
Must apply:
Through Department of State:
Division of Notaries.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APOSTILLE FEE RULE
==================================================

Apostille fee:
$20.00:
Per page.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APOSTILLE PAYMENT RULE
==================================================

Checks/money orders:
For apostille:
Must be payable to:
Department of State.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APOSTILLE LINK RULE
==================================================

Apostille instructions:
Available at:
http://notaries.dos.state.fl.us/notproc7.html

==================================================
DEFINITION — APOSTILLE
==================================================

Apostille:
Simplified certification:
Issued by Secretary of State:
For documents used internationally
under Hague Convention countries.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HAGUE CONVENTION RULE
==================================================

Apostille:
Recognized:
Without additional embassy legalization:
In participating Hague Convention countries.
