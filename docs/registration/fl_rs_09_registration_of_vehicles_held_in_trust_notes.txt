==================================================

Vehicle titled:
In name of trust or trustee:
May be registered:
In name of individual:
Who has beneficial use of vehicle.

==================================================
TRUSTEE RULES
==================================================

If vehicle titled:
"John Doe Trustee"
Customer record:
Must be created:
As business customer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST FLAG RULE
==================================================

Customer Detail Maintenance:
Must mark:
TRUST Flag:
With "X".

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUSTEE REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Registration may issue:
In name:
"John Doe Trustee"
Using:
06/30:
As DOB.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INDIVIDUAL REGISTRANT RULE
==================================================

Registration may alternatively issue:
In name:
"John Doe"
As individual registrant.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEX/DOB RULE
==================================================

If individual registrant used:
Use:
Individual's actual:
- Sex
- Date of birth.

==================================================
TRUSTEE PLATE TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Plate issued:
To "John Doe":
May transfer:
To vehicle titled:
In name of:
"John Doe Trustee."

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUSTEE ASSUMPTION RULE
==================================================

Plate issued:
To trustee:
May be assumed:
Into individual's name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION TAX RULE
==================================================

Appropriate registration tax and fees:
Must charge:
When changing:
To individual's DOB.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INITIAL REGISTRATION FEE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Initial Registration Fee:
NOT required:
When plate transferred:
From trustee:
To same individual.

==================================================
TRUST ENTITY RULES
==================================================

Trust:
Considered:
Separate legal entity:
From natural person.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST ENTITY TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

If title/registration issued:
To Trust:
Plate cannot transfer:
To individual.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INDIVIDUAL TO TRUST RULE
==================================================

If title/registration issued:
To individual:
Plate cannot transfer:
To Trust.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INITIAL REGISTRATION FEE RULE
==================================================

Initial Registration Fee:
Required:
When transferring:
Between:
- Trust
AND
- Individual.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-53 ANALOGY RULE
==================================================

Trust transfer logic:
Similar to:
Lease class code transfer:
Under RS-53.

==================================================
BENEFICIAL USE RULES
==================================================

If title transferred:
Into Trust:
Trustee may provide affidavit:
Naming beneficial user.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BENEFICIAL USER RULE
==================================================

Named beneficial user:
May become:
Registrant:
By transferring plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST BUSINESS CUSTOMER RULE
==================================================

Trust customer record:
Must be:
Business customer:
With TRUST Flag marked "X".

==================================================
DOCUMENTATION RULES
==================================================

Beneficial use affidavit:
Required from Trustee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AFFIDAVIT SUBMISSION RULE
==================================================

Affidavit:
Does NOT need:
Submission to FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENTS RULE
==================================================

Transaction comments:
Must contain:
Information concerning:
Beneficial use transaction.

VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida trust registration procedures:
Allow vehicles titled in trust or trustee names
to be registered to individuals with beneficial use,
but strict distinctions exist between:
- Trustee ownership
- Trust entity ownership
- Individual registrants
for purposes of plate transfer and Initial Registration Fee assessment.
