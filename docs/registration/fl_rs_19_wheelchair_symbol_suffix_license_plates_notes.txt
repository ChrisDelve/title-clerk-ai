==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Wheelchair Symbol license plates:
May issue:
To owners or lessees:
Who qualify:
For disabled parking permits.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PARKING PRIVILEGE RULE
==================================================

Wheelchair Symbol plate:
Provides:
All parking privileges:
Afforded:
To disabled parking permit holders.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MULTIPLE OWNER RULE
==================================================

If multiple registrants:
Eligible customer:
Must be identified:
On registration certificate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LONG-TERM MOBILITY RULE
==================================================

Person with long-term mobility problems:
May receive:
Wheelchair Symbol plate:
In lieu of disabled parking permit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Do NOT qualify:
For Wheelchair Symbol plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Organizations may still qualify:
For disabled parking permit:
But NOT wheelchair suffix plate.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- HSMV 83007
OR
- HSMV 83039

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORM RULE
==================================================

Forms must be:
Accurately completed.

==================================================
ELIGIBLE VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

Eligible vehicles:
- Private automobiles
- Trucks 5,000 lbs or less
- For-hire autos seating fewer than 9
- Motor homes
- Truck campers not for hire
- Heavy trucks under 8,000 lbs GVW

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL USE RULE
==================================================

Motor homes and truck campers:
Cannot be:
For hire or commercial use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DRIVER LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Require:
Florida driver license
OR
Florida identification card.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

- Branch fee if applicable

RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PHYSICIAN RULE
==================================================

Wheelchair Symbol plate renewals:
Do NOT require:
Physician recertification.

==================================================
TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Transfers:
May process:
Through county license plate agencies.

COMMON TITLE CLERK / REGISTRATION ERRORS
==================================================

- Issuing wheelchair plate to organization
- Missing HSMV 83007 or HSMV 83039
- Missing Florida ID
- Missing insurance verification
- Incorrect vehicle weight qualification
- Allowing commercial-use motor home
- Requiring unnecessary physician recertification
- Missing HSMV 83146
- Missing registration copy
- Incorrect fee calculations
- Failing to note eligible registrant
- Misunderstanding parking privileges
- Incorrect replacement routing
- Improper truck qualification

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida Wheelchair Symbol license plates:
Provide disabled parking privileges
to qualifying individuals with long-term mobility impairments,
while requiring strict compliance with:
- eligibility standards
- vehicle restrictions
- identity verification
- insurance requirements
and excluding organizational ownership eligibility.
