==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

One or more PVA license plates:
May issue:
To motor vehicles or motorcycles:
Owned or leased:
By Paralyzed Veterans of America members.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABLED PARKING RULE
==================================================

Vehicles displaying:
- PVA plate
- Disabled Veteran plate
- Disabled Veteran Wheelchair plate
- International Wheelchair plate
- Motorcycle Wheelchair emblem

May park:
In handicapped-designated parking spaces.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTORCYCLE RULE
==================================================

All PVA motorcycle plate requests:
Must be submitted to:

Titles and Registration Issuance MS#72
2900 Apalachee Parkway
Tallahassee FL 32399

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION NAME RULE
==================================================

PVA plate:
Must issue:
In veteran's name:
As:
- Owner
- Co-owner
OR
- Lessee/registrant

==================================================
PROOF OF ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Require:
Proof of membership:
In Paralyzed Veterans of America.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP RULES
==================================================

Acceptable ownership proof:
- Florida registration certificate
- Florida title
- HSMV 82041 title receipt
- Lease agreement

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEASE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle leased:
Require:
Copy of lease agreement.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Fees include:
- Regular registration tax
- Appropriate regular class code fees
- Trust fund fee
- Original plate fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEE CHART RULE
==================================================

Refer:
Registration Fees and Flat Taxes Distribution Chart:
For applicable fees.

==================================================
RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

Renewals:
May process:
Through any county license plate agency.

==================================================
TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Transfers:
May process:
Through any county license plate agency.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFER NAME RULE
==================================================

Transfers:
Must remain:
In paralyzed veteran's name:
As:
- Owner
- Co-owner
OR
- Lessee

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement PVA plates:
May process:
Through county license plate agencies.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTORCYCLE REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

Replacement PVA motorcycle requests:
Must be submitted to:
Titles and Registration Issuance MS#72.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- Copy of registration certificate
- HSMV 83146
- Applicable fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN PLATE RULE
==================================================

If stolen:
Police report or agency card:
Allows free replacement.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT FEES RULE
==================================================

Replacement fees may include:
- Replacement fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable
- Air Pollution Control fee
- EMS fee
- FRVIS fee
- Reflectorization fee
- Advanced Replacement fee

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS CANCELLATION RULES
==================================================

PVA plate must be surrendered and canceled if:
- Veteran dies
- Veteran no longer owns vehicle
- Veteran no longer Florida resident

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALTERNATE PLATE RULE
==================================================

If PVA eligibility ends:
Applicant must purchase:
Alternate eligible plate.

- Improper motorcycle routing

- Missing fee assessment
- Incorrect Tallahassee routing

VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida PVA license plates:
Provide specialized recognition
and disabled parking privileges
for qualifying members of the Paralyzed Veterans of America,
while requiring strict ownership,
membership,
and residency compliance.
