==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicles:
55,000 pounds GVW or more:
Require proof of:
- filing
- payment
OR
- exemption
From Federal Heavy Vehicle Use Tax:
Before registration issuance.

==================================================
VEHICLES SUBJECT TO HVUT
==================================================

Vehicles requiring HVUT include:
- Truck tractors
- Heavy trucks
- Buses

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMBINED GVW RULE
==================================================

Truck tractors:
Require HVUT:
At combined GVW:
55,000 pounds or more.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HEAVY TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Heavy trucks:
Require HVUT:
At GVW:
55,000 pounds or more.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUS RULE
==================================================

Buses:
Require HVUT:
At GVW:
55,000 pounds or more.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE HVUT PROOF
==================================================

Acceptable proof includes:
- IRS stamped Schedule 1 Form 2290
- Completed Form 2290 with cancelled check
- IRS stamped exempt Form 2290
- Electronically filed Form 2290

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STAMPED FORM RULE
==================================================

IRS stamped Schedule 1:
Acceptable proof:
When stamped:
"Received" by IRS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CANCELLED CHECK RULE
==================================================

If non-receipted Form 2290 used:
Require:
Copy of both sides:
Of cancelled check.

==================================================
HVUT EXEMPTION RULES
==================================================

Exempt proof allowed for:
- Vehicles traveling under mileage thresholds
- Agricultural vehicles
- Blood collector vehicles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 5,000 MILE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle:
55,000 pounds or more:
Traveling less than:
5,000 miles:
May qualify for exemption.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AGRICULTURAL RULE
==================================================

Agricultural vehicle:
Traveling less than:
7,500 miles:
May qualify for exemption.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BLOOD COLLECTOR RULE
==================================================

Blood collector vehicles:
May qualify:
For HVUT exemption.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRONIC FILING RULE
==================================================

Electronically filed Form 2290:
Must show:
Visible IRS watermark:
"Received XX/XX/XXXX"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WATERMARK RULE
==================================================

IRS watermark:
May appear faint.

Clerk must:
- verify watermark
- circle stamped area
- enter IRS received date
- enter full User ID

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALTERED VIN RULE
==================================================

Form 2290:
Must NOT be accepted:
If VIN or category:
Appears altered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 25 VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Businesses:
25 or more vehicles:
Required by IRS:
To electronically file Form 2290.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CURRENT TAX YEAR RULE
==================================================

Form 2290:
Must be:
For current IRS tax year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT JULY-SEPTEMBER RULE
==================================================

Registrations:
During July, August, September:
May use:
Current OR previous fiscal year:
Schedule 1 Form 2290.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURCHASE WITHIN 60 DAYS RULE
==================================================

If vehicle purchased:
Within 60 days:
Customer may avoid:
HVUT proof requirement:
With:
- bill of sale
OR
- acceptable purchase documentation
AND
- HSMV 82040/82041.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LATE TITLE RULE
==================================================

If HSMV 82040 submitted:
More than 60 days after purchase:
HVUT proof required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVW MATCH RULE
==================================================

GVW declared:
On registration:
Must be:
Equal to or less than:
GVW category:
Declared on Form 2290.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COPY RETENTION RULE
==================================================

License plate agent:
Must retain copy:
Of HVUT proof submitted.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS IRS RULES
==================================================

If customer lacks acceptable proof:
Customer must visit:
IRS office:
For assistance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-62 RULE
==================================================

HVUT reporting requirements:
Governed by:
TL-62.

==================================================
HVUT ERROR REPORT RULES
==================================================

If county receives:
HVUT Error Report:
Response required:
Within 15 working days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL FIVE DAYS RULE
==================================================

Additional:
5 working days:
May be granted:
If more time required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION STOP RULE
==================================================

Failure to submit required HVUT documents:
May result in:
Registration Stop:
Preventing renewal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FRAUD RULE
==================================================

If suspicious/questionable documents:
Notation must be made:
On EOD Report.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FHWA VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Suspicious documents:
Forwarded by FLHSMV:
To FHWA:
For verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EIN RULE
==================================================

EIN:
Must be completed:
On Form 2290.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SSN RULE
==================================================

If:
- Social Security Number
OR
- "Applied For"

Used instead of EIN:
Application should be rejected.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUNBIZ RULE
==================================================

EIN may be checked:
Using Sunbiz.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-INCORPORATED RULE
==================================================

If business not incorporated:
EIN may NOT verify:
Within Sunbiz.

Application should NOT automatically reject.

==================================================
LEASE / REGISTRANT MISMATCH RULE
==================================================

If registrant differs:
From Form 2290 owner:
Verification required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEASE DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Require:
Lease documentation:
Showing:
- lessee/registrant relationship
- vehicle description.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIVISION OF CORPORATIONS RULE
==================================================

Alternative proof:
Division of Corporations (Sunbiz) documentation:
Showing connection:
Between owner and registrant.

==================================================
ALTERNATE PROOF OF PAYMENT PROGRAM
==================================================

Some counties participate:
In:
Alternate Proof of Payment of 2290 Program.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUPPLEMENTAL DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Participating counties:
Require:
Supplemental reporting documents.
