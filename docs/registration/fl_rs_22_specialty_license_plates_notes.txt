==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Specialty license plates:
Available to public:
Without specific qualifications:
Unless plate has statutory eligibility requirements.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST FUND RULE
==================================================

Specialty plates:
Often referred to as:
Trust-fund license plates.

==================================================
SPECIAL QUALIFICATION EXCEPTIONS
==================================================

Specialty plates requiring qualifications include:
- Fraternal Order of Police
- US Paratrooper
- Divine Nine organizations

==================================================
DOCUMENTATION AND SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS
==================================================

Any county tax collector or license plate agency:
May process:
Specialty plate applications.

==================================================
US PARATROOPER RULE
==================================================

US Paratrooper plate:
Requires:
- HSMV 83034
- Jump school proof
OR
- Decorated parachutist proof

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PARATROOPER ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Only current/former U.S. military:
Trained to jump from aircraft:
Eligible.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-15 RULE
==================================================

US Paratrooper procedures:
Governed by:
RS-15.

==================================================
FRATERNAL ORDER OF POLICE RULE
==================================================

FOP plate:
Requires:
Proof member in good standing.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOP LETTER RULE
==================================================

Customer must provide:
FOP letter:
On organization verification.

==================================================
DIVINE NINE RULE
==================================================

Divine Nine plates require:
- Current membership card
OR
- Membership letter on organization letterhead.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIVINE NINE CURRENT RULE
==================================================

Membership proof:
Cannot be expired.

==================================================
ELIGIBLE VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

Specialty plates may issue to:
- Owned vehicles
- Leased vehicles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PROHIBITED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Cannot issue specialty plates to:
- Tractors
- Restricted plates
- Apportioned plates
- Indefinite plates
- Motorcycle-size plates
Except:
Motorcycle specialty plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-53 RULE
==================================================

Leased vehicle registration:
Governed by:
RS-53.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Refer:
HSMV 83140:
For specialty fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DOUBLE FEES RULE
==================================================

If registration exceeds:
13 months:
All applicable fees doubled:
Including:
- Annual use fee
- EMS
- Law Enforcement Radio fee
- Juvenile Justice fee
- Transportation Disadvantage fee
- State Transportation Surcharge

==================================================
SPECIALTY VOUCHER RULES
==================================================

Anyone may purchase:
Specialty Plate Voucher:
For presale specialty plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECIPIENT RULE
==================================================

Voucher issued:
For:
Specific customer
And specific specialty plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VEHICLE OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Voucher cannot redeem:
Unless recipient:
Has vehicle in their name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO SWAP RULE
==================================================

Voucher for one specialty plate:
Cannot apply:
To different specialty plate.

==================================================
VOUCHER FEES RULE
==================================================

Voucher purchase includes:
- Processing fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee
- Specialty plate fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FUTURE FEES RULE
==================================================

Remaining fees:
Collected:
When actual plate issued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Vouchers:
Have no expiration date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ONE-TIME USE RULE
==================================================

Voucher:
Valid one time only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONTRANSFERABLE RULE
==================================================

Voucher cannot be:
- Transferred
- Replaced
- Exchanged

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REFUND RULE
==================================================

Refund available:
If:
- Plate met presale requirement but not issued
OR
- Plate deauthorized.

==================================================
GIFT CERTIFICATE RULES
==================================================

Gift certificates:
Available:
For established specialty plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT CERTIFICATE REFUND RULE
==================================================

Gift certificates:
Cannot be refunded.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPONSORING ORGANIZATION RULE
==================================================

Gift certificate money:
Goes to:
Sponsoring organization.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-20 RULE
==================================================

Presale specialty plate information:
Governed by:
RS-20.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-67 RULE
==================================================

Voucher processing:
Governed by:
RS-67.

==================================================
RENEWAL AND TRANSFER RULES

REPLACEMENT REQUIREMENTS

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-06 RULE
==================================================

Replacement fees and procedures:

RS-06.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

If customer attests:
Plate/decal stolen:
And reported to law enforcement:
Replacement fee waived.

DISTRIBUTION RULES

==================================================
PERSONALIZED SPECIALTY PLATE RULE
==================================================

All specialty plates:
May be personalized.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-27 RULE
==================================================

Personalized plate rules:
Governed by:
RS-27.

==================================================
DISCONTINUATION RULES
==================================================

FLHSMV may discontinue:
Specialty plate:
Below 3,000 active registrations:
For 12 consecutive months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WARNING LETTER RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV sends:
Warning letter:
To sponsoring organization:
Below threshold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COLLEGIATE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Collegiate plates:
Exempt:
From minimum sales requirement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT JANUARY 1 RULE
==================================================

January 1 each year:
FLHSMV discontinues:
Plate with fewest registrations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOTTOM 10 PERCENT RULE
==================================================

Bottom 10% specialty plates:
Receive warning notification:
Each December 1.

==================================================
REDESIGN RULES
==================================================

Sponsoring organization:
Cannot redesign plate:
Unless:
- Inventory depleted
OR
- Organization purchases remaining inventory.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIDE RULE
==================================================

Remaining inventory:
Sent to PRIDE:
For destruction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO INVENTORY RELEASE RULE
==================================================

Destroyed inventory:
Not available:
To organization.

==================================================
AUDIT RULES
==================================================

Organizations receiving annual use fees:
Must submit:
Annual audit or attestation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-39 RULE
==================================================

Audit/attestation requirements:
Governed by:
RS-39.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION CLOSURE RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Must notify FLHSMV:
If organization ceases to exist.

==================================================

supporting organizations,
causes,
universities,
and interests,

qualification rules,
vehicle eligibility,
annual use fees,
voucher restrictions,
and ongoing registration thresholds.
