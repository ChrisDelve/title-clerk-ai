==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Temporary employed 90-day registration:
May be issued:
To applicant:
Temporarily employed in Florida:
But NOT Florida resident.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VALIDITY RULE
==================================================

Temporary employed plate:
Valid:
90 days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

One additional:
90-day registration:
May be issued.

Maximum:
Two temporary employed plates:
Within 12-month period.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 12-MONTH RULE
==================================================

12-month limitation:
Begins:
Date first temporary employed plate issued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 180-DAY RULE
==================================================

After:
180 days:
Applicant must apply:
For permanent registration:
If continued Florida residency/use required.

==================================================
PLATE INVENTORY RULE
==================================================

Plate range:
TE00000 through ZZ99999:
Used for temporarily employed plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INVENTORY RULE
==================================================

Temporary employed plates:
Must be entered:
Into motor vehicle issuance system inventory.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SYSTEM VALIDATION RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle issuance system:
Tracks:
- original issue date
- number of plates issued

To prevent:
More than two plates:
Within same 12-month period.

==================================================
DISPLAY RULES
==================================================

Temporary employed plates:
Must display:
Conspicuously:
On rear bracket.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FRONT DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

If vehicle normally requires front plate:
Temporary plate:
Must display:
On front:
Where normal plate would be mounted.

==================================================
VEHICLE ELIGIBILITY RULES
==================================================

Temporary employed plates:
May issue:
On most motor vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Cannot issue:
To:
- commercial truck tractors
OR
- heavy trucks
26,001 pounds GVW or more

Unless:
Owned or operated by government entity.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Applicant must provide:
- out-of-state registration copy
AND
- proof of temporary employment.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE EMPLOYMENT VERIFICATION
==================================================

Acceptable proof includes:
- employer verification letter
- customer affidavit
- HSMV 83080.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EMPLOYER LETTER RULE
==================================================

Employer letter:
Must state:
Applicant temporarily employed in Florida.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Customer affidavit:
Must specify:
- temporary employment
AND
- employment location in Florida.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83080 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 83080:
May be used:
As employment verification affidavit/application.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Temporary employed plate fee:
$40:
Plus applicable statutory/service fees.

==================================================
ADDITIONAL FEES RULE
==================================================

Additional fees may include:
- service fee
- branch fee
- FRVIS fee
- EMS fee
- Air Pollution Control fee
- Transportation Disadvantaged fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CLASS CODE RULE
==================================================

Fee determination:
Requires:
Motor Vehicle/Mobile Home Class Code/Fee Chart.

==================================================
INSURANCE RULES
==================================================

Proof of insurance:
Required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state insurance:
Acceptable:
For temporarily employed plate issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance processing:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Lost, stolen, or defaced plates:
Processed:
Per RS-06.

==================================================
LOST-IN-TRANSIT RULES
==================================================

Lost-in-transit temporary employed plates:
May be replaced:
Using HSMV 83146.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LOST-IN-TRANSIT FEE RULE
==================================================

No fee:
For lost-in-transit replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Replacement plate:
Keeps:
Original expiration date.

==================================================
CORRECTION RULES
==================================================

Corrected registration:
May process:
Without fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORRECTION EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Corrected registration:
Carries forward:
Original expiration date.

==================================================
DUPLICATE REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Duplicate registration certificate:
May issue:
With required service fee.

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK / REGISTRATION ERRORS
==================================================

- Issuing more than two plates in 12 months
- Missing out-of-state registration copy
- Missing employment verification
- Accepting invalid affidavit
- Issuing plate to Florida resident improperly
- Ignoring heavy truck restriction
- Incorrect fee calculation
- Rejecting valid out-of-state insurance
- Missing inventory entry
- Incorrect plate display instructions
- Charging lost-in-transit replacement fee
- Incorrect expiration date on replacement
- Missing HSMV 83146
- Incorrect correction processing
- Failing to require permanent registration after 180 days

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida temporarily employed 90-day registration procedures:
Allow non-residents
temporarily employed in Florida
to legally operate vehicles
through:
- short-term registration
- limited renewals
- employment verification
- temporary insurance acceptance

while enforcing:
- strict issuance limits
- vehicle eligibility restrictions
- inventory tracking
- insurance compliance
and transition to permanent registration after 180 days.
