==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Before specialty plate manufacturing:
Minimum:
3,000 pre-sale vouchers:
Must be sold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 24-MONTH RULE
==================================================

Specialty plate organizations:
Must meet:
3,000 voucher sales:
Within:
24 months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXTENSION RULE
==================================================

If minimum sales:
Not achieved during initial 24 months:
Pre-sale period:
Extended:
Additional 24 months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MANUFACTURING RULE
==================================================

Plate manufacturing:
Cannot begin:
Until:
3,000 voucher requirement met.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE RANGE RULE
==================================================

Plate ranges:
Will NOT be assigned:
Until pre-sale requirements met.

==================================================
SPECIALTY PLATE ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Specialty plates may issue:
To owner or lessee:
Of motor vehicle:
Except:
- IRP vehicles
- commercial trucks requiring two plates
- truck tractors.

==================================================
VOUCHER PROCESSING RULES
==================================================

Pre-sale vouchers:
Processed:
Using Specialty Plate Voucher transaction:
In motor vehicle issuance system.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES STATUS RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle issuance system:
Determines:
Whether voucher may issue:
Based on:
Sales status code.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Pre-sale voucher transaction fees include:
- specialty plate fee
- processing fee
- service fee
- branch fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FUTURE FEES RULE
==================================================

All other applicable registration fees:
Collected:
When specialty plate issued.

==================================================
VOUCHER REDEMPTION RULES
==================================================

Once specialty plate authorized:
Voucher:
May ONLY be used:
For that specific specialty plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Voucher may be redeemed:
- when specialty plate becomes available
OR
- at customer renewal.

==================================================
FAILED PRESALE RULES
==================================================

If specialty plate:
Fails presale requirement:
Plate:
Will not be manufactured.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REFUND RULE
==================================================

If specialty plate:
Fails minimum presale requirements:
Customer may apply:
For refund:
Of specialty plate fee only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83363 RULE
==================================================

Refund requests:
Require:
HSMV 83363.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ISSUED BUT UNAVAILABLE RULE
==================================================

If specialty plate:
Met presale requirement:
But not yet issued:
Customer may request refund:
Using HSMV 83363.

==================================================
DEAUTHORIZATION RULES
==================================================

FLHSMV may deauthorize:
Specialty license plate:
If valid registrations:
Fall below:
3,000 registrations:
For 12 consecutive months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEAUTHORIZED VOUCHER RULE
==================================================

If plate:
Met voucher minimum:
But later deauthorized:
Unused voucher:
Cannot:
- transfer to another specialty plate
OR
- be refunded.

==================================================
GIFT CERTIFICATE RULES
==================================================

Gift certificates:
May be purchased:
For established specialty plates:
NOT in presale process.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT CERTIFICATE PROCESSING RULE
==================================================

Gift certificates:
Processed and redeemed:
Same as pre-sale vouchers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT CERTIFICATE MONEY RULE
==================================================

Gift certificate proceeds:
Go directly:
To sponsoring organization.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT CERTIFICATE REFUND RULE
==================================================

Gift certificates:
ARE NOT refundable.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CREDIT RULE
==================================================

If specialty plate:
Deauthorized
OR
met presale but not issued:
Annual use fee:
May apply:
As credit toward another specialty plate:
With same annual use fee.
