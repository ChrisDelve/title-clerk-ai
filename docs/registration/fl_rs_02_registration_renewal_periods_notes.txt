==================================================
DEFINITIONS
==================================================

==================================================
REGISTRATION PERIOD
==================================================

Registration period:
Time vehicle registration remains valid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION LENGTH RULE
==================================================

Registration periods:
- 12 months
OR
- 24 months.

==================================================
ANNUAL REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Annual registration:
Cannot exceed:
15 months.

==================================================
BIENNIAL REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Biennial registration:
Cannot exceed:
27 months.

==================================================
RENEWAL PERIOD DEFINITION
==================================================

Renewal period:
Time registration renewal must occur.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NATURAL PERSON RULE
==================================================

Natural person registration:
Expires:
At midnight on owner's birthday.

==================================================
ADVANCE RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Advance renewal:
Allowed during:
Three months before birth month:
Through owner's birthday.

==================================================
EXTENDED REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Extended registration:
24-month registration validity.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BIENNIAL FEE RULE
==================================================

Biennial registration:
Requires:
Two-year total:
Of taxes and fees.

==================================================
SHORT TERM RENTAL VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Short-term rental vehicle:
Lease period:
Less than 12 months.

==================================================
LONG TERM LEASE RULE
==================================================

Long-term lease:
Lease period:
12 months or longer.

==================================================
REGISTRATION AND RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

==================================================
NATURAL PERSON REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Natural person registration:
Begins:
Midnight on owner's birthday.

Ends:
Midnight on next birthday.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MULTIPLE OWNER RULE
==================================================

Multiple owners:
First listed owner's birthday:
Determines registration period.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NATURAL PERSON RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Renewal period:
30 days prior to owner's birthday.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Expiration:
Midnight of birthday.

==================================================
MOBILE HOME REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Mobile home registration:
January 1 through December 31.

==================================================
MOBILE HOME RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Renewal period:
31 days before expiration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Mobile home expiration:
December 31.

==================================================
BUSINESS REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Company/entity registration:
June 1 through May 31:
Unless otherwise specified.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUSINESS EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Business registration expiration:
June 30.

==================================================
SHORT TERM RENTAL RULE
==================================================

Short-term rental vehicles:
Use same June-May cycle.

REG USE:
SL

==================================================
RENTAL CAR PERMANENT REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Rental companies:
May choose:
Regular or permanent registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PM DECAL RULE
==================================================

Permanent rental registration:
Yellow decal:
Displays:
PM

==================================================
PERMANENT DECAL ISSUED RULE
==================================================

Paper registration:
Displays:
"Permanent Decal Issued"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOR-HIRE SIX MONTH RULE
==================================================

For-hire vehicles:
May obtain:
Six-month registration.

REG USE:
HH

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOR-HIRE GRACE PERIOD RULE
==================================================

No grace period:
For six-month for-hire registrations.

==================================================
DUI REINSTATEMENT RULE
==================================================

Driver license reinstated after DUI suspension:
Requires:
Six-month registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUI REGISTRATION PERIOD RULE
==================================================

DUI reinstatement registration:
Begins:
First day of birth month.

Ends:
Last day of fifth month after birth month.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUI FEES RULE
==================================================

DUI reinstatement:
Requires:
One-half annual tax:
Plus applicable fees.

==================================================
HEAVY TRUCK RULES
==================================================

Heavy trucks:
5,001–7,999 lbs:
Use birthday registration cycle.

==================================================
HEAVY TRUCK OVER 7999 RULE
==================================================

Heavy trucks over 7,999 lbs:
Truck tractors:
Semi-trailers:
Buses:
December 1 through November 30 cycle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HEAVY TRUCK EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck expiration:
December 31.

==================================================
SEMIANNUAL HEAVY TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Semiannual registration allowed:
For:
- Heavy trucks
- Truck tractors
- Buses

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEMIANNUAL ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Semiannual allowed only if:
Annual tax exceeds $100.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APPORTIONED RULE
==================================================

Apportioned vehicles:
Cannot use semiannual registration.

==================================================
SEMIANNUAL REG USE RULE
==================================================

Heavy trucks/truck tractors:
REG USE:
HY

Buses:
REG USE:
HY

==================================================
FIRST SEMIANNUAL PERIOD RULE
==================================================

First semiannual period:
December 1 through May 31.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FIRST PERIOD GRACE RULE
==================================================

No grace period:
For first semiannual period.

==================================================
SECOND SEMIANNUAL PERIOD RULE
==================================================

Second semiannual period:
June 1 through November 30.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SECOND PERIOD GRACE RULE
==================================================

Second semiannual period:
Grace period exists through December 31.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NEW DECAL RULE
==================================================

Additional tax months collected:
Requires:
New validation decal issuance.

==================================================
FOR-HIRE LEASED VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

For-hire leased vehicles:
Must be registered:
In lessee's name.

==================================================
NATURAL PERSON LESSEE RULE
==================================================

Natural person lessee:
Uses birthday registration cycle.

==================================================
COMPANY LESSEE RULE
==================================================

Company lessee:
Uses staggered expiration:
Based on company name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STAGGERED EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Company registration expiration:
Determined by:
First alpha character:
Of company name.

==================================================
STAGGERED COMPANY EXPIRATION CHART
==================================================

A = June 30
B/C = July 31
D/E = August 31
F = September 30
G = October 31
H/I/J = November 30
K/L = December 31
M/N = January 31
O/P/Q = February 29
R/S/T/U = March 31
V = April 30
W/X/Y/Z = May 31

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NUMERIC COMPANY RULE
==================================================

Numeric company names:
Use:
First alpha character.

Example:
123 Leasing:
Expires December 31.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALL NUMERIC COMPANY RULE
==================================================

All numeric company names:
Assigned:
June 30 expiration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT "THE" COMPANY RULE
==================================================

If company begins with "The":
Use first alpha character:
After "The"

Example:
The Hertz Corporation:
Expires November 30.

==================================================
DEALER REGISTRATION RULES
==================================================

Dealer plates:
Use same period:
As dealer license.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Current dealer license:
Required:
For registration period.

==================================================
FRANCHISED DEALER RULE
==================================================

Franchised dealers:
January through December.

Expiration:
December 31.

==================================================
INDEPENDENT DEALER RULE
==================================================

Independent dealers:
May through April.

Expiration:
April 30.

==================================================
RV DEALER RULE
==================================================

RV dealers:
October through September.

Expiration:
September 30.

==================================================
MARINE TRAILER DEALER RULE
==================================================

Marine boat trailer dealers:
July through June.

Expiration:
June 30.

==================================================
TRANSPORTER PLATE RULE
==================================================

Transporter plates:
January through December.

Expiration:
December 31.

==================================================
DELINQUENT REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Delinquent fee may apply:
If renewal not completed:
By 11th calendar day:
Of month following due month.

==================================================
DECEASED OWNER RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV allows:
One-time 12-month renewal:
In deceased owner's name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECEASED OWNER RULE
==================================================

Renewal must occur:
Within:
One year of death.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECEASED OWNER DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Requires:
Will or legal proof.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VESSEL EXCLUSION RULE
==================================================

Vessels:
Do NOT qualify:
For deceased-owner renewal.

==================================================
BIENNIAL REGISTRATION RESTRICTIONS
==================================================

Cannot use biennial registration:
- Restricted plates
- National Guard exemption
- Out-of-state military class 14 or 50
- Heavy trucks over 8,000 lbs
- Semi-trailers class 56 or 103
- Transporters
- Dealer registrations
- Government vehicles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BIENNIAL HEAVY TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Class code 41:
Only eligible:
If under 8,000 lbs.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER BIENNIAL RULE
==================================================

Dealer registrations:
Cannot:
Use biennial registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOVERNMENT BIENNIAL RULE
==================================================

Government vehicles:
Cannot:
Use biennial registration.
