==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Owner/registrant:
Should surrender:
License plate and decal:
Before cancelling insurance:
On registered vehicle.

==================================================
WHERE TO SURRENDER LICENSE PLATE
==================================================

License plates may be surrendered:
At:
- Tax collector office
- License plate agency
- Driver license office

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MAIL SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

If surrendering by mail:
Require:
- Signed written statement
- Reason for surrender
- Copy of photo identification

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IN-PERSON RULE
==================================================

If surrendering in person:
Identification required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT THIRD-PARTY RULE
==================================================

If person other than owner:
Surrenders plate:
Their photo ID:
Must be scanned or retained:
With affidavit/documentation:
For one year.

==================================================
WHEN TO SURRENDER LICENSE PLATE
==================================================

Plate should be surrendered:
Before:
Cancelling insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE OF SALE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle disposed of:
Customer must:
- Surrender plate
OR
- Submit HSMV 82050

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Failure to surrender plate:
Or file HSMV 82050:
May cause:
Financial responsibility suspension.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UNAUTHORIZED USE RULE
==================================================

Owner remains responsible:
For unauthorized use:
If plate not surrendered.

==================================================
TOWING / REPOSSESSION RULES
==================================================

If towed/repossessed vehicle:
Has valid or expired plate:
Owner should retrieve plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPOSSESSION SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

If owner does not retrieve plate:
Towing/repo company:
Should surrender plate:
To tax collector or agent.

==================================================
UNAVAILABLE PLATE RULES
==================================================

If plate physically unavailable:
Perjury-clause affidavit:
Accepted:
Instead of actual plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UNAVAILABLE REQUIREMENTS RULE
==================================================

Require:
- Proof of identity
- Signed affidavit
- Plate number
- Reason unavailable

==================================================
UNAVAILABLE REASONS
==================================================

Examples:
- Lost
- Destroyed
- Stolen

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN RULE
==================================================

If plate stolen:
Customer must provide:
- Police report
OR
- Case number

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AFFIDAVIT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Unavailable plate affidavit:
Must retain:
One year.

==================================================
DISPOSAL RULES
==================================================

Surrendered/cancelled/obsolete/defaced plates:
Listed on:
HSMV 84003.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIDE DISPOSAL RULE
==================================================

Plates packaged:
For PRIDE pickup:
With:
Original or copy:
Of HSMV 84003.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AUDIT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Copy of HSMV 84003:
Retained:
Until year-end audit completed.

==================================================
AUTHENTICATED PLATE RULES
==================================================

Authenticated plate owner:
May retain plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL-ONLY RULE
==================================================

Authenticated plate owner:
May surrender:
Decal only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT RULE
==================================================

Issuance system comments:
Must state:

"Authenticated plate retained by owner/registrant."

==================================================
STOLEN PLATE REPORTING RULE
==================================================

Owner responsible:
For reporting stolen plate:
To law enforcement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-06 RULE
==================================================

Stolen plate procedures:
Governed by:
RS-06.

==================================================
SURRENDER RECEIPT RULES
==================================================

Driver license office:
Must provide receipt:
Showing:
- Plate number
- Proof of surrender

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTOR VEHICLE OFFICE RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle office:
Must provide receipt:
Showing:
- Plate number
- Proof of surrender

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUAL-SYSTEM RULE
==================================================

If office processes:
Driver license and motor vehicle:
Plate surrender must process:
In:
- Motor vehicle issuance system
AND
- ORION

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOTH RECEIPTS RULE
==================================================

Customer receives:
Receipt from both systems.

==================================================
REFUND RULES
==================================================

Customer may qualify:
For refund:
Under RS-65.

==================================================
INSURANCE SUSPENSION REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

If plate surrendered:
Due to insurance suspension:
Replacement processing:
Must complete:
Specific FRVIS cancellation steps first.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAYMENT RULE
==================================================

Replacement plate:
Cannot issue:
Without payment:
After insurance suspension.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEIZED PLATE RULE
==================================================

If law enforcement seized plate:
Due to insurance suspension:
Replacement:
Requires FRVIS cancellation process first.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AD CANCEL RULE
==================================================

Seized insurance-suspension plate:
Uses:
Cancel reason:
"AD"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE STATUS RULE
==================================================

Even if insurance existed:
Replacement fee still required:
For suspended/seized plate replacement.

COMMON TITLE CLERK / REGISTRATION ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing HSMV 82050
- Missing identification
- Improper third-party surrender handling
- Missing unavailable plate affidavit
- Missing police report/case number
- Failure to retain affidavit one year
- Improper disposal tracking
- Missing HSMV 84003
- Missing PRIDE pickup packaging
- Failure to enter authenticated plate comment
- Failure to process in ORION
- Missing surrender receipt
- Incorrect insurance suspension replacement handling
- Incorrect AD cancellation processing
- Issuing replacement without required payment

Florida license plate surrender procedures:
Protect vehicle owners from:
- insurance suspensions
- unauthorized plate use
- financial responsibility penalties

while requiring strict compliance with:
- identification verification
- surrender documentation
- cancellation processing
- disposal tracking
and insurance suspension replacement rules.
