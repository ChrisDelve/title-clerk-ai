==================================================
PRESS LICENSE PLATE RULES
==================================================

PRES plates:
Reserved for:
Members of press:
Associated with reporting media.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRESS NUMBER RULE
==================================================

PRES plate configurations:
Use:
"PRES"
Plus numbers:
1 through 999.

==================================================
LEGISLATIVE LICENSE PLATE TYPES
==================================================

Legislative plates include:
- Official Congress
- Official Senate
- Official House
- Senate President
- House Speaker
- Retired Senate
- Retired House

==================================================
OFFICIAL CONGRESS RULE
==================================================

Current U.S. Senator or Congress member:
Eligible for:
"Official Congress" plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONGRESS CONFIGURATION RULE
==================================================

Congress member:
May choose:
Any unused configuration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 4 CHARACTER RULE
==================================================

Configurations over 4 characters:
Should be reviewed.

==================================================
STATE SENATOR RULE
==================================================

Current State Senator:
Eligible for:
"Official Senate" plate.

==================================================
STATE HOUSE RULE
==================================================

Current State Representative:
Eligible for:
"Official House" plate.

==================================================
SENATE PRESIDENT RULE
==================================================

Current or former Senate President:
Eligible for:
"Senate President" plate.

==================================================
HOUSE SPEAKER RULE
==================================================

Current or former House Speaker:
Eligible for:
"House Speaker" plate.

==================================================
RETIRED SENATOR RULE
==================================================

Former State Senator:
Eligible for:
"Retired Senate" plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETIRED SENATOR REQUIREMENT RULE
==================================================

Must have served:
- At least 4 years total
AND
- At least 2 years prior to 01/01/2021.

==================================================
RETIRED REPRESENTATIVE RULE
==================================================

Former State Representative:
Eligible for:
"Retired House" plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETIRED REPRESENTATIVE REQUIREMENT RULE
==================================================

Must have served:
- At least 4 years total
AND
- At least 2 years prior to 01/01/2021.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLHSMV ONLY RULE
==================================================

Only FLHSMV:
May issue:
Legislative and Retired Legislative plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OFFICE OF LEGISLATIVE AFFAIRS RULE
==================================================

Applications submitted to:
FLHSMV Office of Legislative Affairs.

LEGISLATIVE APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- HSMV 83109
- Ownership proof
- Florida insurance
- Applicable fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Ownership proof may include:
- Registration certificate
- Florida title
- HSMV 82041 title receipt

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEAVING OFFICE RULE
==================================================

When elected official leaves office:
Legislative plate:
Must be surrendered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT PLATE RULE
==================================================

After surrender:
Customer must receive:
- Specialty plate
- Special plate
OR
- Florida graphic plate.

==================================================
RETIRED LEGISLATIVE FEE RULE
==================================================

Retired legislative plate:
Requires:
One-time $500 fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CAPITOL MUSEUM RULE
==================================================

$450 distributed:
To direct-support organization:
For Florida Historic Capitol Museum.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OPERATING TRUST FUND RULE
==================================================

Remaining $50:
Deposited:
Into Highway Safety Operating Trust Fund.

==================================================
PRESS PLATE APPLICATION RULES
==================================================

PRES plates:
Only ordered:
Through county license plate agency.

==================================================
PRESS APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- HSMV 83081
- Ownership proof
- Florida insurance
- Applicable fees

==================================================
PRESS FEES RULE
==================================================

Press plate fees include:
- Regular registration tax
- Personalized fee
- Processing fee
- Original plate fee
- Decal on demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGULAR CLASS CODE RULE
==================================================

Press plates:
Use:
Appropriate regular class codes:
Based on weight and vehicle type.

==================================================
RENEWAL AND TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Renewals and transfers:
May process:
Through county license plate agencies.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement PRES plates:
Processed by:
County license plate agent.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEGISLATIVE REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

Replacement Legislative or Retired Legislative plates:
Must be processed:
By FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83146 RULE
==================================================

Replacement requires:
HSMV 83146.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION COPY RULE
==================================================

Replacement requires:
Copy of registration certificate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-06 RULE
==================================================

Replacement procedures and fees:
Governed by:
RS-06.

==================================================
BUSINESS OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Business owned by:
- Legislative member
- Immediate family
- Dependent child

May receive:
Legislative plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETIRED BUSINESS RULE
==================================================

Retired legislative plates:
Cannot issue:
To business entities.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETIRED NAME RULE
==================================================

Retired legislative plate:
Only issued:
In legislator's own name.

AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is applicant legislative or press?
2. Does applicant qualify?
3. Is current or former office involved?
4. Is retired legislative eligibility satisfied?
5. Was 2-year pre-2021 requirement met?
6. Is FLHSMV-only issuance required?
7. Is county issuance allowed?
8. Is HSMV 83109 required?
9. Is HSMV 83081 required?
10. Is Florida insurance verified?
11. Is configuration over 4 characters?
12. Is replacement handled by FLHSMV?
13. Is HSMV 83146 required?
14. Must plate be surrendered?
15. Is retired one-time fee due?
16. Is business ownership eligible?
17. Is retired plate prohibited for business?
18. Are regular class codes used?
19. Is PRESS configuration valid?
20. Is Office of Legislative Affairs involved?

COMMON TITLE CLERK / REGISTRATION ERRORS
==================================================

- Attempting county issuance of legislative plate
- Incorrect retired legislator qualification
- Missing HSMV 83109
- Missing HSMV 83081
- Missing Florida insurance
- Improper business ownership approval
- Allowing retired plate to business
- Missing surrender after office departure
- Incorrect fee calculations
- Incorrect replacement routing
- Missing registration copy
- Not reviewing long configurations
- Incorrect PRES numbering
- Using wrong class codes

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida legislative and press plates:
Are restricted prestige registrations
available only to specifically qualified legislators,
retired legislators,
and members of the reporting media,
with strict issuance authority,
eligibility,
and replacement controls.
