==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Transporter plates:
Issued to businesses:
That transport unregistered motor vehicles:
Incidental to business operations.

==================================================
ELIGIBLE BUSINESS RULES
==================================================

Eligible businesses may include:
- Motor vehicle detail shops
- Van conversion shops
- Equipment installation businesses
- Licensed repossessors
- Drive-away services
- Motor vehicle dealers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INCIDENTAL BUSINESS RULE
==================================================

Transport activity:
Must be incidental:
To primary business activity.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPOSSESSOR RULE
==================================================

Licensed repossessors:
Eligible:
If contracted with lending institutions:
To repossess/recover vehicles or mobile homes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DRIVE-AWAY RULE
==================================================

Drive-away services:
Eligible:
When providing drivers:
To deliver unregistered vehicles.

==================================================
NOT ELIGIBLE RULES
==================================================

Not eligible:
- Banks/lending institutions
- Vehicle transport operators using carriers
- Businesses transporting people
- Mobile home dealers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BANK RULE
==================================================

Banks/lending institutions:
Must use:
Temporary license plates:
When demonstrating or repossessing vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CAR CARRIER RULE
==================================================

Vehicle transport operators using:
- Trucks
- Truck tractors
- Car carriers
- Low-boys
- Trailers

NOT eligible:
For transporter plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT POWER UNIT RULE
==================================================

Power unit transporting vehicles:
Must display:
Regular GVW plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER RULE
==================================================

Trailer transporting vehicles:
Must display:
Regular trailer plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PEOPLE TRANSPORT RULE
==================================================

Businesses transporting people:
NOT eligible:
For transporter plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME DEALER RULE
==================================================

Mobile home dealers:
NOT eligible:
For transporter plates.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- HSMV 83065
- Insurance proof
- Business documentation
- Applicable fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FIVE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Maximum:
Five transporter plates:
May process:
At county office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGIONAL OFFICE RULE
==================================================

If entity exceeds:
Five transporter plates:
Application must process:
Through FLHSMV Regional Office.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
Garage or commercial automobile liability insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MINIMUM INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Minimum insurance:
$100,000 liability coverage.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACCEPTABLE INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Acceptable insurance proof:
- Florida insurance policy
- Binder
- Certificate of insurance

==================================================
BUSINESS DOCUMENTATION RULE
==================================================

Require:
- Occupational license
- Business license
OR
- Department of State documentation

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Fees based on:
Class code:
70

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APPLICATION FEES RULE
==================================================

Application fees include:
- Registration tax
- Original plate fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEE CHART RULE
==================================================

Refer:
Registration Fees and Flat Taxes Distribution Chart:
For applicable fees.

==================================================
RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

Renewals:
Processed:
Through county license plate agencies.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENEWAL INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Renewal requires:
Proof of sufficient liability insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENEWAL FEES RULE
==================================================

Renewal fees include:
- Registration tax
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement/additional plates:
Limited:
To five plates:
Per entity:
At county office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT THRESHOLD RULE
==================================================

If:
- More than five plates issued
OR
- More than five replacements in calendar year

Must process:
Through FLHSMV Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83146 RULE
==================================================

Replacement requires:
HSMV 83146.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Replacement requires:
Proof of sufficient liability insurance.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT FEES RULE
==================================================

Replacement fees include:
- Replacement plate fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable
- Advanced Replacement fee
- Air Pollution Control fee
- EMS fee
- FRVIS fee
- Reflectorization fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN PLATE RULE
==================================================

If stolen plate/decal:
Refer:
RS-06.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION COPY RULE
==================================================

Replacement requires:
Copy of registration certificate.

==================================================
SURRENDER RULES
==================================================

Transporter plates:
May surrender:
To:
- Local Tax Collector
OR
- Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida transporter license plates:
Are limited-use commercial registrations
intended only for businesses
that incidentally transport unregistered vehicles
as part of legitimate business operations,
subject to strict eligibility,
insurance,
and plate quantity controls.
