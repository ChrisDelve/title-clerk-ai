==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Miccosukee and Seminole Tribal license plates:
Issued:
At no fee:
To eligible tribal members:
For vehicles owned and operated:
By tribal members.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIRECT MAIL RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV Direct Mail Section:
Processes ALL:
- Issuance
- Renewal
- Transfer
- Replacement
Transactions:
For Indian Tribal plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COUNTY OFFICE RULE
==================================================

County offices:
Do NOT directly issue:
Indian Tribal plates.

==================================================
TRIBAL ELIGIBILITY RULES
==================================================

Tribal Clerk:
Determines:
Whether customer:
- Is tribal member
AND
- Meets tribal eligibility requirements.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRIBAL CLERK RULE
==================================================

If member eligible:
Tribal Clerk:
Submits documentation:
To FLHSMV for processing.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MAILING RULE
==================================================

Registration,
license plate,
and decal:
Mailed:
To Tribal Clerk office:
For distribution.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP RULES
==================================================

Acceptable proof:
- Florida registration certificate
- Florida title
- Florida title receipt

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE ONLY RULE
==================================================

Florida title applications:
Must process:
At tax collector or license plate agency:
As:
"Title Only" transactions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RULE
==================================================

Dealer selling vehicle:
To tribal member:
Must process:
Title Only transaction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRIBAL AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Tribal member:
Must sign affidavit:
Stating:
- Eligible for tribal plate
AND
- Registration request will be submitted through Tribal Clerk.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY PLATE RULE
==================================================

Dealer may issue:
Temporary plate:
If tribal member:
Has no plate to transfer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-31 RULE
==================================================

Temporary plate procedures:
Governed by:
RS-31.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-33 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-33.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

Tribal Clerk may notify FLHSMV:
When tribal plate surrendered:
For insurance compliance purposes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXHIBIT C RULE
==================================================

Exhibit C:
May be used:
To notify FLHSMV:
Plate surrendered due to lack of insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO OPERATION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle:
Cannot operate:
Until:
- New insurance provided
AND
- Valid plate obtained.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Indian Tribal plates:
Issued:
Free of charge.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNED AND OPERATED RULE
==================================================

Vehicle/motorcycle:
Must be:
Owned and operated:
By tribal member.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement plates/decals:
Issued:
By FLHSMV:
Upon Tribal Office submission.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- HSMV 83146
- Completed by tribal member

==================================================
HEAVY TRUCK RULES
==================================================

Commercial truck:
26,001 lbs or more:
Requires:
Front and rear plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Validation decal:
Affixed:
To front plate:
On heavy truck.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUMP TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Dump truck rear plate:
May mount:
On gate:
No higher than 60 inches.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUCK TRACTOR RULE
==================================================

Truck tractor:
Displays:
One front plate only.

==================================================
REGISTRATION EXPIRATION RULES
==================================================

Registration expires:
Midnight:
On first owner's birthday.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUCK EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Trucks over 5,000 lbs:
Expire:
December 31.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida Miccosukee and Seminole Indian Tribal license plates:
Are no-fee registrations
issued only to eligible tribal members,
processed centrally through FLHSMV Direct Mail
and coordinated through Tribal Clerk offices,
with strict ownership,
insurance,
and documentation requirements.
