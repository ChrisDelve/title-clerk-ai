==================================================
DEFINITION — WRECKER
==================================================

Wrecker:
Means:
Motor vehicle:
Used to:
- tow
- carry
OR
- transport motor vehicles

Using:
- boom
- winch
- car carrier
OR
similar equipment.

==================================================
DEFINITION — TOW
==================================================

Tow:
Means:
Pulling, drawing, or carrying:
Motor vehicle:
Using:
- direct attachment
- drawbar
- connection
OR
vehicle transport carrier.

==================================================
DEFINITION — DISABLED MOTOR VEHICLE
==================================================

Disabled motor vehicle:
Means:
Vehicle:
- not operable under own power
OR
- unsafe for highway operation.

==================================================
DEFINITION — REPLACEMENT MOTOR VEHICLE
==================================================

Replacement motor vehicle:
Means:
Vehicle transported:
To replace disabled vehicle:
And transfer:
- operator
- passengers
- load.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Wrecker classification:
Determined by:
Declared operational use:
And GVW usage.

==================================================
REGULAR WRECKER RULES
==================================================

Regular Wrecker:
May tow:
- disabled vehicles
- abandoned vehicles
- stolen recovered vehicles
- impound vehicles
- replacement motor vehicles
- vessels.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGULAR WRECKER RULE
==================================================

Regular Wrecker:
Requires:
Net weight and GVW entry:
Into motor vehicle issuance system.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HVUT RULE
==================================================

If GVW:
55,000 pounds or more:
IRS Schedule 1 Form 2290:
Required:
Even if exempt from payment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-34 RULE
==================================================

HVUT processing:
Governed by:
RS-34.

==================================================
GVW WRECKER RULES
==================================================

GVW Wrecker:
Registered:
Based on:
What it tows/hauls.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVW DECLARATION RULE
==================================================

If towing:
- nondisabled motor vehicle
- vessel
OR
- cargo

Declared GVW:
Must include:
Vehicle weight plus load carried.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABLED VEHICLE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

If GVW Wrecker:
Tows:
- disabled vehicle
- abandoned vehicle
- stolen recovered vehicle
- impound vehicle
- replacement vehicle

Weight restrictions:
Do NOT apply.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CARGO RULE
==================================================

GVW restrictions:
Still apply:
When hauling cargo.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Customer:
Must declare:
Intended wrecker usage.

==================================================
INSURANCE RULES
==================================================

Commercial motor vehicle insurance:
Required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-33 RULE
==================================================

Commercial insurance processing:
Governed by:
RS-33.

==================================================
HVUT APPLICATION RULE
==================================================

If combined GVW:
55,000 pounds or more:
HVUT proof required.

==================================================
FEE RULES — REGULAR WRECKER
==================================================

Regular Wrecker:
Uses:
Class code 92.

Fees include:
- Original plate fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee
- Registration tax
- Trust fund fees.

==================================================
FEE RULES — GVW WRECKER
==================================================

GVW Wrecker:
Uses:
Class code 41.

Fees based on:
Declared GVW.

==================================================
PLATE ISSUANCE RULES
==================================================

Regular Wrecker:
Single plate:
Displayed rear of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVW 26,000 RULE
==================================================

GVW Wrecker:
26,000 pounds or less:
Single rear plate required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVW 26,001 RULE
==================================================

GVW Wrecker:
26,001 pounds or more:
Requires:
Dual plates:
Front and rear.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VALIDATION DECAL RULE
==================================================

Validation decal:
Placed:
On front plate:
For dual plate GVW Wreckers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INVENTORY CODE RULE
==================================================

No separate inventory code:
Exists:
For:
- GVW Wrecker over 26,001
OR
- under 26,000.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXISTING DUAL PLATE RULE
==================================================

Previously issued dual plates:
For lighter wreckers:
May continue:
Until replacement cycle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIDE RULE
==================================================

PRIDE:
Continues issuing:
Dual plates:
Regardless of GVW.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUPLICATE HOLD RULE
==================================================

For lighter GVW Wreckers:
Unused duplicate plate:
Held:
For PRIDE pickup.

==================================================
RENEWAL AND TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Renewals/transfers:
May process:
At any county license plate agency.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WRECKER TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

"WRECKER" embossed plates:
May transfer ONLY:
To another wrecker vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-05 RULE
==================================================

Transfer processing:
Governed by:
RS-05.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement requires:
- registration certificate copy
- HSMV 83146
- applicable fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT FEE RULE
==================================================

Replacement fees may include:
- replacement fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- service fee
- branch fee
- Air Pollution fee
- Reflectorization fee
- EMS fee
- Advanced Replacement fee
- FRVIS fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN PLATE RULE
==================================================

If stolen:
And customer attests:
On HSMV 83146:
Replacement fee waived.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-06 RULE
==================================================

Replacement processing:
Governed by:
RS-06.
