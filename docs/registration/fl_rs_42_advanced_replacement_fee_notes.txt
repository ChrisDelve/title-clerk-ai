==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Florida metal license plates:
Must be replaced:
Every 10 years.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ARF RULE
==================================================

$28 replacement fee:
Collected incrementally:
At:
$2.80 per year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST FUND RULE
==================================================

ARF funds:
Deposited into:
Highway Safety Operating Trust Fund.

==================================================
ORIGINAL ISSUANCE RULES
==================================================

First-time plate issuance:
Requires:
- $28 plate fee
PLUS
- $2.80 ARF.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INITIAL ISSUE RULE
==================================================

Original metal plate issuance:
Total:
$30.80:
Before other applicable fees.

==================================================
ANNUAL REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Annual registration:
Collect:
$2.80 ARF.

==================================================
BIENNIAL REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Biennial registration:
Collect:
$5.60 ARF.

==================================================
RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

Each renewal:
Requires:
Additional $2.80 ARF assessment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DATABASE RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV system:
Retains:
ARF accumulation records.

==================================================
FORCED REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Plate replacement:
Required:
Every 10 years:
Based on original issue date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORCED CREDIT RULE
==================================================

During forced 10-year replacement:
Accumulated ARF credit:
May apply:
Toward replacement plate fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPECIALTY PLATE CREDIT RULE
==================================================

Customer:
Changing to:
- specialty plate
OR
- personalized plate

At forced replacement time:
May receive ARF credit.

==================================================
VOLUNTARY REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Voluntary replacement:
Occurs:
Before required 10-year replacement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO VOLUNTARY CREDIT RULE
==================================================

Voluntary replacement:
Does NOT allow:
Use of accumulated ARF credit.

==================================================
VOLUNTARY REPLACEMENT EXAMPLES
==================================================

Examples include:
- lost plate
- defaced plate
- voluntary specialty plate upgrade
- voluntary personalized plate upgrade.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO REFUND RULE
==================================================

No refund or credit:
Given:
For previously collected ARF:
If customer voluntarily replaces or surrenders plate early.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEPARTMENT-FORCED RULE
==================================================

Credit MAY apply:
If department requires replacement:
Under:
320.08056(8)(a) or (b) FS.

==================================================
EXEMPTION RULES
==================================================

Certain class codes:
Exempt:
From:
$28 replacement fee
AND
$2.80 ARF.

==================================================
EXEMPT CLASS CODES
==================================================

Exempt class codes include:
- 37
- 82
- 95
- 97

Old exempt codes:
- 24
- 81
- 82
- 83
- 85
- 87
- 97

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Mobile home class codes exempt:
- 50
- 51
- 37

Old codes:
- 50
- 51
- 86

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HISTORICAL PLATE RULE
==================================================

Historical Authenticated plates:
Class code 15:
Exempt from ARF.
