==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Motorcycles, mopeds, autocycles, and disability access vehicles:
Use:
Regular motorcycle-style license plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ANNUAL RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

These license plates:
Must be renewed:
Annually.

==================================================
MOTORCYCLE DEFINITION
==================================================

Motorcycle:
Vehicle:
- seat or saddle
- designed for not more than three wheels
- excludes tractor or moped.

==================================================
MOPED DEFINITION
==================================================

Moped:
Vehicle:
- pedals permitting human propulsion
- seat or saddle
- not more than three wheels
- maximum 2 brake horsepower
- maximum 30 MPH
- automatic/direct power-drive system
- internal combustion engine:
50cc or less.

==================================================
MOTORIZED DISABILITY ACCESS VEHICLE DEFINITION
==================================================

Motorized disability access vehicle:
Vehicle designed primarily:
For handicapped individuals:
- maximum 2 brake horsepower
- maximum 30 MPH
- not more than three wheels
- automatic/direct power-drive system
- internal combustion engine:
50cc or less.

==================================================
AUTOCYCLE DEFINITION
==================================================

Autocycle:
Three-wheeled motorcycle:
- two wheels front
- one wheel rear
- roll cage or roll hoops
- seat belts
- anti-lock brakes
- steering wheel
- non-straddle seating
- manufactured to federal motorcycle safety standards.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AUTOCYCLE RULE
==================================================

Motorcycle endorsement:
NOT required:
To operate autocycle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UNDER-21 RULE
==================================================

Motorcycles registered:
To persons under 21:
Must display:
Unique design/color license plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTORCYCLE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Motorcycle/moped plates:
Must remain:
Clearly visible from rear:
At all times.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONCEALMENT RULE
==================================================

Concealing or obscuring:
Motorcycle plate visibility:
Prohibited.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VERTICAL PLATE RULE
==================================================

Vertical motorcycle plate display:
Allowed:
If:
- numbers/letters read top-to-bottom
AND
- all tolls paid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REDUCED DIMENSION RULE
==================================================

Motorcycle-sized plates:
Dimensions:
4 inches × 7 inches.

==================================================
OFF-ROAD VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

Not registered:
- dirt bikes for off-road use
- Go-Peds
- electric motorized bicycles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GAS-POWERED BICYCLE RULE
==================================================

Gas-powered bicycles:
Defined as mopeds:
ARE registered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-68 RULE
==================================================

Motorized gas-powered bicycle registration:
Governed by:
RS-68.

==================================================
MOTOR SCOOTER RULE
==================================================

If MCO states:
"Motor scooter":
Use statutory definitions:
To determine:
- title requirement
- registration requirement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Entities selling:
- mopeds
OR
- motorcycles 50cc or less

Do NOT require:
Dealer license.

==================================================
AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

Autonomous motorcycles:
Processed:
Like similar standard motorcycles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEID RULE
==================================================

AV fleets:
Should title/register:
In company name:
Using FEID.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOVERNMENT AV RULE
==================================================

Government AV motorcycles:
Processed:
Like standard government vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-68 RULE
==================================================

AV registration requirements:
Governed additionally by:
TL-68.

==================================================
OWNERSHIP DOCUMENTATION RULES
==================================================

Motorcycles and autocycles:
Are titled and registered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOPED TITLE RULE
==================================================

Mopeds:
NOT titled:
BUT ARE registered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABILITY ACCESS TITLE RULE
==================================================

Motorized disability access vehicles:
NOT titled:
BUT ARE registered.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE OWNERSHIP DOCUMENTS
==================================================

For mopeds/disability access vehicles:
Acceptable ownership proof includes:
- bill of sale
- registration certificate
- Manufacturer’s Certificate of Origin
- ownership affidavit.

==================================================
SALES TAX RULES
==================================================

Sales tax:
Required:
If vehicle not already in customer’s name.

==================================================
INSURANCE RULES
==================================================

Proof of insurance:
NOT required:
For:
- motorcycles
- mopeds
- autocycles
- disability access vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AV INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

TL-68.

FEE RULES

VERY IMPORTANT NO IRF RULE
