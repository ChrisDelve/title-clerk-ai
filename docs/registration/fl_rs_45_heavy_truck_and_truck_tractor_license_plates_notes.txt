==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck and truck tractor registrations:
Based on:
Declared Gross Vehicle Weight (GVW).

==================================================
DEFINITION — HEAVY TRUCK
==================================================

Heavy truck:
Motor vehicle:
Net vehicle weight:
More than 5,000 pounds:
Registered by GVW:
Designed to carry goods
OR
Tow trailers.

==================================================
DEFINITION — TRUCK TRACTOR
==================================================

Truck tractor:
Motor vehicle:
With fifth wheel:
Designed primarily:
To pull semi-trailer.

==================================================
DEFINITION — COMMERCIAL MOTOR VEHICLE
==================================================

Commercial motor vehicle:
Vehicle:
- 26,001 pounds or more GVW
OR
- 3 or more axles
OR
- Combination over 26,001 pounds.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONCOMMERCIAL MOTORSPORT RULE
==================================================

Closed-course motorsport transport:
May avoid commercial classification:
If:
- Not for profit
AND
- No corporate sponsorship.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require ONE:
- Florida registration
- Florida title
- Florida title receipt
- Florida title application with supporting docs

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVW RULE
==================================================

Applicant must declare:
Gross Vehicle Weight.

==================================================
HVUT RULES
==================================================

If GVW:
55,000 pounds or more:
Require:
Proof of:
- Filing
- Payment
OR
- Exemption
From Federal Heavy Vehicle Use Tax.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-34 RULE
==================================================

HVUT procedures:
Governed by:
RS-34.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

If GVW:
26,001 pounds or more:
Require:
Commercial vehicle insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-33 RULE
==================================================

Commercial insurance:
Governed by:
RS-33.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

General insurance:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Fees include:
- Registration tax
- Original plate fee
- Decal on Demand fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CLASS CODE RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck registrations:
Use:
Class code 41.

==================================================
REGISTRATION USE RULES
==================================================

Reg Use FY:
Annual registration.

Reg Use HY:
Semiannual registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEMIANNUAL FEE RULE
==================================================

Semiannual registration:
Requires:
Additional $2.50 fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEE CHART RULE
==================================================

Refer:
Registration Fees and Flat Taxes Distribution Chart:
For applicable fees.

==================================================
DISPLAY RULES
==================================================

Heavy truck:
26,000 pounds or less:
One rear plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUAL PLATE RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck:
26,001 pounds or more:
Requires:
Front and rear plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Validation decal:
Affixed:
To front plate:
On dual-plate trucks.

==================================================
TRUCK TRACTOR DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

Truck tractor:
Displays:
One front plate only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUMP TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Dump truck rear plate:
May mount:
On gate:
Maximum 60 inches high.

==================================================
REGISTRATION PERIOD RULES
==================================================

Trucks:
May register:
- Semiannually
OR
- Annually.

==================================================
SEMIANNUAL PERIODS
==================================================

Semiannual periods:
- December through May
- June through November

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECEMBER 31 RULE
==================================================

Second semiannual period:
Extended grace period:
Expires December 31.

==================================================
BIRTH MONTH RULE
==================================================

Trucks:
5,001-7,999 pounds:
Class code 41:
Vehicle type TR:
Body code TR/TK:
Register by birth month:
Of first registrant.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HEAVY TRUCK EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Heavy trucks:
8,001 pounds or more:
Use:
Semiannual or annual periods.

==================================================
IRP RULES
==================================================

Heavy trucks:
Less than 26,001 pounds:
May optionally register:
Under IRP.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INTERSTATE RULE
==================================================

Heavy trucks:
Over 26,000 pounds:
Traveling interstate:
Must:
- Register under IRP
OR
- Have trip permit
OR
- Have restricted plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AXLE RULE
==================================================

Vehicles:
3 axles or more:
Traveling interstate:
Must register under IRP:
Regardless of GVW.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMP TAG INTERSTATE RULE
==================================================

Commercial vehicle:
Traveling out-of-state:
With temporary tag:
Requires:
- Trip permit
AND
- Fuel tax permits.

==================================================
GVW CALCULATION RULES
==================================================

Truck:
5,001-7,999 pounds:
GVW:
Net truck weight
PLUS
Load weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER EXCLUSION RULE
==================================================

Trucks:
5,001-7,999 pounds:
Do NOT include:
Trailer weight:
In GVW calculation.

==================================================
GVW CALCULATION — HEAVY TRUCKS 8,000+
==================================================

Truck:
8,000 pounds or more:
GVW:
Truck weight
PLUS
Trailer weight
PLUS
Load weight.

==================================================
GVW CALCULATION — TRUCK TRACTOR
==================================================

Truck tractor GVW:
Truck tractor weight
PLUS
Semi-trailer gross weight
PLUS
Cargo weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Truck or truck tractor:
Pulling mobile home:
GVW calculated:
Length of mobile home × 430 pounds
PLUS
Net vehicle weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DRIVER/PASSENGER RULE
==================================================

Driver,
passengers,
and cargo:
Should be included:
In GVW calculation.

==================================================
RENEWAL AND TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Renewals and transfers:
May process:
At any county license plate agency.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES

Replacement plates:

- HSMV 83146

VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida heavy truck and truck tractor registrations:
Are governed primarily by:
Declared Gross Vehicle Weight (GVW),
vehicle configuration,
and interstate commercial use,
while requiring strict compliance with:
- HVUT documentation
- commercial insurance
- IRP requirements
- proper plate display
and weight-based registration taxation.
