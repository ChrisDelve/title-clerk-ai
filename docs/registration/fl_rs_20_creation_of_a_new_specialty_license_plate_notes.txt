- Design/manufacturing standards
- Deauthorization rules
- Inventory rules
- Audit requirements

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

New specialty license plates:
Cannot be created:
Without:
- Legislative approval
- Governor signature
- Successful pre-sale voucher process.

==================================================
SPECIALTY PLATE LIMIT RULE
==================================================

If Florida has:
135 or more specialty plates:
FLHSMV:
Cannot issue new specialty plates:
Until sufficient plates are discontinued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 135 RULE
==================================================

135-plate limit includes:
- Plates above minimum threshold
- Plates exempt from threshold

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ICUF RULE
==================================================

ICUF and Divine Nine:
Each count:
As one plate.

==================================================
LEGISLATIVE PROCESS RULES
==================================================

Organization:
Must obtain:
Representative or Senator:
To sponsor specialty plate bill.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL PASSAGE RULE
==================================================

Pre-sale process:
Cannot begin:
Until:
- Bill passes Legislature
AND
- Governor signs bill into law.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EFFECTIVE DATE RULE
==================================================

Pre-sale process begins:
On effective date:
Established by law.

==================================================
LEGISLATION CONTENT RULES
==================================================

Legislation includes:
- Annual use fee amount
- Fund expenditure instructions
- Basic design specifications
- Bottom verbiage
- Personalization details

==================================================
PRE-SALE VOUCHER RULES
==================================================

Organization has:
24 months:
To sell:
3,000 vouchers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 3000 VOUCHER RULE
==================================================

Specialty plate:
Cannot be manufactured:
Until:
3,000 vouchers sold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COLLEGIATE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

In-state collegiate plates:
Created after 10/01/2002:
Exempt:
From 3,000 voucher requirement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEAUTHORIZATION RULE
==================================================

If 3,000 vouchers:
Not sold:
Within 24 months:
Plate is deauthorized.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REFUND RULE
==================================================

Voucher purchaser:
May request refund:
Using:
HSMV 83363.

==================================================
DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT RULES
==================================================

Division of Motorist Services:
Coordinates:
Plate design and development.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ARTWORK DEADLINE RULE
==================================================

Organization must submit:
Proposed artwork:
Within:
60 days after bill becomes law.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DESIGN APPROVAL RULE
==================================================

Design and colors:
Must be approved:
By FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REDESIGN RULE
==================================================

Organization:
Cannot request redesign:
Unless:
- Inventory depleted
OR
- Organization purchases remaining inventory at cost.

==================================================
DEAUTHORIZATION / DISCONTINUATION RULES
==================================================

FLHSMV shall discontinue:
Specialty plates:
Below minimum registrations:
For 12 consecutive months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION THRESHOLD RULE
==================================================

Minimum valid registrations:
- 3,000 for most plates
- 4,000 for out-of-state colleges/universities

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WARNING LETTER RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV sends:
Warning letter:
To organizations:
Below registration threshold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MONTHLY EMAIL RULE
==================================================

After warning letter:
Monthly emails:
Sent with current registration totals.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COLLEGIATE EXEMPTION RULE 2
==================================================

In-state collegiate plates:
Exempt:
From discontinuation threshold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT JANUARY 1 RULE
==================================================

On January 1 each year:
FLHSMV discontinues:
Specialty plate:
With fewest valid registrations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOTTOM 10 PERCENT RULE
==================================================

Organizations in:
Bottom 10%:
Receive notification letter:
As of December 1 annually.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COLLEGIATE EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Collegiate plates under:
320.08058(3):
Exempt from bottom 10% discontinuation process.

==================================================
AUDIT RULES
==================================================

Audits and attestations:
Governed by:
RS-30.

==================================================
SPECIALTY PLATE DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS
==================================================

Plate dimensions:
12 inches by 6 inches.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CMYK RULE
==================================================

Plate printing uses:
CMYK 4-color process:
- Cyan
- Magenta
- Yellow
- Black

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA FONT RULE
==================================================

"FLORIDA":
Must appear:
At top of plate:
Arial Bold:
75 point font.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOTTOM FONT RULE
==================================================

Bottom wording:
Must use:
Arial Bold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONTRAST RULE
==================================================

Contrast:
Must allow:
Law enforcement readability:
Day and night.

==================================================
CENTER DESIGN RULES
==================================================

Center graphic maximum:
2.5 inches wide
By
3 inches high.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CENTER CHARACTER RULE
==================================================

Center design plates:
Use:
3 characters left
And
3 characters right.

==================================================
LEFT SIDE DESIGN RULES
==================================================

Left graphic maximum:
3.5 inches wide
By
3 inches high.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEFT SIDE CHARACTER RULE
==================================================

Left-side graphic plates:
Limited:
To 5 alphanumeric characters.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BORDER RULE
==================================================

Graphics:
Cannot bleed:
Off plate edge.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WHITE BORDER RULE
==================================================

Must maintain:
1/4 inch white or clear border.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PANTONE RULE
==================================================

Metallic and fluorescent Pantone colors:
Not allowed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT THIN LINE RULE
==================================================

Lines under:
0.008 inch:
May fail to print.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FILE FORMAT RULE
==================================================

Preferred artwork formats:
- EPS
- AI
- PDF
- PSD

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RESOLUTION RULE
==================================================

Digital images:
Minimum:
300 DPI:
At actual size.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WEBSITE IMAGE RULE
==================================================

Website images:
Not acceptable:
Due to low resolution.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VECTOR RULE
==================================================

Preferred artwork:
Vector-based:
Adobe Illustrator.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FONT OUTLINE RULE
==================================================

Fonts:
Must be outlined
Or included.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 3M RULE
==================================================

3M TSSD:
Uses:
Adobe Illustrator
And Adobe Photoshop.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPRESSION RULE
==================================================

Do not use:
Compression software:
For vector files.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COPYRIGHT RULE
==================================================

Scanning copyrighted printed material:
May create:
Copyright violation liability.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DROPBOX RULE
==================================================

Files over 5 MB:
May use:
Web Dropbox upload process.
