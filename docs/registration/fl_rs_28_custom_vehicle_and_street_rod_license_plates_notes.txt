VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Custom Vehicle and Street Rod plates:
Require:
Vehicle title/record:
To carry proper brand:
Before plate issuance.

==================================================
CUSTOM VEHICLE DEFINITION
==================================================

Custom vehicle:
Motor vehicle:
25 years old or older:
And model year after 1948:
OR
Manufactured to resemble vehicle:
25 years old or older:
And model year after 1948.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOM VEHICLE ALTERATION RULE
==================================================

Custom vehicle must:
- Be altered from manufacturer's original design
OR
- Have body constructed from non-original materials.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOM VEHICLE BRAND RULE
==================================================

Vehicle title/record:
Must carry:
"Custom Vehicle"
Brand:
Before issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MODEL YEAR RULE
==================================================

Model year listed:
Based on:
Body style vehicle resembles:
NOT actual manufacture date.

==================================================
STREET ROD DEFINITION
==================================================

Street rod:
Motor vehicle:
Model year 1948 or older:
OR
Manufactured after 1948:
To resemble vehicle:
1948 or older.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STREET ROD ALTERATION RULE
==================================================

Street rod must:
- Be altered from manufacturer's original design
OR
- Have body constructed from non-original materials.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STREET ROD BRAND RULE
==================================================

Vehicle title/record:
Must carry:
"Street Rod"
Brand:
Before issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STREET ROD YEAR RULE
==================================================

Street rod qualification:
Limited to:
1948 or older appearance.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
HSMV 83045:
Application for Registration of a Street Rod,
Custom Vehicle,
Horseless Carriage,
or Antique (Permanent).

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BRANDING REQUIREMENT RULE
==================================================

Before applying:
Vehicle record:
Must already contain:
Proper title brand.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Proof of Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

VERY IMPORTANT CLASS CODE RULE
==================================================

Custom Vehicle and Street Rod:
Use:
Class code 95.

RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

Renewals:
May process:

Or license plate agency.

==================================================
TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Transfers:
May process:
At any local tax collector office
Or license plate agency.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFER ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Custom Vehicle or Street Rod plates:
May ONLY transfer:
To another vehicle:
Meeting same qualification standards.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is vehicle Custom Vehicle or Street Rod?
2. Does vehicle meet age/model-year requirements?
3. Does vehicle resemble qualifying historic model?
4. Has vehicle been altered from original design?
5. Does vehicle use non-original body materials?
6. Does title carry correct brand?
7. Is Custom Vehicle brand required?
8. Is Street Rod brand required?
9. Is HSMV 83045 completed?
10. Is Florida insurance verified?
11. Does RS-36 apply?
12. Is class code 95 correct?
13. Are all registration fees assessed?
14. Is transfer vehicle also qualified?
15. Does replica vehicle qualify?
16. Is model year determined correctly?
17. Is body-style resemblance valid?
18. Is renewal allowed?
19. Is transfer allowed?
20. Is registration office authorized?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK / REGISTRATION ERRORS
==================================================

- Issuing plate before title branding
- Incorrectly classifying Custom Vehicle vs Street Rod
- Using actual manufacture year instead of appearance year
- Missing HSMV 83045
- Missing Florida insurance
- Incorrect class code assignment
- Allowing transfer to nonqualifying vehicle
- Failing to verify altered/non-original construction
- Incorrect fee calculation
- Misunderstanding replica qualification
- Missing title brand verification
- Incorrectly issuing post-1948 Street Rod
- Incorrectly issuing pre-1949 Custom Vehicle
- Missing trust fund fee
- Incorrect renewal assumptions

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida Custom Vehicle and Street Rod license plates:
Provide specialty registration status
for altered,
replica,
or reconstructed historic-style vehicles,
while requiring:
- proper title branding
- strict year/style qualifications
- modified construction standards
- insurance verification
and specialized registration processing.
