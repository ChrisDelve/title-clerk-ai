==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Organizations receiving:
- specialty plate revenues
OR
- voluntary contributions

Must annually report:
Revenue and expenditures:
To FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AUDIT RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Subject to 215.97 FS:
Must submit:
Independent single audit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPLIANCE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Organizations NOT subject to audit:
Must submit:
Compliance affidavit:
Under penalties of perjury.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEADLINE RULE
==================================================

Compliance affidavits:
Due:
No later than:
9 months after fiscal year end.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SINGLE AUDIT DEADLINE RULE
==================================================

Single audits:
Must be submitted:
Within 45 days:
Of auditor completion.

==================================================
SUBMISSION ADDRESS RULE
==================================================

Submit to:

Division of Motorist Services
Bureau of Motorist Services Support
Attn: Compliance Monitoring
(Specialty License Plate and Voluntary Contributions Unit)
2900 Apalachee Pkwy
Room B364 MS 77
Tallahassee FL 32399-0500

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REVIEW PERIOD RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
Must determine compliance:
Within 120 days:
After receipt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONCOMPLIANCE RULE
==================================================

If organization:
Fails compliance:
FLHSMV:
May withhold revenue distributions.

==================================================
CORRECTIVE ACTION RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
May issue:
Corrective action affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORRECTIVE ACTION RULE
==================================================

Organization officers:
Must sign:
Corrective action affidavit:
Under penalties of perjury.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEGISLATIVE HOLD RULE
==================================================

If organization:
Fails corrective action:
Revenue remains withheld:
Until legislative direction received.

==================================================
DEPARTMENT RESPONSIBILITY RULES
==================================================

FLHSMV responsibilities include:
- compliance monitoring
- annual reviews
- organization communication
- on-site visits
- suborganization monitoring
- withholding/releasing funds
- compliance notifications.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECORD ACCESS RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
May examine:
All specialty plate and voluntary contribution records.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ANNUAL REVIEW RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
Conducts annual compliance reviews:
For all active organizations.

==================================================
COMPLIANCE REVIEW COMPONENTS
==================================================

Reviews may include:
- audit analysis
- affidavit review
- on-site visits
- supporting document requests
- expenditure verification
- suborganization verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMUNICATION RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
Communicates results:
Within 60 days:
Of compliance review completion.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPECIALTY LICENSE PLATE EXPENSE RULE
==================================================

Specialty plate funds:
Cannot be used:
For:
- commercial activities
- for-profit activities
- lobbying
- unauthorized admin expenses
- unauthorized marketing expenses.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIRECT PROGRAM RULE
==================================================

All expenditures:
Must directly support:
Program associated:
With specialty license plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AUDIT THRESHOLD RULE
==================================================

Audit threshold:
$750,000.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STATE FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE RULE
==================================================

Specialty plate and voluntary contribution funds:
Are considered:
State financial assistance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTARIZATION RULE
==================================================

Compliance affidavits:
Must be notarized.

==================================================
SPECIALTY PLATE AFFIDAVIT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Affidavit includes:
- organization name
- counties of operation
- specialty plate type
- fiscal year
- CSFA number
- beginning balance
- deposits/checks received
- interest income
- expenditures
- ending balance
- endowment disclosure.

==================================================
VOLUNTARY CONTRIBUTION AFFIDAVIT RULES
==================================================

Voluntary contribution affidavits:
Require:
Same reporting structure:
As specialty plate affidavits.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ENDOWMENT FUND RULE
==================================================

If endowment exists:
Additional endowment reporting:
Required.

==================================================
ENDOWMENT REPORTING RULES
==================================================

Endowment reporting includes:
- beginning balance
- transfers in
- realized gains/losses
- unrealized gains/losses
- dividends
- interest income
- fees
- transfers out
- ending balance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ENDOWMENT MIXING RULE
==================================================

If specialty plate or voluntary contribution funds:
Are mixed:
With other funds:
Organization should contact FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUBRECIPIENT RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Must provide:
CSFA number and related information:
To sub-recipient entities.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REMINDER NOTICE RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
Sends annual reminder notices:
At least 30 days:
Before reporting period end.

==================================================
PROHIBITED EXPENDITURES RULE
==================================================

Funds cannot be used:
For:
- lobbying
- unauthorized admin expenses
- unauthorized marketing
- unrelated activities
- commercial profit purposes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALLOWABLE AUDIT EXPENSE RULE
==================================================

Funds MAY be used:
For audit/report costs:
If authorized by statute.
