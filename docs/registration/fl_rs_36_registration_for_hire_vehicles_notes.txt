==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Vehicles:
Used to transport:
Passengers or property:
For compensation:
Must comply:
With Florida for-hire registration requirements.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOR-COMPENSATION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle operation:
For compensation or hire:
Triggers:
Commercial registration review.

==================================================
TAXICAB RULE
==================================================

Taxicabs:
Require:
For-hire registration classification.

==================================================
LIMOUSINE RULE
==================================================

Limousines:
Transporting passengers for compensation:
Require:
For-hire registration.

==================================================
SHUTTLE RULE
==================================================

Shuttle/passenger transport vehicles:
Used commercially:
Require:
Appropriate for-hire registration class.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PASSENGER VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Passenger-carrying vehicles:
May require:
Different registration classifications:
Based on:
- seating capacity
- weight
- operational use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL USE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle use:
Determines registration class:
Not merely vehicle body type.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENTAL RULE
==================================================

Rental vehicles:
Must be evaluated separately:
From for-hire passenger transport operations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INTERSTATE RULE
==================================================

Interstate for-hire operations:
May require:
Additional interstate or apportioned registration compliance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WEIGHT CLASS RULE
==================================================

Registration taxes/fees:
May vary based on:
- vehicle weight
- passenger capacity
- operational classification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL PLATE RULE
==================================================

For-hire vehicles:
Receive:
Commercial registration classification
and plate assignment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PASSENGER TRANSPORT RULE
==================================================

Primary classification determination:
Is whether vehicle:
Regularly transports passengers:
For compensation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PROPERTY TRANSPORT RULE
==================================================

Vehicles transporting property:
For compensation:
May also qualify:
For for-hire registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLEET RULE
==================================================

Fleet-operated passenger vehicles:
Must maintain:
Consistent commercial registration compliance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT USE CLASSIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Improper registration class:
May result in:
Incorrect taxes/fees
and registration violations.
