==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP RULES
==================================================

Vehicle registration requires:
- Florida title in applicant name
OR
- Application for Florida title submitted simultaneously.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE RULE
==================================================

Registration cannot be issued:
Without:
Valid ownership documentation.

==================================================
OWNER / REGISTRANT NAME RULES
==================================================

Application must include:
- Full first name
- Full middle/maiden name
- Full last name

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DRIVER LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Natural persons:
Should provide:
Florida driver license or ID number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUSINESS RULE
==================================================

Businesses:
Require:
FEID number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RULE
==================================================

Dealer submitting registration:
Must provide:
- Driver license
- ID card
- Valid passport
- Foreign passport with DHS stamp
- I-94
- Permanent resident card
OR
- Immigrant visa documentation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MINOR RULE
==================================================

Minor registrant:
Must include:
Parent, guardian, or custodian:
As co-registrant.

==================================================
DEAF / HARD OF HEARING RULES
==================================================

Customer may voluntarily indicate:
- Deaf
OR
- Hard of hearing

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOMER PRESENCE RULE
==================================================

Customer must be present:
To voluntarily add:
Deaf/Hard of Hearing indicator.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VOID RULE
==================================================

If transaction voided:
Indicator must be manually removed:
From MTRFM010.

==================================================
ADDRESS REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Registration requires:
Residential street address:
Or permanent business address.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA ADDRESS RULE
==================================================

Florida address required:
For Florida registration issuance.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE IDENTIFICATION
==================================================

Acceptable identification:
- Driver license
- Identification card
- Valid passport

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUSINESS VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Business customers may provide:
- FEID
- Business authorization verification
- Florida business license

==================================================
NO PERMANENT ADDRESS RULES
==================================================

If customer lacks permanent address:
Additional information required.

==================================================
BUSINESS ALTERNATIVE ADDRESS RULE
==================================================

Business registration may use:
Residence address of:
- Owner
- Officer
- Supervisory employee

==================================================
INDIVIDUAL ALTERNATIVE ADDRESS RULE
==================================================

Individual registration may use:
Residence address of:
- Relative
OR
- Friend residing in Florida

==================================================
MILITARY EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Florida resident active-duty military:
Exempt from:
Permanent residence street address requirement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DL/ID ADDRESS RULE
==================================================

If customer has valid Florida DL/ID:
Only mailing address may be updated.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VOTER REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Residential address updates:
Should occur during:
DL/ID renewal or replacement:
To preserve voter registration compliance.

==================================================
PO BOX RULE
==================================================

PO Box mailing address:
Requires:
Residential address also provided.

==================================================
ADDRESS CHANGE RULE
==================================================

Owner/registrant:
Must notify FLHSMV:
Within 30 days:
Of address change.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-FLORIDA RESIDENT RULE
==================================================

Customer without Florida residence:
Cannot:
Title or register vehicle in Florida.

==================================================
EXEMPT ADDRESS CONFIDENTIALITY RULE
==================================================

Certain public officials and protected persons:
May use protected addresses:
Per RS-08.

==================================================
VEHICLE INFORMATION RULES
==================================================

Application requires:
Complete vehicle description including:
- Make
- Year
- Model
- Body
- Color
- Weight
- Length
- BHP/CC
- GVW
- VIN

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-1955 RULE
==================================================

Vehicles manufactured through 1954:
Use:
Motor number:
Instead of VIN.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVW RULE
==================================================

Applicant declares:
Maximum gross vehicle weight:
For:
- Trucks over 5,000 lbs
- Truck tractors
- Buses

==================================================
AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

Fully autonomous vehicles:
Should be titled and registered:
In company name:
Using FEID.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AV INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

Autonomous vehicles require:
Minimum:
$1 million liability coverage.

==================================================
AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Required:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- Insurance verification

==================================================
AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Must include:
- Liability coverage
- PIP
- Uninsured motorist coverage
- Underinsured motorist coverage

==================================================
SEX / DOB RULES
==================================================

Sex codes:
- F = Female
- M = Male

==================================================
DATE FORMAT RULE
==================================================

Date format:
MM/DD/YY

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance proof required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Insurance proof not required for:
- Vehicles under four wheels
- Trailers
- Mobile homes
- Motorcycles
- Government vehicles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION REFUSAL RULE
==================================================

Registration must be refused:
If proof of insurance:
Not provided or verified.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE CARD RULE
==================================================

Insurance card must include:
- Insurance company name
- Coverage identification number
- Make
- Year
- VIN

==================================================
FIVE DIGIT INSURANCE CODE RULE
==================================================

Insurance verification:
Requires:
Five-digit insurance company code.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE COMPANY RULE
==================================================

If company not in insurance table:
Contact insurer:
For proper code.

==================================================
MILITARY INSURANCE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Certain Florida resident military members:
May qualify:
For Florida insurance exemption.

==================================================
ASSIGNED IDENTIFICATION NUMBER RULES
==================================================

Individuals may use:
- Driver license number
- ID card number
- System customer number

==================================================
BUSINESS IDENTIFICATION RULES
==================================================

Businesses may use:
- FEID
- FEID suffix
- System customer number

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOMER CREATION RULE
==================================================

If no customer number exists:
System creates:
Customer number.

==================================================
SAFE DESIGNATION RULES
==================================================

SAFE designation:
Alerts law enforcement:
To communication-sensitive conditions.

==================================================
SAFE ELIGIBLE CONDITIONS
==================================================

Conditions include:
- Autism
- ADHD
- Down Syndrome
- Alzheimer’s
- PTSD
- Diabetes
- Deafness
- Blindness
- Other limiting disorders

==================================================
SAFE REQUIRED FORM RULE
==================================================

Requires:
HSMV 72120.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAFE FEE RULE
==================================================

SAFE designation:
No fee:
When processed as registration correction.

==================================================
FLORIDA FREEDOM BOATER DECAL RULES
==================================================

Vessel safety inspection decal:
Issued upon:
Safety equipment attestation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOATER DECAL LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Decal must be located:
Within 6 inches:
Of vessel registration decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOATER DECAL VALIDITY RULE
==================================================

Florida Freedom Boater decal:
Valid:
5 years.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOATER REQUIRED FORM RULE
==================================================

Requires:
HSMV 87191.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LOST DECAL RULE
==================================================

Lost decal replacement:
Processed:
No fee:
As duplicate registration:
Reason code LT.

==================================================
IMPOSTER ALERT RULE
==================================================

If system displays:
"Possible Imposter – Identity Verification Required"
Review:
Fraud stops.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FRAUD STOP RULE
==================================================

If fraud stops present:
Contact:
MV Fraud Unit.

LICENSE PLATE RULES
==================================================

Registration documentation includes:
- Metal license plate
- Registration certificate
- Validation decal

==================================================
LICENSE PLATE CATEGORIES
==================================================

Categories:
- Regular graphic
- Special
- Specialty
- Personalized

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Standard plate:
Displayed:
Rear of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUCK TRACTOR RULE
==================================================

Truck tractor plate:
Displayed:
Front of vehicle.

==================================================
VALIDATION DECAL RULES
==================================================

Validation decal:
Affixed:
Upper right corner:
Of license plate.

==================================================
VALIDATION DECAL INFORMATION
==================================================

Validation decal contains:
- Expiration month/year
- Sequential decal number
- Plate number
- Vehicle type

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUAL PLATE RULE
==================================================

Dual plate vehicles:
Only front plate:
Receives decal.

==================================================
LICENSE PLATE TYPES
==================================================

Includes:
- Dealer
- Restricted
- Permanent semi-trailer
- Motorcycle
- Moped
- Government
- Specialty
- Military
- Antique
- Transporter
- Fleet
- HOV
- Temporary

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER PLATE RULE
==================================================

Dealer plates:
Cannot be used:
On wreckers or car carriers.

==================================================
RESTRICTED PLATE RULES
==================================================

Restricted plates:
Have limited authorized usage.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AGRICULTURE RULE
==================================================

Agriculture restricted vehicles:
Must:
- Be owned by grower
- Operate within 150-mile radius

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TOOL VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Tool vehicles:
Cannot:
Carry loads or pull trailers.

==================================================
PERMANENT SEMI-TRAILER RULE
==================================================

Permanent semi-trailer plates:
- Non-transferable
- No expiration

==================================================
MOTORCYCLE RULES
==================================================

Motorcycle:
Motor-driven vehicle:
With seat/saddle:
Kick or electric start.

==================================================
MOPED RULES
==================================================

Moped:
- Human pedals
- ≤2 BHP
- ≤30 MPH
- ≤50cc

==================================================
MOTORIZED DISABILITY ACCESS VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Motorized disability access vehicle:
- ≤2 BHP
- ≤30 MPH
- ≤50cc if combustion engine
- Body type DA

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTORIZED BICYCLE RULE
==================================================

Motorized bicycles:
No longer registered:
Unless gas motor classification applies:
Per RS-68.
