==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Customers:
May voluntarily contribute:
To authorized trust funds:
During motor vehicle or mobile home registration transactions.

==================================================
CONTRIBUTION SELECTION RULE
==================================================

Customers may select:
One or more:
Voluntary trust funds.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONTRIBUTION METHOD RULE
==================================================

Customer contribution choices:
May be made:
- verbally
- by written correspondence
- by checking renewal notice boxes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALL TRANSACTION RULE
==================================================

Contributions:
Allowed on:
- all registration transactions
- miscellaneous revenue transactions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL DONATION RULE
==================================================

Additional contributions:
May be collected:
From:
$1 to $999.

==================================================

==================================================

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

Registration:
Displays:
Total amount:
Of voluntary contributions.

==================================================
SAVE THE MANATEE RULE
==================================================

Customers donating:
$5 or more:
Receive:
Sticker or emblem:
Signifying support.

==================================================
MARINE TURTLE RULE
==================================================

Customers donating:
$5 or more:
Receive:
Sticker or emblem:
Signifying support for Marine Turtle Protection Trust Fund.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HEALTH CARE TRUST FUND RULE
==================================================

Renewal notices:
May still reference:
Florida Organ and Tissue Donor Education and Procurement Trust Fund.

==================================================
DISTRIBUTION RULES
==================================================

Each contribution:
Must be distributed:
According to statutory requirements.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CHILD SAFETY SEAT RULE
==================================================

Child Safety Seat contributions:
Paid into:
Highway Safety Operating Trust Fund:
To purchase child safety seats.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAVE THE MANATEE DISTRIBUTION RULE
==================================================

Save the Manatee contributions:
Deposited into:
Save the Manatee Trust Fund.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MARINE TURTLE DISTRIBUTION RULE
==================================================

Marine Turtle Protection contributions:
Deposited into:
Marine Resources Conservation Trust Fund.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGAN DONOR RULE
==================================================

Health Care Trust Fund contributions:
Used for:
- organ donor education
- tissue donor education
- donor registry maintenance.

==================================================
AUDIT RULES
==================================================

Organizations receiving contributions:
Must submit:
Annual audit:
Or annual attestation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 215.97 RULE
==================================================

Organizations subject to:
215.97 FS:
Must comply:
With audit requirements.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ANNUAL ATTESTATION RULE
==================================================

Organizations not subject to audit:
Must annually attest:
Funds used:
In compliance with law.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INTEREST RULE
==================================================

Audit/attestation information:
Must include:
- interest earned
- expenditures made.

==================================================
CHARITABLE COMPLIANCE RULES
==================================================

Organizations collecting charitable contributions:
Must:
- maintain records
- avoid commingling funds
- properly account for funds.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONCHARITABLE FUNDS RULE
==================================================

Charitable contributions:
Cannot be commingled:
With noncharitable funds.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DOCUMENTATION RULE
==================================================

Failure to properly document expenditures:
Creates rebuttable presumption:
Funds improperly used.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION CLOSURE RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Must notify FLHSMV:
If organization no longer exists.

==================================================
MAIL TRANSACTION RULES
==================================================

If payment exceeds amount due:
Customer may direct excess amount:
To voluntary trust funds.
