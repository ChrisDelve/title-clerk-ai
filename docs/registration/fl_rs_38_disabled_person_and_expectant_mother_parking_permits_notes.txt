==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Disabled parking permits:
Require:
Valid medical certification:
Using approved FLHSMV procedures and forms.

==================================================
PERMANENT / LONG-TERM PERMIT RULES
==================================================

Permanent or long-term permits:
May be issued:
To:
- person unable to walk 200 feet without stopping
OR
- legally blind individual.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA ID RULE
==================================================

Permanent or long-term disabled permit:
Requires:
- Florida driver license
OR
- Florida ID card.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Permanent or long-term permit:
Valid:
Up to 4 years:
Expires:
On customer birthday.

==================================================
ORGANIZATION PERMIT RULES
==================================================

Organizations:
Providing transportation:
To disabled persons:
May receive permits.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Must provide:
- FEID
OR
- proof authorized to do business in Florida.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Organization permits:
Expire:
June 30:
Every 4 years.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY TO PERMANENT RULE
==================================================

Temporary permit:
Must be cancelled:
Before permanent permit issuance.

==================================================
ADVANCE RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Advance registration:
Allowed:
Within 3 months:
Of expiration.

==================================================
SECOND PERMIT RULES
==================================================

Only one permanent permit:
Allowed:
Unless:
- quadriplegic
OR
- frequent traveler
OR
- approved special situation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FREQUENT TRAVELER RULE
==================================================

Frequent traveler:
May receive second permit:
For airport/train/bus/boat travel situations.

==================================================
WHEELCHAIR LICENSE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Wheelchair symbol plate:
May be issued:
To qualifying disabled owner/co-owner.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABLED VETERAN RULE
==================================================

Disabled Veteran:
With qualifying disability:
May receive:
Disabled Veteran Wheelchair plate.

==================================================
TEMPORARY PERMIT RULES
==================================================

Temporary permit:
May issue:
For temporary mobility impairment
OR
temporary sight impairment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Temporary permit:
Cannot exceed:
6 months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY FEE RULE
==================================================

Temporary permit fee:
$15.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ONE TEMPORARY RULE
==================================================

Only ONE temporary permit:
May be active:
At one time.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REAPPLICATION RULE
==================================================

New temporary permit:
Requires:
New HSMV 83039.

==================================================
EXPECTANT MOTHER RULES
==================================================

Effective:
July 1, 2025:
Expectant mothers:
May receive:
Temporary parking permit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPECTANT MOTHER CERTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Expectant mother permit:
Must be certified:
By Florida physician:
Licensed under:
Chapter 458 or 459.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPECTANT MOTHER EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Expectant mother permit:
Valid:
Up to 1 year:
Not renewable.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPECTANT MOTHER FORM RULE
==================================================

Require:
HSMV 83040.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE SIGNATURE RULE
==================================================

Acceptable signatures include:
- electronic
- printed
- faxed
- photocopied
- wet signature
- stamped signature.

==================================================
CERTIFYING AUTHORITY RULES
==================================================

Approved certifying authorities:
Include:
- physicians
- APRNs
- chiropractors
- optometrists
- physician assistants
- podiatrists
and others listed in procedure.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE PHYSICIAN RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state physician:
Requires:
Signed statement:
Acknowledging Florida eligibility guidelines.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-COUNTRY RULE
==================================================

Out-of-country certifying authority:
NOT acceptable.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE CUSTOMER RULES
==================================================

Temporary permit:
May issue:
To out-of-state visitor.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE IDENTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Acceptable ID:
- state DL/ID
- US passport
- US territory DL/ID.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE PERMIT RULE
==================================================

Customer with valid out-of-state temporary permit:
Cannot receive:
Florida temporary permit.

==================================================
OUT-OF-COUNTRY CUSTOMER RULES
==================================================

Out-of-country visitor:
May receive:
Temporary permit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONUS RULE
==================================================

For out-of-country customer:
Enter:
"NONUS"
In certification authority field.

==================================================
RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

Permanent permit renewal:
Requires:
HSMV 83039:
Signed within last 12 months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABLED VETERAN RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Disabled veterans:
May renew:
Using VA Summary of Benefits letter:
Instead of HSMV 83039.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DVA RULE
==================================================

For disabled veteran renewal:
Enter:
"DVA"
In CERT AUTH NUM field.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMPORARY NONRENEWABLE RULE
==================================================

Temporary permits:
Are NOT renewable.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement permit:
Requires:
- HSMV 83146
AND
- updated disability proof.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT FEE RULE
==================================================

No fee:
For replacement permits.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN PERMIT RULE
==================================================

If permit stolen:
Customer should provide:
Police report or police card.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Replacement permit:
Keeps same expiration date:
As original permit.

==================================================
DISPLAY RULES
==================================================

Permit:
Must hang:
From rearview mirror:
When parked.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISPLAY DIRECTION RULE
==================================================

Permit number:
Should face windshield:
Visible from outside vehicle.

==================================================
ENFORCEMENT RULES
==================================================

Fraudulent use:
Second-degree misdemeanor:
Up to:
- $1,000 fine
- 1 year jail
OR both.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONFISCATION RULE
==================================================

Law enforcement:
May confiscate:
Fraudulently obtained/unlawfully used permit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIVILEGE REVOKED RULE
==================================================

Confiscated permits:
Cancelled:
Using reason code:
"PP" (Privilege Revoked).

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONFISCATED REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

After confiscation:
Replacement:
Must process:
As Replacement transaction:
NOT Original transaction.

==================================================
DOCUMENT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Issuing agency:
Must retain:
HSMV 83039:
For minimum:
12 months.
