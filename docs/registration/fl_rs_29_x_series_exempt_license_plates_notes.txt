==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

X-Series license plates:
Issued:
To vehicles owned and operated exclusively:
By qualifying exempt organizations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COUNTY ISSUANCE RULE
==================================================

X-Series license plates:
Issued and replaced:
At county tax collector offices.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NONPROFIT RULE
==================================================

Organizations:
Must provide:
Statement confirming:
Qualified nonprofit status:
With current articles:
On file with Florida Department of State.

==================================================
SPECIAL CATEGORY EXEMPT ORGANIZATIONS
==================================================

Special category organizations include:
- Civil Air Patrol
- U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary
- Mobile blood bank units
- Mobile x-ray units
- Nonprofit school buses
- Search and rescue vehicles
- Transportation disadvantaged operators

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUNBIZ RULE
==================================================

Organizational charter:
May be obtained:
From:
Sunbiz.org.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPECIAL CATEGORY AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Special category organizations:
Require affidavit:
Explaining exempt use.

==================================================
MOBILE X-RAY RULES
==================================================

Mobile x-ray units:
Must be used exclusively:
For public health purposes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PUBLIC HEALTH AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Mobile x-ray affidavit must include:
- Description of public health purpose
- Statement whether use is exclusive
- Public availability information
- Qualification process for public use

==================================================
RELIGIOUS ORGANIZATION RULES
==================================================

Church,
temple,
or synagogue vehicles:
May qualify:
If used exclusively:
- As community service van
OR
- To transport passengers without compensation
To religious services or religious education.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMUNITY SERVICE VAN RULE
==================================================

Community service van:
Defined as:
Van used:
- To collect clothes
- Provide service
OR
- Transport persons.

==================================================
TRANSPORTATION DISADVANTAGED RULES
==================================================

Community Transportation Coordinators
And Transportation Operators:
May qualify:
If vehicle used exclusively:
To transport transportation disadvantaged persons.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSPORTATION DISADVANTAGED DEFINITION
==================================================

Transportation disadvantaged:
Persons unable:
To transport themselves
Or purchase transportation:
Due to:
- Physical disability
- Mental disability
- Income status
- Age
OR
Other limitations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Commercial exempt vehicles:
Require:
- Insurance proof
AND
- Heavy Vehicle Use Tax proof.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-34 RULE
==================================================

Heavy Vehicle Use Tax requirements:
Governed by:
RS-34.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP RULES
==================================================

Acceptable proof:
- Current Florida registration
- Florida title
- Florida title receipt

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Vehicle title:
Does NOT need:
To be solely in exempt organization's name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUAL NAME RULE
==================================================

Individual name:
May appear:
Along with exempt organization:
On title.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Proof of Florida insurance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Fees include:
- Registration tax
- Original plate fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable
- Decal on Demand fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CLASS CODE RULE
==================================================

X-Series exempt registrations:
Use:
Class code 96.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEE CHART RULE
==================================================

Refer:
Registration Fees and Flat Taxes Distribution Chart:
For applicable fees.

==================================================
RENEWAL PERIOD RULE
==================================================

Vehicles owned by entity/company:
Renewal period:
June 1 through June 30.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ENTITY EXPIRATION RULE
==================================================

Entity-owned exempt registrations:
Expire:
June 30.

==================================================
RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

Renewals:
May process:
At any local county license plate agency.

==================================================
TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Transfers:
May process:
At any local county license plate agency.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFER ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Replacement vehicle:
Must also qualify:
For X-Series exempt registration.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement plates:
May issue:
At any local county license plate agency.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- Current registration copy
- HSMV 83146
- Applicable fees

==================================================
REPLACEMENT FEES RULE
==================================================

Replacement fees include:
- Replacement fee
- Service fee
- Branch fee if applicable
- Air Pollution Control fee
- Reflectorization fee
- FRVIS fee
- Emergency Medical Service fee
- Advanced Replacement fee
- Decal on Demand fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN PLATE RULE
==================================================

If plate/decal reported stolen:
And customer attests on HSMV 83146:
Replacement fee waived.

for qualifying nonprofit,
religious,
educational,

- insurance compliance
and organizational qualification standards.
