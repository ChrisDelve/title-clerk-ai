==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Front-End Emergency Service plates:
Issued:
For display on front:
Of motor vehicle:
Owned by qualifying public safety officer.

==================================================

==================================================

Authorized front-end plates:
- Emergency Medical Services
- Firefighter
- Law Enforcement Officer
- Emergency Management Personnel (Civil Defense)

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
HSMV 83020:
Application for Front End License Plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE TYPE RULE
==================================================

HSMV 83020:
Must specify:
Exact type:
Of front-end plate requested.

SUBMISSION RULE
==================================================

Applications submitted to:

Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles
2900 Apalachee Parkway MS-72
Tallahassee FL 32399

- Front End Emergency Service plate fee
- Mail fee

==================================================
PROOF OF ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Proof of eligibility:
Required:

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83020 PROOF RULE
==================================================

HSMV 83020:
Specifies:
Required proof documents.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement front-end plates:
Available:
If plate:
- Lost
- Stolen
- Damaged

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO FREE REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

Front-end emergency service plates:
Do NOT receive:
Free replacements.

==================================================
DISPLAY RULES
==================================================

Front-end plates:
Displayed ONLY:
On front:
Of motor vehicle:
Owned by customer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOT ASSIGNED RULE
==================================================

Front-end emergency service plates:
NOT assigned:
To specific vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFERABLE DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

Customer may move:
Front-end plate:
Between owned vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PERMANENT RULE
==================================================

Front-end emergency service plates:
Permanent plates.

==================================================
CANCELLATION RULES
==================================================

Plate must be returned:
Within 10 days:
If owner:
No longer associated:
With emergency service organization.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORGANIZATION STATUS RULE
==================================================

Loss of association:
With qualifying organization:
Requires cancellation.

==================================================
MULTIPLE PLATE RULE
==================================================

One or more:
Front-end emergency service plates:
May issue:
To authorized customer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida Front-End Emergency Service license plates:
Provide permanent front-display recognition plates
for qualifying public safety personnel,
while requiring:
- proof of active eligibility
- proper front-only display
- ownership compliance
- fee payment
and mandatory cancellation
upon separation from qualifying service.
