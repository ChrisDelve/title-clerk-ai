ELIGIBLE VEHICLE RULES
==================================================

Military plates may issue to:
- Private automobiles
- Private trucks under 7,999 lbs
- Recreational vehicles
- Motor homes
- Truck campers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL USE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle:
Cannot be:
For hire or commercial use.

==================================================
OWNERSHIP REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
Florida title:
In applicant name
OR
Application for Florida title.

==================================================
LEASED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Leased vehicles:
Eligible:
For military plates.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEASE COPY RULE
==================================================

Require:
Copy of lease agreement.

==================================================
TRUST VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Trust vehicle:
May qualify:
If applicant has beneficial use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRUST AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Applicant must provide:
Affidavit:
Confirming beneficial use.

==================================================
FORM REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
HSMV 83034.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NATIONAL GUARD FORM RULE
==================================================

National Guard or Reservist plates:
Require:
HSMV 83030.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETIRED GUARD RULE
==================================================

Retired National Guard or Reservist:
Must provide:
Retirement proof:
Such as DD214.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACTIVE GUARD RULE
==================================================

Active National Guard exemption:
Requires:
- Valid Common Access Card
AND
- Signed statement from First Sergeant or Commanding Officer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NATIONAL GUARD EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Active Florida National Guard member:
Eligible:
For one tax-free registration annually.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL FEES RULE
==================================================

Even with exemption:
May still owe:
- Initial registration fee
- Specialty fee
- Personalized fee
- Processing fee
- Amateur Radio fee

==================================================
EX-POW RULES
==================================================

Ex-POW:
May qualify:
For exempt or non-exempt plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EX-POW SURVIVING SPOUSE RULE
==================================================

Unremarried surviving spouse:
May retain eligibility.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EX-POW DOCUMENTS RULE
==================================================

Require:
- Death certificate
- Affidavit of unmarried surviving spouse

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REMARRIAGE RULE
==================================================

If surviving spouse remarries:
Eligibility ends.

==================================================
PEARL HARBOR RULES
==================================================

Pearl Harbor Survivor:
Requires:
- Pearl Harbor Survivors Association membership
OR
- Proof of active duty at Pearl Harbor on 12/07/1941.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PEARL HARBOR EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Disabled veteran with 100% service-connected disability:
May qualify:
For exempt Pearl Harbor plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ONE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Only one exempt Pearl Harbor plate:
Per registrant.

==================================================
PURPLE HEART RULES
==================================================

Purple Heart:
Requires:
Proof from U.S. Government.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURPLE HEART EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Purple Heart recipient:
With qualifying disabled veteran status:
May receive reduced-fee exemption.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ONE PURPLE HEART RULE
==================================================

Only one reduced-fee Purple Heart plate:
Per registrant.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURPLE HEART SURVIVING SPOUSE RULE
==================================================

Unremarried surviving spouse:
May retain Purple Heart plate eligibility.

==================================================
PARATROOPER RULES
==================================================

U.S. Paratrooper:
Requires proof:
- Decorated parachutist
OR
- Completion of Army Jump School.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACTIVE MILITARY RULE
==================================================

Paratrooper plate:
Only for:
Current or former U.S. military personnel.

GOLD STAR RULES
==================================================

Gold Star:
Requires proof:
Service member died while serving.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOLD STAR FREE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Qualified spouse or parent:
Eligible:
For three free Gold Star plates per household.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOLD STAR EXTENDED FAMILY RULE
==================================================

- Parent
- Adoptive parent
- Foster parent
- Grandparent
- Child
- Stepchild
- Adopted child
- Brother
- Sister
- Half-brother
- Half-sister

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOLD STAR AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Extended family applicants:
Require affidavit:
Confirming relationship.

==================================================
COMBAT INFANTRY BADGE RULES
==================================================

- Combat Infantrymen's Association membership
- DD214
- Army orders

VERY IMPORTANT SUPPORTER RULE
==================================================

CIA membership card:
Ending with letter "S":
NOT eligible.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUPPORT GROUP RULE
==================================================

Cards stating:
"Support Group Member"
NOT eligible.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FL CARD RULE
==================================================

Valid Florida CIA membership card:
Contains:
"FL" within membership number.

VERY IMPORTANT GOLD STAR LIMIT RULE
==================================================

==================================================
RENEWAL RULES
==================================================

All renewals/transfers:
May process:
At county license plate agency.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONTINUED ELIGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Most renewals:
Do NOT require:
Continued proof of eligibility.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NATIONAL GUARD RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

National Guard fee exemption renewals:
Require updated HSMV 83030.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement plates:
Require:
- HSMV 83146
- Registration copy

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN PLATE RULE
==================================================

If stolen:
Replacement may issue:
At no charge:
With police report.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Processing office:
Must retain:
HSMV 83146:
For one year.
