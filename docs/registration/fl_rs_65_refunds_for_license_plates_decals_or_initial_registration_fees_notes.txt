==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Registration refunds:
Only allowed:
Under specific statutory circumstances.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 3-YEAR RULE
==================================================

Refund applications:
Must be filed:
Within:
3 years:
After refund right accrued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADVANCE RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Refund allowed:
If plate surrendered:
Before effective date:
Of renewed registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BIRTHDAY RULE
==================================================

For natural persons:
Renewal period ends:
At midnight:
On owner’s birthday.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TWO-YEAR REFUND RULE
==================================================

If two-year registration renewed:
And surrendered before effective date:
Full two-year fees:
May be refunded:
Less service fees.

==================================================
REFUND ELIGIBILITY RULES
==================================================

Refunds allowed when:
- plate/decal surrendered before effective date
- duplicate registrations issued
- IRF qualified for refund
- processing error occurred
- incorrect plate issued instead of transfer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUPLICATE REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

If two registrations:
Issued during same registration period:
Full refund may apply.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Mobile home decal:
May qualify for refund:
If converted to real property:
Using DR-402 documentation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IRF REFUND RULE
==================================================

Initial Registration Fee refund:
Allowed:
If vehicle disposed of:
Within:
3 months after paying IRF.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISPOSED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle owner:
Must:
- sell
- transfer
OR
- otherwise dispose of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

Plate of disposed vehicle:
Must be surrendered:
For IRF refund eligibility.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PROCESSING ERROR RULE
==================================================

Refund allowed:
If transaction error:
Caused:
- incorrect fees
OR
- incorrect vehicle renewal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFERABLE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Refund allowed:
If new plate issued:
When existing transferable plate:
Was available.

==================================================
REFUND APPLICATION RULES
==================================================

Refund application requires:
HSMV 83363.

==================================================
REFUND MAILING ADDRESS
==================================================

Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles
Direct Mail and Issuance
2900 Apalachee Parkway
Room A330 MS #72
Tallahassee FL 32399

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Refund application:
Must include:
- plate
- decal
- registration

unless previously surrendered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SECTION IV RULE
==================================================

If plate already surrendered:
Section IV:
Tax Collector Use Only:
Must be completed.

==================================================
IRF REFUND DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

IRF refund requires:
- HSMV 83363
- registration copy
OR
- MVR report
OR
- issuance system screen print
showing IRF paid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE RULE
==================================================

IRF refund:
Requires:
Bill of sale
OR
copy of title:
Showing transfer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE CONTENT RULE
==================================================

Bill of sale must include:
- VIN
- year
- make
- color
- sale price
- seller signature
- purchaser signature.

==================================================
SPECIALTY GIFT CERTIFICATE RULE
==================================================

Gift certificates:
May be purchased:
For specialty plates:
Not in pre-sale voucher process.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT CERTIFICATE REFUND RULE
==================================================

Gift certificates:
ARE NOT refundable.

==================================================
PRE-SALE VOUCHER RULE
==================================================

If specialty plate:
Deauthorized
OR
not issued:
Voucher purchaser:
May:
- apply annual use fee credit
OR
- request refund.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME FEE RULE
==================================================

Voucher credit:
May apply:
Toward specialty plate:
With same annual use fee.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS RULES
==================================================

No refund:
If plate surrendered:
After renewal period ends.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION CREDIT RULE
==================================================

If replacement vehicle purchased:
Before surrendered plate expires:
Registration credit:
May transfer:
For remaining months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EMAIL SUBMISSION RULE
==================================================

Tax collector personnel:
May email:
Refund applications/documents:
To:
mvrefunds@flhsmv.gov

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-43 RULE
==================================================

When plate surrendered:
Motor vehicle issuance system:
Must be updated.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

Customer should receive:
Registration showing:
Plate surrendered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO PRORATED RULE
==================================================

No prorated refunds:
For unused time:
After effective registration date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BIENNIAL REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Biennial registration:
Not refundable:
After effective date.
