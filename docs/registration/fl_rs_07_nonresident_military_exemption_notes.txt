==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Nonresident U.S. Armed Forces members:
Stationed in Florida:
May qualify:
For Florida registration exemptions:
Without becoming Florida residents.

==================================================
MILITARY REGISTRATION TAX EXEMPTION
==================================================

Exemption applies to:
Registration tax:
Under:
320.08 FS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PERSONAL USE RULE
==================================================

Exemption applies only:
If vehicle:
Owned and operated:
Exclusively for personal use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA RESIDENCY RULE
==================================================

Military member:
Must NOT be:
Florida resident.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORDERS RULE
==================================================

Military member:
Must be stationed in Florida:
Under military orders.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPOUSE OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Exemption:
Does NOT apply:
To vehicle owned exclusively:
By spouse or dependent child.

==================================================
INITIAL REGISTRATION FEE EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Military member,
spouse,
or dependent child:
May qualify:
For Initial Registration Fee exemption.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-30 RULE
==================================================

Initial Registration Fee exemption:
Additional guidance:
RS-30.

==================================================
GENERAL INFORMATION RULES
==================================================

Military families:
May:
- Place children in public schools
- Obtain employment

Without:
Creating Florida registration requirement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SCRA RULE
==================================================

Servicemembers Civil Relief Act:
Allows:
Military member:
To display:
- Home state plate
OR
- Florida plate.

==================================================
OVERSEAS DEPLOYMENT RULE
==================================================

If military member deployed overseas:
Family may continue:
Using out-of-state registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETURN RULE
==================================================

If military member returns:
To state other than Florida:
Vehicle operated in Florida:
Must be registered in Florida.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IMMEDIATE FAMILY RULE
==================================================

Immediate family includes:
- Spouse
- Natural child
- Adoptive child
- Stepchild

==================================================
BUSINESS USE RULE
==================================================

Business-use vehicles:
NOT eligible:
For exemption.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required:
- Military orders
- Out-of-state driver license
- HSMV 82002
- Florida insurance

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MILITARY ORDERS RULE
==================================================

Orders must show:
Assignment:
To Florida military duty station.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE DL RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state driver license:
Required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SIX BRANCH RULE
==================================================

Qualifying branches:
- Air Force
- Army
- Coast Guard
- Marines
- Navy
- Space Force

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOREIGN MILITARY RULE
==================================================

Foreign military:
NOT included:
Under U.S. Armed Forces exemption.

==================================================
NON-US MILITARY RULE
==================================================

Non-US military:
May obtain:
Registration Only:
If proof of ownership:
In member name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-US REVIEW RULE
==================================================

Proof of non-US military status:
May require:
Department review.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-04 RULE
==================================================

Non-US military Registration Only:
Governed by:
RS-04.

==================================================
SCRA TITLE OPTIONS RULE
==================================================

SCRA allows:
Nonresident U.S. Armed Forces member:
To choose:
- Registration Only
OR
- Florida title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-COUNTRY PURCHASE RULE
==================================================

Military member purchasing vehicle:
Outside United States:
Must first title vehicle:
In home state:
Before Florida Registration Only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA TITLE OPTION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-country ownership proof:
Acceptable:
If applying:
For Florida title.

==================================================

HSMV 82002.

==================================================

HSMV 82002:

By U.S. Armed Forces member.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida nonresident military exemptions:
Allow qualifying U.S. Armed Forces members
stationed in Florida
to avoid certain registration taxes and fees
while preserving home-state residency protections
under federal military relief laws.
