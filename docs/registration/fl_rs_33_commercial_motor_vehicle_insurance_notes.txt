VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Commercial motor vehicle:
Cannot legally operate:
During registration period:
Without required commercial liability insurance.

==================================================
COMMERCIAL MOTOR VEHICLE DEFINITION
==================================================

Commercial motor vehicle:
Vehicle:
- 26,001 pounds or more GVW
OR
- 3 or more axles
OR
- Combination exceeding 26,001 pounds GVW.

==================================================
COMBINED SINGLE LIMIT (CSL) DEFINITION
==================================================

Combined Single Limit:
Single maximum liability amount:
For:
- Bodily injury
AND
- Property damage combined.

==================================================
SPLIT LIMIT POLICY DEFINITION
==================================================

Split limit policy:
Separates:
- Bodily injury limits
- Property damage limits

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SPLIT LIMIT RULE
==================================================

Split limit policy acceptable:
ONLY IF:
Coverage equals or exceeds:
Required CSL amount.

==================================================
MINIMUM COVERAGE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

26,000-34,999 pounds:
Minimum:
$50,000.

35,000-43,999 pounds:
Minimum:
$100,000.

44,000 pounds or more:
Minimum:
$300,000.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INTERSTATE HAZMAT RULE
==================================================

Interstate for-hire carriers
OR
Hazmat carriers:
10,000 pounds or more:
Require:
$750,000 CSL minimum.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PASSENGER CARRIER RULE
==================================================

Nonpublic interstate passenger carriers:
15 passengers or less:
Require:
$1,500,000 minimum.

16 passengers or more:
Require:
$5,000,000 minimum.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PIP RULE
==================================================

Commercial insurance:
Must include:
$10,000 PIP coverage.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PDL/BIL RULE
==================================================

Commercial policy must indicate:
- PIP
- PDL
- BIL
Coverage.

==================================================
LEASED COMMERCIAL VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If owner/operator:
Leases vehicle:
To carrier company:
Customer must provide:
Proof of insurance:
Under lease arrangement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LESSEE RULE
==================================================

If insurance proof:
Shows carrier company:
As insured:
Customer must also prove:
Lease relationship.

==================================================
SCHEDULED AUTOS RULE
==================================================

If policy marked:
"Scheduled Autos":
Specific VINs:
Must appear:
On insurance proof.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INVALID SCHEDULED AUTOS RULE
==================================================

If:
"Scheduled Autos"
Appears
AND VIN absent:
Insurance proof:
INVALID.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE INSURANCE PROOF
==================================================

Acceptable proof includes:
- Certificate of Liability Insurance (COI)
- ACORD form
- Self-insurance certificate
- Form E
- FMCSA self-insurance
- Insurance policy
- Insurance binder
- Insurance certificate
- Surety bond

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACORD RULE
==================================================

ACORD form:
Acceptable:
If it shows:
- Policy number
- Coverage levels
- PIP coverage.

VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83330 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 83330 insurance affidavit:
NOT acceptable:
For commercial vehicle insurance proof.

==================================================
INSURANCE CANCELLATION RULES
==================================================

If insurer submits cancellation notice:
FLHSMV may suspend:
Commercial registration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CANCELLATION NOTICE RULE
==================================================

Insurer:
Must notify FLHSMV:
At same time:
Cancellation notice sent to insured.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE LEASE RULE
==================================================

Florida resident:
With Florida commercial registration:
Leased to out-of-state company:
Still requires:
Florida commercial insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida commercial motor vehicle insurance requirements:
Mandate specific liability coverage levels
based on:
- vehicle weight
- interstate operation
- hazardous materials transport
- passenger capacity

while requiring strict proof of:
- PIP
- liability coverage
- valid commercial insurance documentation
and ongoing insurance compliance.
