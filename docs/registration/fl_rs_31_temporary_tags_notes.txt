==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Temporary tag:
May ONLY be issued:
For one vehicle:
And cannot be reissued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REISSUANCE RULE
==================================================

Re-issuance:
Of same temporary tag:
Not lawful.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISCRETIONARY RULE
==================================================

Regional Office or tax collector:
May authorize:
More than two temporary tags.

==================================================
ETR SYSTEM RULES
==================================================

ETR:
Electronic Temporary Registration system:
Used for electronic temp tag issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRINT-ON-DEMAND RULE
==================================================

All temporary tags:
Must be print-on-demand:
Except:
- ETR outage
OR
- Trailer under 2,000 lbs.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER LIMIT RULE
==================================================

Dealer:
May issue:
Maximum two temporary tags:
Per customer:
Per vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOUR TAG RULE
==================================================

Dealer:
May issue:
Maximum four temporary tags:
Per vehicle:
Across multiple customers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER TAG RULE
==================================================

If dealer plate use valid:
Temporary tag:
May NOT be issued.

==================================================
TEMP TAG APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
HSMV 83091:
Application for Temporary License Plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE/MCO RULE
==================================================

Copy of:
- Title
OR
- MCO

Required:
To verify VIN.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOMER POSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Customer:
Must carry:
Original HSMV 83091:
While operating vehicle:
Until temp tag expiration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Issuing office:
Must retain:
Duplicate HSMV 83091:
Minimum 12 months.

==================================================
IDENTITY REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Proof of identity:
Required per:
TL-01.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXPIRED ID RULE
==================================================

Expired:
- Driver license
- ID card
- Passport

NOT acceptable.

==================================================
BUSINESS CUSTOMER RULE
==================================================

Business applicants:
Require:
- FEID
OR
- Business verification
OR
- Local business license.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Florida insurance required:
Except:
- Motorcycles
- Trailers
- Federally owned vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-36 RULE
==================================================

Insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-36.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

If vehicle registering out-of-state:
Proof of out-of-state issuance required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

For out-of-state insurance:
Use insurance code:
"S"

Policy field:
"out of state"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT FIELD RULE
==================================================

Comments must include:
- Insurance company
- Policy number
- Effective date
- Phone number

==================================================
TEMP TAG FEES
==================================================

Temporary tag fee:
$2.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL FEES RULE
==================================================

Additional fees may include:
- Service fee
- Branch fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-USE RULE
==================================================

Temporary tag:
Cannot issue:
If owner filed:
Non-use certification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER DISCLOSURE RULE
==================================================

Dealer:
May only identify:
$2 fee:
As state-assessed fee.

==================================================
TEMP TAG DURATIONS
==================================================

Temporary tags may issue:
For:
- 10 days
- 30 days
- 60 days
- 90 days

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER DURATION RULE
==================================================

Dealers:
May issue ONLY:
30-day temporary tags.

==================================================
10-DAY TEMP TAG RULES
==================================================

10-day temp tags allowed for:
- Vehicle weighing
- VIN verification
- Rebuilt inspection
- Shipping vehicle out of country

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REBUILT INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle scheduled:
For rebuilt inspection:
Within 10 days:
Eligible.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SHIPPING RULE
==================================================

Vehicle being shipped out of country:
Eligible:
For one 10-day temporary tag.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER SHIPPING RULE
==================================================

Dealer:
Cannot issue:
10-day temp tag directly.

Must process:
Through tax collector/agent.

==================================================
30-DAY TEMP TAG RULES
==================================================

30-day temp tags allowed for:
- Casual/private sales
- Demonstrations
- Title delays
- Repossession demonstrations
- Out-of-state buyer registrations
- Out-of-state title procurement
- Rental car companies
- Marine trailer dealers
- Off-premises dealer transport
- Heavy truck movement

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEMONSTRATION RULE
==================================================

Demo temporary tag:
Cannot issue:
If seller never registered vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE DELAY RULE
==================================================

If lienholder delay exists:
Require:
- Payoff verification letter
- HSMV 82050

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMON CARRIER RULE
==================================================

Certified common carriers:
May use:
30-day temporary tags.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPOSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Banks/credit unions:
May use:
30-day temp tags:
For repossession demonstrations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE BUYER RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state residents:
May receive:
30-day temp tag:
For Florida purchases.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-08 RULE
==================================================

Sales tax processing:
Governed by:
TL-08.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENTAL CAR RULE
==================================================

Rental car companies:
May use:
30-day temporary tags.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HEAVY TRUCK RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck temporary tags:
Allowed:
If vehicle remains in Florida.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HVUT RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck temp tag:
Requires:
HVUT proof:
Unless first 60 days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IRP/IFTA RULE
==================================================

Heavy truck leaving Florida:
Requires:
- IRP trip permit
AND
- IFTA permit.

==================================================
60-DAY TEMP TAG RULES
==================================================

60-day temporary tags allowed for:
- Out-of-state title with active lien
- Active-duty military stationed outside Florida

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACTIVE LIEN RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state title:
With active lien:
Eligible:
For 60-day temp tag.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MILITARY 60-DAY RULE
==================================================

Military member:
Stationed outside Florida:
Eligible:
With:
- Orders
OR
- Affidavit.

==================================================
90-DAY TEMP TAG RULES
==================================================

90-day temporary tags:
Issued:
While waiting:
For personalized/specialty plate manufacturing.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-27 RULE
==================================================

90-day personalized plate procedures:
Governed by:
RS-27.

==================================================
ETR OUTAGE RULES
==================================================

ETR outage:
Allows:
Pre-printed temp tag issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82082 RULE
==================================================

Outage issuance:
Requires:
HSMV 82082.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ONE BUSINESS DAY RULE
==================================================

Outage issuance:
Must report to FLHSMV:
Within one business day.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FIVE YEAR RULE
==================================================

Dealers:
Must retain:
Outage issuance records:
5 years.

==================================================
PRE-PRINTED TEMP TAG RULES
==================================================

Pre-printed temp tags:
Allowed for:
- ETR outages
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83090 RULE
==================================================

Dealer pre-printed tag requests:
Require:
HSMV 83090.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUSPENSION RULE
==================================================

Dealers with suspended temp privileges:
Cannot purchase:
Pre-printed temporary tags.

==================================================
DISPLAY RULES
==================================================

Temporary tag:
Must display:
In rear license plate bracket.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FRONT DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

Vehicles requiring front plates:
Display temp tag:
Front location.

==================================================
SALES TAX RULES
==================================================

Florida temporary tag issuance:
Requires:
Florida sales tax collection:
For out-of-state buyers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DR123 RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state buyer sales tax:
Requires:
DR123:
Partial Exemption affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COUNTY SURTAX RULE
==================================================

County discretionary surtax:
NOT applicable:
To out-of-state residents.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EFS PENDING RULE
==================================================

If title shows:
"EFS Pending"
No ETR transaction allowed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT THIRD TEMP TAG RULE
==================================================

Third dealer temp tag:
Requires:
Tax collector/agent processing
And reason entry.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida temporary tags:
Provide short-term lawful operation
for vehicles pending registration,
title completion,
inspection,
transport,
or specialty plate manufacturing,
while requiring strict compliance with:
- ETR issuance rules
- duration limitations
- insurance verification
- dealer issuance restrictions
- sales tax compliance
and anti-fraud accountability standards.
