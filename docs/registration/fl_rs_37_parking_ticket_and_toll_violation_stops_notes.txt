==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

License plate or revalidation decal:
Cannot be issued:
If registration stop exists:
Until:
- fines paid
OR
- stop removed
OR
- verification of error provided.

==================================================
PARKING VIOLATION STOP RULE
==================================================

Registration stops may apply:
For:
- three or more unpaid parking tickets
OR
- disabled parking violations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DISABLED PARKING RULE
==================================================

ANY outstanding:
Disabled parking violation:
May trigger registration stop.

==================================================
TOLL VIOLATION STOP RULE
==================================================

One or more:
Outstanding toll violations:
May trigger registration stop.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEASE VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Registration stop:
Does NOT apply:
To owner of leased vehicle:
If vehicle registered:
In lessee name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIVATE TAG AGENT RULE
==================================================

Private tag agent:
May lose authority:
If issuing plate:
Contrary to stop restrictions.

==================================================
DESCRIPTION OF STOP PROCESSING
==================================================

System displays:
"Registration Stop" message:
During registration transaction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO RENEWAL RULE
==================================================

Existing registration:
Cannot be renewed:
Until stop:
- satisfied
OR
- deleted.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE APPLICATION RULE
==================================================

Title and original registration:
May still process:
Despite stop.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Transfer of existing plate:
May still process:
Despite stop.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXTENSION RULE
==================================================

Registration period extension:
Cannot process:
Until stop cleared.

==================================================
EXEMPT TRANSACTIONS RULE
==================================================

Registration stops:
Do NOT apply to:
- mobile homes
- vessels
- parking permits.

==================================================
STOP REMOVAL REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Require:
- proof of payment
OR
- verification of error.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SCAN COVERSHEET RULE
==================================================

Tax collector removing stop:
Must create:
Scan coversheet:
With:
- proof of payment documentation
OR
- verification of error documentation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSACTION SUMMARY RULE
==================================================

Documentation:
Submitted with:
Transaction Summary Report:
For imaging.

==================================================
ORIGINAL QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Traffic bureaus:
Must submit:
Local ordinance copy:
For parking-related stops.

After fines paid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida registration stop procedures:
Prevent renewal issuance
when customers have:
- unpaid parking violations
- disabled parking violations
- toll violations

while still allowing limited title/transfer processing,
until:
- proof of payment
- verification of error
OR
- electronic stop deletion occurs.
