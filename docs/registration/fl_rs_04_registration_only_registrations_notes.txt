==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Registration Only:
Allows Florida registration issuance:
Without Florida title:
ONLY under specifically authorized circumstances.

==================================================
AUTHORIZED REGISTRATION ONLY TRANSACTIONS
==================================================

Authorized situations:
A. Title not required
B. Out-of-state lienholder/lessor
C. Temporarily employed
D. Nonresident military
E. Vehicles loaned to government
F. Intrastate leased heavy trucks
G. Certain mobile homes/travel trailers
H. Non-US military ownership

==================================================
TITLE NOT REQUIRED RULE
==================================================

Title not required for:
- Mopeds
- Electric motorized bicycles
- Gas motorized bicycles
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs

MOBILE HOME REGISTRATION ONLY RULE
==================================================

Registration Only allowed:
For mobile homes/travel trailers:
If:
- Affixed to land
- In Florida over 6 months
- VIN unknown
- Ownership unsubstantiated

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME TN RULE
==================================================

Reason code:
TN
Still applies:
For certain mobile homes/travel trailers.

==================================================

Registration Only:

For future title issuance.

VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Registration Only transactions:
Allow limited Florida registration issuance
without Florida title,
but ONLY under narrowly authorized statutory situations
with strict documentation,
reason code,
and ownership verification requirements.
