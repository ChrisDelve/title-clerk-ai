TITLE:\
FLHSMV RS-02 \'97 Registration Renewals\
\
CATEGORY:\
Registration Renewals\
Plate Renewals\
Renewal Eligibility\
Insurance Verification\
Registration Stops\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Florida vehicle registrations may ONLY be renewed when:\
- Insurance is valid\
- Registration is eligible\
- Required fees are paid\
- No registration stop/suspension exists\
\
VERIFY:\
- Registration expiration date\
- Insurance status\
- Owner information\
- Plate eligibility\
- Registration stop status\
- VIN consistency\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Insurance suspension exists\
- Registration stop exists\
- Ownership conflict exists\
- Fraud concerns arise\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Florida electronically verifies insurance before renewal issuance.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Insurance active\
- Florida-authorized insurer\
- Owner name consistency\
- VIN consistency if applicable\
- Policy effective dates\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Insurance unverifiable\
- Out-of-state insurance presented\
- Policy expired\
- Insurance mismatch exists\
\
COMMON MISTAKE:\
Attempting renewal with insurance not reporting electronically to Florida system.\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Registration renewals may be denied for:\
- Toll violations\
- Parking violations\
- Driver license suspensions\
- Insurance suspensions\
- Administrative stops\
\
VERIFY:\
- DMV stop status\
- Suspension status\
- Outstanding compliance requirements\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Active stop unresolved\
- Suspension conflict exists\
- Court/administrative issue unresolved\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
VIN must match ALL registration and supporting records exactly.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Registration VIN\
- Insurance VIN\
- System VIN\
- Supporting paperwork VIN\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Any VIN discrepancy exists\
- Altered VIN suspected\
- VIN unreadable/incomplete\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Plate renewals must maintain proper registration classification.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Passenger plate\
- Commercial plate\
- Specialty plate\
- Disabled parking eligibility\
- Weight classification if applicable\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Plate class conflict exists\
- Incorrect weight classification\
- Specialty eligibility unresolved\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Commercial registrations may require:\
- Weight verification\
- IRP review\
- Business information\
- GVWR confirmation\
\
VERIFY:\
- Declared weight\
- Business/entity records\
- Commercial registration class\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Weight discrepancy exists\
- Commercial eligibility conflict exists\
- IRP requirements unresolved\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Address information should remain current during registration renewal processing.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Garaging address\
- Mailing address\
- County information\
- Owner information consistency\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Address mismatch exists\
- County tax/fee conflict exists\
- Fraud concerns arise\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Plate transfers and renewals require:\
- Same owner eligibility\
- Active registration status\
- Correct registration class\
\
VERIFY:\
- Plate ownership\
- Expiration status\
- Registration eligibility\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Plate ownership mismatch\
- Expired/ineligible transfer\
- Incorrect registration class\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Certain renewals may require emissions, inspection, or specialty eligibility verification depending on jurisdiction/type.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Specialty qualification\
- Commercial requirements\
- Disabled parking eligibility if applicable\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Supporting documentation missing\
- Eligibility conflict exists\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Late renewals may incur penalties or additional fees.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Registration expiration\
- Applicable late fees\
- Renewal period eligibility\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Registration expired excessively\
- Plate eligibility unclear\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Fraud prevention review is critical during registration renewal processing.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Identity legitimacy\
- Insurance validity\
- Registration ownership\
- Supporting documentation authenticity\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Fraud concerns\
- Fake insurance documentation\
- Altered registration records\
- Suspicious ownership activity\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
COMMON DEALERSHIP FAILURE POINTS:\
- Insurance not reporting electronically\
- Insurance name mismatch\
- Registration stop overlooked\
- Incorrect plate classification\
- Expired registration transfer\
- Wrong county fees\
- Incorrect commercial weight\
- Missing specialty documentation\
- Address mismatch\
- Suspended registration overlooked\
- VIN mismatch\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
HIGH-RISK ESCALATION SITUATIONS:\
- Active insurance suspension\
- Registration stop exists\
- VIN discrepancies\
- Fraud concerns\
- Ownership disputes\
- Commercial classification conflicts\
- Fake insurance cards\
- Administrative suspensions\
- Plate ownership conflicts\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
BEST PRACTICE:\
Before processing registration renewal:\
1. Verify insurance electronically FIRST.\
2. Verify registration stop/suspension status.\
3. Verify VIN across ALL records.\
4. Verify plate and registration classification.\
5. Verify address and county information.\
6. Escalate any irregularity immediately.\
\
