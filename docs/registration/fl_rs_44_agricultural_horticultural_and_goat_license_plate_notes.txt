==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Agricultural and horticultural restricted plates:
May issue ONLY:
To qualifying trucks/truck tractors:
Used exclusively:
For qualifying agricultural/horticultural operations.

==================================================
DEFINITION — AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS
==================================================

Agricultural products include:
- food products
- livestock
- agricultural products
- horticultural products
- plant food materials
- raw agricultural materials.

==================================================
DEFINITION — POINT OF PRODUCTION
==================================================

Point of production:
Means:
Initial harvest, collection, or gathering point:
Of agricultural products.

==================================================
DEFINITION — TRANSPORTING
==================================================

Transporting:
Means:
Initial hauling:
From point of production:
To:
- primary manufacture
- assembling
OR
- shipping point.

==================================================
DEFINITION — NOT-FOR-HIRE
==================================================

Not-for-hire:
Means:
Vehicle owner:
Must also own:
- agricultural/horticultural products
- livestock/livestock products
OR
- farm equipment/fertilizer being transported.

==================================================
DEFINITION — GOAT VEHICLE
==================================================

Goat:
Means:
Vehicle designed and used:
Primarily for:
- citrus fruit transportation within groves
OR
- transportation of crops on farms.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOAT RULE
==================================================

Goat vehicles:
May also haul:
- associated equipment
- supplies
- sanitary equipment
- farm trailers.

==================================================
GVW RULES
==================================================

GVW calculations vary:
Based on:
- heavy truck size
- trailer combinations
- truck tractor configurations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-45 RULE
==================================================

GVW guidance:
Refer to:
RS-45 Exhibit A.

==================================================
ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS — AGRICULTURAL/HORTICULTURAL
==================================================

Vehicle must:
- be truck tractor/heavy truck
- NOT operate for hire
- exclusively transport qualifying products
- operate within 150 miles of home address
- be owned by grower/owner
- driver paid by grower/owner.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 150-MILE RULE
==================================================

Agricultural/horticultural restricted vehicles:
May operate ONLY:
Within:
150-mile radius:
Of home address.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER RULE
==================================================

Truck owner:
Must own:
Products/livestock being transported.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INCIDENTAL HAULING RULE
==================================================

Vehicles may incidentally haul:
- farm implements
- fertilizers
Delivered directly to growers.

==================================================
GOAT ELIGIBILITY RULES
==================================================

Goat vehicles:
Must:
- NOT operate for hire
- primarily transport citrus/crops
- operate on farms/groves.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PUBLIC ROAD RULE
==================================================

Goat vehicles:
Cannot operate:
On public roads:
While carrying load.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Applications:
Require:
HSMV 83110.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 83110 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 83110:
Must certify:
Vehicle used solely:
For qualifying agricultural/horticultural purposes.

==================================================
GVW DECLARATION RULE
==================================================

Owner:
Must declare:
Gross Vehicle Weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GT RULE
==================================================

For goat registrations:
Enter:
"GT"
In motor vehicle issuance system Reg Use field.

==================================================
FEE RULES
==================================================

Fees:
Based on:
- GVW
- registration type
- goat designation
- applicable trust fund/service fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RATE CHART RULE
==================================================

Registration taxes:
Determined:
Using License Plate Rate Chart.

==================================================
HVUT RULES
==================================================

If GVW:
55,000 pounds or more:
HVUT proof required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-34 RULE
==================================================

HVUT processing:
Governed by:
RS-34.

==================================================
INSURANCE RULES
==================================================

Proof of Florida insurance:
Required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMERCIAL INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

If GVW:
26,001 pounds or more:
Commercial motor vehicle insurance:
Required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-33 RULE
==================================================

Commercial insurance requirements:
Governed by:
RS-33.

==================================================
RENEWAL AND TRANSFER RULES
==================================================

Renewals/transfers:
May process:
At any county license plate agency.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RENEWAL CERTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Renewals/transfers:
Require:
HSMV 83080:
Certifying continued qualifying agricultural use.

==================================================
REPLACEMENT RULES
==================================================

Replacement plates:
Require:
- HSMV 83146
- registration certificate copy.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RS-06 RULE
==================================================

Replacement processing:
Governed by:
RS-06.

==================================================
STOLEN PLATE RULES
==================================================

Stolen plate replacements:
May process:
Without fee:
If police report provided.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LAW ENFORCEMENT CARD RULE
==================================================

Law enforcement agency card:
Containing case number:
Acceptable:
Instead of full police report.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle issuance system comments:
Must include:
- law enforcement agency
- case number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SP CODE RULE
==================================================

If replacement uses:
Code "SP":
Police report or law enforcement card:
Must attach:
To HSMV 83146.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO REPORT RULE
==================================================

If no police report/card:
Replacement fees required.
