CORE FEE COMPONENTS
==================================================

Electronic title totals generally include:
- statutory title fees
- applicable trust fund fees
- security fee
- lien fee if applicable
- service fee where applicable

Printed paper title total:
Electronic title total
PLUS:
$2.50 paper title mail fee.

Electronic title total
PLUS:
issuing agency fee
PLUS:
fast title GR fee

fast title HSOTF fee.

AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is transaction motor vehicle, mobile home, OHV, or vessel?
2. Is transaction original, transfer, duplicate, lien add, salvage, rebuilt, certificate, or inspection?
3. Is vehicle for-hire?
4. Is there a lien?
5. Is title electronic, printed paper, or fast title?
6. Is the vehicle previously titled in Florida?
7. Is the vehicle previously titled/registered out of state or country?
8. Does NGW fee apply?
9. Does mobile home fee apply?
10. Does lemon law fee apply?
11. Does late penalty apply?
12. Does paper title mail fee apply?
13. Does fast title fee apply?
14. Does branch fee apply?
15. Is OHV title decal involved?
16. Is vessel out-of-state fee involved?
17. Is transaction lost in transit?
18. Is transaction certificate of destruction or derelict?
19. Is rebuilt inspection fee needed?
20. What exact total should be quoted?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK / FEE ERRORS
==================================================

- Quoting electronic title fee when customer wants paper title
- Forgetting paper title mail fee
- Forgetting fast title add-on fees
- Applying standard vehicle title fee to vessel transaction
- Applying motor vehicle title logic to OHV title
- Missing lien fee
- Missing out-of-state fee
- Missing mobile home fee
- Missing lemon law fee for new applicable vehicles
- Missing late penalty
- Charging late penalty when not applicable
- Confusing duplicate title with duplicate-with-transfer
- Confusing salvage title with certificate of destruction
- Incorrect for-hire title total
- Missing branch fee
- Quoting title-only fee when registration fees are also due
- Treating printed paper title and fast title as the same thing
- Forgetting rebuilt inspection fee
- Missing OHV decal/mail fee
- Incorrect vessel lien total

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

FEES-01 is a fee schedule only.

It should be used to calculate:
- title fees
- lien fees
- title issuance totals
- paper title totals
- fast title totals
- inspection fees
- certificate fees
- vessel title totals
- OHV title totals

It does NOT replace procedural requirements.

AI must combine FEES-01 with the applicable procedure file when answering workflow questions.
