==================================================

INITIAL REGISTRATION FEE:
Statute:
320.072(1)

Amount:
$225.00

Applies to:
Initial Florida registration transactions.

Does NOT apply to:
- heavy truck/tractor
- semis
- vehicles for hire
- travel trailers
- camper trailers

Distribution:
$124.88 General Revenue Fund

IMPORTANT:
This is one of the most common fees missed by inexperienced title clerks.

AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is registration initial or renewal?
2. Is transfer fee required?
3. Is original plate issue fee required?
4. Is replacement plate fee required?
5. Is specialty plate selected?
6. Is specialty processing fee required?
7. Is motorcycle specialty processing fee required?
8. Is vehicle commercial?
9. What is vehicle weight class?
10. Does initial registration fee apply?
11. Does mobile home surcharge apply?
12. Does temporary plate fee apply?
13. Does plate mail fee apply?
14. Does decal mail fee apply?
15. Does branch fee apply?
16. Does service fee apply?
17. Does delinquent fee apply?
18. Are voluntary contributions selected?
19. Are transfer and replacement fees both applicable?
20. Does vehicle qualify for exempt or special registration class?
