QUESTION:
"Duplicate title needs mileage."

ROUTE TO:
- 319.24_Odometer_Operational_Notes.txt
- 319.29_Operational_Notes.txt
- forms_required_logic_map.txt

EXPECTED ANSWER:
Current odometer reading is required on duplicate title application. Dealer duplicate also requires dealer license number.

------------------------------------------------------------

QUESTION:
"Customer says odometer was replaced."

ROUTE TO:
- 319.35_Operational_Notes.txt
- validation_logic_map.txt
- rejection_prevention_logic_map.txt

EXPECTED ANSWER:
If odometer cannot show same mileage after repair/replacement, it must be set to zero and written notice must be attached showing prior mileage and repair/replacement date. Review for fraud/tampering risk.

============================================================
REJECTION PREVENTION SUMMARY
============================================================

High rejection risk exists when:
- odometer disclosure is missing
- transferor did not complete mileage statement
- transferee did not acknowledge disclosure
- secure POA is missing or misused
- mileage from POA does not match title exactly
- used car original lacks odometer affidavit
- duplicate title application lacks current odometer reading
- dealer duplicate title lacks dealer license number
- mileage discrepancy is ignored
- kilometers/miles issue is not reviewed
- not actual mileage brand is treated as simple correction
- odometer tampering is suspected
- false odometer statement is submitted
- odometer replacement notice is missing
- customer requests mileage brand removal without authority/proof
- auction/dealer/purchaser mileage discrepancy is unresolved
