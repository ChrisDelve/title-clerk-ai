STATUTE: 320.0605
TITLE: Certificate of Registration; Possession Required; Exception
SOURCE: https://www.leg.state.fl.us/statutes/index.cfm?App_mode=Display_Statute&Search_String=&URL=0300-0399/0320/Sections/0320.0605.html
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Florida Law Reference / Operational Notes
CATEGORY: REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE / PROOF OF REGISTRATION / RENTAL DOCUMENTS / LEASE DOCUMENTS / CAB CARD / POSSESSION REQUIRED

============================================================
PURPOSE
============================================================

This statute governs possession of the registration certificate or acceptable substitute documentation while a vehicle is operated on Florida roads.

It is the legal reference for:
- registration certificate possession
- official copy / true copy
- electronic copy of rental or lease documentation
- temporary receipt from online renewal
- IRP cab card
- first 30 days after purchase of replacement vehicle exception
- rental/lease documentation requirements

============================================================
PLAIN ENGLISH SUMMARY
============================================================

The vehicle operator must have the registration certificate or acceptable substitute document in possession or carried in the vehicle while the vehicle is used on Florida roads.

Acceptable documents may include:
- registration certificate
- official copy
- true copy
- electronic copy of rental or lease documentation
- temporary online renewal receipt
- IRP cab card

The document must be shown on demand to law enforcement or department agents.

This requirement does not apply during the first 30 days after purchase of a replacement vehicle.

A violation is a noncriminal traffic infraction punishable as a nonmoving violation.

For rental/lease vehicles, electronic display of rental or lease documentation does not give law enforcement consent to access other information on the device.

============================================================
TITLE CLERK IMPORTANCE
============================================================

320.0605 is important when:
- customer asks what registration proof must be in vehicle
- customer only has electronic rental/lease docs
- dealer issued temporary online renewal receipt
- IRP cab card is used
- replacement vehicle purchased within 30 days
- rental/lease documentation is being used instead of registration certificate
- customer receives citation for no registration proof

REJECTION PREVENTION
============================================================

- customer is told insurance proof replaces registration proof
- rental/lease documentation is incomplete
- IRP cab card is missing
- replacement vehicle exception is misunderstood
- electronic document privacy/consent rule is misstated
