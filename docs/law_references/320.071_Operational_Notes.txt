============================================================
PURPOSE
============================================================

This statute governs advance renewal of Florida motor vehicle and mobile home registrations.

It is the legal reference for:
- renewing registration before expiration
- 3-month advance renewal period
- maximum registration period
- advance renewal for IRP/apportionable vehicles
- issuance of validation sticker or mobile home sticker after advance renewal
- unlawful sticker use

============================================================
PLAIN ENGLISH SUMMARY
============================================================

The owner of a currently registered Florida motor vehicle or mobile home may renew registration during the 3 months before the registration expires.

The registration period may not exceed 27 months.

The owner of an apportionable vehicle registered under the International Registration Plan may also file renewal during the 3 months before expiration.

After application and payment of required license taxes, service charges, and additional fees, the department or agent issues the appropriate validation sticker or mobile home sticker.

Using a validation sticker or mobile home sticker without lawful authority, or willfully violating department rules under this section, is a second-degree misdemeanor.

REJECTION PREVENTION
============================================================

- renewal is attempted outside the allowed advance window
- registration period would exceed 27 months
- required fees are not paid
- wrong validation sticker/mobile home sticker is used
- sticker is used without lawful authority
- IRP advance renewal is treated exactly like non-IRP without review

DEALERSHIP WORKFLOW
============================================================

1. Confirm current registration.
- Vehicle/mobile home must be currently registered in Florida.

2. Confirm renewal window.
- Renewal may be filed during the 3 months before expiration.

3. Confirm maximum registration period.
- Registration period may not exceed 27 months.

4. Collect correct fees.
- License tax.
- Service charges.
- Additional required fees.

5. Issue correct sticker.
- Validation sticker or mobile home sticker.

6. Hold/escalate if issue.
- Outside renewal window.
- Wrong sticker.
- IRP/apportioned uncertainty.
