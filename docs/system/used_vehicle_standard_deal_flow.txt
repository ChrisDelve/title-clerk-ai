==================================================
USED VEHICLE STANDARD DEAL FLOW
FLORIDA TITLE & REGISTRATION WORKFLOW
==================================================

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

This workflow outlines the standard dealership
processing flow for a used vehicle retail transaction
in Florida.

Applies to:
- retail used vehicle sales
- financed deals
- cash deals
- dealer-assisted registrations

==================================================
STEP 1 — VERIFY OWNERSHIP DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Verify:
- title assigned properly
- reassignment completed
- seller signatures present
- buyer information complete
- odometer disclosure complete
- lien status verified

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- TL-08
- TL-17

ESCALATE IF:
- altered title
- missing signatures
- VIN mismatch
- incomplete reassignment
- lien conflict

==================================================
STEP 2 — VERIFY CUSTOMER IDENTITY
==================================================

Verify:
- valid driver license
- customer legal name
- customer address
- identity consistency

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- TL-01

ESCALATE IF:
- expired ID
- identity mismatch
- suspicious documents

==================================================
STEP 3 — VERIFY INSURANCE
==================================================

Verify:
- Florida insurance
- policy active
- VIN matches if applicable

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- RS-36
- RS-33 if commercial

ESCALATE IF:
- invalid insurance
- commercial coverage uncertainty
- fake insurance concerns

==================================================
STEP 4 — DETERMINE TITLE STATUS
==================================================

Determine:
- paper title
- ELT title
- duplicate title needed
- lien satisfaction needed

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- TL-17
- TL-28

ESCALATE IF:
- lien unreleased
- title unavailable
- ELT conflict

==================================================
STEP 5 — DETERMINE REGISTRATION TYPE
==================================================

Determine:
- transfer plate
- new plate
- temporary tag needed
- specialty plate
- personalized plate

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- RS-22
- RS-27
- RS-31

==================================================
STEP 6 — DETERMINE IRF STATUS
==================================================

Determine:
- IRF due
- prior registration exemption
- military exemption
- operation of law exemption
- trust exemption

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- RS-30

ESCALATE IF:
- exemption dispute
- ownership conflict
- trust confusion

==================================================
STEP 7 — DETERMINE COMMERCIAL STATUS
==================================================

Determine:
- passenger vehicle
- commercial vehicle
- heavy truck
- interstate operation
- GVW classification

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- RS-33
- RS-45

ESCALATE IF:
- IRP uncertainty
- GVW uncertainty
- commercial insurance issue

==================================================
STEP 8 — CALCULATE FEES
==================================================

Calculate:
- registration fees
- plate fees
- title fees
- IRF
- specialty fees
- taxes

VERIFY:
- correct class code
- proper registration period

==================================================
STEP 9 — ISSUE TEMPORARY TAG IF NEEDED
==================================================

Determine:
- temp tag duration
- ETR eligibility
- title delay eligibility

RELATED PROCEDURES:
- RS-31

ESCALATE IF:
- excessive temp tag requests
- missing title paperwork
- repeated temp issuance

==================================================
STEP 10 — SUBMIT TITLE AND REGISTRATION
==================================================

Verify:
- all signatures complete
- all forms complete
- funding package complete
- insurance verified
- fees collected

SUBMIT:
- title application
- registration application
- supporting documents

==================================================
STEP 11 — FINAL DEAL REVIEW
==================================================

Verify:
- deal jacket complete
- copies retained
- lender requirements satisfied
- customer copies issued

ESCALATE IF:
- missing paperwork
- funding issues
- unresolved compliance concerns

==================================================
COMMON FAILURE POINTS
==================================================

- missing reassignment
- incorrect odometer disclosure
- incorrect IRF exemption
- invalid insurance
- missing lien release
- temp tag misuse
- incorrect GVW
- unsigned title docs
- stale funding paperwork

==================================================
SYSTEM PURPOSE
==================================================

This workflow exists to:
- standardize dealership processing
- reduce compliance risk
- guide title clerks
- support AI workflow reasoning
- improve transaction accuracy
- identify escalation points
