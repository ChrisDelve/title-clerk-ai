============================================================
ARIZONA SOURCE SUMMARY
============================================================

Arizona ADOT says the following are required to obtain Arizona title, registration, and license plate for a vehicle previously titled or registered in another state:

- Completed, signed Title and Registration Application
- Emissions compliance form for Phoenix and Tucson areas only
- Out-of-state title, or registration if title is held by lienholder
- Lien clearance, if applicable
- Power of Attorney from lessor, original or certified copy, if leased vehicle
- Payment of applicable title and registration fees

Arizona fee estimate section shows:
- Registration Fee: $8.00
- Title Fee: $4.00
- Air Quality Research Fee: $1.50
- Additional postage may be required

============================================================
FLORIDA DEALER WORKFLOW FOR ARIZONA CUSTOMER
============================================================

When a Florida dealership sells a vehicle to an Arizona customer and the customer wants Arizona title/registration:

1. Prepare ownership package:
   - MCO/MSO or properly assigned title
   - Dealer reassignment if applicable
   - Bill of sale / buyer’s order
   - Odometer disclosure/statement if applicable
   - Lienholder information if financed

2. Arizona-specific requirements to review:
   - Arizona Title and Registration Application
   - Emissions compliance form if customer is in Phoenix/Tucson area
   - Out-of-state title or registration if title is held by lienholder
   - Lien clearance if applicable
   - Original or certified lessor POA if leased

3. If financed:
   - Confirm lender/lienholder instructions.
   - Confirm whether Arizona title will be sent to lienholder.
   - Confirm lien clearance or lienholder documentation if a prior lien exists.

4. If leased:
   - Obtain original or certified copy of lessor POA.
   - Verify lease company/titleholder information matches documents.

5. If registering in Arizona:
   - Arizona emissions may be required in Phoenix/Tucson areas.
   - Do not assume Florida insurance/title requirements replace Arizona requirements.
   - Confirm Arizona insurance/registration requirements with agency or Arizona MVD if unclear.

============================================================
REJECTION / DELAY RISKS
============================================================

High risk if:

- Arizona Title and Registration Application is missing or unsigned.
- Phoenix/Tucson emissions compliance form is missing when applicable.
- Title is held by lienholder and supporting registration/lienholder documents are unclear.
- Lien clearance is missing.
- Leased vehicle lacks original/certified lessor POA.
- Tax/DR-123 handling is unclear.
- Lender instructions conflict with Arizona requirements.

============================================================
COMMON CLERK ANSWER
============================================================

For an Arizona customer buying from a Florida dealership, Arizona requires a completed signed Title and Registration Application, out-of-state title or registration if the title is held by lienholder, lien clearance if applicable, lessor POA if leased, and payment of applicable fees. If the customer is in the Phoenix or Tucson emissions area, an emissions compliance form may be required. Prepare the dealer ownership package, buyer’s order, odometer disclosure/statement if applicable, lienholder documents if financed, and review Florida DR-123/tax handling. Escalate if emissions, lien clearance, lessor POA, or tax handling is unclear.
