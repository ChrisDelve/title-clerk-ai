ARKANSAS SOURCE SUMMARY
============================================================

Arkansas myDMV Vehicle Registration and Title page states:

Customer completes the application by entering personal information and vehicle information.

After all required information is provided:
- Customer pays fees online.
- After application is submitted, customer receives email notifications of title status.
- Once application is accepted, customer receives plate and registration in the mail.
- Title will not be mailed until all documents have been received and processed.

What customer needs for Registration and Title:

Primary documents:
- Arkansas Title
- Bill of Sale
- Manufacturer Statement of Origin
- Out-of-State Title

Other required items:
- Insurance Card
- Federal Odometer Statement

Customer may need:
- Personal Property Tax Number, PPAN, assigned from county assessor
- Lien Contract / Security Agreement
- Current Year County Tax Assessment
- Proof of paid tax receipt

Arkansas page warning:
- Customer should have primary documents and insurance card ready before starting the process.
- Information from those documents is needed to complete the process.
- If transferring a plate, registration must be current.

Eligibility requirements:
- Customers can only register passenger cars online.
- This includes personal cars, pickup trucks, vans, and motor homes.
- Customers applying for or using a Title Purpose Only Certificate cannot register online.
- A TPOC does not require proof of insurance.
- No license plate will be issued with TPOC.
- Only a title is received with TPOC.

============================================================
FLORIDA DEALER WORKFLOW FOR ARKANSAS CUSTOMER
============================================================

When a Florida dealership sells a vehicle to an Arkansas customer and the customer wants Arkansas title/registration:

1. Prepare dealer ownership package:
   - MCO/MSO for new vehicle, or properly assigned out-of-state title for used vehicle
   - Dealer reassignment if applicable
   - Bill of Sale / buyer’s order
   - Federal odometer statement or odometer disclosure/statement if applicable
   - Lien contract / security agreement if financed
   - Lienholder information if financed

2. Arkansas-specific items to review:
   - Primary ownership document
   - Insurance card if registration is requested
   - Federal odometer statement if applicable
   - PPAN if property tax review is required
   - Current year county tax assessment if required
   - Proof of paid tax receipt if required
   - Current registration if customer is transferring a plate

3. If financed:
   - Include lien contract / security agreement.
   - Confirm lienholder/title instructions.
   - Confirm whether Arkansas title will be mailed to lienholder or customer depending on lien status.

4. If registering online:
   - Confirm vehicle is eligible for online registration.
   - Arkansas source says online registration is only for passenger cars, including personal cars, pickup trucks, vans, and motor homes.
   - Customer should have all primary documents and insurance card ready before starting.

5. If customer asks for Title Purpose Only Certificate:
   - Do not treat it as registration.
   - TPOC does not require proof of insurance.
   - No license plate is issued.
   - Customer receives title only.
   - Arkansas online registration is not available for TPOC.

6. If plate transfer:
   - Confirm the registration is current before attempting transfer.

============================================================
REJECTION / DELAY RISKS
============================================================

High risk if:

- Primary ownership document is missing.
- Out-of-state title or MSO/MCO is missing.
- Bill of sale is missing.
- Insurance card is missing when registration is requested.
- Federal odometer statement is missing when applicable.
- PPAN is required but missing.
- Current year county tax assessment is required but missing.
- Proof of paid tax receipt is required but missing.
- Lien contract/security agreement is missing when financed.
- Customer tries to transfer a plate but registration is not current.
- Customer tries to register online with a vehicle not eligible for online registration.
- Customer uses Title Purpose Only Certificate but expects plate/registration.
- Title documents are not submitted/processed, delaying mailing of title.

============================================================
COMMON CLERK ANSWER
============================================================

For an Arkansas customer buying from a Florida dealership, Arkansas myDMV says the customer needs a primary ownership document such as an Arkansas title, bill of sale, Manufacturer Statement of Origin, or out-of-state title, plus an insurance card and Federal Odometer Statement if applicable. The customer may also need a PPAN assigned by the county assessor, lien contract/security agreement if financed, current year county tax assessment, and proof of paid tax receipt. If transferring a plate, the registration must be current. Arkansas online registration is limited to passenger cars, including personal cars, pickup trucks, vans, and motor homes. If the customer is applying for or using a Title Purpose Only Certificate, they cannot register online; TPOC does not require proof of insurance, no license plate is issued, and only a title is received. Prepare the dealer package, buyer’s order, odometer disclosure/statement if applicable, lien documents if financed, and review Florida DR-123/Arkansas tax handling. Escalate if PPAN, county tax assessment, paid tax receipt, lien/security agreement, online eligibility, TPOC, or tax handling is unclear.
