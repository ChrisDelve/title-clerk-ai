============================================================
FLORIDA DEALER WORKFLOW FOR VERMONT CUSTOMER
============================================================

When a Florida dealership sells a vehicle to a Vermont customer and the customer wants Vermont title/registration:

1. Prepare dealer ownership package:
   - MCO/MSO for new vehicle or properly assigned title for used vehicle
   - Dealer reassignment if applicable
   - Buyer’s order / bill of sale
   - Odometer disclosure/statement if applicable
   - Lienholder information if financed

2. Vermont-specific items to verify before submission:
   - Current Vermont Registration/Tax/Title Application
   - Title or MCO/MSO requirements
   - Vermont insurance requirement
   - Vermont purchase/use tax requirement
   - Vermont inspection requirement
   - VIN verification requirement, if applicable
   - Lienholder/title mailing requirements if financed

3. If financed:
   - Confirm lienholder information.
   - Confirm title/lien recording requirements.
   - Confirm whether lienholder receives title.

4. If registering:
   - Verify Vermont insurance and inspection requirements.
   - Do not assume Florida insurance satisfies Vermont registration requirements unless Vermont DMV/agency confirms.

5. If source cannot be verified:
   - Hold and escalate to title lead, agency, lender, or Vermont DMV before promising completion.
