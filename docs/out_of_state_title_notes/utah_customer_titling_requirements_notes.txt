============================================================
UTAH SOURCE SUMMARY
============================================================

Utah DMV says that every title situation is unique, but for transfer of an out-of-state title the customer usually needs:

- Certificate of Title
- Seller must provide original certificate of title
- VIN/HIN on document should match VIN/HIN on vehicle
- Seller must sign where necessary to release ownership
- Previous lienholder, if printed on the face of the title, must sign to release lien
- Purchase date and purchase price must be correctly filled in
- Application for Utah Title, Form TC-656, Application for Utah Title and Registration
- Form TC-656 must be completed by the new vehicle owners
- All owners listed on the application must provide government-issued identification
- Driver license
- Emissions Inspection Certificates if registering and customer lives in Salt Lake, Davis, Weber, Cache, or Utah County

============================================================
FLORIDA DEALER WORKFLOW FOR UTAH CUSTOMER
============================================================

When a Florida dealership sells a vehicle to a Utah customer and the customer wants Utah title/registration:

1. Prepare dealer ownership package:
   - Original title or MCO/MSO
   - Dealer reassignment if applicable
   - Buyer’s order / bill of sale
   - Odometer disclosure/statement if applicable
   - Lienholder information if financed
   - Purchase date and price

2. Utah-specific items to review:
   - Original certificate of title
   - VIN/HIN matches vehicle
   - Seller signed to release ownership
   - Prior lienholder signed/released if printed on title
   - Utah Form TC-656
   - Government-issued ID for all owners listed on application
   - Driver license
   - Emissions certificate if registering in Salt Lake, Davis, Weber, Cache, or Utah County

3. If financed:
   - Confirm lienholder/title instructions.
   - Confirm new lienholder information for Utah title package.
   - Confirm prior lien release if prior lien appears on title.

4. If registration is involved:
   - Confirm emissions requirement by Utah county.
   - Do not assume no emissions unless county is verified.

5. If title has prior lienholder:
   - Do not submit until lien release/signature issue is resolved.
   - Escalate if prior lienholder release is missing or unclear.

============================================================
COMMON CLERK ANSWER
============================================================

For a Utah customer buying from a Florida dealership, Utah usually requires the original certificate of title, seller signature releasing ownership, purchase date and purchase price, and prior lienholder release if a lienholder is printed on the title. Utah also requires Form TC-656 Application for Utah Title and Registration, government-issued ID for all owners listed on the application, and driver license. If registration is requested and the customer lives in Salt Lake, Davis, Weber, Cache, or Utah County, an emissions inspection certificate may be required. Prepare the dealer ownership package, buyer’s order, odometer disclosure/statement if applicable, lienholder information if financed, and review Florida DR-123/Utah tax handling. Escalate if original title, lienholder release, TC-656, ID, emissions, or tax handling is unclear.
