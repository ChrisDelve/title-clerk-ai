IMPORTANT DELINQUENCY RULES
==================================================

Single asterisk (*):
Fees doubled after 13th month.

Double asterisk (**):
Fees tripled after 25th month.

AI should:
- identify delinquent registration timing
- determine whether doubled/tripled penalties apply
