This is a Florida title and registration assistant built around dealership workflows.

It is not just a general chatbot. It uses Florida statute notes, FLHSMV procedure notes, dealership operational notes, rejection-prevention logic, and real tested title-clerk scenarios.

The purpose is to help clerks, F&I, managers, and office staff quickly answer common title and registration questions before a deal is submitted incorrectly.

It helps identify:
- what workflow applies
- what documents are required
- whether the transaction should be held
- whether escalation is needed
- whether the issue is title-only, registration, plate transfer, EFS, lien, odometer, insurance, ID, or audit-trail related
- what source files support the answer

The goal is fewer preventable rejections, less repeated title-office questions, and faster guidance for people who are not title specialists.
