DOCUMENT: HSMV-82101
TITLE: Application for Duplicate or Lost in Transit Title
SOURCE: https://www.flhsmv.gov/pdf/forms/82101.pdf
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Notes

PURPOSE:
Used to request a duplicate title or replace a title lost in transit.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Lost title
- Damaged title
- Title never received
- Duplicate title issuance
- Dealer title processing

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Owner information must match DMV records.
- VIN accuracy is critical.
- Lienholder information must be reviewed.
- Electronic title status should be checked before processing.

COMMON ERRORS:
- Incorrect VIN
- Missing signatures
- Incorrect owner information
- Missing lienholder information
- Duplicate request when electronic title exists
