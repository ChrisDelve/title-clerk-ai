DOCUMENT: HSMV-82042
TITLE: VIN and Odometer Verification
SOURCE: https://www.flhsmv.gov/pdf/forms/82042.pdf
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Notes

PURPOSE:
Used to verify VIN and odometer information for title and registration processing.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Out-of-state title transfer
- VIN verification
- Registration processing
- Odometer verification
- Inspection workflows

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- VIN must match vehicle and title paperwork.
- Authorized personnel must complete verification.
- Odometer disclosure accuracy is critical.
- Incorrect VINs commonly cause DMV rejection.
