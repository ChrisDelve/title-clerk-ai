DOCUMENT: HSMV-82050
TITLE: Notice of Sale and Bill of Sale
SOURCE: https://www.flhsmv.gov/pdf/forms/82050.pdf
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Notes

PURPOSE:
Used to document sale/transfer of vehicle ownership.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Private party sale
- Dealer sale support
- Ownership transfer
- Tax documentation
- Odometer support

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- VIN accuracy is critical.
- Sale price affects tax calculations.
- Buyer/seller information must match supporting documents.
- Odometer disclosure may be required.
- Signatures are required.

COMMON ERRORS:
- Incorrect VIN
- Missing sale amount
- Missing signatures
- Incorrect buyer information
- Odometer inconsistencies

END OF FILE
