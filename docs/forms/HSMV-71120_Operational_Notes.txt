PURPOSE:
This form is used to apply for a Florida Certificate of Destruction.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Vehicle permanently destroyed
- Scrap vehicle processing
- Insurance total loss destruction
- Non-rebuildable vehicle processing

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Certificate of Destruction generally prevents future titling.
- VIN accuracy is critical.
- Supporting ownership documents are required.
- Insurance company documentation may be required.

COMMON ERRORS:
- Incorrect VIN
- Missing owner signatures
- Missing insurance information
- Incomplete destruction certification
