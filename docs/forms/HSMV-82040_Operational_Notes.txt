PURPOSE:
Primary Florida title and registration application form.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Vehicle title transfer
- New vehicle title
- Registration processing
- Out-of-state title transfer
- Lien recording
- Plate issuance
- Dealer processing
- Registration renewals
- Duplicate title requests

IMPORTANT TITLE CLERK NOTES:
- This is one of the most important forms in dealership title work.
- VIN accuracy is critical.
- Owner information must exactly match supporting documents.
- Odometer disclosure must be accurate.
- Lienholder information must match lender instructions.
- Insurance requirements may apply.
- Plate transfer eligibility must be reviewed.
- IRF applicability must be reviewed.

COMMON ERRORS:
- Missing signatures
- Incorrect VIN
- Incorrect lienholder ELT number
- Missing odometer disclosure
- Registration type mismatch
- Missing insurance information
- Owner name mismatch
- Incomplete dealer reassignment
