DOCUMENT: HSMV-82053
TITLE: Power of Attorney for Motor Vehicle, Mobile Home or Vessel
SOURCE: https://www.flhsmv.gov/pdf/forms/82053.pdf
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Notes

PURPOSE:
Used to authorize another individual or entity to act on behalf of the owner regarding title, registration, lien, or odometer transactions.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Dealer title processing
- Odometer disclosure authority
- Remote transactions
- Lender/title processing
- Out-of-state owner transactions
- Title correction processing

IMPORTANT TITLE CLERK NOTES:
- Owner names must match title documents exactly.
- VIN accuracy is critical.
- POA authority scope should be reviewed carefully.
- Signatures must be properly completed.
- POA may authorize odometer disclosure.
- Some lenders require specific POA wording.

COMMON ERRORS:
- Incorrect VIN
- Missing signatures
- Owner mismatch
- Incomplete authorization language
- Missing notarization where applicable
