DOCUMENT: HSMV-82260
TITLE: Satisfaction of Lien
SOURCE: https://www.flhsmv.gov/pdf/forms/82260.pdf
DOCUMENT_TYPE: Operational Notes

PURPOSE:
Used to release/satisfy a lien recorded on a title.

COMMON USE CASES:
- Vehicle payoff
- Trade payoff processing
- Title clearance
- Lender lien release
- Title transfer preparation

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Lienholder information must match DMV records.
- VIN accuracy is critical.
- Authorized lender signature required.
- ELT status should be reviewed.
- Incorrect lien releases may delay title processing.

COMMON ERRORS:
- Incorrect VIN
- Missing lender signature
- Incorrect lienholder information
- Unauthorized signer
- Missing release date
