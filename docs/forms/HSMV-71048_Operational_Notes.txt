PURPOSE:
This form is used to certify that a vehicle has passed a required highway or highway safety inspection.

============================================================
TITLE CLERK OPERATIONAL NOTES
============================================================

1. FORM PURPOSE

This form documents that a vehicle inspection has been completed and certified.

The inspection is generally related to:
- rebuilt vehicles
- reconstructed vehicles
- specially constructed vehicles
- inspection-related title processing

2. COMMON USE CASES

This form may commonly appear during:
- rebuilt title processing
- salvage rebuild processing
- inspection verification
- title branding workflows
- DMV compliance review

3. INSPECTION CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENT

The form must be completed by:
- authorized inspection personnel
- law enforcement
- approved FLHSMV inspection entities
- authorized compliance examiners

4. VIN ACCURACY IS CRITICAL

Always verify:
- VIN matches title paperwork
- VIN matches vehicle
- VIN contains 17 characters
- no altered VIN indicators exist

5. COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS

Common issues include:
- incomplete VIN
- missing inspector signature
- missing inspection date
- illegible writing
- altered inspection information
- mismatch between supporting documents and inspection data

6. DEALERSHIP WORKFLOW IMPORTANCE

This form may be required before:
- rebuilt title issuance
- registration processing
- title conversion
- final DMV submission

7. AI TRAINING NOTES

This document should be treated as:
- inspection certification form
- rebuilt title workflow form
- VIN validation source
- compliance inspection reference
