FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — APPLICATION FOR CERTIFICATE OF TITLE FOR A MOTOR VEHICLE PURCHASED FROM A SHERIFF'S SALE
PROCEDURE: TL-22
CATEGORY: SHERIFF SALES / OPERATION OF LAW / COURT ORDER TRANSFERS
RELATED STATUTE:
- 319.28 FS

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Vehicles purchased from sheriff sales
- Mobile homes purchased from sheriff sales
- Court-ordered seizure sales
- Transfers by operation of law

Applies when:
- Sheriff seizes property under court order
- Public auction/sheriff sale occurs
- Purchaser applies for Florida title

==================================================
CORE LEGAL PRINCIPLE
==================================================

A Writ of Execution authorizes:
- Sheriff seizure of property
- Public sale of property
- Transfer of ownership by court authority

Purchaser must properly retitle vehicle/mobile home after sale.

==================================================
WRIT OF EXECUTION DEFINITION
==================================================

A Writ of Execution:
- Is issued by court
- Directs sheriff to seize property
- Allows public sale to satisfy debt

This document is required for title processing.

==================================================
FLORIDA-TITLED VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

IF vehicle/mobile home currently titled in Florida:

REQUIRED:

1. HSMV 82040 title application
2. Original sheriff bill of sale
3. HSMV 82993 odometer disclosure if applicable
4. Copy of Writ of Execution
5. Lien satisfactions if required
6. Sales tax information
7. Registration/decal information
8. Title fees

==================================================
SHERIFF BILL OF SALE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Bill of sale must contain:
- Sheriff information
- Purchaser name
- Selling price
- Complete vehicle description

Original required.

==================================================
ODOMETER REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

HSMV 82993 required:
- For vehicles subject to odometer law

Must be completed by:
- Sheriff
AND
- Purchaser

==================================================
LIEN REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Lien satisfactions required:
- For liens shown on records

IF lien carried forward:
- Affidavit or lienholder statement required

==================================================
IMPORTANT LIEN EXCEPTION
==================================================

IF lien recorded AFTER Writ of Execution issued:
- Lien satisfaction NOT required

==================================================
REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Motor vehicles:
- Plate transfer
OR
- New plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit

Mobile homes:
- Decal required
- Non-use affidavit NOT allowed

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

IF vehicle previously titled out of state:

REQUIRED:

1. VIN verification
2. Ownership verification
3. Sheriff sale documents
4. Writ of Execution
5. Sales tax
6. Registration requirements
7. Fees

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION RULES
==================================================

VIN verification required for:
- Most out-of-state vehicles

May use:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN section on HSMV 82040

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Semi-trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel recreation trailers

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE OWNERSHIP PROOF
==================================================

ACCEPTABLE:
- Out-of-state title
- State printout
- State letter
- Teletype verification
- Ownership/lien verification records

==================================================
DEALER REQUIREMENT
==================================================

IMPORTANT:

Dealer purchasing from sheriff sale:
- MUST title vehicle in dealer name first

Cannot:
- Immediately resell without titling

Applies to:
- Dealers
- Individuals

==================================================
COMMON DEALERSHIP ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing Writ of Execution
- Missing sheriff bill of sale
- Missing odometer disclosure
- Missing lien satisfaction
- Missing out-of-state verification
- Attempting resale before dealer titles vehicle
- Using non-use affidavit on mobile home
- Missing VIN verification
- Accepting incomplete ownership proof
