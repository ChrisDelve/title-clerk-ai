==================================================
ASPT DEFINITION
==================================================

ASPT means:
- Vehicle assembled from parts
OR
- Combined from parts

Using:
- New parts
OR
- Used parts

Placed on:
- Existing frame
OR
- Homemade frame.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-REBUILT RULE
==================================================

ASPT:
DOES NOT mean:
- Rebuilt vehicle

If vehicle declared:
- Total loss under 319.30 FS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CD RULE
==================================================

Parts from Certificate of Destruction (CD):
MAY be used

ONLY IF:
- 49% or less of parts
come from ONE CD vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 50% RULE
==================================================

If:
- 50% or more of parts
come from ONE CD vehicle

THEN:
Vehicle considered:
- Rebuild of CD vehicle
NOT ASPT.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MULTIPLE-CD RULE
==================================================

Parts from:
- 3 or more CD vehicles

MAY create ASPT

IF:
- 49% or less from each single vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPLETED-STATE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle/mobile home/trailer:
MUST be fully completed

BEFORE:
Applying for title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGIONAL OFFICE RULE
==================================================

ASPT applications:
MUST be submitted to:
- Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Compliance Examiner:
- Inspects ASPT vehicle
AND
- Assigns FLA/FLT number.

==================================================
INSPECTION FEES
==================================================

Initial inspection:
- $40

Subsequent inspections:
- $20 each.

==================================================
TITLE DATA RULES
==================================================

MAKE:
- ASPT

YEAR:
- Year assembled.

BODY:
- Actual body type.

BRAND:
- Assembled From Parts

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA RULE
==================================================

FLA decal assigned to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Motorcycles
- Off-highway vehicles
- Some trailers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLT RULE
==================================================

FLT number assigned to:
- ASPT trailers (TL)
weighing:
- 2,000 pounds or more

AND titled by:
- Weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER WEIGHT RULE
==================================================

Trailers weighing:
- 1,999 pounds or less

CANNOT:
- Be titled.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-WEIGHT TRAILER RULE
==================================================

Trailers NOT titled by weight:
(TV, CA, HS etc.)

Receive:
- FLA number
NOT:
- FLT number.

==================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS — STANDARD ASPT
==================================================

Need:
1. Florida title or out-of-state ownership
2. HSMV 82040 MV/MH
3. HSMV 84490
4. Major component receipts
5. Sales tax documentation
6. Certified weight slip if required
7. Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE RULE
==================================================

Florida title used for donor frame:
Will be marked:
- Cancelled (Junked)

AFTER:
ASPT processing.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-07 RULE
==================================================

If title unavailable:
Follow:
- TL-07.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA ASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

FLA decal assigned:
ONLY AFTER:
Application reviewed and approved.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

Original receipts required for:
- ALL major component parts.

==================================================
RECEIPT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Receipts must show:
- Seller name
- Seller address
- Seller signature
- Identification number for component

Business receipts:
- Signature NOT required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ENGINE RULE
==================================================

Engine receipt must show:
- Engine year.

==================================================
MOTOR VEHICLE MAJOR COMPONENTS
==================================================

Includes:
- Fender
- Hood
- Bumper
- Firewall/cowl assembly
- Rear quarter panel
- Trunk lid
- Door
- Decklid
- Floor pan
- Engine
- Frame
- Transmission
- Catalytic converter
- Airbag

==================================================
TRUCK MAJOR COMPONENTS
==================================================

Includes standard components PLUS:
- Dump bed
- Wrecker bed
- Crane bed
- Mixer body
- Cargo box

==================================================
MOTORCYCLE MAJOR COMPONENTS
==================================================

Includes:
- Frame/body assembly
- Fenders
- Gas tanks
- Engine
- Cylinder block
- Heads
- Engine case
- Crank case
- Transmission/drive train
- Front fork assembly
- Wheels

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HARLEY RULE
==================================================

Harley Davidson frames before 1970:
Bills of sale accepted without VIN.

Other brands:
- NOT acceptable.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MCO RULE
==================================================

MCO for:
- Crank case
OR
- Transmission case

DOES NOT replace:
- Engine receipt
OR
- Transmission receipt.

==================================================
MOBILE HOME COMPONENT RULE
==================================================

Mobile home major component:
- Frame only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax due on:
- Vehicle purchase price
AND
- All untaxed component parts.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WEIGHT SLIP RULE
==================================================

Certified weight slip required:
Except:
- Motorcycles
- Mobile homes
- Length-based trailers.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEALED ENVELOPE RULE
==================================================

Compliance Examiner provides:
- Sealed envelope

Containing:
- Original forms
- Receipt copies

IF seal broken:
- DO NOT ACCEPT.

Refer customer:
- Back to Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL COUNT RULE
==================================================

Tax Collector:
MUST verify:
Receipt count matches:
- HSMV 84490.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT FIELD RULE
==================================================

Processing agent must enter:
- Region number
AND
- FLA audit number

In:
- Comment Desc field.

==================================================
HOMEMADE FRAME RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Affidavit describing frame construction
AND
- Verifying homemade frame.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTORCYCLE RULE
==================================================

Motorcycle previously titled out-of-state:
Using aftermarket VIN:
Requires:
- New FLA assignment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER TEMP TAG RULE
==================================================

Regional Office may issue:
- Temporary plate

To move trailer:
- For weighing/inspection.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLT PLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

FLT number must be:
- Welded
OR
- Die-cut stamped

In:
- Permanent visible location.

Usually:
- Tow hitch/front trailer area.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER LEGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

If FLT number not legible:
- Certification denied.

==================================================
TRAILER COMPONENT PARTS
==================================================

Includes:
- Axle
- Frame
- Trailer body/cargo area

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER ROADWORTHY RULE
==================================================

Need affidavit stating:
- Trailer roadworthy.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPAIR RULE
==================================================

Factory-built trailer:
With only repairs:
- NOT ASPT.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA DECAL PLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicles:
- Driver door jamb

Motorcycles:
- Right-side frame down tube

Mobile homes:
- Main door jamb below lower hinge

Decals:
- NOT on removable parts.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL INSTALL RULE
==================================================

Must:
- Slice through decal
before applying:
- Clear laminate strip.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ODOMETER RULE
==================================================

Odometer branded:
- NOT ACTUAL MILEAGE

For:
- ASPT vehicles/motorcycles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUILDER TITLE RULE
==================================================

If builder provides ALL parts:
Builder MUST:
- Title vehicle in own name
BEFORE:
- Selling vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIOR FLA RULE
==================================================

Old FLA decal:
MUST be removed
WHEN:
New FLA assigned.

==================================================
OFF-HIGHWAY RULE
==================================================

Off-highway ASPT:
MAKE:
- ASPT

BODY:
- OH

BRAND:
- Assembled From Parts

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE RESIDENT RULE
==================================================

Florida resident temporarily out-of-state:
MAY process through:
- Regional Office.

FLA decal may be mailed to:
- Law enforcement authority
for verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-FLORIDA RESIDENT RULE
==================================================

Non-Florida legal residents:
CANNOT receive:
- Florida ASPT title
OR
- FLA assignment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMP TAG RULE
==================================================

10-day temporary plate:
MAY be issued
FOR:
- Inspection/title purposes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CAB REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

If ONLY cab replaced:
Vehicle becomes:
- ASPT

AND:
- Requires FLA assignment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUMP TRUCK RULE
==================================================

TR truck converted to dump truck:
MUST process as:
- ASPT

Tax collector:
- SHOULD NOT perform simple body correction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUNE BUGGY RULE
==================================================

Homemade dune buggy:
Must contact:
- Regional Office.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is vehicle ASPT or rebuilt?
2. Are CD parts involved?
3. Does 49% rule apply?
4. Is vehicle completed?
5. Is Regional Office inspection required?
6. Is FLA or FLT assignment needed?
7. Is homemade frame involved?
8. Are all major component receipts present?
9. Is certified weight slip required?
10. Is sealed envelope intact?
11. Is trailer over 2,000 pounds?
12. Is vehicle off-highway?
13. Is odometer branding required?
14. Is applicant Florida resident?
15. Is dump truck conversion involved?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Accepting broken sealed envelope
- Missing HSMV 84490
- Missing major component receipts
- Ignoring 49% CD rule
- Incorrect FLA/FLT assignment
- Titling sub-2000 lb trailer
- Missing weight slip
- Incorrect body type
- Missing comment desc entry
- Processing dump truck as body correction
- Missing homemade frame affidavit
- Incorrect decal placement
- Missing inspection fees
