FLHSMV TL-01
SIGNATURE REQUIREMENTS
DEALERSHIP OPERATIONAL NOTES
REVISION DATE: 04/2026

========================================================
PRIMARY PURPOSE
========================================================

This procedure establishes required signature standards,
identity verification requirements, ownership conjunction
rules, business authorization standards, acceptable proof
of identification, power of attorney handling, and
transfer endorsement requirements for Florida title and
registration transactions.

This procedure is critical for:
- Title transfers
- Duplicate titles
- Original title applications
- ELT transactions
- Registration processing
- Odometer disclosures
- Business-owned vehicles
- Out-of-state title transfers
- Power of attorney transactions
- Deceased owner scenarios
- Joint ownership processing

Failure to obtain proper signatures can result in:
- Title rejection
- Fraud exposure
- Ownership disputes
- Compliance violations
- Delayed funding
- Delayed registration issuance

========================================================
CORE CLASSIFICATION LOGIC
========================================================

FIRST STEP:
Determine how ownership names are connected on title.

Critical conjunctions:
- OR
- AND
- AND/OR
- No conjunction shown

This determines whether one signature or all signatures
are required.

========================================================
FLORIDA TITLE CONJUNCTION RULES
========================================================

--------------------------------------------------------
OWNERS CONNECTED BY "OR"
--------------------------------------------------------

Operational Meaning:
Joint tenancy with survivorship rights.

One owner may:
- Transfer ownership
- Sign reassignment
- Sign duplicate title application
- Sign most HSMV forms

Rules:
- Original application requires all signatures
- Duplicate title requires one owner signature
- Transfer requires one owner signature
- HSMV forms generally require one owner signature

Upon death:
Ownership interest passes automatically to surviving owner.

High Risk:
Do NOT incorrectly require both signatures if title uses OR.

========================================================
OWNERS CONNECTED BY "AND"
========================================================

Operational Meaning:
All owners must participate.

Required:
- All titled owners signatures
- All reassignment signatures
- All duplicate title signatures
- All HSMV form signatures

Upon death:
Estate involvement may become necessary.

High Risk:
Missing one owner signature will reject transfer.

========================================================
AND/OR CONJUNCTION
========================================================

Rules vary by state.

Always review:
- Out-of-state title chart
- State-specific conjunction rules
- Any title messaging regarding ownership type

========================================================
ORIGINAL TITLE APPLICATION RULE
========================================================

Regardless of conjunction:
ALL CUSTOMERS MUST SIGN ORIGINAL APPLICATION FOR TITLE.

This is frequently missed by dealerships.

========================================================
PROOF OF IDENTITY REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

Proof of identity REQUIRED at title application.

Acceptable:
- Valid Florida DL
- Valid out-of-state DL
- Valid ID card
- Valid Canadian DL/ID
- Valid passport
- Valid passport card

Expired identification is NOT acceptable.

If proof is NOT Florida DL/ID:
- Submit legible copy with paperwork
- Enter proof type and number into comments section

========================================================
VALID PASSPORT REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

Foreign passport acceptable ONLY if:
- Unexpired
AND
- Has lawful presence documentation

Examples:
- I-94
- Permanent resident card
- Immigrant visa

========================================================
BUSINESS SIGNATURE REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

Corporate signatures MUST include:
- Officer name
- Official corporate title

Examples:
- President
- Manager
- Vice President
- Authorized Agent

Verify authorization through:
https://dos.myflorida.com/sunbiz/

If Sunbiz unavailable:
Require business letter authorizing signer.

Letter must:
- Be on company letterhead
- Be signed by different officer
- State signer has authority

Does NOT apply to licensed dealers.

========================================================
BUSINESS VALIDATION REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

Possible verification:
- FEID documentation
- Corporation papers
- Certificate of Status
- Fictitious name filings
- Business license

Customer business name must match:
- Corporate charter
- FEID records
- Sunbiz records

========================================================
OWNER NAME REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

Title application name MUST match:
- Driver license
- Identification card
- Passport

Required format:
- Full first name
- Middle or maiden name
- Last name

Nicknames should not be used.

========================================================
NAME CHANGE WORKFLOW
========================================================

If customer changed name:
- System may already reflect updated name
- Old paper title may still show previous name

To physically update title:
Require:
- Existing title
- Affidavit requesting updated name

Process as:
MODIFY TITLE TRANSACTION

Fees apply.

Applies to:
- Marriage
- Divorce
- Court order

========================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE NAME CHANGE
========================================================

If:
- Same owner
- No ownership change
- Only name changed

Require:
- Proof of name change
- Affidavit

Customer may alternatively:
Transfer title into new name.

========================================================
ADDRESS REQUIREMENTS
========================================================

Application must contain:
- Residential address
OR
- Business address

If customer has valid Florida DL/ID:
- Only mailing address may be updated

Residential changes must occur through:
- DL replacement
- DL renewal

Reason:
Voter registration compliance.

========================================================
FRAUD ALERT PROCEDURES
========================================================

If system displays:
"POSSIBLE IMPOSTER – IDENTITY VERIFICATION REQUIRED"

Check:
- MV fraud stops
- Identity alerts
- Customer notes

If fraud stop exists:
CONTACT MV FRAUD UNIT.

========================================================
ODOMETER DISCLOSURE RULES
========================================================

VERY IMPORTANT.

Computer-generated signatures acceptable on:
- Applications
- Affidavits
- Most forms

NOT acceptable on odometer disclosures.

Federal law requires:
- Signature
- Printed name

Applies to:
- Buyer
- Seller

Reject if:
- Printed name missing

========================================================
ELECTRONIC SIGNATURE EXCEPTIONS
========================================================

Dealers and manufacturers may use electronic signatures
for odometer disclosures IF:

- Audit trail provided
- Timestamp available
- IP/location history available
- Certificate of Completion included

DocuSign certificate acceptable.

Attestation letter required stating:
Electronic signatures comply with 49 CFR 580.

========================================================
INSURANCE COMPANY EXCEPTION
========================================================

Insurance companies may use electronic odometer
signatures in total loss transactions.

Require:
- Audit trail
- Supporting timestamps
- Attestation statement

========================================================
POWER OF ATTORNEY RULES
========================================================

Power of Attorney accepted for title work.

Require:
- Copy of POA with transaction

EXCEPTION:
HSMV 82995 MUST BE ORIGINAL.

Photocopy NOT acceptable.

High Risk:
Dealers frequently attempt to use copied 82995 forms.

========================================================
SIGNATURE FORMAT RULES
========================================================

Acceptable signatures:
- Handwritten
- Printed
- Stamped
- "X"

Unless federal odometer rules apply.

========================================================
CHILD IDENTIFICATION RULE
========================================================

Children under age 5:
Birth certificate acceptable as ID.

========================================================
ELT TITLE PRINTING RULE
========================================================

Important exception:

If printing ELT title:
AND conjunction only requires ONE signature.

Frequently misunderstood by clerks.

========================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE TRANSFER RULES
========================================================

ALWAYS review Exhibit A for out-of-state titles.

States differ significantly.

Some states:
- Require all signatures regardless of OR
- Require one signature only
- Require state-specific authorization

High Risk States:
- Maryland
- Massachusetts
- Virginia
- Oregon
- Washington
- Georgia

========================================================
DEALERSHIP HIGH-RISK ERRORS
========================================================

MOST COMMON ERRORS:
- Wrong conjunction interpretation
- Missing co-owner signature
- Missing POA copy
- Using copied 82995
- Accepting expired ID
- Missing printed odometer names
- Business signer missing title
- Wrong name format
- Missing ID copy for out-of-state customer
- Failing to verify fraud stop
