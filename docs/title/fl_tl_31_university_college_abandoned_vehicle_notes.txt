FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — ABANDONED VEHICLES ON UNIVERSITY OR COLLEGE CAMPUSES
PROCEDURE: TL-31
CATEGORY: OPERATION OF LAW / CAMPUS ABANDONED VEHICLES
RELATED STATUTES:
- Section 705.18 F.S.

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure explains:
- Abandoned vehicles on university campuses
- Abandoned vehicles on college campuses
- Disposal authority for state institutions
- Sale of abandoned campus vehicles
- Title transfer requirements
- Operation of law transfers

Applies when:
- Vehicle abandoned on state university campus
- Vehicle abandoned on Florida College System campus
- Vehicle unclaimed by owner
- Institution disposes of vehicle

==================================================
GENERAL PROCESS FLOW
==================================================

1. Vehicle found abandoned on campus
2. University/college records finding date
3. Waiting period expires
4. Institution disposes/sells vehicle
5. Purchaser applies for title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 30-DAY RULE
==================================================

Vehicle:
- Must remain unclaimed
for minimum:
- 30 days after found

==================================================
LONGER HOLD RULE
==================================================

University/college president or designee:
May extend holding period if necessary.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER RECLAIM RULE
==================================================

Owner may reclaim vehicle:
- Any time before sale

==================================================
GENERAL DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Usually required:
- HSMV 82040 MV/MH/VS
OR
- HSMV 82041 (older transitional usage)
- Bill of sale
- Lien satisfactions
- Sales tax handling
- Plate/decal handling
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORM UPDATE RULE
==================================================

As of July 1, 2023:
HSMV 82040 split into:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82040 MH

==================================================
FORM TYPE LOGIC
==================================================

HSMV 82040 MV:
Used for:
- Autos
- Trucks
- Motorcycles
- Buses
- Travel trailers
- Vehicle trailers
- Off-highway vehicles
- Tools

HSMV 82040 VS:
Used for:
- Vessels only

HSMV 82040 MH:
Used for:
- Mobile homes only

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 2024 RULE
==================================================

Effective January 1, 2024:
Old combined HSMV 82040:
- No longer accepted
for motor vehicles/mobile homes.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE RECORD REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle currently titled in Florida:
Need:
- HSMV 82040 or approved equivalent
- Bill of sale
- Lien satisfactions
- Sales tax handling
- Plate/non-use handling
- Title fees

==================================================
BILL OF SALE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Bill of sale must come from:
- University president
OR
- College president
OR
- Official designee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DESIGNEE RULE
==================================================

If designee signs:
Need:
- Letter of appointment
from president.

==================================================
BILL OF SALE CONTENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Must include:
- Selling price
- Complete vehicle description
- Vehicle identifying information

==================================================
LIEN SATISFACTION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need lien satisfactions for:
- Florida liens
- Out-of-state liens

==================================================
SALES TAX REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- Florida sales tax
OR
- Sales tax exemption information

==================================================
PLATE / NON-USE RULES
==================================================

Need:
- Florida plate transfer/purchase
OR
- Non-use affidavit

==================================================
MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

For mobile homes:
Need:
- Mobile home decal
OR
- RP decal

Non-use affidavit:
- NOT acceptable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RULE
==================================================

If dealer/individual purchases:
- Vehicle obtained through operation of law
AND
- No negotiable title exists

Then:
Certificate of title MUST issue:
- In purchaser name first
before resale.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle previously titled out of state:
Need:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN verification on HSMV 82040

==================================================
ALTERNATIVE VIN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Instead of HSMV 82042:
May submit affidavit from:
- Seller
AND
- Purchaser

Affidavit must confirm:
- VIN on documents matches vehicle

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

Verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel recreational trailers

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE OWNERSHIP VERIFICATION
==================================================

Need ownership/lien verification from previous state.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE OWNERSHIP VERIFICATION
==================================================

Acceptable proof:
- Computer printout
- State agency letter
- Teletype printout
- Photocopy of title

==================================================
UNKNOWN STATE RULE
==================================================

If previous state unknown:
Need affidavit from:
- University president
OR
- Designee

Affidavit must state:
- Previous registration state unknown

==================================================
EXHIBIT A CHECKLIST SUMMARY
==================================================

Checklist includes:
- HSMV 82040 or HSMV 82041
- HSMV 82042
- Ownership/lien verification
- Bill of sale
- Letter of appointment (if needed)
- Lien satisfactions
- Sales tax handling
- Plate/non-use handling
- Title fees

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Florida or out-of-state title?
2. 30-day holding period met?
3. Owner reclaimed vehicle?
4. President or designee signed?
5. Letter of appointment needed?
6. VIN verification required?
7. Ownership verification present?
8. Lien satisfactions included?
9. Dealer operation-of-law issue involved?
10. Mobile home rules applicable?
11. Sales tax handled properly?
12. Plate/decal requirements met?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing bill of sale
- Missing president authorization
- Missing appointment letter
- Missing VIN verification
- Missing out-of-state ownership verification
- Missing lien satisfactions
- Missing sales tax handling
- Missing title fees
- Missing non-use affidavit
- Dealer skipping retitle requirement

==================================================
IMPORTANT OPERATIONAL NOTES
==================================================

Campus abandoned vehicle titles are:
- Operation-of-law transactions
- Documentation-sensitive
- Authorization-sensitive

Most common rejection reasons:
- Missing appointment letter
- Missing ownership verification
- Missing VIN verification
- Missing lien satisfaction
- Improper form usage
- Missing operation-of-law title issuance
