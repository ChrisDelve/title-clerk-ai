PROCEDURE: TL-35
CATEGORY: SALVAGE / DERELICT / JUNKED / CERTIFICATE OF DESTRUCTION

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

DERELICT MOTOR VEHICLE:
- Value under $1,000
- At least 10 model years old
- Primary value is scrap/dismantling

TRAILER DERELICT RULE:
- Value under $5,000
- At least 10 model years old

==================================================
CERTIFICATE TYPES
==================================================

Possible title outcomes:
- Salvage Rebuildable
- Salvage Rebuildable Flood
- Certificate of Destruction
- Derelict Motor Vehicle Certificate

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TOTAL LOSS RULE
==================================================

Uninsured vehicle:
TOTAL LOSS if repair cost:
- 80% or more
of replacement value.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Mobile home exempt from salvage law if:
- Retail value under $1,500
OR
- Repair cost less than 80%

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER RULE
==================================================

Owner of uninsured total loss vehicle:
MUST obtain:
- Salvage title
OR
- Certificate of destruction

BEFORE:
- Scrapping
- Dismantling
- Disposal

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEADLINE
==================================================

Owner/self-insured must submit:
- Title
within:
- 72 hours
after vehicle becomes salvage.

==================================================
CERTIFICATE OF DESTRUCTION RULE
==================================================

Once issued:
Vehicle may ONLY be:
- Sold for parts
OR
- Sold for scrap

==================================================
SALVAGE APPLICATION — FL TITLE AVAILABLE
==================================================

Need:
1. Florida title
2. HSMV 82363
3. Odometer disclosure
4. Lien satisfactions
5. Title fees

==================================================
SALVAGE APPLICATION — FL TITLE MISSING
==================================================

Must first obtain:
- Duplicate title

Using:
- HSMV 82101

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TOTAL LOSS RULE
==================================================

Need:
1. MCO or out-of-state ownership
2. HSMV 82363
3. VIN verification
4. Lien satisfactions
5. Sales tax info
6. Title fees

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Use:
- HSMV 82042

OR:
- Affidavit verifying VIN

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Small trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth wheel recreation trailers

==================================================
JUNKED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle destroyed/dismantled:
Title must show:
- "JUNKED"

==================================================
JUNKED TITLE PROCESS
==================================================

Need:
- Title marked JUNKED
- Owner/insurance signature
- Lien satisfactions

No title fee required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE JUNK RULE
==================================================

Florida does NOT junk:
- Out-of-state title records

Out-of-state title must be:
- Sent back to issuing state

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DERELICT RULE
==================================================

If title NOT available:
Use:
- HSMV 82137

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 24-HOUR RULE
==================================================

Salvage dealer/recycler must submit:
- HSMV 82137

Within:
- 24 hours
of receiving vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REJECTION RULE
==================================================

If 24-hour limit exceeded:
Application:
- MUST be rejected

Unless:
- Holiday
- Executive order
- Court order exception

==================================================
HSMV 82137 SECTION RULES
==================================================

SECTION 1:
Completed by:
- Seller/owner

Requires:
- Driver license copy

If seller not owner:
Need:
- Right thumbprint

SECTION 2:
Completed by:
- Transporter

SECTION 3:
Completed by:
- Salvage dealer/recycler

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN RULES
==================================================

LIEN 3 YEARS OLD OR MORE:
- System removes lien automatically
- Vehicle held 3 business days

LIEN LESS THAN 3 YEARS:
- 10-day DV stop placed
- Destruction date shows PENDING
- Lienholder notified

==================================================
DV STOP RULE
==================================================

DV:
- Derelict Vehicle Stop
- 10 calendar days

==================================================
DA STOP RULE
==================================================

DA:
- Derelict Administrative Stop
- 30 calendar days

Used when:
- Lienholder protests

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PENDING RULE
==================================================

If destruction date:
- PENDING

Vehicle:
- MAY NOT be destroyed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AUTHORIZED DESTRUCTION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may ONLY be destroyed after:
- Authorized destruction date assigned

Dealer MUST:
- Print MV Check screen
- Keep with derelict certificate

==================================================
MV CHECK RULE
==================================================

Dealers/recyclers verify:
- Destruction authorization

Using:
https://services.flhsmv.gov/MVCheckWeb/

==================================================
LIENHOLDER PROTEST RULE
==================================================

If lienholder protests within 10 days:
- DA stop added
- 30-day hold begins

==================================================
LIENHOLDER NO RESPONSE RULE
==================================================

If no response:
- Stop removed
- Destruction date assigned

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DERELICT LIEN RULE
==================================================

A lien:
- CANNOT be added
to:
- Derelict certificate

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAYMENT RULE
==================================================

If seller NOT owner of record:
Dealer/recycler must pay:
- Seller directly

Cannot pay:
- Transporter

==================================================
SALVAGE DEALER RECORD RETENTION
==================================================

Must keep:
- Titles
- Salvage titles
- Certificates
- Applications

For:
- 3 years

==================================================
DERELICT CERTIFICATE RULE
==================================================

Derelict certificate:
- Plain white paper
- One reassignment only

==================================================
DUPLICATE DERELICT RULE
==================================================

Lost derelict certificate:
Use:
- HSMV 82137
for duplicate.

==================================================
TOWING/STORAGE COMPANY RULE
==================================================

Tow/storage companies:
CANNOT use:
- Derelict application

Must obtain:
- Proper title
OR
- Certificate of destruction

==================================================
NMVTIS RULE
==================================================

Salvage dealer/recycler:
Must notify:
- NMVTIS

BUT:
No proof required with application.

==================================================
ERROR CORRECTION RULES
==================================================

If title junked in error:
Need:
- Affidavit
- HSMV 82101
- Duplicate title fee

If derelict issued in error:
Need:
- Original derelict certificate
OR affidavit
- Error affidavit
- VIN verification

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing 24-hour deadline
- Accepting incomplete HSMV 82137
- Missing thumbprint
- Destroying vehicle before authorization
- Missing lien satisfactions
- Missing VIN verification
- Attempting lien add on derelict certificate
- Junking out-of-state title incorrectly
- Missing owner signatures
- Not checking lien age

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is vehicle salvage, junked, or derelict?
2. Is title available?
3. Is vehicle titled in Florida?
4. Is lien active?
5. How old is lien?
6. Does DV stop apply?
7. Does DA stop apply?
8. Is destruction authorized?
9. Was HSMV 82137 timely submitted?
10. Is thumbprint required?
11. Does vehicle qualify as derelict?
12. Is VIN verification required?
13. Is duplicate title required?
14. Is out-of-state process required?
15. Is junk process or salvage process proper?
