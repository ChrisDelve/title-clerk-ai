FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — ELECTRONIC LIEN AND TITLE (ELT) PROGRAM
PROCEDURE: TL-69
CATEGORY: ELECTRONIC TITLES / LIENS / ELT / E-TITLES

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Electronic titles (e-titles)
- Electronic liens
- ELT participation
- Mandatory lender participation
- ELT exception reasons
- Electronic lien satisfactions
- E-title printing
- Electronic title transfers
- Non-ELT lienholders
- ELT service providers
- Repossessions involving ELT titles

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Vessels
- Mobile homes
- Lienholders
- Dealers
- Tax collectors
- License plate agencies
- ELT providers
- FLHSMV personnel

- Electronic lien processing
- Electronic title handling
- ELT enrollment
- E-title transfer procedures
- ELT exception handling

==================================================
GENERAL INFORMATION
==================================================

All titles with liens:
Held electronically.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN TRANSMISSION RULE
==================================================

Lien notifications and satisfactions:
Must be transmitted electronically:
Between:
FLHSMV and lienholder.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MANDATORY PARTICIPATION RULE
==================================================

Most lienholders:
Required to participate:
In ELT program.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFAULT ELECTRONIC TITLE RULE
==================================================

Beginning:
January 1, 2013:
Titles without liens:
Default to electronic titles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOMER REQUEST RULE
==================================================

Paper title printed:
ONLY if customer:
Explicitly requests paper title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT E-TITLE RULE
==================================================

Electronic title:
Exists only in:
Department database.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELT BENEFIT RULE
==================================================

ELT program reduces:
- Paper title handling
- Mailing costs
- Storage costs
- Fraud risk

==================================================
PARTICIPATING LIENHOLDER BENEFITS
==================================================

ELT lienholders no longer:
- Retrieve paper titles
- Physically sign off liens
- Mail titles to customers
- Request duplicate titles

==================================================
MANDATORY PARTICIPATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Businesses regularly financing:
Vehicles or vessels:
Must participate in ELT.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Individuals not regularly financing vehicles:
Exempt from ELT participation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-ELT RULE
==================================================

Non-ELT lienholder:
Requires:
ELT exception reason.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXISTING LIEN RULE
==================================================

Existing non-ELT liens:
Also require:
ELT exception reason.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state lienholders:
Financing Florida-titled vehicles:
Must participate in ELT.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SERVICE PROVIDER RULE
==================================================

Lenders must contract:
With FLHSMV-approved:
ELT service provider.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELT PROVIDER RULE
==================================================

ELT provider:
Must have:
Written agreement with FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURETY BOND RULE
==================================================

ELT service providers:
Must secure:
Surety bond and power of attorney.

==================================================
ENROLLMENT PROCESS
==================================================

Lender enrollment requires:
1. Select ELT provider
2. Complete HSMV 82150
3. Submit to provider
4. Install software/hardware
5. Receive startup date

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82150 RULE
==================================================

Required enrollment form:
Application and Notice of Interest – Electronic Lien and Title Process
HSMV 82150.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ACCOUNT NUMBER RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV assigns:
ELT account number:
To lienholder.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SERVICE PROVIDER ROLE RULE
==================================================

Service provider:
Matches transactions
AND
Maintains records.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

Lien satisfactions:
Must be transmitted electronically:
To FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SOLE LIEN RULE
==================================================

When sole remaining lien satisfied:
Department sends owner:
Electronic title notification letter.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRONIC RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Title remains electronic:
Until owner requests:
Paper title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REMAINING LIEN RULE
==================================================

If another lien remains:
Title stays electronic.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIMARY LIENHOLDER RULE
==================================================

If primary lienholder:
ELT participant:
Electronic title sent electronically.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-ELT LETTER RULE
==================================================

Non-ELT lienholder:
Receives letter:
Explaining title held electronically.

==================================================
HOW E-TITLE PROCESS WORKS
==================================================

All titles:
Remain electronic:
Until printing requested.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRINT REQUEST RULE
==================================================

Owner may request:
Printed paper title:
At any time.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MAIL FEE RULE
==================================================

Fee to convert e-title to mailed paper title:
$2.50

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FAST TITLE RULE
==================================================

Fast title available:
At participating tax collectors:
For:
$10.00

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Electronic titles:
Require same documentation:
As paper titles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DATABASE PROCESSING RULE
==================================================

Electronic title processing:
Entered into:
Department database:
Same as paper title.

==================================================
REPOSSESSION RULES
==================================================

Repossession procedures:
Generally unchanged.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPOSSESSION TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

When repossession selected:
System:
- Deletes lien
- Transfers ownership to lienholder
- Allows fast or mailed title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELT REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Repossession with lien reassignment:
NOT allowed:
With primary ELT lien:
Until title converted to paper.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-ELT REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Repossession reassignment:
Allowed:
With primary non-ELT lien.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ROUNDABOUT REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

To process reassignment:
User must:
- Perform title transfer
- Answer NO to repossession
- Reassign lien
- Change owner
- Delete lien

==================================================
BENEFITS OF ELT PROGRAM
==================================================

Benefits include:
- Reduced title fraud
- Less paper usage
- Faster lien notifications
- Less storage
- Expedited title printing
- Better customer service
- Reduced costs

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS INFORMATION
==================================================

When title status:
"Electronic":
Special reassignment procedures apply.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82994 RULE
==================================================

Florida dealer trade-ins:
Require:
HSMV 82994.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Dealer must complete:
Additional HSMV 82994:
When reselling to Florida resident.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE BUYER RULE
==================================================

Dealer selling to out-of-state buyer:
Must request:
Printed certificate of title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE PRINT TIMING RULE
==================================================

Paper titles:
Generally mailed:
Within 2 days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE HISTORY RULE
==================================================

Printing paper title:
Does NOT change:
Original issue date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PROCESS DATE RULE
==================================================

Process history:
Updates:
To reflect print date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRONIC TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Electronic title transfer:
Requires:
Buyer and seller reassignment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VESSEL/MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Vessels and mobile homes:
May use:
HSMV 82092:
Instead of HSMV 82994.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ODOMETER RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82092:
Allowed when:
- Odometer not required
OR
- Odometer exempt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN CORRECTION RULE
==================================================

Electronic title lien corrections:
See:
TL-04.

==================================================
ELT EXCEPTION REASONS
==================================================

Valid ELT exception reasons:
- Individual lienholder
- Multi-individual business customer
- Pre-existing out-of-state lien
- Government entity
- Trust
- Court ordered lien
- Delinquent support lien
- Lien received prior to 2/9/2013
- Infrequent business lienholder

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INFREQUENT BUSINESS RULE
==================================================

Business non-ELT lienholder:
Maximum:
2 liens:
Within 12 months.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-2013 RULE
==================================================

Pre-2/9/2013 liens:
May use:
Historical exception reason.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EFS RULE
==================================================

EFS providers:
May only select:
ELT lienholders.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-ELT REPORT RULE
==================================================

Non-ELT Liens Report:
Used for supervisor monitoring.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-EXISTING LIEN RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state lien:
If lienholder not ELT:
Use:
"Pre-existing out-of-state lien" exception.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAPER TITLE RULE
==================================================

Using pre-existing out-of-state lien exception:
Causes paper title:
To be mailed to lienholder.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HELPDESK RULE
==================================================

Helpdesk may assist:
In determining:
Lienholder ELT status.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOMER NUMBER RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV customer number:
Used as:
ELT account number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDRESS RULE
==================================================

Must use:
ELT customer record:
Even if address differs from application.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-ELT NOTICE RULE
==================================================

Non-ELT lienholder:
Receives letter:
Confirming lien recorded electronically.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MVCHECK RULE
==================================================

MVCheck:
Displays:
- Lien information
- Electronic title status

VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Florida’s ELT system:
Requires most liens and titles:
To exist electronically,
with electronic lien transmission,
satisfaction,
and title management replacing traditional paper title handling.
