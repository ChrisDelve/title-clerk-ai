==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE LOAN RULE
==================================================

Title loan:
Loan secured by:
- Motor vehicle title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SECURITY RULE
==================================================

Lender possesses:
- Certificate of title

NOT necessarily:
- Vehicle itself.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFAULT RULE
==================================================

Borrower defaults when:
- Full amount not repaid
AND
- No payment made within 30 days after maturity date or extension.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPOSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Lender may repossess vehicle:
ONLY through:
- Licensed repossession agent.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PHYSICAL POSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Lender MUST have:
- Physical possession of vehicle

Before:
- Titling vehicle
OR
- Selling vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER SALE RULE
==================================================

If lender sells vehicle without titling into lender name:
Vehicle MUST be sold through:
- Licensed Florida motor vehicle dealer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE IN LENDER NAME RULE
==================================================

If lender first titles vehicle into lender name:
Vehicle later:
DOES NOT have to be sold through dealer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURCHASER RIGHTS RULE
==================================================

Purchaser receives:
- Right
- Title
- Interest

Of:
- Owner
AND
- Title loan lender.

Vessel titles require:
- HSMV 82040 VS.

VERY IMPORTANT MOTOR VEHICLE FORM RULE
==================================================

Effective January 1, 2024:
Motor vehicle titles require:
- HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
TITLE LOAN DEFAULT DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
==================================================

Required documents:
1. Florida or out-of-state title
2. Certified notice copy
3. Returned certified receipt/unclaimed letter
4. HSMV 82994 if dealer sale
5. HSMV 82040
6. HSMV 82042 if needed
7. Copy of 537.008 FS
8. Default affidavit
9. Lien satisfactions
10. Sales tax or Chapter 537 statement
11. Plate or non-use affidavit
12. Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE RULE
==================================================

Title:
Does NOT have to be completed for transfer:
To title loan lender.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE RULE
==================================================

Notice must:
- Be mailed certified
- Be sent 10 days prior to sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE CONTENT RULE
==================================================

Notice must contain:
- Year
- Make
- VIN
- Sale date
- Sale time
- Sale place
- Loan payoff accounting
- Interest amount
- Expenses incurred

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CERTIFIED MAIL RULE
==================================================

Must provide:
- Returned receipt card
OR
- Unclaimed unopened certified letter.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

If sold through dealer:
Require:
- HSMV 82994.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BORROWER PURCHASE RULE
==================================================

Borrower may:
Purchase vehicle at sale:
If not redeemed before sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE VIN RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state vehicles require:
- VIN verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN FORM RULE
==================================================

VIN verification:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN section on HSMV 82040.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth wheel recreational trailers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFAULT AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Affidavit must state:
- Last payment date
- No payment within required period
- Lender took possession under 537.012(1)
- Whether vehicle being sold or titled into lender name

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALERSHIP NAME RULE
==================================================

If sold through dealer:
Affidavit must include:
- Dealership name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

All recorded liens:
Must be satisfied.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CHAPTER 537 STATEMENT RULE
==================================================

If lender taking title:
HSMV 82040 must state:
- "Obtained per Chapter 537, Florida Statutes, for Title Loan"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Must provide:
- Florida plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE LOAN LIEN RULE
==================================================

Lender may:
Record lien on title.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE LIEN REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle already titled in Florida:
Required:
- Florida title
- HSMV 82139
- Lien satisfactions
- Fees

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE LIEN REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required:
- Out-of-state title
- HSMV 82040
- HSMV 82042 if required
- HSMV 82139
- Lien satisfactions
- Fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82139 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82139:
Notice of Lien.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN FORM EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82139 not needed:
If lien already shown:
On completed HSMV 82040.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-23 RULE
==================================================

After lien recorded:
If default occurs:
Refer to:
- TL-23.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS RULES
==================================================

Before repossession:
Borrower must have opportunity:
To voluntarily surrender vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PERSONAL PROPERTY RULE
==================================================

Borrower must have:
Reasonable opportunity:
To remove personal belongings.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT THIRD PARTY POSSESSION RULE
==================================================

After repossession:
Lender may authorize:
Third party physical possession.

IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

LOAN PROPERTY:
Title deposited as collateral.

MATURITY DATE:
30 days after execution date.

TITLE LOAN:
Loan secured by title certificate.

TITLE LOAN AGREEMENT:
Written loan agreement.

TITLE LOAN LENDER:
Person servicing title loans.

TITLE LOAN OFFICE:
Location conducting title loan business.

TITLED PERSONAL PROPERTY:
Vehicle with state-issued title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE LOAN AGREEMENT RULE
==================================================

Agreement must include:
- Vehicle information
- VIN
- Borrower information
- Loan amount
- Finance charge
- APR
- Maturity date
- Payment totals

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MATURITY RULE
==================================================

Maturity date:
30 days after agreement executed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REQUIRED DISCLOSURE RULE
==================================================

Agreement must warn:
Vehicle may be repossessed and sold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUPLICATE TITLE RULE
==================================================

Borrower represents:
Will NOT apply for duplicate title:
While agreement active.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SIGNATURE RULE
==================================================

All owners:
Must sign:
Title loan agreement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BORROWER COPY RULE
==================================================

Borrower must receive:
Exact copy:
Of executed agreement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REDEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Borrower may redeem:
By repaying:
All amounts legally due.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SECURITY INTEREST RULE
==================================================

After redemption:
Lender must:
Release security interest.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT POSSESSION RULE
==================================================

During agreement:
Lender may retain:
ONLY title document.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL SECURITY RULE
==================================================

Lender cannot require:
Additional collateral or guaranty.
