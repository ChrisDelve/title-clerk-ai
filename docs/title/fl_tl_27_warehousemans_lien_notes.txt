==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure explains:
- Warehouseman's liens
- Sale of stored vehicles through lien process
- Transfer of title after warehouse lien sale
- Required notices
- Required advertisements
- Required documentation
- Timeline requirements

Applies when:
- A warehouse stores a vehicle
- Charges remain unpaid
- Warehouse enforces lien
- Vehicle sold at auction

==================================================
LEGAL DEFINITIONS
==================================================

WAREHOUSEMAN:
A person engaged in the business of storing goods for hire.

WAREHOUSEMAN'S LIEN:
A lien for:
- Storage expenses
- Charges
- Security interest
- Advances
- Interest

==================================================
GENERAL PROCESS FLOW
==================================================

1. Vehicle stored by warehouse
2. Charges remain unpaid
3. Notice sent to owners/lienholders
4. Waiting period expires
5. Advertisement published or posted
6. Auction held
7. Purchaser applies for title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice must be:
- Personally delivered
OR
- Sent by certified/registered mail

Notice must go to:
- Registered owner
- All lienholders
- All known interested parties

Addresses:
- Must use vehicle records
- Florida or out-of-state records

==================================================
NOTICE CONTENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice MUST include:

1. Itemized statement of claim
2. Vehicle description
3. Year
4. Make
5. VIN
6. Demand for payment
7. Payment deadline
8. Sale warning
9. Auction time/place statement

==================================================
PAYMENT DEADLINE RULE
==================================================

Demand for payment must allow:
- Minimum 10 days after notice delivery/receipt

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE WARNING RULE
==================================================

Notice MUST clearly state:
- Vehicle will be advertised
- Vehicle will be sold by auction
- Sale conducted under 677.210 F.S.

==================================================
MAIL PROOF REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If mailed:
Must submit:
- Original return receipt
OR
- Certified copy
OR
- Unclaimed unopened returned letter

==================================================
HAND DELIVERY RULE
==================================================

If notice hand-delivered:
Must submit:
- Notarized affidavit
OR
- Perjury clause affidavit

Affidavit must confirm:
- Notice personally delivered

==================================================
WAREHOUSE RECEIPT REQUIREMENT
==================================================

Must submit warehouse receipt showing:
- Vehicle description
- Year
- Make
- VIN

==================================================
PUBLIC SALE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Vehicle must be:
- Advertised for sale
OR
- Posted publicly if no newspaper exists

==================================================
NEWSPAPER ADVERTISEMENT RULES
==================================================

If newspaper available:
Advertisement must:
- Publish once weekly
- For 2 consecutive weeks
- In newspaper of general circulation

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE TIMING RULE
==================================================

Sale may occur:
- 15 calendar days after first publication

==================================================
NO NEWSPAPER AVAILABLE RULE
==================================================

If no general circulation newspaper exists:
- Notice must be posted

Posting requirements:
- Minimum 6 conspicuous places
- Neighborhood of proposed sale

==================================================
POSTED NOTICE TIMING RULE
==================================================

Posted notice:
- Minimum 10 calendar days before sale

DO NOT include:
- Posting date
- Sale date

==================================================
ADVERTISEMENT CONTENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Advertisement must include:
- Vehicle description
- Year
- Make
- VIN
- Warehouse tenant name
- Sale date
- Sale location
- Sale time
- Publication/posting dates

==================================================
SALE LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Sale must occur:
- At nearest suitable place
to where vehicle stored

==================================================
BILL OF SALE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle sold:
Need bill of sale showing:
- Selling price
- Complete vehicle description

==================================================
UNSOLD VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle NOT sold:
- Warehouseman MUST title vehicle in own name

==================================================
LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

Must provide:
- Satisfaction for all liens
shown on state records

==================================================
SALES TAX REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Must provide:
- Florida sales tax
OR
- Sales tax exemption information

==================================================
REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Must provide:
- Florida plate number
OR
- Non-use affidavit

MOBILE HOME RULE:
- Non-use affidavit NOT allowed

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle previously titled out of state:
Additional documents required.

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN verification section on title form
OR
- Affidavit confirming VIN match

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXCEPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Certain recreational trailers

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE OWNERSHIP PROOF
==================================================

Acceptable proof:
- Computer printout
- Official state letter
- Copy of out-of-state title
- Law enforcement teletype

Must verify:
- Ownership
- Lien status

==================================================
UNKNOWN STATE RULE
==================================================

If previous state unknown:
Need affidavit stating:
- State unknown
- Vehicle had no plate at storage intake

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REDEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Before sale:
Any interested person may:
- Pay lien amount
- Pay expenses

IF PAID:
- Vehicle CANNOT be sold

==================================================
PROCEEDS RULE
==================================================

Warehouseman may:
- Keep lien amount

Remaining balance:
- Must be held for rightful claimant

==================================================
TITLE APPLICATION FORMS
==================================================

Possible forms:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 MH
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82041
- HSMV 82042

==================================================
TIMELINE LOGIC
==================================================

DAY 1:
- Notice sent

DAY 11 OR LATER:
- Advertisement/posting phase begins

IF NEWSPAPER:
- Publish once weekly
- 2 consecutive weeks
- Sale 15+ days after first publication

IF NO NEWSPAPER:
- Post in 6 visible places
- 10 days before sale

COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS

- Missing VIN in notice
- Missing itemized charges
- Incorrect publication timing
- Missing warehouse receipt
- Missing lien satisfactions
- Missing VIN verification
- Sale location incorrect
- Missing proof of advertisement
- No affidavit for hand delivery

IMPORTANT OPERATIONAL NOTES
==================================================

Warehouseman's lien procedures are:
- Extremely document-sensitive
- Timeline-sensitive
- Notice-sensitive

The most common rejection reasons:
- Improper notice timing
- Missing mail proof
- Improper advertisement
- Missing VIN verification
- Missing lien releases
