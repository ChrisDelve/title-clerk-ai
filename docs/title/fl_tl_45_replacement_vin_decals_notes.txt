FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — REPLACEMENT VIN DECALS
PROCEDURE: TL-45
CATEGORY: VIN / IDENTIFICATION / REPLACEMENT DECALS

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Replacement VIN decals
- Lost VIN decals
- Defaced VIN decals
- Stolen VIN decals
- DMS Compliance Examiner procedures
- VIN replacement inspections
- Out-of-state replacement decal processing
- Dealer VIN decal requests
- Mobile home VIN replacements
- Motorcycle VIN replacements

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Motorcycles
- Off-highway vehicles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEGAL RULE
==================================================

It is unlawful to:
- Possess
- Sell
- Exchange
- Give away

A removed:
- VIN plate
- Identification plate
- Serial plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONTRABAND RULE
==================================================

If identifying numbers:
- Missing
- Destroyed
- Altered
- Covered
- Defaced

Vehicle may become:
- Contraband.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COURT ORDER RULE
==================================================

If true identity cannot be determined:
Vehicle:
- Cannot operate

Unless:
- Court orders replacement VIN assignment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER PENALTY RULE
==================================================

If confiscated from dealer:
Dealer license:
May be revoked.

==================================================
PROCEDURE PURPOSE
==================================================

Procedure assists:
- Tax collectors

- FLHSMV

With:
- Replacement manufacturer VIN decals.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT VIN REQUIREMENT
==================================================

To apply for replacement VIN decal:
Must meet:
- 319.30 FS
AND
- 319.33 FS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DOCUMENT COPY RULE
==================================================

Applicant:
Must make photocopies
before submitting originals.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGIONAL OFFICE RULE
==================================================

Request must go to:
- DMS Regional Office

Serving:
- Applicant county.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle:
MUST be brought to:
- DMS Regional Office

For:
- Compliance Examiner inspection.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE / NO OWNERSHIP CHANGE
==================================================

If:
- Florida titled
AND
- No ownership change

Need:
- Copy front/back Florida title
- Owner ID.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRONIC TITLE RULE
==================================================

If title electronic:
Copy of title:
NOT required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO-FEE RULE
==================================================

Replacement VIN decal:
Issued:
- FREE.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSTALLATION RULE
==================================================

After inspection:
Replacement decal:
- Ordered
- Owner contacted
- Vehicle returns for decal installation.

==================================================
TRANSFER TITLE WITH VIN REPLACEMENT
==================================================

If title assigned to buyer:
Need:
- Assigned title
- HSMV 82040
- Sales tax
- Plate transfer or non-use affidavit
- Fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME NON-USE RULE
==================================================

Non-use affidavit:
NOT accepted
for:
- Mobile homes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DMS IMAGING RULE
==================================================

Compliance Examiner sends:
- Title
- HSMV 82040
- HSMV 84044

To department:
For imaging.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TAX COLLECTOR SUBMISSION RULE
==================================================

Tax collector submits:
- HSMV 82040
- HSMV 84044
- Copy of title
- Computer generated HSMV 82041.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state vehicle requires:
- Out-of-state title
AND
- HSMV 82040
AND
- Sales tax
AND
- Registration/non-use affidavit
AND
- Fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CERTIFICATE OF DESTRUCTION RULE
==================================================

If:
- Certificate of destruction issued

Replacement VIN decal:
NOT required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RULE
==================================================

Dealer:
Does NOT need
title in dealer name
for replacement decal request.

==================================================
DEALER REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Dealer submits:
- Title copy
AND
- Reassignment document.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle:
- Driver doorjamb

Motorcycle:
- Near right-side frame down tube

Mobile home:
- Main doorjamb hinge side above lower hinge.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REMOVABLE PART RULE
==================================================

Replacement decal:
MUST NOT:
- Be installed on removable part.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT APPOINTMENT RULE
==================================================

Applicant:
Must schedule:
- Compliance Examiner appointment.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE RESIDENT / MILITARY RULE
==================================================

Florida residents:
Living out-of-state temporarily
OR
Active military:
May process remotely.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Need affidavit from:
- Out-of-state law enforcement
OR
- Military law enforcement.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE AFFIDAVIT MUST INCLUDE
==================================================

Affidavit must state:
- Confidential VIN
AND
- Replacement decal needed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MAILING RULE
==================================================

DMS may:
- Mail replacement decal
AND
- Certification letter.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSTALLATION CERTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

After installation:
Law enforcement/military:
Must return:
- Certification letter.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE HOLD RULE
==================================================

If title application held:
Application processed after:
- Certification returned.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is VIN decal lost/stolen/defaced?
2. Is vehicle titled in Florida?
3. Is ownership changing?
4. Is title electronic?
5. Is out-of-state title involved?
6. Is mobile home involved?
7. Is motorcycle involved?
8. Is vehicle potentially contraband?
9. Is court order required?
10. Is dealer requesting decal?
11. Is military/out-of-state residency involved?
12. Is non-use affidavit acceptable?
13. Is replacement decal free?
14. Is inspection required?
15. Which office has jurisdiction?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Forgetting Compliance Examiner inspection
- Requesting title copy when ELT exists
- Accepting non-use affidavit for mobile home
- Installing decal on removable part
- Sending customer to wrong regional office
- Missing HSMV 84044 imaging process
- Requiring dealer title unnecessarily
- Ignoring contraband implications
- Missing military/out-of-state process
- Misplacing decal location
