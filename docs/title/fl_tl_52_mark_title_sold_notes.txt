FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — MARK TITLE SOLD
PROCEDURE: TL-52
CATEGORY: TITLE STATUS / NOTICE OF SALE / SOLD FLAG PROCESSING

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Marking Florida titles as sold
- Notice of sale processing
- Seller liability limitation
- DMV sold status updates
- Dealership trade-in sold reporting
- Temporary plate issuance during pending title transfers
- Sold flag corrections
- Duplicate title processing after sold status

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Vessels
- Off-highway vehicles

LEGAL AUTHORITY
==================================================

Governed by:
- 319.22(2)(b) FS
- 319.23(6) FS
- 328.01(3)(d) FS

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIABILITY RULE
==================================================

Seller:
Must notify department:
Within:
- 30 days after sale or transfer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIABILITY PROTECTION RULE
==================================================

Notice of sale:
Protects seller from:
- Future civil liability.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Notice requirement:
Does NOT apply:
When:
- Vehicle traded to licensed dealer
OR
- Insurance company takes possession.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER TRADE-IN RULE
==================================================

Licensed dealer:
Must submit:
- Notice of sale

Within:
- 30 days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VESSEL RULE
==================================================

Vessel owner:
Not considered owner after:
- Properly endorsed title delivered
OR
- Notice submitted.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VOID RULE
==================================================

Void means:
- Vacate or undo incorrect pending transaction record.

==================================================
DESCRIPTION AND USE
==================================================

Procedure assists:
- Tax collector employees
- License plate agents
- FLHSMV employees

With:
- Processing notice of sale transactions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DELAYED TITLE TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Seller may not accompany buyer:
Therefore:
- Transfer may not complete immediately.

==================================================
MARK TITLE SOLD REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Seller must submit:
- HSMV 82050
OR
- Notice of Sale section on back of title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82050 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82050:
Used for:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Off-highway vehicles
- Vessels.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALERSHIP TRADE-IN RULE
==================================================

For trade-ins:
Dealer completes:
- Section 7 on HSMV 82040 MV
OR
- HSMV 82040 MH.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DATABASE UPDATE RULE
==================================================

DMV database:
Must reflect:
- "Sold"

After trade-in paperwork submitted.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NAME MATCH RULE
==================================================

Title clerk must verify:
- Names on DMV system
Match:
- Names on HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO DUPLICATE SOLD RULE
==================================================

If vehicle already transferred:
DO NOT:
- Mark title sold again.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIVATE SALE RULE
==================================================

Private/casual sale:
Seller should complete:
- HSMV 82050
OR
- Notice of Sale on back of title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT DESCRIPTION RULE
==================================================

When marking sold:
Enter in comment description:
- Purchaser/dealer name
- Actual date of sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE DATE RULE
==================================================

Actual sale date:
May differ from:
- Transaction processing date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UNKNOWN DATE RULE
==================================================

If exact sale date unknown:
Enter:
- Partial date information.

Examples:
- 07/03
- 2003

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SELLER NAME RULE
==================================================

Seller name:
Remains on DMV database:
Until:
- Title officially transferred.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PHYSICAL DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Title clerk:
Must physically view:
- Signed HSMV 82050
OR
- Signed Notice of Sale section.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RETENTION RULE
==================================================

Department:
Does NOT retain:
- HSMV 82050.

Customer:
Responsible for:
- Keeping records.

==================================================
DUPLICATE TITLE AFTER SOLD STATUS
==================================================

If buyer loses title:
Seller must apply for duplicate.

==================================================
DUPLICATE TITLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82101

Completed by:
- Seller.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SOLD STATUS REAPPLICATION RULE
==================================================

After duplicate issued:
If transfer not ready:
Title clerk must:
- Restore sold status.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS INFORMATION
==================================================

System prompts:
- Sold status
After mark sold transaction completed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION DETACH RULE
==================================================

Registration:
Will detach from title record.

Allows:
- Temporary plate issuance.

==================================================
PENDING LIEN RELEASE RULE
==================================================

If purchaser waiting for lien release:
License plate agent may:
- Mark title sold
AND
- Issue temporary plate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Lienholder verification letter.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE LIEN LETTERS
==================================================

Acceptable letters:
1. Previous owner's lienholder payoff letter
OR
2. Purchaser's lienholder payoff confirmation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMP PLATE RULE
==================================================

Temporary plate issuance:
Requires:
- HSMV 82050.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VESSEL EXCLUSION RULE
==================================================

Pending lien temporary plate process:
Does NOT apply:
To:
- Vessels.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PENALTY DATE RULE
==================================================

Use transfer date on title:
To determine:
- Penalty fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FALLBACK DATE RULE
==================================================

If no title transfer date:
Use:
- Mark title sold status date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT FIELD DATE RULE
==================================================

If comment description contains date:
Use:
- Comment field date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALTERED PURCHASER RULE
==================================================

If purchaser/dealer name altered:
AND
System reflects sold:
Refer to:
- TL-11 Section III.

==================================================
SOLD FLAG ERROR CORRECTION RULE
==================================================

If sold status entered in error:
Need:
- Seller affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE CORRECTION RULE
==================================================

Incorrect sold flag:
May be removed:
Using:
- Title correction transaction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO TITLE PRINT RULE
==================================================

When removing incorrect sold flag:
DO NOT:
- Print certificate of title.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is seller reporting sale?
2. Is transaction dealer trade-in?
3. Is transaction private sale?
4. Is HSMV 82050 completed?
5. Is Notice of Sale section completed?
6. Has vehicle already transferred?
7. Does sold status already exist?
8. Is temporary plate requested?
9. Is lien release pending?
10. Is acceptable lien letter included?
11. Is duplicate title needed?
12. Was sold status entered by mistake?
13. Is affidavit required?
14. Is penalty fee due?
15. Is vessel involved?
16. Is registration detached?
17. Is purchaser information altered?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Forgetting to enter purchaser/dealer name
- Using wrong sale date
- Marking already transferred title sold
- Forgetting to restore sold status after duplicate
- Retaining HSMV 82050 unnecessarily
- Issuing title during sold flag correction
- Missing lien verification letter
- Applying temp plate process to vessels
- Missing seller affidavit for correction
- Incorrectly calculating penalty fee date

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUSINESS PURPOSE
==================================================

Mark title sold:
Primarily protects:
- Seller liability exposure

While allowing:
- Delayed title transfer processing.
