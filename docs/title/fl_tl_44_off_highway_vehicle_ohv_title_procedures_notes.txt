==================================================
OFF-HIGHWAY VEHICLE DEFINITIONS
==================================================

OHV:
- Off-highway vehicle
- Not registered for highway use.

==================================================
ATV DEFINITION
==================================================

ATV:
- 55 inches or less wide
- 1,500 pounds or less dry weight
- Three or more non-highway tires
- Recreational use.

==================================================
TWO-RIDER ATV DEFINITION
==================================================

Two-rider ATV:
- Manufacturer designed
for:
- One operator
AND
- One passenger.

==================================================
OHM DEFINITION
==================================================

OHM:
- Off-highway motorcycle

Characteristics:
- Seat or saddle
- Two wheels or less touching ground
- Off-road use

Excludes:
- Tractor
- Moped.

==================================================
ROV DEFINITION
==================================================

ROV:
- Recreational off-highway vehicle

Characteristics:
- 80 inches or less wide
- 3,500 pounds or less dry weight
- Four or more non-highway tires
- Recreational use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ROV EXCLUSION RULE
==================================================

ROV does NOT include:
- Golf cart
- Low-speed vehicle.

==================================================
PUBLIC LANDS RULE
==================================================

Public lands:
- Government owned lands
available for public use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE REQUIREMENT RULE
==================================================

OHVs purchased after:
- 07/01/2002

MUST:
- Be titled.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ROV TITLE RULE
==================================================

ROVs purchased after:
- 07/01/2009

MUST:
- Be titled.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE RULE
==================================================

OHVs and ROVs:
DO NOT receive:
- License plate
OR
- Registration decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VALIDATION DECAL RULE
==================================================

OHVs and ROVs:
DO receive:
- Title validation decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE VALIDATION DECAL RULE
==================================================

Title validation decal:
Proves:
- Title issued.

==================================================
FORMS REQUIRED
==================================================

Primary forms:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82101
- HSMV 82139
- HSMV 82054

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORM RULE
==================================================

As of:
- 07/01/2023

Use:
- HSMV 82040 MV

For:
- OH body type vehicles.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP — PRE-2002 OHV
==================================================

For OHV purchased before:
- 07/01/2002

OR ROV before:
- 07/01/2009

Need ONE:
- MCO
- Bill of sale
- Ownership affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-2002 SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax proof:
NOT required
for:
- OHV before 07/01/2002
- ROV before 07/01/2009.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP — NEW OHV/ROV
==================================================

NEW vehicle:
Requires:
- MCO.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MCO RULE
==================================================

MCO must show:
- Complete description
AND
- Complete ownership chain.

==================================================
USED OUT-OF-STATE OHV RULE
==================================================

Used out-of-state OHV:
Requires:
- Out-of-state title

OR if untitled state:
- Dealer bill of sale
OR
- Registration and BOS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Ownership documents:
Must:
- Be in applicant name
OR
- Properly assigned.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 30-DAY PENALTY RULE
==================================================

Penalty fee applies if:
- More than 30 days
after assignment/delivery.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOREIGN DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Foreign language documents:
MUST:
- Be translated to English.

==================================================
TRANSLATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Translation must:
- Be notarized
- Be certified accurate
- Be signed by translator

Translator must:
- Know both languages
- Normally translate documents
- Have no ownership interest.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN RULE
==================================================

If lien recorded:
- Lien date required.

==================================================
LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

If prior lien exists:
Need:
- Lien satisfaction
OR
- Carry lien forward.

==================================================
USED FLORIDA OHV RULE
==================================================

Used Florida OHV:
Requires:
- Florida title

OR limited bill of sale exception.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE LIMITATION RULE
==================================================

Bill of sale accepted ONLY IF:
Prior owner:
- Bought before mandatory title law
AND
- Was not required to title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER TITLE RULE
==================================================

Dealer cannot sell untitled OHV:
Without:
- First titling in dealer name.

==================================================
TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Florida OHV transfer requires:
- Seller section completed
- Purchaser application completed
- Sales tax
- Fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFER OF EQUITY RULE
==================================================

Transfer of equity:
Requires:
- Lienholder authorization.

==================================================
LIENHOLDER AUTHORIZATION RULE
==================================================

Lienholder authorization:
Must:
- Be on letterhead
AND
- Authorize transfer.

==================================================
DUPLICATE TITLE RULE
==================================================

Duplicate title requires:
- HSMV 82101
- Duplicate box checked
- Fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDRESS VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

If address differs from DHSMV:
Verification section:
MUST be completed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 82041 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82041:
CANNOT replace:
- HSMV 82101
for duplicate titles.

==================================================
MODIFY TITLE / ADD LIEN RULE
==================================================

Lost title plus lien add:
Process as:
- Modify title.

==================================================
LIEN ADD REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- Title
- HSMV 82139 or 82041
- Fees.

==================================================
REPOSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Untitled repossessed OHV:
Requires:
- HSMV 82040
- Lien contract copy
- Fees.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPOSSESSION TITLE RULE
==================================================

If already titled:
Follow:
- TL-23.

==================================================
TITLE VALIDATION DECAL REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82054
- Decal fee.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WEIGHT RULE
==================================================

OHV over:
- 1,500 pounds

NOT subject to title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ROV WEIGHT RULE
==================================================

ROV over:
- 3,500 pounds

NOT subject to title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALVAGE RULE
==================================================

Salvage law:
DOES NOT apply
to:
- OHV
- ROV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE TOTAL LOSS RULE
==================================================

Insurance total loss:
Requires:
- Clear title
before sale.

NO salvage branding allowed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

If confidential VIN missing:
- Assign FLA decal.

==================================================
TOWING/STORAGE RULE
==================================================

Towing/storage charges:
May apply under:
- 713.78 FS.

==================================================
LABOR/STORAGE RULE
==================================================

Repair/storage charges:
May apply under:
- 713.58 FS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AGRICULTURAL EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

OHV/ROV used:
ONLY for:
- Farm
OR
- Forest agricultural purposes

EXEMPT from:
- Sales tax.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECREATIONAL TAX RULE
==================================================

If recreational use:
- Full sales tax applies.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOLEN DECAL RULE
==================================================

Replacement decal:
NO fee
IF:
- Reported stolen to law enforcement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEFACED DECAL RULE
==================================================

Replacement decal:
NO fee
IF:
- Citation issued for damaged/defaced decal.

==================================================
DECAL PLACEMENT — ATV
==================================================

Place decal:
- Left rear quadrant
- Permanent plastic or metal
- Visible externally.

==================================================
DECAL PLACEMENT — OHM
==================================================

Place decal:
- Left fork leg
- Horizontal or vertical
- Visible from left side.

==================================================
DECAL PLACEMENT — ROV
==================================================

Place decal:
- Driver side left rear
- Flat body surface
- Visible externally.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL INSTALL RULE
==================================================

Surface must be:
- Clean
AND
- Dry.

Incorrect placement:
May result in:
- Citation.
