- Vehicle repossessions

Applies when:
- Borrower defaults
- Vehicle is repossessed
- Court awards possession
- Lienholder seeks title

==================================================
CORE LEGAL PRINCIPLE
==================================================

Repossession transfers ownership rights through:
- Lienholder repossession rights
OR
- Court-ordered replevin

Repossessor must:
- Possess vehicle
- Follow statutory requirements
- Apply for Florida title

==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

DEFAULT:
Failure to meet financial obligations.

REPOSSESSION:
Recovery of collateral after loan default.

REPLEVIN:
Court process recovering property wrongfully withheld.

WRIT OF REPLEVIN:
Court document authorizing sheriff recovery only.

FINAL JUDGMENT IN REPLEVIN:
Court order awarding ownership/possession.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLEVIN RULE
==================================================

A Writ of Replevin:
- ONLY authorizes recovery of vehicle/property

A Writ of Replevin ALONE:
- DOES NOT authorize title issuance

A Final Judgment in Replevin:
- Must award ownership
- Must specifically describe vehicle/mobile home

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE WITH FLORIDA LIENHOLDER
==================================================

REQUIRED:

1. Florida title
UNLESS:
- Electronic title exists

2. HSMV 82040 application

3. Sales tax or exemption

4. Non-use affidavit if applicable

5. Title fees

==================================================
SUBSEQUENT LIENHOLDER REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

IF subsequent lien exists:

PRIMARY lienholder must notify subsequent lienholder.

Acceptable proof:
- Lien satisfaction
OR
- HSMV 82048
OR
- Notice of intent to repossess

AND:
- Certified mail receipt
OR
- Returned unclaimed letter

==================================================
15-DAY REPOSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Subsequent lienholder has:
- 15 calendar days
after mailing notice

To:
- Protest repossession

IMPORTANT:
Applications submitted BEFORE expiration of 15-day period:
- MUST be rejected

==================================================
STOP CONDITIONS
==================================================

Administrative stop placed if:
- Subsequent lienholder protests repossession

==================================================
ELECTRONIC TITLE RULES
==================================================

Paper title not required if:
- DMS reflects Electronic Title

IF title lost:
- Affidavit required
UNLESS:
- Electronic title exists

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE RULES
==================================================

IF out-of-state title:
- Florida repossession title required
IF repossessed in Florida

Requires:
- Ownership verification
- VIN verification
- Title/lien status verification

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

VIN verification may be completed by ONLY:

- Florida law enforcement
- Florida tax collector/DMS employee
- Florida DMS compliance examiner
- Florida licensed dealer
- Florida licensed auction (VF/VI/VA)

Auction verification:
- Must be on auction letterhead

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Semi trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel trailers

==================================================
VESSEL REPOSSESSION RULES
==================================================

VESSELS require:
- Copy of lien instrument
(front and back)

Must show:
- Lienholder
- Customer
- Year
- Make
- HIN
- Execution date

Verbal contracts:
- NOT acceptable

==================================================
VESSEL SPECIAL RULE
==================================================

Lienholder repossessing vessel:
- Does NOT have to title vessel before resale

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE REPOSSESSION STATES
==================================================

Certain states require:
- Special repossession affidavits/forms

Exhibit B contains:
- State-by-state form chart

AI should flag:
- Out-of-state repo involving non-Florida title

==================================================
FLOOR PLAN REPOSSESSION RULES
==================================================

Floor planner not shown as lienholder:
- Must provide floor plan agreement

If blanket inventory clause exists:
- Vehicle list may not be required

Otherwise:
- Inventory list required

==================================================
FIRST LIENHOLDER RULE
==================================================

ONLY first lienholder:
- Has repossession rights

Subsequent lienholder:
- Cannot repo title vehicle
until becoming first lienholder

==================================================
MCO REPOSSESSION RULE
==================================================

IF:
- MCO assigned
BUT
- Original title never issued

THEN:
- Original title must issue first
showing lien

AFTER:
- Repo title may issue

==================================================
STOLEN REPOSSESSED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

IF repossessed vehicle stolen while in lienholder possession:

REQUIRED:
- Police report
- Affidavit
- Loan default confirmation

==================================================
LIEN RECORDING RULE
==================================================

IF lien missing from title:
- HSMV 82139 required
- Lien recording fee required

==================================================
REPO TITLE ERROR CORRECTION
==================================================

IF repossession title issued in error:
- Title must be mailed to FLHSMV
- Affidavit explanation required
- Prior record reinstated

==================================================
MOBILE HOME SPECIAL RULE
==================================================

Repossessed mobile home exempt from registration IF:
- Not transferred for occupancy
AND
- Remains unoccupied

==================================================
COMMON DEALERSHIP ERRORS
==================================================

- Accepting Writ of Replevin alone
- Missing 15-day waiting period
- Missing certified mail proof
- Missing subsequent lienholder notice
- Missing VIN verification
- Incorrect repossession state affidavit
- Missing lien instrument for vessel
- Attempting repo by non-first lienholder
- Missing out-of-state title verification
- Missing HSMV 82139 lien assignment
- Trying verbal vessel contract
- Incorrectly rejecting electronic title repos
