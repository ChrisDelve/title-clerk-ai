==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

ANCIENT VEHICLE:
Motor vehicle:
Manufactured for private use:
Model year 1945 or earlier.

ANTIQUE MOTOR VEHICLE:
Motor vehicle:
More than 30 years old:
After model year.

ANTIQUE VESSEL:
Vessel:
- Recreational use only
- At least 30 years old
- Powered by original-type power plant

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA TITLE RULE
==================================================

Florida resident seller:
Must first obtain:
Florida title in seller name:
Before sale or transfer.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP OPTIONS
==================================================

Acceptable proof includes:
1. Certificate of title
2. Bill of sale + registration
3. Bill of sale + state record letter
4. Bill of sale + hold harmless affidavit

==================================================
CERTIFICATE OF TITLE RULE
==================================================

If title presented:
Process as:
- OUT per TL-10
OR
- TRT per TL-11

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REASSIGNED TITLE RULE
==================================================

Reassigned title:
Accepted:
If reassigned into customer name.

==================================================
BILL OF SALE RULES
==================================================

Bill of sale must include:
- VIN/HIN
- Engine number if applicable
- Year
- Make
- Model
- Body type
- Color
- Selling price
- Seller address
- Purchaser address
- Printed names
- Signatures
- Sale date

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTARY RULE
==================================================

Bill of sale:
Must be:
- Notarized
OR
- Include perjury clause

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state registration:
Must be:
In seller name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LETTERHEAD RULE
==================================================

Instead of registration:
Customer may provide:
State letter on official letterhead:
Showing:
- Seller was last owner
- Vehicle no longer title eligible due to age

==================================================
HOLD HARMLESS AFFIDAVIT RULES
==================================================

Hold harmless affidavit:
Must state:
- Seller owns vehicle
- No liens exist
- Seller defends title against claims

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HOLD HARMLESS RULE
==================================================

Hold harmless affidavit:
Cannot be included:
On same document:
Signed by both buyer and seller.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEPARATE DOCUMENT RULE
==================================================

Hold harmless affidavit:
Must be:
Separate document.

==================================================
REQUIRED TRANSACTION DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Required:
1. HSMV 82040
2. Ownership proof
3. VIN/HIN inspection if applicable
4. Valid identification
5. POA if applicable
6. Insurance if registering
7. Title fees
8. Registration fees if applicable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORM SPLIT RULE
==================================================

Beginning:

HSMV 82040 split into:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82040 MH

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VESSEL RULE
==================================================

Vessel titles:
Must use:
HSMV 82040 VS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTOR VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle titles:
Must use:
HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Mobile home titles:
Must use:
HSMV 82040 MH.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82041 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82041:
No longer accepted:
Instead of:
HSMV 82040 VS.

==================================================
VIN INSPECTION RULES
==================================================

Original used antique vehicle:
Requires:
VIN inspection.

==================================================
AUTHORIZED VIN INSPECTORS
==================================================

VIN inspection may be completed by:
- Military Police Officer
- Law Enforcement Officer
- Licensed Florida Dealer
- Florida Notary
- DMS Compliance Examiner
- Tax Collector employee/agent
- HSMV 82042 Part A affidavit

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURCHASER/SELLER AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Purchaser and seller:
May jointly verify VIN:
Using:
HSMV 82042 Part A.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HIN RULE
==================================================

HIN inspection:
Not required:
If antique vessel title presented:
As ownership proof.

==================================================
IDENTIFICATION RULES
==================================================

Copies required for:
- Purchaser
- Seller
- POA holder
- Authorization signer

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE IDENTIFICATION
==================================================

Acceptable ID:
- Driver license
- Identification card
- Passport

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEGIBILITY RULE
==================================================

Identification copies:
Must be:
Clear and legible.

==================================================
TITLE SERVICES RULE

- Identification
- Current business license

- City license

EXPEDITED TITLE RULES

Fast title:
