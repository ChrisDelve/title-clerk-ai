FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — APPLICATION FOR CERTIFICATE OF TITLE BY PAWNBROKERS
PROCEDURE: TL-47
CATEGORY: PAWNBROKER TITLES / OPERATION OF LAW / FORFEITED VEHICLES

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Pawnbroker title transfers
- Pawn transaction defaults
- Purchase transaction title transfers
- Operation of law title issuance
- Pawnbroker documentation requirements
- Vehicle forfeiture to pawnbroker
- Vehicle ownership transfers involving pawnshops

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Motorcycles
- Trucks
- Buses
- Travel trailers
- Vehicle trailers
- Off-highway vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Vessels

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAWNBROKING ACT RULE
==================================================

Chapter 539:
Known as:
- Florida Pawnbroking Act.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 30 DAY HOLD RULE
==================================================

Goods delivered to pawnbroker:
Must remain:
- Securely stored
- Unaltered

For:
- 30 calendar days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURCHASE TRANSACTION RULE
==================================================

Purchase transaction goods:
Cannot:
- Be sold
- Be disposed of

Before:
- 30-day holding period expires.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORFEITURE RULE
==================================================

If pledged goods:
NOT redeemed within:
- 30 days after maturity date

Ownership:
Automatically transfers:
To pawnbroker.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OPERATION OF LAW RULE
==================================================

After default:
Ownership transfers:
- By operation of law.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO FURTHER NOTICE RULE
==================================================

After pawn default:
- No additional notice required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VEHICLE POSSESSION RULE
==================================================

If transaction involves vehicle:
Vehicle:
MUST be in possession
of pawnbroker.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSACTION FORM RULE
==================================================

Pawnbroker:
MUST complete:
- Pawnbroker Transaction Form.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SELLER SIGNATURE RULE
==================================================

Pledgor/seller:
MUST sign:
- Pawnbroker Transaction Form.

==================================================
PAWN DEFAULT TITLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

When pawn in default:
Pawnbroker submits:
- Title/proof of ownership
- HSMV 82040 or 82041
- VIN verification if needed
- Registration certificate
- Pawnbroker transaction form
- Affidavit of non-redemption
- Lien satisfactions
- Plate/non-use affidavit
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAWN TITLE ASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

For pawn transaction:
Title:
Does NOT have to be assigned
for transfer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MISSING TITLE RULE
==================================================

If title unavailable:
Need:
- Pawnbroker affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNERSHIP VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Pawnbroker:
Should always verify:
Vehicle titled in:
- Pledgor's name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE IN PAWNBROKER NAME RULE
==================================================

Pawnbroker:
MUST obtain title
in pawnbroker name
before:
- Selling vehicle.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE VIN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

If vehicle not titled in Florida:
Need:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN section of HSMV 82040.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Semi-trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth wheel recreational trailers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Copy of Pawnbroker Registration Certificate

Issued by:
- Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-REDEMPTION AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Need affidavit stating:
Vehicle:
NOT redeemed within:
- 30 days after pawn maturity date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Lien satisfactions

For:
- All liens shown in Florida or other states.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CHAPTER 539 STATEMENT RULE
==================================================

Must state on:
- HSMV 82040
OR
- HSMV 82041

The wording:
"Obtained pursuant to Chapter 539, Florida Statutes."

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit.

==================================================
PURCHASE TRANSACTION RULES
==================================================

If vehicle acquired through:
- Purchase transaction

Pawnbroker may title vehicle after:
- 30 calendar days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURCHASE TITLE RULE
==================================================

For purchase transaction:
Title:
MUST be assigned
to pawnbroker.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PURCHASE HOLDING RULE
==================================================

Need affidavit stating:
Vehicle held:
- 30 calendar days
after purchase date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PENALTY RULE
==================================================

Penalty fee:
NOT applicable.

Reason:
- Operation of law transaction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXTENSION RULE
==================================================

Pawnbroker:
May extend:
- Maturity date.

Must provide:
- Written memorandum/new transaction form.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RE-PURCHASE RULE
==================================================

If original owner:
Repurchases vehicle
and resells to new buyer:

Need affidavit explaining:
- Why title assigned to pawnbroker
- Transfer to pawnbroker void
- New purchaser information

==================================================
PAWNBROKER DEFINITIONS
==================================================

PAWN:
Advancement of funds
secured by pledged goods.

PURCHASE:
Transfer of goods to pawnbroker
through:
- Purchase
- Trade
- Consignment.

PLEDGED GOODS:
Property delivered to pawnbroker.

PLEDGOR:
Person delivering pledged goods.

DEFAULT DATE:
Date ownership vests in pawnbroker.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLEDGED GOODS RULE
==================================================

"Pledged goods":
DOES NOT include:
- Titles only
- Written security only

Without:
- Physical possession.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSACTION FORM CONTENT RULE
==================================================

Pawnbroker transaction form must include:
- Pawnshop information
- Goods description
- Seller information
- Thumbprint
- Identification
- Transaction date
- Amount financed
- Finance charge
- APR
- Default date
- Ownership warranty statement

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT THUMBPRINT RULE
==================================================

Transaction form:
Must include:
- Right thumbprint.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FALSE OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

False ownership representation:
Can result in:
- Third degree felony
OR
- Second degree felony.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COPY DELIVERY RULE
==================================================

Pawnbroker:
Must give:
- Exact copy
Of completed transaction form
To pledgor/seller.

1. Pawn or purchase transaction?
2. Was vehicle redeemed?
3. Did 30-day period expire?
4. Is title assigned correctly?
5. Is operation of law applicable?
6. Is title in pledgor name?
7. Is VIN verification required?
8. Is out-of-state title involved?
9. Is pawnbroker registration included?
10. Is non-redemption affidavit included?
11. Are liens satisfied?
12. Is Chapter 539 statement listed?
13. Is title penalty exempt?
14. Is vehicle in possession of pawnbroker?
15. Is proper HSMV form used?
16. Is non-use affidavit acceptable?
17. Is purchase transaction fully transferred?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Requiring assigned title on pawn default
- Forgetting operation of law exemption
- Missing Chapter 539 statement
- Missing lien satisfactions
- Missing pawnbroker registration certificate
- Accepting incomplete purchase assignment
- Missing VIN verification
- Ignoring 30-day waiting requirement
- Allowing sale before pawnbroker titles vehicle
- Confusing pawn vs purchase transaction
- Forgetting affidavit requirements

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAWN VS PURCHASE DIFFERENCE
==================================================

PAWN:
- Title assignment NOT required
- Ownership transfers after default

PURCHASE:
- Title assignment REQUIRED
- 30-day holding period required
