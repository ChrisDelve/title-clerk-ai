==================================================
GLIDER KIT DEFINITION
==================================================

Glider kits consist of:
- Cab
- Front axle
- Frame
- Accessories

Placed on:
- Existing truck tractors
OR
- Truck tractors assembled from multiple donor trucks.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE BRAND RULE
==================================================

All glider kit titles:
MUST show:
- GLIDER KIT

Brand carries forward:
- For life of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE STATUS RULE
==================================================

Glider kits are titled as:
- Used motor vehicles
- Second level title.

==================================================
TITLE DATA RULES
==================================================

YEAR:
- Year shown on MCO
OR
- Application year if MCO missing year.

MAKE:
- Make shown on MCO.

VIN:
- VIN shown on MCO.

BODY:
- Body type shown on MCO.

==================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS — FLORIDA TITLE VEHICLE
==================================================

Need:
1. Florida title for donor truck tractor
2. Glider kit MCO
3. HSMV 82040 MV or HSMV 82041
4. Road-operable affidavit
5. Major component receipts
6. Sales tax documentation
7. Plate transfer or non-use affidavit
8. Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DONOR TITLE RULE
==================================================

Florida donor title:
MUST be cancelled.

Cancellation reason:
- TA

Comments field:
MUST include:
- "Glider Kit"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-07 RULE
==================================================

If Florida title unavailable:
Follow:
- TL-07 procedures

BEFORE:
Processing glider title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Need notarized/perjury affidavit stating:
- Vehicle road operable
AND
- Repairs completed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MAJOR COMPONENT RULE
==================================================

Receipts required for all major components:
NOT covered by:
- Title
OR
- MCO.

==================================================
RECEIPT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Receipts/bills of sale must show:
- Seller name
- Seller address
- Seller signature

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-1955 ENGINE RULE
==================================================

For pre-1955 vehicles:
Engine receipt must show:
- Engine number
- Engine year.

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS
==================================================

Includes:
- Front end assembly
- Fender
- Hood
- Grill
- Bumper
- Rear body section
- Quarter panels
- Deck lid
- Floor pan
- Door assemblies
- Engine
- Transmission
- Frame
- Cowl assembly/firewall
- Airbag

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Must:
- Collect Florida sales tax
OR
- Specify tax exemption/tax information
on HSMV application.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida plate transfer
OR
- Non-use affidavit.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE GLIDER KIT RULE
==================================================

If donor vehicle titled out-of-state:
Need:
- Out-of-state ownership proof
PLUS
- All remaining required documents.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE VIN RULE
==================================================

VIN verification:
NOT required

Because:
Vehicle titled using:
- Manufacturer-assigned glider VIN.

==================================================
MULTIPLE DONOR TRUCK RULE
==================================================

If assembled from several trucks:
AND
- No title exists

Need:
- All standard documents
PLUS
- Affidavit stating vehicle assembled from stock parts.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALVAGE RULE
==================================================

Florida title CANNOT issue if ownership document is:
- Salvage Unrebuildable
OR
- Certificate of Destruction.

These vehicles:
- Parts/scrap only.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is this a glider kit vehicle?
2. Is donor vehicle Florida titled?
3. Is donor vehicle out-of-state?
4. Is donor title available?
5. Was title cancellation completed?
6. Was cancellation reason TA entered?
7. Was “Glider Kit” comment added?
8. Is MCO present?
9. Is road-operable affidavit included?
10. Are all major component receipts present?
11. Is sales tax addressed?
12. Is plate transfer/non-use affidavit included?
13. Is salvage unrebuildable ownership involved?
14. Was vehicle assembled from multiple trucks?
15. Is additional affidavit required?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing title cancellation
- Wrong cancellation reason
- Missing “Glider Kit” comment
- Missing MCO
- Missing affidavit
- Missing receipts
- Accepting salvage unrebuildable ownership
- Missing sales tax documentation
- Missing plate transfer/non-use affidavit
- Incorrect VIN verification requirement
- Missing second-level title processing
