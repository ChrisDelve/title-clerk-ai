- Dealers

==================================================
GENERAL INFORMATION
==================================================

NHTSA defines:
- Low-speed vehicles
As:
- Motor vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LSV ENGINE RULE
==================================================

Florida allows:
LSVs to be:
- Electric
OR
- Gasoline powered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LSV SPEED RULE
==================================================

Maximum LSV speed:
25 MPH.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ROAD SPEED RULE
==================================================

LSVs may NOT operate:
On roads where minimum speed exceeds:
35 MPH.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MINI-TRUCK SPEED RULE
==================================================

Mini-trucks may reach:
55 MPH.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MINI-TRUCK ROAD RULE
==================================================

Mini-trucks may NOT operate:
On roads where posted speed exceeds:
35 MPH.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVWR RULE
==================================================

LSV GVWR:
Must be:
Under 3,000 pounds.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEDERAL STANDARD RULE
==================================================

LSVs must conform to:
49 CFR 571.500
Standard No. 500.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MCO RULE
==================================================

New LSVs and mini-trucks:
Issued:
Manufacturer's Certificate of Origin (MCO).

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

LSVs and mini-trucks require:
17-digit VIN.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEDERAL COMPLIANCE RULE
==================================================

MCO must state:
Vehicle conforms to:
49 CFR 571.500.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BODY TYPE RULE
==================================================

LSV body types:
- 2P
- 4P
- 6P
- 9P

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BRANDING RULE
==================================================

Selecting LSV body type:
Automatically creates:
"Low-speed vehicle" brand.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MINI-TRUCK BODY TYPE RULE
==================================================

Mini-truck body type:
- MT

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRIC RULE
==================================================

Electric LSV:
Must use:
Electric vehicle use type.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE PRINT RULE
==================================================

Electric LSV titles print:
- Low-speed Vehicle brand
AND
- "Electric" in model space.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION CLASS RULE
==================================================

LSVs register as:
- AU Auto

According to:
Weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FOR-HIRE RULE
==================================================

For-hire LSV:
Uses class code:
Based on weight.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OLD TITLE RULE
==================================================

Pre-FRVIS LSVs:
May not already have:
LSV brand.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSFER BRAND RULE
==================================================

Older unbranded LSV:
Must receive:
LSV brand
During transfer.

==================================================
LSV OPERATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

LSV or mini-truck:
May operate ONLY on:
Roads with posted speed:
35 MPH or less.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CROSSING RULE
==================================================

LSVs may cross:
Higher speed roads:
At intersections.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EQUIPMENT RULE
==================================================

LSVs and mini-trucks require:
- Headlamps
- Stop lamps
- Turn signals
- Tail lamps
- Reflex reflectors
- Parking brake
- Rearview mirrors
- Windshield
- Seat belts
- VIN

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE RULE
==================================================

LSVs and mini-trucks:
Must be:
Registered
AND
Insured.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSE RULE
==================================================

LSV operator:
Must possess:
Valid driver license.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COUNTY RESTRICTION RULE
==================================================

County or municipality:
May prohibit:
LSV operation:
For safety reasons.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DOT RESTRICTION RULE
==================================================

Florida DOT:
May prohibit:
LSV operation:
For safety reasons.

==================================================
GOLF CART CONVERSION RULES
==================================================

Golf carts:
Cannot normally:
Be titled or registered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOLF CART EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Golf carts MAY be converted:
To:
Low-speed vehicles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPLETED CONVERSION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle must be:
Fully converted:
Before applying for title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGIONAL OFFICE RULE
==================================================

Applications for golf cart conversion:
Must be submitted:
To Motorist Services Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Compliance examiner:
Reviews paperwork
AND
Schedules virtual inspection.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA NUMBER RULE
==================================================

Converted LSV:
Receives:
FLA number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONVERTED YEAR RULE
==================================================

Converted LSV year:
Equals:
Year converted.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONVERTED MAKE RULE
==================================================

Converted LSV make:
- ASPT

==================================================
REQUIRED CONVERSION DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Required:
1. Original MCO or bill of sale
2. HSMV 82040
3. HSMV 84490
4. HSMV 86064
5. Bills of sale/receipts for parts
6. Sales tax documentation
7. Insurance proof
8. Vehicle weight
9. Title fees
10. Photos
11. Inspection fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUILDER STATEMENT RULE
==================================================

HSMV 84490:
Must state:
Vehicle conforms to:
49 CFR 571.500
AND
Florida statutes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Golf cart conversion requires:
HSMV 86064.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PARTS RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

All conversion parts:
Require:
Bills of sale or receipts.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax due on:
- Golf cart purchase
- Conversion parts
If tax not previously collected.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE MINIMUM RULE
==================================================

Minimum insurance:
- $10,000 PDL
AND
- $10,000 PIP

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STANDARD WEIGHT RULE
==================================================

Standard conversion weights:
- 2 passenger = 980 lbs
- 4 passenger = 1,240 lbs
- 6 passenger = 1,460 lbs

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WEIGHT SLIP RULE
==================================================

Certified weight slip:
NO LONGER REQUIRED.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT DESC RULE
==================================================

Processing agency must enter:
- Region number
- FLA decal audit number

In:
Comment Desc field.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PHOTO RULE
==================================================

Required photos:
- Front
- Back
- Left side
- Right side

==================================================
PREVIOUSLY CANCELLED TITLE RULE
==================================================

If previously cancelled LSV title found:
FLHSMV sends:
14-day notice letter:
To prior owner.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 14-DAY RULE
==================================================

If no objection received:
Within 14 days:
Title may be reinstated.

==================================================
LSV TO GOLF CART CONVERSION RULES
==================================================

LSV may be converted:
Back into:
Golf cart.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURRENDER RULE
==================================================

Owner must surrender:
- Current title
- Registration plate

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAILER RULE
==================================================

Vehicle must be:
Trailed:
To regional office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CONVERSION INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Regional office inspection fee:
- $40

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOLF CART AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Conversion to golf cart requires:
HSMV 86066.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOLF CART SPEED RULE
==================================================

Converted golf cart:
Must comply with:
20 MPH golf cart restriction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL RULE
==================================================

Regional office issues:
Rear decal reading:
"CONVERTED VEHICLE. Max speed 20 mph."

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

Decal must:
Be visible on rear:
Of vehicle.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS RULES
==================================================

Golf carts:
Cannot be titled or registered.

LSVs and mini-trucks:
MUST be titled and registered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Retailers selling:
LSVs or mini-trucks:
Must have dealer license.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ODOMETER RULE
==================================================

LSVs:
Exempt from odometer disclosure laws.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OFF-ROAD VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Off-road vehicles:
Cannot be converted:
To LSVs.

Examples:
- Mule
- ATV

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GVWR CALCULATION RULE
==================================================

GVWR includes:
- Vehicle weight
- Cargo load
- 150 lbs per seat position

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRI-VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Tri-vehicles:
Refer to:
TL-67.
