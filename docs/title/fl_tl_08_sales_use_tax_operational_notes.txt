FLORIDA TITLE & LIEN PROCEDURE
SALES AND USE TAX – MOTOR VEHICLES, MOBILE HOMES, AND VESSELS
SOURCE: TL-08
REVISION DATE: 06/26/2023

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Florida sales tax collection
- Use tax collection
- Trade-in tax calculations
- Tax exemptions
- Out-of-state transactions
- Military exemptions
- Dealer tax responsibilities
- Corporate and trust transfers
- Gift transfers
- Inheritance transfers
- Mobile home taxation
- Agricultural exemptions
- Foreign country vehicle taxation

This is one of the MOST IMPORTANT dealership procedures.

Incorrect tax handling can:
- Create dealership liability
- Trigger audits
- Cause customer complaints
- Result in under-collected taxes
- Delay title issuance
- Cause compliance violations

==================================================
CORE TAX RULE
==================================================

DEFAULT RULE:
Florida sales tax applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Vessels

STANDARD FLORIDA RATE:
6%

PLUS:
Possible county discretionary surtax.

==================================================
STRAIGHT SALE RULE
==================================================

STRAIGHT SALE:
No trade involved.

TAXABLE AMOUNT:
FULL purchase price.

==================================================
TRADE-IN RULE
==================================================

IF trade-in involved:

TAXABLE AMOUNT:
Sale price MINUS trade allowance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRADE-IN RULE
==================================================

Trade vehicle:
DOES NOT need to be titled
in purchaser’s name.

Third-party trade-ins ARE allowed
IF transaction is structured as ONE transaction.

VERY IMPORTANT for dealership structuring.

==================================================
COUNTY SURTAX RULE
==================================================

County surtax:
Based on purchaser residence county.

ONLY applies to:
FIRST $5,000 of sales price.

==================================================
VESSEL TAX CAP
==================================================

VESSEL MAXIMUM TAX:
$18,000 cap.

==================================================
NEW MOBILE HOME TAX RATE
==================================================

NEW mobile home:
3% sales tax.

ONLY applies to:
NEW mobile homes never transferred
to ultimate purchaser.

==================================================
DEALER TAX RESPONSIBILITIES
==================================================

Dealer MUST:
- Collect sales tax
- Collect surtax
- Report tax collected
- Enter dealer information
- Include sales tax registration number

Dealer information required EVEN IF:
No tax due.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE RESIDENT SALES
==================================================

Florida dealer selling to out-of-state resident:

Collect:
Resident state equivalent tax rate.

HOWEVER:
Cannot exceed Florida 6% rate.

NO Florida discretionary surtax applies.

==================================================
DR-123 REQUIREMENT
==================================================

Out-of-state partial exemption requires:
DR-123.

Customer must:
License vehicle in home state within 45 days.

==================================================
HOPE SCHOLARSHIP RULE
==================================================

Before tax payment:
Offer DR-HS1 form.

==================================================
TAXABLE TRANSFERS
==================================================

TAXABLE:
- Individual to individual
- Partnership to individual
- Partnership to corporation
- Individual to corporation
- Corporation to corporation
- Corporation to individual
- Transfers with lien assumption
- Most trust transfers for consideration
- Taxicab classifications
- Contest winnings
- Certain out-of-state imports

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN ASSUMPTION RULE
==================================================

IF lien assumed:
Sales tax applies.

Taxable amount includes:
- Cash paid
PLUS
- Outstanding balance assumed

This is a MAJOR dealership mistake area.

==================================================
CORPORATE TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Transfers involving corporations:
PRESUMED TAXABLE.

Even if:
Same owners/shareholders.

Unless specific exemption applies.

==================================================
TRUST RULE
==================================================

Transfer to/from trust:
TAXABLE IF consideration involved.

NOT taxable IF:
No consideration.

==================================================
"OR" OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Removing party from OR title:
NOT taxable.

Reason:
Each party already owns 100%.

==================================================
"AND" OWNERSHIP RULE
==================================================

Removing or adding party on AND title:
GENERALLY taxable.

Unless exemption applies.

VERY IMPORTANT ownership distinction.

==================================================
EXEMPT TRANSFERS
==================================================

EXEMPT:
- Even trade
- Trade down
- Divorce
- Inheritance
- Gift with no lien assumption
- Corporate merger
- Corporate name change
- Dealer inventory transfers
- Repossessed vehicle redemption
- Certain tribal purchases
- Certain farm/agriculture exemptions
- Court ordered transfers
- Certain nonprofit/religious transfers

==================================================
DIVORCE EXEMPTION
==================================================

REQUIRED:
Divorce decree.

==================================================
INHERITANCE EXEMPTION
==================================================

Inheritance transfers:
EXEMPT from sales tax.

==================================================
GIFT RULE
==================================================

Gift transfer exempt IF:
- No consideration
AND
- No lien assumed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT RULE
==================================================

IF donor ALSO financing vehicle:
NOT a gift.

Sales tax applies.

==================================================
MARRIED COUPLE RULE
==================================================

Transfers between married couple:
EXEMPT.

Even if:
Outstanding lien exists.

==================================================
DEALER INVENTORY RULE
==================================================

Dealer titling inventory into dealer name:
EXEMPT.

Applies to:
Licensed dealers holding for resale.

==================================================
REPOSSESSION REDEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Original owner redeeming repossessed vehicle:
NOT taxable.

ONLY IF:
Redemption within 90 days.

==================================================
TRIBAL EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Seminole or Miccosukee exemption requires:
- Sale on tribal land
- Tribal residency
- Tribal identification

==================================================
OUTBOARD MOTOR RULE
==================================================

Outboard motors/accessories:
Can be exempt from vessel sales price
IF:
- Casual sale
- Separately itemized

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE VEHICLE TAX RULE
==================================================

Owned AND used outside Florida:
6 months or more:
NO Florida use tax.

LESS THAN 6 MONTHS:
Florida tax due.

Credit allowed for other state taxes paid.

==================================================
FOREIGN COUNTRY RULE
==================================================

Foreign country purchases:
Florida use tax applies.

NO credit for foreign taxes paid.

==================================================
MILITARY FOREIGN IMPORT EXEMPTION
==================================================

Military exemption requires:
- Active military
- Vehicle owned/used abroad 6+ months
- Personal use
- Military proof

==================================================
MOBILE HOME RULES
==================================================

MH decal:
Taxable.

RP decal:
Exempt IF land and mobile home sold together.

==================================================
PRIVATE SALE MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Attachments may be deducted IF:
- Itemized bill of sale provided
- Mobile home retains value

==================================================
SERVICE WARRANTY RULE
==================================================

Service warranties:
TAXABLE.

Even if:
Vehicle tax capped for surtax purposes.

==================================================
REBATE RULE
==================================================

MANUFACTURER rebate:
Still taxable.

DEALER discount:
Not taxable.

VERY IMPORTANT difference.

==================================================
LEASE TRADE-IN RULE
==================================================

Leased vehicle trade-in:
NO trade-in tax credit.

Reason:
Lessee not actual owner.

==================================================
FOREIGN COUNTRY TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Florida resident importing vehicle:
Use tax due.

Foreign tax credits:
NOT allowed.

==================================================
AGRICULTURAL EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

Certain farm equipment/trailers:
EXEMPT.

Requires:
Signed exemption certificate.

==================================================
NONPROFIT / RELIGIOUS EXEMPTION
==================================================

Exempt organizations:
Must have Consumer Certificate of Exemption.

==================================================
SPECIAL NEEDS EQUIPMENT RULE
==================================================

Wheelchair lifts and accessibility modifications:
EXEMPT IF:
Prescription provided.

Vehicle itself still taxable.

==================================================
DEALERSHIP HIGH-RISK ERROR AREAS
==================================================

MOST COMMON ERRORS:
- Wrong trade-in calculation
- Incorrect surtax handling
- Assuming OR/AND same tax treatment
- Missing lien assumption tax
- Wrong out-of-state tax
- Wrong gift classification
- Improper military exemption
- Dealer rebate miscalculation
- Wrong mobile home classification
- Improper corporate exemption
- Missing DR-123
- Incorrect trust taxation
- Assuming all family transfers exempt
- Wrong leased vehicle trade treatment

WORKFLOW BEST PRACTICE
==================================================

Before calculating tax:

1. Identify transfer type
2. Determine ownership structure
3. Verify consideration
4. Verify lien assumption
5. Verify trade-in structure
6. Verify residence county
7. Verify exemptions
8. Verify out-of-state timing
9. Verify supporting documents
10. Verify dealer tax reporting
