FLORIDA TITLE & LIEN PROCEDURE
LANDLORD-TENANT ABANDONED PROPERTY
SOURCE: TL-16
REVISION DATE: 06/26/2023

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Abandoned vehicles
- Abandoned vessels
- Abandoned mobile homes
- Landlord-tenant title transfers
- Operation of law title processing
- Public sale title issuance

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

TL-16 ONLY applies when:
- Landlord exists
- Tenant exists
- Rental payments existed
- Lease/rental relationship can be proven

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

NO landlord-tenant relationship:
Requires court order.

==================================================
COURT ORDER RULE
==================================================

If customer cannot prove:
- Rental agreement
OR
- Periodic rental payments

Then:
Florida declaratory judgment required.

==================================================
PROPERTY TYPES COVERED
==================================================

TL-16 applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Recreational vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Manufactured homes
- Vessels
- Off-highway vehicles

==================================================
ABANDONED PROPERTY NOTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Landlord MUST notify:
- Former tenant
AND
- Any known owner of property

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEADLINE RULE
==================================================

Personal delivery:
Minimum 10 days notice.

Mailed notice:
Minimum 15 days notice.

==================================================
NOTICE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice must include:
- Vehicle description
- VIN/HIN
- Make
- Model
- Year if known
- Plate number
- Claim deadline
- Storage charges
- Sale warning
- Property location

==================================================
VESSEL REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Vessel notices must include:
- Registration number
- Hull Identification Number (HIN)
- Motor manufacturer
- Horsepower

==================================================
TRAILER REQUIREMENT
==================================================

If vessel left on trailer:
Trailer information required too.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

If property believed under $500:
Additional proof required.

==================================================
UNDER $500 DOCUMENTATION
==================================================

Must provide:
- Kelley Blue Book or NADA
- Exterior photos
- Interior photos
- VIN/HIN photos
- Condition proof

==================================================
MOBILE HOME SPECIAL RULE
==================================================

Mobile homes under $500:
Require:
- Exterior photos
- Interior photos
- Serial number photo

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Certificate of destruction
may be required for damaged homes.

==================================================
STORAGE COST RULE
==================================================

Landlord may charge:
- Storage fees
- Advertising fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Landlord cannot charge
multiple parties same storage costs.

==================================================
PUBLIC SALE RULE
==================================================

Sale requires:
- Newspaper advertisement
- Once weekly
- 2 consecutive weeks

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE TIMING RULE
==================================================

Sale timing:
- At least 10 days after first publication
- Last publication minimum 5 days before sale

==================================================
PUBLIC SALE LOCATION RULE
==================================================

Sale location:
Must be nearest suitable location
within county where property abandoned.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Landlord AND tenant
may bid at sale.

==================================================
LIEN RULE
==================================================

Existing liens:
Remain attached unless satisfied.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Unsatisfied liens:
Must carry forward to new owner.

==================================================
BILL OF SALE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Bill of sale must include:
- VIN/HIN
- Title number if available
- Buyer information
- Seller information
- Sale date
- Sale amount
- Signatures

==================================================
SALE PROCEEDS RULE
==================================================

Remaining proceeds:
Must be sent to county treasury
within 30 days after sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Former owner:
Has 1 year to claim proceeds.

==================================================
IDENTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Acceptable ID:
- State driver license
- State ID card
- Passport
- Passport card
- Active Florida business verification

==================================================
BUSINESS RULE
==================================================

Business transactions:
Must verify ACTIVE SunBiz status.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

If business inactive:
Reject transaction.

==================================================
POWER OF ATTORNEY RULE
==================================================

POA transactions require:
- Original POA
- Copy of agent ID

==================================================
RENTAL AGREEMENT RULE
==================================================

Must provide:
Signed lease/rental agreement.

==================================================
ORAL LEASE RULE
==================================================

Oral lease under 1 year:
Requires proof of periodic payments.

==================================================
FORM REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required forms may include:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 MH
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82042
- Notice to Reclaim Property
- Landlord Affidavit
- Affidavit of Mailing/Delivery

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state vehicles:
Require confidential VIN inspection.

==================================================
MODULAR HOME RULE
==================================================

Modular homes obtained through
landlord-tenant process:
Require Dealer Services inspection.

==================================================
MOBILE HOME MULTI-SECTION RULE
==================================================

Doublewide/triplewide homes:
Use separate HSMV 82040
for each side.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

All supporting documentation:
Placed with Side A.

==================================================
LANDLORD AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Landlord affidavit required:
- Individual form
OR
- Business/corporation form

==================================================
MAIL DELIVERY AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Must document:
- Mailing
OR
- Personal delivery

==================================================
DEALERSHIP HIGH-RISK ERROR AREAS
==================================================

MOST COMMON ERRORS:
- Missing lease agreement
- Missing proof of rental payments
- Wrong notification dates
- Sale before waiting period
- Incomplete newspaper advertisements
- Missing VIN/HIN
- Missing lien carry-forward
- Missing landlord affidavit
- Improper under-$500 documentation
- Missing proof of mailing
- Missing bill of sale
- Business not active on SunBiz
- Missing POA documentation
- Wrong sale timelines
- No proof of public sale

==================================================
WORKFLOW BEST PRACTICE
==================================================

Before approving transaction:

1. Verify landlord-tenant relationship
2. Verify rental agreement
3. Verify rental payments
4. Verify notices sent correctly
5. Verify timing requirements
6. Verify publication requirements
7. Verify public sale documentation
8. Verify bill of sale
9. Verify liens
10. Verify identity documents
11. Verify SunBiz status if business
12. Verify VIN/HIN inspections
13. Verify title application forms
14. Verify landlord affidavit
15. Verify proof of mailing/delivery
