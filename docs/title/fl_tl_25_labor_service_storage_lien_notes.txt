FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — LABOR, SERVICE, AND STORAGE LIENS

CATEGORY: TITLE LIEN / REPAIR SHOP LIEN / STORAGE LIEN / PUBLIC SALE TITLE

- 713.585 F.S.

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

- Labor liens
- Repair liens
- Storage liens
- Repair shop title processes
- Public sale title procedures
- Notice requirements
- Newspaper publication requirements
- Certificate of Compliance requirements
- Report of Sale requirements
- FDACS repair registration requirements

This procedure is used when:
- Repair shop is unpaid
- Vehicle storage charges accrue
- Vehicle abandoned after repair
- Repair facility enforces lien rights
- Vehicle sold at public sale

==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

MOTOR VEHICLE:
Includes:
- Automobile
- Truck
- Bus
- Motorcycle
- Recreational vehicle
- Motor scooter

DOES NOT INCLUDE:
- Trailers
- Mobile homes
- Travel trailers without motive power
- Watercraft
- Aircraft

==================================================
IMPORTANT AGENCIES
==================================================

FLHSMV:
- Title processing
- Lien title issuance

FDACS:
- Repair shop registration oversight

NMVTIS:
- State title research
- Ownership research

CLERK OF COURT:
- Certificate of Compliance
- Report of Sale
- Hearing requests
- Bond processing

==================================================
IMPORTANT TIMELINE
==================================================

DAY 0:
- Repairs completed
- Customer notified

WITHIN 7 BUSINESS DAYS:
- Notice of Claim of Lien mailed
- Good Faith Notice if owner unknown

15 DAYS BEFORE SALE:
- Notice of Sale mailed
- Newspaper publication

60 DAYS AFTER REPAIR COMPLETION:
- Earliest vehicle may be sold

61ST DAY:
- Public sale allowed

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULES
==================================================

A vehicle:
- Cannot be sold before 60 days after repairs completed

Notice of lien:
- Must be mailed within 7 business days after storage charges begin

Failure to notify properly:
- Can eliminate storage charges
- Can invalidate process
- May require restart

==================================================
REPAIR SHOP REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Repair shop MUST:
- Be registered with FDACS
- Have valid registration
- Registration valid on repair drop-off date

REQUIRED:
- Copy of Motor Vehicle Repair Registration Certificate

If registration invalid:
- Transaction rejected
- Court order required

If address mismatch:
- Transaction rejected

==================================================
EXCEPTION — UPHOLSTERY SHOPS
==================================================

Upholstery shops:
- May be exempt from FDACS registration

Instead of registration certificate:
- FDACS exemption letter required

==================================================
WRITTEN REPAIR ESTIMATE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Repair estimate MUST include:
- Shop name/address/phone
- Customer information
- Date/time
- Vehicle information
- Proposed completion date
- Repair description
- Estimated costs
- Labor method
- Storage charge disclosure
- Warranty statement
- Payment method
- Authorization information

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT $100 RULE
==================================================

If repair exceeds $100:
- Special disclosure section required

Customer must:
- Request estimate
OR
- Waive estimate
OR
- Set max repair amount

==================================================
INVOICE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Invoice MUST include:
- Odometer reading
- Work completed
- Itemized labor
- Itemized parts
- Warranty information
- FDACS registration number

==================================================
ADMINISTRATIVE FEE RULE
==================================================

Administrative fee:
- Maximum $250

Cannot exceed:
- $250 combined extra fees

==================================================
NOTICE OF CLAIM OF LIEN REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice must be sent:
- Certified mail

Must go to:
- Registered owner
- Customer
- Lienholders
- Interested parties

Must contain:
- VIN
- Vehicle description
- Amount owed
- Repair information
- Storage charges
- Sale information
- Hearing rights
- Bond rights
- Inspection rights

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

Last 8 digits of VIN:
- Must appear on envelope
- Must appear in delivery address area

==================================================
SALE DATE RULE
==================================================

If Notice of Claim of Lien includes:
- Sale date
- Time
- Location

THEN:
- Separate Notice of Sale not required

==================================================
INSPECTION RIGHTS
==================================================

Interested parties:
- May inspect vehicle

Lienor must:
- Allow inspection within 3 business days

==================================================
PERSONAL PROPERTY RULE
==================================================

Lienor must release:
- Personal property not attached to vehicle

==================================================
GOOD FAITH NOTICE
==================================================

If owner/lienholder cannot be located:
- Law enforcement notification required

Must include:
- VIN
- Vehicle description
- Good faith effort statement

==================================================
GOOD FAITH EFFORT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required checks:
- FLHSMV database
- NMVTIS
- Vehicle tags
- Stickers/decals
- Interior papers/documents

==================================================
LAW ENFORCEMENT BLOCK RULE
==================================================

If Law Enforcement Block exists:
- Notice sent to FLHSMV DMS Law Enforcement Block section

==================================================
PRIVACY ACT BLOCK RULE
==================================================

If Privacy Act Block exists:
- Address may be obtained through tax collector office

==================================================
APPROVED THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
==================================================

Approved providers:
- Auto Data Direct (ADD123)
- Beacon Software / TowLien

Third-party service handles:
- Notice generation
- Certified mail
- Tracking
- NMVTIS lookup
- FLHSMV reporting

==================================================
NEWSPAPER AD REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Advertisement required:
- At least 15 days before sale

Must contain:
- Sale date/time/location
- Vehicle description
- Amount owed
- Lien statement
- Hearing rights
- Bond rights

==================================================
CERTIFICATE OF COMPLIANCE
==================================================

Must be:
- Filed with Clerk of Court
- Filed BEFORE sale

Requires:
- Notice copies
- Proof of mailing
- Publication proof
- NMVTIS checks

==================================================
REPORT OF SALE
==================================================

Filed AFTER sale.

Must include:
- Purchaser
- Sale price
- Amount retained
- Vehicle description
- Hearing information

==================================================
PUBLIC SALE RULES
==================================================

Sale:
- Public sale only
- Cash payment required

Purchaser receives:
- Clear title free of liens

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RULE
==================================================

Dealer purchaser:
- MUST title vehicle in dealership name before resale

==================================================
IF VEHICLE DOES NOT SELL
==================================================

If unsold:
- Repair shop must title vehicle in shop name

==================================================
TITLE APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required documents:
- HSMV 82040
- Repair estimate
- Notice of Claim of Lien
- Notice of Sale
- Newspaper publication
- Certificate of Compliance
- Report of Sale
- FDACS registration certificate
- Bill of sale
- Identification
- Fees/taxes

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle previously titled out of state:
- Confidential VIN inspection required

==================================================
UNKNOWN TITLE STATE RULE
==================================================

If title state unknown:
- Good Faith Notice required
- NMVTIS proof required

==================================================
IMPORTANT TITLE COMMENTS
==================================================

If lienor obtaining title:
- "Obtained per s. 713.585, F.S." required

==================================================
REJECTION CONDITIONS
==================================================

Transaction rejected if:
- Missing documents
- Illegible documents
- Incorrect notice timing
- Invalid publication
- Missing signatures
- Invalid repair registration
- Incorrect advertisement county
- Improper mailing dates

==================================================
CRITICAL TIMING FAILURES
==================================================

Notice mailed late:
- Storage charges limited

Notice errors:
- Re-mail required

Major defects:
- Entire process restarted

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Was repair shop properly registered?
2. Were notices timely mailed?
3. Was certified mail used?
4. Was newspaper publication timely?
5. Was 60-day waiting period met?
6. Was Certificate of Compliance filed?
7. Was Report of Sale filed?
8. Were all lienholders notified?
9. Was NMVTIS checked?
10. Was law enforcement notified if owner unknown?
11. Was sale conducted properly?
12. Is title application complete?
