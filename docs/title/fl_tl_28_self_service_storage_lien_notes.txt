FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — SELF-SERVICE STORAGE FACILITY LIEN
PROCEDURE: TL-28
CATEGORY: STORAGE LIEN / SELF-STORAGE LIEN / TITLE TRANSFER
RELATED STATUTES:
- 83.805 F.S.
- 83.806 F.S.
- 50.011 F.S.
- 50.031 F.S.

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure explains:
- Self-storage facility liens
- Self-contained storage unit liens
- Sale of vehicles/vessels from storage units
- Transfer of title after storage lien sale
- Required notices
- Auction/publication requirements
- Online lien sale procedures

Applies when:
- Property stored in self-storage facility
- Charges unpaid
- Facility owner enforces lien
- Vehicle or vessel sold

==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

SELF-SERVICE STORAGE FACILITY:
Property designed for renting/leasing storage space.

OWNER:
Owner/operator/lessor/sublessor of storage facility.

TENANT:
Person entitled to use storage space.

RENTAL AGREEMENT:
Agreement governing storage occupancy/use.

SELF-CONTAINED STORAGE UNIT:
Unit at least 600 cubic feet used primarily for storage.

==================================================
GENERAL PROCESS FLOW
==================================================

1. Tenant defaults on payment
2. Facility sends notice
3. Waiting period expires
4. Advertisement published/posted
5. Auction held
6. Purchaser applies for title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice must be:
- Personally delivered
OR
- Sent by certified mail

Notice must go to:
- Tenant
- Lienholders

Addresses:
- From Florida records
OR
- Out-of-state records

==================================================
NOTICE CONTENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice MUST include:

1. Itemized storage claim
2. Amount due
3. Date amount became due
4. Property description
5. Payment demand
6. Deadline for payment
7. Sale warning
8. Sale time/place
9. Facility contact information
10. Phone number
11. Facility address

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAYMENT DEADLINE RULE
==================================================

Notice must allow:
- Minimum 14 days after delivery

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE WARNING RULE
==================================================

Notice MUST clearly state:
- Property will be advertised
- Property will be sold/disposed
- Sale date/time/location

==================================================
MAIL PROOF REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If mailed:
Must submit:
- Original certified mail receipt
OR
- Certified copy
OR
- Unclaimed unopened certified letter

==================================================
HAND DELIVERY RULE
==================================================

If notice hand-delivered:
Need:
- Notarized affidavit
OR
- Perjury clause affidavit

==================================================
RENTAL AGREEMENT REQUIREMENT
==================================================

Must submit:
- Copy of rental agreement

==================================================
PUBLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Advertisement must:
- Publish once weekly
- 2 consecutive weeks
- Newspaper of general circulation

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PUBLICATION TIMING RULE
==================================================

Advertisement must publish:
- At least 15 calendar days before sale

DO NOT include:
- Advertisement date
- Sale date

==================================================
NO NEWSPAPER RULE
==================================================

If no newspaper exists:
Advertisement must be:
- Posted publicly

Posting requirements:
- Minimum 3 conspicuous places
- Neighborhood of facility

==================================================
POSTED NOTICE TIMING RULE
==================================================

Posting must occur:
- Minimum 10 calendar days before sale

DO NOT include:
- Posting date
- Sale date

==================================================
ADVERTISEMENT CONTENT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Advertisement must include:
- General property description
- Facility address
- Tenant name
- Sale time
- Sale place
- Sale manner
- Publication/posting dates

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE TIMING RULE
==================================================

Sale may occur:
- No sooner than 15 days after first publication

==================================================
ONLINE AUCTION RULES
==================================================

Florida law allows:
- Public website auctions

Applies to:
- Vehicles
- Watercraft

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ONLINE SALE CONDITIONS
==================================================

Online sale allowed when:
- Balance unpaid for 60 days after due date

==================================================
PROPERTY VALUE LIMIT RULE
==================================================

If rental agreement limits property value:
- That amount becomes maximum stored value

==================================================
ONLINE AUCTION LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Storage facility owner:
- DOES NOT need auction license
for online sale posting

==================================================
POST-SALE RULES
==================================================

Facility owner may:
- Sell property
OR
- Tow property

==================================================
BILL OF SALE REQUIREMENT
==================================================

Good-faith purchaser must receive:
- Bill of sale

==================================================
TOWING RULE
==================================================

If vehicle towed:
- Storage owner not liable after tow company possession

Tow company:
- Must comply with all notification/sale rules

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle titled out of state:
Additional documentation required.

==================================================
VIN/HIN VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN/HIN verification on title form
OR
- VIN/HIN affidavit

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

Verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel recreational trailers

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE OWNERSHIP PROOF
==================================================

Acceptable proof:
- State printout
- Official state letter
- Copy of out-of-state title
- Law enforcement teletype

Must verify:
- Ownership
- Lien status

==================================================
UNKNOWN STATE RULE
==================================================

If previous state unknown:
Need affidavit stating:
- State unknown
- No plate on vehicle when stored

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REDEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Before sale:
Tenant may:
- Pay lien
- Pay expenses
- Redeem property

Upon payment:
- Property returned to tenant

==================================================
PROCEEDS RULE
==================================================

After lien satisfied:
Remaining balance:
- Held for tenant

==================================================
ABANDONED PROCEEDS RULE
==================================================

If proceeds unclaimed:
- After 2 years
- Considered abandoned

Facility owner:
- No further obligation

==================================================
OPERATION OF LAW TITLE RULE
==================================================

If purchaser buys vehicle without negotiable title:
- Purchaser MUST title vehicle in own name

==================================================

- Vessels

- Mobile homes

IMPORTANT FORM CHANGE NOTES
==================================================

As of 2023:
HSMV 82040 split into:
- MV
- VS
- MH versions

==================================================
TIMELINE LOGIC
==================================================

DAY 1:
- Notice sent to tenant/lienholders

DAY 15 OR AFTER:
- Publication/posting stage

IF NEWSPAPER:
- Publish once weekly
- 2 consecutive weeks
- Sale 15 days after first publication

IF NO NEWSPAPER:
- Post in 3 visible places
- Sale 10 days after posting

12. Purchaser must retitle?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing rental agreement
- Missing certified mail proof
- Sale held too early
- Missing tenant name in advertisement
- Missing publication dates
- Wrong publication timing
- Missing VIN verification
- Missing ownership verification
- Missing bill of sale
- Improper online auction assumptions

==================================================
IMPORTANT OPERATIONAL NOTES
==================================================

Storage lien titles are:
- Extremely timeline-sensitive
- Documentation-sensitive
- Notice-sensitive

Most common rejection reasons:
- Improper publication timing
- Missing notice proof
- Missing rental agreement
- Missing ownership verification
- Missing VIN verification
