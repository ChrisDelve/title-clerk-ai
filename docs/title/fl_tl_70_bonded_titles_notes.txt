VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Bonded title:
Used when applicant:
Cannot provide:
Assigned title certificate.

==================================================
PROCESS FOR BONDED TITLE ISSUANCE
==================================================

Upon receiving bonded title application:
1. Review all documents immediately
2. Verify signatures and affidavits
3. Reject incomplete applications
4. Scan all documents clearly
5. Send documents to BMSS
6. Do NOT collect payment initially
7. Contact customer within 5 business days

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BMSS RULE
==================================================

Bonded title documents:
Must be emailed to:
MSSG@flhsmv.gov

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAYMENT RULE
==================================================

Do NOT collect payment:
Before FLHSMV review approval.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN STOP RULE
==================================================

Rejected bonded title VIN:
Receives stop:
"INFO23-010 Contact (850) 617-3174"

==================================================
VEHICLES ELIGIBLE FOR BONDED TITLE
==================================================

Eligible vehicles:
- Automobiles
- Trucks under 8,000 lbs net weight
- Motorcycles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIVATE SALE RULE
==================================================

Bonded titles:
Only for:
Private casual sales:
Where money exchanged.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLORIDA TITLE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle must have:
Current Florida paper title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO LIEN RULE
==================================================

Vehicle must have:
NO:
- Security interest
- Liens
- Encumbrances

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MODEL YEAR RULE
==================================================

Eligible vehicles:
2010 model year:
Through rolling maximum year table.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEDERAL ODOMETER RULE
==================================================

Eligibility tied to:
Federal odometer disclosure changes:
Effective:
01/01/2021.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOT ELIGIBLE RULE
==================================================

Bonded titles NOT allowed for:
- Electronic titles
- 2011 or newer vehicles
- Vehicles older than allowable range
- Out-of-state titled vehicles
- NMVTIS non-Florida records
- Vehicles with liens
- Vehicles adding liens
- Vessels
- Mobile homes
- RVs
- Off-highway vehicles
- Trucks 8,000 lbs or more

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRANSACTION TYPE RULE
==================================================

Bonded titles NOT allowed for:
- Inheritance
- Gift
- Divorce
- Transfer between spouses
- Even trade
- Trade down

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TL-07 RULE
==================================================

If bonded title ineligible:
Refer customer:
To TL-07.

==================================================
APPLICANT REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Applicant must be:
- Florida resident
OR
- Florida based business.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE IDENTIFICATION
==================================================

Acceptable ID includes:
- Driver license
- Identification card
- Passport
- Passport card
- Active Florida business verification

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BUSINESS RULE
==================================================

Business must:
- Be active in SunBiz
OR
- Have verifiable active local business license.

TITLE RECORD REVIEW

Must show:

Current owner.

Current owner in system.

- Seller name mismatch
- Liens exist

- Encumbrances exist

HSMV 82026 value.

LIEN RULES
==================================================

If purchaser owes balance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FINANCING RULE
==================================================

No financing allowed:
On bonded title issuance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT POST-ISSUANCE LIEN RULE
==================================================

Liens cannot be added:
After bonded title issued.

==================================================
KELLEY BLUE BOOK RULES
==================================================

FLHSMV selected:
Kelley Blue Book:
For all bonded title valuations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SCREENSHOT RULE
==================================================

Must be clear and legible.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT KBB DATA RULE
==================================================

KBB valuation must include:
- ZIP code
- Mileage
- Options
- Color
- Condition

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax collected:
On:
- Current retail value
OR
- Valid bill of sale amount

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE RULE
==================================================

Valid bill of sale requires:
- Vehicle description
- Purchase date
- Buyer signature
- Seller signature
- Selling price

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 80% RULE
==================================================

If sale price:
Less than 80% of retail value:
Department of Revenue review possible.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GIFT RULE
==================================================

Gift transactions:
NOT eligible:
For bonded title.

==================================================
BONDED TITLE BRAND REMOVAL
==================================================

BT brand:
After 3 years.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HISTORY RULE
==================================================

System retains:
Bonded title history:
Even after brand removed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DUPLICATE TITLE RULE
==================================================

Customer may request:
Duplicate clean title.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE BONDED TITLE RULES
==================================================

NOT carried forward:
To Florida title.

VERY IMPORTANT COURT ORDER RULE
==================================================

Florida court order.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

A bonded title:
Allows issuance of a Florida title
when ownership exists
but properly assigned title documentation is unavailable,
provided strict eligibility,
surety bond,
and valuation requirements are met.
