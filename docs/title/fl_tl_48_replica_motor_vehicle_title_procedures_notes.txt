==================================================

Applies to:
- Replica automobiles
- Replica trucks
- Replica motorcycles
- Reproduction vehicles
- Newly manufactured look-alike classic vehicles

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLICA DEFINITION
==================================================

Replica:
A completely NEW motor vehicle
manufactured to:
- Look like an older vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLICA RULE
==================================================

Replica vehicles:
Are NOT:
- Assembled from kits.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGIONAL OFFICE RULE
==================================================

Replica vehicles:
MUST NOT be titled
in:
- Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPRODUCTION RULE
==================================================

Replica vehicles:
May also be called:
- Reproductions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NHTSA RULE
==================================================

Replica manufacturer:
MUST be registered with:
- National Highway Traffic Safety Administration.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SUNBIZ RULE
==================================================

Replica manufacturer:
MUST register business with:
- Florida Division of Corporations (Sunbiz).

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MANUFACTURER LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Replica manufacturer:
MUST meet:
- Manufacturer licensing requirements

Per:
- 320.63 FS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLICA BRAND RULE
==================================================

Replica vehicles:
Will receive:
- "Replica" brand.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BRAND PERMANENCY RULE
==================================================

Replica brand:
Carries forward:
- For life of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT YEAR RULE
==================================================

Title "YR":
Will reflect:
- Year vehicle resembles.

NOT:
- Actual manufacturing year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENTS FIELD RULE
==================================================

Actual manufacture year:
MUST be entered in:
- Comments description field
In issuance system.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MAKE RULE
==================================================

"Make":
Will be:
- Make shown on MCO.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

Identification number:
Will be:
- VIN from MCO.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BODY TYPE RULE
==================================================

Body:
Will be:
- Body type shown on MCO.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PREVIOUS STATE RULE
==================================================

Title:
Will show:
- "N" for new

In:
- Previous state field.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WHOLESALE/RETAIL RULE
==================================================

Licensed replica manufacturers:
May:
- Retail through own franchise dealer license
OR
- Wholesale through authorized franchised dealers.

==================================================
DOCUMENTATION REQUIRED
==================================================

Required documents:
- Manufacturer's Certificate of Origin
- HSMV 82040
- Sales tax information
- Plate or non-use affidavit
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MCO RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Manufacturer's Certificate of Origin

MUST be:
- Properly assigned to purchaser.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida sales tax
OR
- Sales tax information listed on HSMV 82040.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Florida or out-of-state title:
Issued WITHOUT replica brand:
Must be referred to:
- DMS Regional Office

For:
- Physical inspection.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE BRANDING RULE
==================================================

Replica vehicles:
MUST remain branded:
- Replica

On:
- Initial title
- All future titles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DIFFERENCE FROM ASSEMBLED VEHICLES
==================================================

Replica vehicle:
- Completely new vehicle
- Manufactured to resemble older vehicle

NOT:
- Homemade vehicle
- Assembled from parts
- Traditional kit car build

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is vehicle a replica?
2. Is vehicle completely new?
3. Does it resemble older vehicle?
4. Is manufacturer licensed?
5. Is manufacturer NHTSA registered?
6. Is manufacturer registered with Sunbiz?
7. Is proper MCO provided?
8. Is MCO assigned correctly?
9. Is vehicle branded replica?
10. Does title need inspection?
11. Is title already improperly issued?
12. Is actual manufacture year entered in comments?
13. Is correct year displayed on title?
14. Is correct VIN used?
15. Is correct body type used?
16. Is sales tax documented?
17. Is plate/non-use affidavit included?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Confusing replica with assembled-from-parts vehicle
- Using actual manufacture year as title year
- Forgetting replica brand
- Missing comments description year
- Using incorrect VIN source
- Failing to inspect improperly branded title
- Missing manufacturer licensing verification
- Missing MCO assignment
- Treating replica like rebuilt vehicle
- Sending replica title to regional office incorrectly

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

Replica title displays:
- Replica year appearance
- MCO make
- MCO VIN
- MCO body type
- "N" previous state indicator
