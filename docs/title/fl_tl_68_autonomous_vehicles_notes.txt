==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

AUTOMATED DRIVING SYSTEM:
Hardware and software capable of performing dynamic driving task on sustained basis.

AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE:
Vehicle equipped with automated driving system.

DYNAMIC DRIVING TASK:
Real-time operational and tactical functions required to operate vehicle in traffic.

FULLY AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE:
Vehicle designed to function without human operator.

OPERATIONAL DESIGN DOMAIN:
Specific operating conditions under which automated driving system functions properly.

MINIMAL RISK CONDITION:
Safe condition such as:
- Complete stop
- Hazard lamps activated

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEDERAL CERTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Autonomous vehicle:
Must comply with:
49 CFR Part 567:
When required by federal law.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CERTIFICATION LABEL RULE
==================================================

Vehicle must bear:
Required certification labels.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAFFIC LAW RULE
==================================================

Autonomous vehicle:
Must be capable of operating:
In compliance with:
Florida traffic laws.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HUMAN OPERATOR RULE
==================================================

If vehicle is NOT fully autonomous:
System must:
- Alert licensed human operator
OR
- Achieve minimal risk condition.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FAILURE ALERT RULE
==================================================

Vehicle must safely alert:
Human operator:
If automated driving system fails.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FULLY AUTONOMOUS RULE
==================================================

Fully autonomous vehicle:
May operate:
Without human operator physically present.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FULLY AUTONOMOUS FAILURE RULE
==================================================

Fully autonomous vehicle:
Must achieve:
Minimal risk condition:
If automated driving system fails.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FEDERAL PREEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Federal NHTSA regulations:
Supersede Florida law:
If conflict exists.

==================================================
GENERAL INFORMATION
==================================================

Fully autonomous vehicles:
May operate on Florida highways.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSED OPERATOR RULE
==================================================

Licensed human operator:
NOT required:
For fully autonomous vehicle operation.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TESTING RULE
==================================================

Autonomous technology testing:
Allowed on Florida roads.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TESTING PERSONNEL RULE
==================================================

Testing may be conducted by:
- Employees
- Contractors
- Manufacturer-designated persons
- Research organizations

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION RULE
==================================================

Research organizations:
May test autonomous vehicles:
If associated with accredited educational institutions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEST DRIVER RULE
==================================================

During testing:
Human operator must be present:
To monitor and intervene:
Unless testing on closed course.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MANUFACTURER LIABILITY RULE
==================================================

Original manufacturer:
Not liable:
For defects caused by:
Third-party autonomous conversion.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORIGINAL DEFECT RULE
==================================================

Original manufacturer:
Still liable:
If defect existed before conversion.

==================================================
APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Autonomous vehicles:
Processed locally:
At tax collector offices and LPAs.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO MAILING RULE
==================================================

AV applications:
No longer mailed:
To FLHSMV for processing.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BRAND RULE
==================================================

Autonomous vehicle brand:
Must be checked:
On HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTOR VEHICLE ISSUANCE RULE
==================================================

Autonomous brand:
Must be entered:
Into motor vehicle issuance system.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE BRAND RULE
==================================================

Autonomous brand:
Will print:
On title record.

==================================================
INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Fully autonomous vehicles:
Operating on:
On-demand autonomous vehicle network
OR
Prearranged ride:
Require enhanced insurance.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIABILITY COVERAGE RULE
==================================================

Required primary liability coverage:
Minimum:
$1 million.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COVERAGE RULE
==================================================

Coverage must include:
- Death
- Bodily injury
- Property damage

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PIP RULE
==================================================

Must carry:
Personal Injury Protection (PIP)
Meeting minimum coverage requirements.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UM/UIM RULE
==================================================

Must carry:
- Uninsured motorist coverage
- Underinsured motorist coverage

==================================================
TITLE FEES RULE
==================================================

Standard title fees apply.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS RULES
==================================================

All AV title and registration transactions:
May be processed:
At tax collector office or LPA.

==================================================
MYDMV PORTAL RULE
==================================================

Allowed online transactions:
- Duplicate Registration
- Print Electronic Title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SYSTEM REMOVAL RULE
==================================================

If automated driving system removed:
Owner may apply:
To remove autonomous brand.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRIOR AUTONOMOUS RULE
==================================================

After autonomous system removed:
"PRIOR AUTONOMOUS" brand added.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BRAND ERROR RULE
==================================================

Prior autonomous brand:
NOT added:
If original autonomous brand:
Applied in error.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNERSHIP CHANGE RULE
==================================================

Until autonomous system removed:
Ownership changes:
Must be performed:
At Department.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NMVTIS RULE
==================================================

NMVTIS:
Will NOT display:
Autonomous brand.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE RULE
==================================================

Registration certificate prints:
"AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LAW ENFORCEMENT RULE
==================================================

Registration wording:
Allows law enforcement:
To identify autonomous vehicles.

==================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Required:
1. HSMV 82040 MV
2. Proof of insurance
3. Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Insurance verification must confirm:
- $1 million liability coverage
- PIP coverage
- UM/UIM coverage
