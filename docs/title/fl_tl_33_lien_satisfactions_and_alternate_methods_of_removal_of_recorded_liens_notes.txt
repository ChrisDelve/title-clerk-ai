FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — LIEN SATISFACTIONS AND ALTERNATE METHODS OF REMOVAL OF RECORDED LIENS
PROCEDURE: TL-33
CATEGORY: LIEN SATISFACTIONS / LIEN REMOVAL / ELT

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure explains:
- Lien satisfactions
- Removing liens from Florida titles
- Alternate methods of lien removal
- ELT lien releases
- Paper lien releases
- Judgment lien handling
- Lienholder stamp/perforation rules
- Certified mail requirements
- Paid-in-full proof requirements

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Vessels

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

When lien paid:
Owner may demand:
- Satisfaction of lien
from lienholder.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 10-DAY RULE
==================================================

Lienholder must:
- Forward title/satisfaction
within:
- 10 days of payoff

==================================================
IMPORTANT ELT RULE
==================================================

If lien electronic:
Lien MUST be released:
- Electronically

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELT WARNING
==================================================

FLHSMV CANNOT manually release:
- Electronic liens

Even if:
- Customer provides paper satisfaction

==================================================
WHEN CUSTOMER PROVIDES PAID TITLE
==================================================

If title shows:
- Paid lien
AND
- No subsequent liens

Then:
Issue clean title.

==================================================
PROCESSING RULES
==================================================

If liens remain on file:
Process as:
- Lien Add/Delete/Reassign

If no liens remain:
Process as:
- Title Correction

==================================================
MERGER / NAME CHANGE RULE
==================================================

If payoff entity differs from title lienholder:
Need:
- Merger papers
OR
- Corporate charter
unless agency confirms entities same.

==================================================
METHODS OF LIEN REMOVAL
==================================================

Acceptable methods:
- Title satisfaction section
- HSMV 82260
- ELT release
- Approved stamp
- Approved perforation

==================================================
HSMV 82260 OVERVIEW
==================================================

Form:
- Lien Satisfaction

Used to:
- Release recorded lien

==================================================
SUBSEQUENT LIEN RULE
==================================================

If title shows:
- Additional lien(s)

Then:
First lienholder must:
- Send title to FLHSMV
within 10 days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELT EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

When satisfying/updating:
- Existing non-ELT

Must select:
- ELT exception reason

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CUSTOMER WARNING
==================================================

If customer provides:
- HSMV 82260
for active ELT lien

Reject transaction.

==================================================
LIENHOLDER STAMP RULES
==================================================

Approved lienholder stamp/perforation must show:
- Lienholder name
- Word "PAID"
- Satisfaction date

==================================================
PERMIT NUMBER STAMP RULE
==================================================

If permit number assigned:
Stamp must include:
- Permit number

==================================================
NO-PERMIT STAMP RULE
==================================================

If no permit number:
Stamp must include:
- Authorized signature

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MULTIPLE LIEN RULE
==================================================

If same lienholder holds:
- Multiple liens

Do NOT use:
- PAID stamp
OR
- Perforation

Because:
- Could remove ALL liens.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state lienholder letter acceptable if:
- Original
- On letterhead
- Includes vehicle info

No notarization required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COPY RULE
==================================================

NO:
- Email copies
- Fax copies
- Photocopies

Accepted for lien satisfaction.

Must be:
- Original
OR
- Certified copy

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE RULE
==================================================

If out-of-state title shows:
- No liens

AND old Florida paper title shows lien:
Florida title may issue:
- Without lien satisfaction

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELT CONFLICT RULE
==================================================

If Florida record shows:
- Active electronic lien

STOP.

Contact:
- Field Support Center

==================================================
ALTERNATE METHOD OF LIEN REMOVAL
==================================================

Used when:
- Lien paid
AND
- Lienholder unavailable/unresponsive

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALTERNATE METHOD RULE
==================================================

Electronic liens:
CANNOT normally be removed:
Using alternate method.

==================================================
ALTERNATE METHOD REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82040 MV/MH/VS
OR
- HSMV 82101
- Florida title if available
- Certified mail proof
- Payoff proof
- Title fees

==================================================
CERTIFIED MAIL REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Need:
- Returned unopened certified mail
OR
- Signed receipt card
OR
- Delivery proof

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 20-DAY RULE
==================================================

Certified letter must be mailed:
- At least 20 days before application

==================================================
UNOPENED ENVELOPE RULE
==================================================

Processing clerk must:
- Open returned envelope
- Verify contents
- Initial envelope
- Date envelope

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE PAYOFF PROOF
==================================================

Acceptable:
- Paid sales contract
- Canceled checks
- Signed receipts
- Lien satisfaction proof

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 5-YEAR RULE
==================================================

If:
- No sales contract
AND
- No payoff record

Owner may need:
- Wait until lien over 5 years old
OR
- Obtain court order

==================================================
JUDGMENT LIEN CERTIFICATE RULES
==================================================

If second Judgment Lien Certificate submitted:
For:
- Same lienor
- Same owner
- Same vehicle/vessel

Then:
- Remove first judgment lien
- Record second judgment lien

==================================================
JUDGMENT LIEN PROCESS
==================================================

Steps:
1. Verify TL-32 requirements
2. Locate first judgment lien
3. Delete original lien
4. Add new judgment lien

==================================================
ASSIGNMENT OF LIEN RULE
==================================================

If lien sold to another lender:
Need:
- Assignment contract
OR
- HSMV 82139

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SIGNATURE RULE
==================================================

Computer-generated signatures:
- NOT acceptable

==================================================
CERTIFIED COPY RULE
==================================================

Acceptable:
- Original HSMV 82260
- Certified copy

NOT acceptable:
- Fax
- Email
- Photocopy

==================================================
DECEASED LIENHOLDER RULE
==================================================

If lienholder deceased:
Refer to:
- TL-18

==================================================
NEW HSMV 82040 FORM RULES
==================================================

HSMV 82040 split into:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82040 MH

==================================================
FORM TYPE LOGIC
==================================================

HSMV 82040 MV:
Motor vehicles

HSMV 82040 VS:
Vessels

HSMV 82040 MH:
Mobile homes

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 2024 RULE
==================================================

Effective January 1, 2024:
Old combined HSMV 82040:
No longer accepted for:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes

==================================================
DEFINITIONS
==================================================

ELT:
Electronic lien title

ELT PROVIDER:
Third-party ELT service company

LIENHOLDER:
Entity loaning money secured by vehicle

LIEN SATISFACTION:
Proof lien paid

SUBSEQUENT LIEN:
Additional lien while first lien still active

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Paper lien or ELT lien?
2. Initial lien or subsequent lien?
3. Proper lien release provided?
4. HSMV 82260 required?
5. Alternate method applicable?
6. Certified mail proof included?
7. Payoff proof included?
8. 20-day requirement met?
9. Active electronic lien conflict?
10. Merger papers needed?
11. Multiple liens involved?
12. Judgment lien involved?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Accepting faxed lien satisfaction
- Accepting emailed release
- Attempting manual ELT release
- Missing certified mail proof
- Missing 20-day waiting period
- Missing payoff proof
- Using PAID stamp incorrectly
- Forgetting merger papers
- Incorrect title transaction type
- Removing wrong judgment lien

==================================================
IMPORTANT OPERATIONAL NOTES
==================================================

This procedure is:
- Extremely documentation sensitive
- Highly dependent on ELT status
- Frequently rejected for copy/fax issues

Most common rejection reasons:
- Non-original lien satisfaction
- Active ELT conflicts
- Missing certified mail proof
- Missing payoff evidence
- Missing signatures
- Wrong processing type
