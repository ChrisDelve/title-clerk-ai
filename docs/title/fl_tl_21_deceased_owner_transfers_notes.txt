TITLE:\
FLHSMV TL-21 \'97 Deceased Owner Transfer Procedures\
\
CATEGORY:\
Deceased Owners\
Estate Transfers\
Survivorship\
Probate\
Ownership Transfers\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Transfers involving deceased owners require heightened ownership and legal authority verification before title processing.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Deceased owner status\
- Ownership conjunction\
- Death certificate if required\
- Probate/legal documentation\
- Surviving owner information\
- VIN consistency\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Ownership unclear\
- Probate issues exist\
- Legal authority unclear\
- Fraud concerns arise\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Ownership conjunction significantly impacts deceased owner transfer processing.\
\
RULE:\
If ownership states OR:\
\uc0\u8594  Surviving owner may have transfer authority depending on title wording and applicable procedures.\
\
RULE:\
If ownership states AND:\
\uc0\u8594  Additional estate/probate documentation may be required.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Exact title conjunction wording\
- Current ownership records\
- Supporting legal documentation\
\
COMMON MISTAKE:\
Assuming surviving owner automatically gains transfer authority in all situations.\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Death certificates may be required to support transfer processing involving deceased owners.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Correct deceased owner name\
- Supporting ownership documentation\
- Legible/acceptable documentation\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Death certificate missing\
- Name mismatch exists\
- Multiple deceased owners involved\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Probate or estate administration documents may be required depending on ownership structure.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Letters of administration\
- Court documents\
- Estate authority\
- Executor/personal representative information\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Probate unresolved\
- Court authority unclear\
- Multiple heirs involved\
- Ownership disputes exist\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Executor, administrator, or personal representative authority must be verified before transfer processing.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Court appointment documents\
- Signature authority\
- Supporting estate paperwork\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Authority unclear\
- Missing court documentation\
- Signature mismatch exists\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
VIN must match ALL title and estate documentation exactly.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Existing title VIN\
- System VIN\
- Supporting paperwork VIN\
- Court/legal document VIN if applicable\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Any VIN discrepancy exists\
- Altered VIN suspected\
- VIN unreadable/incomplete\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Lienholder/title status must still be verified during deceased owner transfers.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Existing lienholder\
- ELT status\
- Lien release status\
- Supporting lender documentation\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Active lien unresolved\
- Missing lien release\
- Electronic title complications\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Trust/entity ownership involving deceased individuals requires additional legal review.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Trust documentation\
- Entity ownership structure\
- Legal authority\
- Supporting estate documentation\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Trust authority unclear\
- Ownership conflict exists\
- Legal documentation incomplete\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Out-of-state titles involving deceased owners may require additional verification and legal review.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Prior state title\
- Estate/probate documentation\
- Ownership consistency\
- VIN consistency\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- State probate requirements unclear\
- Ownership conflict exists\
- Out-of-state documentation incomplete\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
COMMON DEALERSHIP MISTAKES:\
- Missing probate documentation\
- Incorrect conjunction interpretation\
- Missing death certificate\
- Assuming surviving spouse automatically has authority\
- VIN mismatch\
- Missing executor authority\
- Failure to verify lienholder status\
- Incomplete estate paperwork\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
HIGH-RISK ESCALATION SITUATIONS:\
- Ownership disputes\
- Probate conflicts\
- Multiple heirs\
- Fraud concerns\
- Trust/entity ownership complications\
- Missing legal authority\
- VIN discrepancies\
- Active liens unresolved\
- Out-of-state estate complications\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
BEST PRACTICE:\
Before processing deceased owner transfer:\
1. Verify ownership conjunction carefully.\
2. Verify legal authority for transfer.\
3. Verify probate/estate documentation.\
4. Verify lienholder/title status.\
5. Verify VIN consistency.\
6. Escalate any ownership or legal uncertainty immediately.\
\
