FLORIDA TITLE & LIEN PROCEDURE
DUPLICATE AND LOST IN TRANSIT WITH TRANSFER
SOURCE: TL-12
REVISION DATE: 07/07/2020

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Duplicate title with transfer
- Lost in transit title transfers
- Duplicate salvage transfers
- Missing title transfer situations
- Insurance company salvage transfers

==================================================
CORE RULE
==================================================

Duplicate with transfer:
Allows ownership transfer
when seller cannot locate title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Buyer AND seller:
Must BOTH be present.

Valid identification required.

==================================================
POWER OF ATTORNEY RULE
==================================================

Power of attorney:
NOT allowed
for standard duplicate with transfer.

==================================================
EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Insurance company total loss:
May use proper POA.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ELECTRONIC TITLE RULE
==================================================

If title status is electronic:
Duplicate with transfer CANNOT be processed.

==================================================
DUPLICATE WITH TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

When title lost:
Use:
HSMV 82101.

==================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS RULE
==================================================

Required:
- HSMV 82101
- Buyer and seller signatures
- Lien satisfactions
- Tax information
- Plate or non-use affidavit
- Fees

==================================================
DEALER RULE
==================================================

Dealer as seller:
Still considered seller.

Dealer must comply fully.

==================================================
HSMV 82994 RULE
==================================================

Dealer handling transfer:
Must complete HSMV 82994
when applicable.

==================================================
LOST IN TRANSIT RULE
==================================================

Lost in transit applies:
- More than 20 days
- Less than 180 days
since duplicate issued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Lost in transit:
Processed as no-fee fast title.

==================================================
SALVAGE TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Insurance company total loss:
Requires:
- HSMV 82101
- HSMV 82363
- Lien satisfactions
- Non-use affidavit
- Fees

==================================================
VESSEL RULE
==================================================

Salvage section:
Does NOT apply to vessels.

==================================================
NON-USE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicles:
May use non-use affidavit.

Mobile homes:
Cannot use non-use affidavit
except insurance situations.

==================================================
VESSEL NON-USE RULE
==================================================

Vessel non-use affidavit:
Requires non-use registration issuance.

==================================================
LIEN RULE
==================================================

All liens:
Must be satisfied
before duplicate transfer.

==================================================
FEE RULE
==================================================

Duplicate with transfer:
Requires:
- Duplicate fee
- Transfer fee
- System service fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

If seller not present:
Seller must obtain duplicate title FIRST
before transferring ownership.

==================================================
HIGH-RISK ERROR AREAS
==================================================

MOST COMMON ERRORS:
- Attempting process on electronic title
- Missing seller presence
- Using POA improperly
- Missing lien satisfactions
- Wrong lost-in-transit timeline
- Missing HSMV 82363
- Missing non-use affidavit
- Incorrect insurance handling
- Wrong fee collection

==================================================
WORKFLOW BEST PRACTICE
==================================================

Before processing:

1. Verify title is NOT electronic
2. Verify buyer present
3. Verify seller present
4. Verify identification
5. Verify lien satisfaction
6. Verify proper forms
7. Verify insurance status
8. Verify timeline for lost-in-transit
9. Verify tax collection
10. Verify fees
