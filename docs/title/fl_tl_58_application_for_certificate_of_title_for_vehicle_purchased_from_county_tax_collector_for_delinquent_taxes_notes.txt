FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — APPLICATION FOR CERTIFICATE OF TITLE FOR A MOTOR VEHICLE OR MOBILE HOME PURCHASED FROM A COUNTY TAX COLLECTOR FOR DELINQUENT TAXES
PROCEDURE: TL-58
CATEGORY: DELINQUENT TAX SALES / OPERATION OF LAW / COURT-ORDERED TITLE TRANSFERS

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Titles issued after tax collector sales
- Delinquent tax seizure sales
- Court-ordered levy and seizure
- Operation of law title transfers
- Tax deed related title processing
- Mobile home tax sale titles
- Delinquent property title issuance

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Court-ordered tax seizure sales

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DELINQUENT TAX RULE
==================================================

Florida law allows:
- Advertisement
- Levy
- Seizure
- Sale

Of personal property:
For:
- Delinquent taxes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COURT ORDER RULE
==================================================

Tax collector:
Must apply to:
- Circuit court

For:
- Order directing levy and seizure.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PUBLIC SALE RULE
==================================================

After seizure:
Property sold:
- At public sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SURVIVING LIEN RULE
==================================================

Most liens:
Do NOT survive:
- Tax deed issuance.

EXCEPTION:
Governmental liens may survive.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOVERNMENT LIEN RULE
==================================================

Liens held by:
- Municipal government
- County government
- Special district
- Community development district

May survive:
If not satisfied.

- HSMV 82040 VS.

VERY IMPORTANT 2024 MOTOR VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Effective:

Motor vehicle and mobile home titles:

- HSMV 82040 MV
OR

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLED VEHICLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

If vehicle/mobile home:
Currently titled in Florida:

Need:
- HSMV 82040
- Original/certified tax sale bill of sale
- Lien satisfactions if applicable
- Sales tax documentation
- Plate/decal/non-use affidavit
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Original OR certified copy
Of bill of sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE SOURCE RULE
==================================================

Bill of sale issued by:
- Clerk of Court
OR
- Tax collector.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE CONTENT RULE
==================================================

Bill of sale must show:
- Selling price
- Complete vehicle/mobile home description
- Purchaser information

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CHAPTER 197 RULE
==================================================

Bill of sale must state:
Vehicle sold pursuant to:
- Chapter 197 Florida Statutes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

Need lien satisfactions:
ONLY for:
- Governmental unit liens
- Special district liens
- Community development district liens

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OTHER LIEN RULE
==================================================

Other liens:
Generally extinguished by:
- Levy and seizure order.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida sales tax
OR
- Sales tax exemption information.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida license plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

For mobile homes:
Need:
- Mobile home decal
OR
- RP decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-USE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Non-use affidavit:
NOT acceptable:
For:
- Mobile homes.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE OR UNKNOWN STATE RULE
==================================================

If:
- Not titled in Florida
OR
- Previous state unknown

Need:
- VIN verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82042 RULE
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN verification section on HSMV 82040.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

VIN must be verified by:
- Authorized official listed on form.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALTERNATE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Instead of HSMV 82042:
Can use affidavit from:
- Seller
AND
- Purchaser

Verifying:
VIN on paperwork matches vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

Verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 pounds
- Semi-trailers under 2,000 pounds
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel recreation trailers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RESALE RULE
==================================================

Dealer or individual purchasing:
Through operation of law:

MUST obtain title:
In own name
BEFORE:
Reselling vehicle/mobile home.

==================================================
EXHIBIT A CHECKLIST ITEMS
==================================================

Florida record requires:
- HSMV 82040
- Bill of sale
- Lien satisfactions if applicable
- Sales tax documentation
- Plate/decal/non-use affidavit
- Fees

Out-of-state/unknown state requires:
- All above documents
PLUS
- HSMV 82042 or VIN verification.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OPERATION OF LAW RULE
==================================================

Tax collector sales:
Are processed as:
- Operation of law title transfers.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Was vehicle sold for delinquent taxes?
2. Was levy and seizure court ordered?
3. Is vehicle Florida titled?
4. Is previous state unknown?
5. Is VIN verification required?
6. Is proper HSMV form used?
7. Is certified bill of sale included?
8. Does bill of sale reference Chapter 197?
9. Are surviving governmental liens satisfied?
10. Is sales tax documented?
11. Is plate/decal included?
12. Is non-use affidavit improperly used?
13. Is mobile home involved?
14. Is operation of law applicable?
15. Is dealer attempting immediate resale?
16. Are title fees included?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing Chapter 197 statement
- Missing certified bill of sale
- Requiring extinguished liens unnecessarily
- Accepting non-use affidavit for mobile home
- Missing VIN verification
- Allowing dealer resale before titling
- Using wrong HSMV 82040 version
- Forgetting RP decal requirement
- Missing tax exemption information
- Confusing tax sale with repossession

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TAX SALE CONCEPT
==================================================

Tax collector sale:
Transfers ownership:
Through:
- Court-authorized seizure
- Public tax sale
- Operation of law

NOT:
- Traditional reassigned title transfer.
