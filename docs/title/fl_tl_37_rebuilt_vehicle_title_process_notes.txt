FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — REBUILT MOTOR VEHICLE / MOBILE HOME / MOTORCYCLE
PROCEDURE: TL-37
CATEGORY: REBUILT TITLE PROCESSING

==================================================

==================================================

- Rebuilt vehicle title applications
- Salvage rebuildable inspections
- Rebuilt decal issuance
- Rebuilt title branding
- Major component part verification
- PRVIP rebuilt inspections
- Regional office rebuilt inspections
- Flood-damaged rebuilt processing
- ASPT processing
- Rebuilt documentation requirements

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Motorcycles
- Mobile homes
- Dealers
- Rebuilders
- Insurance companies
- PRVIP facilities
- Tax collectors
- License plate agencies

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEGAL RULES
==================================================

NO PERSON may:
- Sell
- Offer for sale
- Exchange

A rebuilt vehicle until:
- Proper rebuilt title application submitted
AND
- Physical inspection completed
AND
- Rebuilt title issued.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REBUILT TITLE BRAND RULE
==================================================

Florida rebuilt title must display:
"REBUILT AND MAY HAVE PREVIOUSLY BEEN DECLARED A TOTAL LOSS VEHICLE DUE TO DAMAGE"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REBUILT DECAL RULE
==================================================

After successful inspection:
- FLHSMV assigns rebuilt decal
- Decal affixed to vehicle
- Decal remains permanently.

Removing rebuilt decal intentionally:
- Third-degree felony.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REBUILT APPLICATION RULE
==================================================

Vehicle MUST already be rebuilt BEFORE:
- Applying for rebuilt title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER RULE
==================================================

If dealer rebuilds salvage rebuildable vehicle:
Dealer MUST:
- Obtain rebuilt title in dealer name
BEFORE:
- Selling vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION RULE
==================================================

Rebuilt applications submitted to:
- FLHSMV Bureau of Dealer Services Regional Office
OR
- PRVIP facility.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION FEES
==================================================

Initial inspection:
- $40

Additional inspections after failure:
- $20 each.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT FIELD RULE
==================================================

Processing agency must enter:
1. Region number OR PRVIP county/agency code
2. Rebuilt decal number

Into:
- Comment Desc field.

==================================================
PROOF OF OWNERSHIP RULES
==================================================

FLORIDA PRE-1990 TOTAL LOSS:
Requires:
- Chain of bills of sale.

FLORIDA 1990+ TOTAL LOSS:
Requires:
- Florida Salvage Rebuildable title.

OUT-OF-STATE SALVAGE:
Requires:
- Rebuilt title process.

OUT-OF-STATE REBUILT:
Florida title issued:
- Rebuilt brand only.
- No rebuilt decal assigned.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ROAD OPERABLE RULE
==================================================

If out-of-state salvage purchased already rebuilt:
Applicant must provide affidavit.

ROAD OPERABLE MEANS:
Vehicle:
- Meets federal/state safety regulations
AND
- Safe for roadway use.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT THEFT RECOVERY RULE
==================================================

Recovered total theft vehicle:
Requires:
- HSMV 84490.

If NO major component parts replaced:
- Receipts not required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state ownership/lien verification may include:
- State printout
- State letterhead verification
- Law enforcement teletype
- Photocopy of title

==================================================
REQUIRED MAIN FORMS
==================================================

PRIMARY FORMS:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 MH
- HSMV 82042
- HSMV 84490

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state vehicles require:
- VIN verification.

May use:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- Section 8 of HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- New dealer-sold vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel recreation trailers

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 84490 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 84490:
MUST be completed by:
- Customer
AND
- Compliance Examiner or PRVIP inspector.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

Original receipts/bills of sale required for:
- ALL major component parts.

Receipts must contain:
- Seller name
- Seller address
- Seller signature
- Part description
- VIN/serial numbers when applicable

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ENGINE RULE
==================================================

Engine receipts must include:
- Engine year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECEIPT NAME RULE
==================================================

Receipts MUST be:
- In rebuilder/customer name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PAYMENT PROOF RULE
==================================================

If receipt not clearly paid:
Need:
- Paid invoice
OR
- Zero balance proof.

==================================================

==================================================

Includes:
- Fender
- Hood
- Bumper
- Cowl assembly
- Firewall
- Rear quarter panel
- Truck lid
- Door
- Decklid
- Floor pan
- Engine
- Frame
- Transmission
- Catalytic converter
- Airbag

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EV COMPONENT RULE
==================================================

Electric/hybrid/plug-in vehicles additionally include:
- Electric traction motor
- Electronic transmission
- Charge port
- DC power converter
- Onboard charger
- Power electronics controller
- Thermal system
- Traction battery pack
- Airbag

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS — TRUCKS
==================================================

Includes:
- All standard major components
PLUS:
- Truck bed
- Dump body
- Wrecker body
- Crane body
- Mixer body
- Cargo box

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS — MOTORCYCLES
==================================================

Includes:
- Body assembly
- Frame
- Fenders
- Gas tanks
- Engine
- Transmission
- Front fork assembly
- Wheels

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HARLEY RULE
==================================================

Harley Davidson frames pre-1970:
- May use bill of sale without VIN.

Other makes:
- NOT allowed.

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS — MOBILE HOMES
==================================================

Major component part:
- Frame.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MISSING RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

If receipts unavailable:
See:
- TL-07.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PHOTO RULE
==================================================

Must provide:
- Photos of vehicle in wrecked condition
- At least 2 angles
- In focus
- Showing all damage.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL DAMAGE RULE
==================================================

Examiner may request:
- Insurance adjuster report
OR
- Repair estimate.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax due on:
- Vehicle purchase price
AND
- Component parts
where sales tax not previously paid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEALED ENVELOPE RULE
==================================================

Regional office/PRVIP provides:
- Sealed inspection envelope.

If seal broken:
- DO NOT accept.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE COUNT RULE
==================================================

Issuing agency must verify:
- Number of receipts submitted
matches:
- HSMV 84490 receipt count.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLOOD BRAND RULE
==================================================

Water/Flood branded out-of-state titles:
Florida title permanently branded:
- FLOOD DAMAGED.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLOOD CARRY FORWARD RULE
==================================================

Flood brand:
- Carries forward permanently
for life of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL PLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

MOTOR VEHICLES:
- Driver door jamb.

MOTORCYCLES:
- Near right-side frame down tube.

MOBILE HOMES:
- Main door jamb hinge side below lower hinge.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DECAL RULE
==================================================

Decal:
- Cannot be placed on removable part.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TEMP TAG RULE
==================================================

10-day temporary tag allowed:
For:
- Rebuilt inspection purposes.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-RESIDENT RULE
==================================================

Non-Florida residents:
- Cannot receive Florida rebuilt title/decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MISSING VIN PLATE RULE
==================================================

If factory VIN removed:
- Rebuilt inspection cannot proceed.

Customer must:
1. Schedule confidential inspection
2. Obtain replacement VIN decal
3. Return for decal installation

ONLY regional offices may perform:
- Replacement VIN process.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE DISPUTE RULE
==================================================

If insurer repairs then later totals vehicle:
Insurance company must provide:
- Original letterhead affidavit.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DASH REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

If ONLY dash replaced:
Vehicle:
- NOT considered rebuilt
- NOT ASPT.

Regional office:
- Examines vehicle
- VIN transferred to replacement dash.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT JUNKED DASH RULE
==================================================

VIN remaining on replacement dash:
- Must be removed and destroyed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT JUNKED TITLE RULE
==================================================

Source junked vehicle:
Title marked:
- Cancelled (Junked).

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Salvage rebuildable titles:
May be reassigned:
- Dealer to dealer unlimited times.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 30-DAY RULE
==================================================

Dealer must submit customer title application:
Within:
- 30 days of delivery.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER RETAINED RULE
==================================================

Insurance company transferring salvage back to owner:
Owner may apply for:
- Salvage Rebuildable
- Rebuildable Flood
- Rebuilt title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CAB REPLACEMENT RULE
==================================================

If ONLY cab replaced:
Vehicle classified:
- ASPT
NOT:
- Rebuilt.

Vehicle receives:
- ASPT brand
- FLA decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRVIP RULE
==================================================

PRVIP:
Private Rebuilt Vehicle Inspection Program.

Authorized private facilities may:
- Conduct rebuilt inspections.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is vehicle already rebuilt?
2. Is proof of ownership acceptable?
3. Is VIN verification required?
4. Are all receipts present?
5. Are receipts properly paid?
6. Are photos sufficient?
7. Are major component parts documented?
8. Is flood brand required?
9. Is rebuilt inspection required?
10. Is PRVIP allowed?
11. Is replacement VIN process required?
12. Is ASPT classification required?
13. Is rebuilt decal assignment required?
14. Is dealer violating pre-sale rebuilt rules?
15. Is customer Florida resident?

==================================================

==================================================

- Missing receipts
- Missing paid invoice proof
- Missing VIN verification
- Broken inspection envelope accepted
- Missing rebuilt decal number
- Incorrect flood branding
- Accepting rebuilt sale before rebuilt title
- Missing wreck photos
- Missing HSMV 84490
- Incorrect ASPT classification
- Improper decal placement
- Missing sales tax collection
- Missing engine year on receipt
