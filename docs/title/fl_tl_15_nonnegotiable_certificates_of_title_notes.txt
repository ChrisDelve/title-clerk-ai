TITLE:\
FLHSMV TL-15 \'97 Nonnegotiable Certificates of Title\
\
CATEGORY:\
Nonnegotiable Titles\
Title Restrictions\
Ownership Verification\
Lienholder Processing\
Special Title Status\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
A nonnegotiable certificate of title indicates restrictions exist preventing standard ownership transfer processing until requirements are satisfied.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Title status\
- Restriction reason\
- Ownership information\
- Lienholder information\
- Supporting documentation\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Restriction unclear\
- Ownership conflict exists\
- Title status inconsistent\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Nonnegotiable titles may be issued in situations involving unresolved title conditions or special title circumstances.\
\
EXAMPLES:\
- Pending lien satisfaction\
- Ownership issue\
- Incomplete title requirements\
- Court/legal restrictions\
\
VERIFY:\
- Supporting paperwork\
- Existing title status\
- Legal/title conditions\
\
COMMON MISTAKE:\
Treating nonnegotiable title as standard transferable title.\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Ownership transfers involving nonnegotiable titles require heightened review before processing.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Current ownership\
- Supporting transfer documents\
- Restriction resolution\
- Signature requirements\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Ownership unclear\
- Restriction unresolved\
- Supporting documentation incomplete\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
VIN must match ALL title and supporting records exactly.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Existing title VIN\
- System VIN\
- Vehicle VIN\
- Supporting paperwork VIN\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Any VIN discrepancy exists\
- Altered VIN suspected\
- VIN unreadable\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Lienholder information must be reviewed carefully on nonnegotiable titles.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Existing lienholder\
- ELT status\
- Lien release status\
- Bank documentation\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Missing lien release\
- Multiple liens exist\
- Lienholder mismatch exists\
- Electronic title status unclear\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Court orders or legal documentation may impact negotiability of title.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Court paperwork\
- Ownership authority\
- Legal restrictions\
- Supporting documentation\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Court documentation unclear\
- Ownership dispute exists\
- Legal restrictions unresolved\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Out-of-state title restrictions may affect Florida title processing.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Prior state title status\
- Branding/restrictions\
- Ownership consistency\
- Supporting paperwork\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Prior state restrictions unclear\
- Branding conflict exists\
- Ownership discrepancy exists\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Business/entity ownership on restricted/nonnegotiable titles requires additional authority verification.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Authorized signer\
- Business documentation\
- Ownership consistency\
- Supporting paperwork\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Signer authority unclear\
- Entity ownership conflict exists\
- Missing business documentation\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
RULE:\
Nonnegotiable title conditions must generally be resolved before standard title transfer processing can occur.\
\
VERIFY:\
- Restriction satisfaction\
- Supporting releases\
- Required approvals\
- Correct title status update\
\
ESCALATE IF:\
- Restriction unresolved\
- Missing required documentation\
- Title history conflict exists\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
COMMON DEALERSHIP MISTAKES:\
- Treating nonnegotiable title as standard title\
- Missing lien release\
- Failure to verify restriction reason\
- Ownership mismatch\
- VIN mismatch\
- Ignoring legal/title restrictions\
- Incomplete supporting paperwork\
- Incorrect ELT assumptions\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
HIGH-RISK ESCALATION SITUATIONS:\
- Ownership disputes\
- Court/legal restrictions\
- Missing lien release\
- VIN discrepancies\
- Fraud concerns\
- Title history inconsistencies\
- Out-of-state title conflicts\
- Altered title paperwork\
- Multiple ownership claims\
\
\
--------------------------------------------------\
\
BEST PRACTICE:\
Before processing nonnegotiable title:\
1. Identify WHY title is nonnegotiable.\
2. Verify restriction resolution requirements.\
3. Verify ownership carefully.\
4. Verify lienholder/title status.\
5. Escalate unresolved title restrictions immediately.\
