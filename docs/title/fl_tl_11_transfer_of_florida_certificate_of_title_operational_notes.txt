FLORIDA TITLE & LIEN PROCEDURE
TRANSFER OF FLORIDA CERTIFICATE OF TITLE
SOURCE: TL-11
REVISION DATE: 06/26/2023

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Transfer of Florida titles
- Dealer reassignments
- Casual sale transfers
- Electronic title transfers
- Open title handling
- Odometer disclosure transfer rules
- Lien transfer requirements
- Title correction issues
- Transfer of equity situations

This is one of the MOST IMPORTANT dealership procedures.

==================================================
CORE TRANSFER RULE
==================================================

Ownership cannot transfer
unless:
- Seller properly assigns title
- Purchaser properly identified
- Required odometer disclosures completed

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Open titles are illegal.

Purchaser name MUST appear on title.

==================================================
TITLE TRANSFER SECTION RULE
==================================================

Transfer of Title by Seller section:
Must be completed fully.

Required:
- Seller printed names
- Seller signatures
- Purchaser printed names
- Odometer disclosure when required
- Selling price

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Printed names:
Can be typed or stamped.

BUT:
Cannot be blank.

==================================================
NOTARIZATION RULE
==================================================

Florida title transfer section:
Does NOT require notarization.

Even if title contains notary area.

==================================================
ODOMETER DISCLOSURE RULE
==================================================

Non-exempt vehicles:
Must complete odometer disclosure
in Transfer of Title by Seller section.

==================================================
DEALER REASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Florida dealer using paper title:
Uses reassignment section on back of title first.

Once reassignment spaces exhausted:
Use HSMV 82994.

==================================================
ELECTRONIC TITLE DEALER RULE
==================================================

Electronic title traded into Florida dealer:
Requires HSMV 82994.

==================================================
HSMV 82995 RULE
==================================================

Secure POA only used when:
- Title held by lienholder
OR
- Title lost

==================================================
EXEMPT VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Exempt vehicle:
Seller signature still required.

Selling price still required.

Purchaser name still required.

==================================================
VESSEL AND MOBILE HOME RULE
==================================================

Vessels and mobile homes:
Always odometer exempt.

==================================================
ELECTRONIC TITLE CASUAL SALE RULE
==================================================

Electronic title staying electronic:
Buyer and seller BOTH must appear at agency.

NO power of attorney allowed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

If title remains electronic:
Use:
- HSMV 82994
OR
- HSMV 82092 when applicable

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE BUYER RULE
==================================================

Florida dealer selling to out-of-state resident:
May provide reassigned title directly.

Requires:
HSMV 84061.

==================================================
CONFORMING TITLE RULE
==================================================

Conforming titles:
Revision date 8/91 or later.

==================================================
NONCONFORMING TITLE RULE
==================================================

Nonconforming titles:
Revision date before 8/91.

==================================================
CASUAL SALE RULE
==================================================

Transfer between individuals:
May use:
- HSMV 82040
- HSMV 82041
OR
- Application for Title by Purchaser section

When accompanied by:
HSMV 82993.

==================================================
BROKER VESSEL RULE
==================================================

Vessel broker:
NOT purchaser unless broker actually bought vessel.

==================================================
LIEN RECORDING RULE
==================================================

Lien information must include:
- Lienholder name
- Address
- Supporting application

Additional liens:
Require HSMV 82139.

==================================================
LIEN SATISFACTION RULE
==================================================

Outstanding liens:
Require satisfaction
unless valid Transfer of Equity.

==================================================
PENALTY FEE RULE
==================================================

Penalty applies:
30 days after seller signs title
AND vehicle delivered.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

30-day countdown begins:
DAY AFTER signing and delivery.

==================================================
CO-OWNER CHANGE RULE
==================================================

Changing:
- AND to OR
- OR to AND
- Name order

Requires:
- Modified title process
- Affidavit or completed title

If lien exists:
Requires lienholder permission.

==================================================
ADDING OR REMOVING OWNER RULE
==================================================

Adding/removing name:
Requires transfer process.

If lien exists:
Lienholder authorization required.

==================================================
TRANSFER OF EQUITY RULE
==================================================

ELT Transfer of Equity:
Must process as Modify Title
to preserve ELT status.

==================================================
OPEN TITLE RULE
==================================================

Open title exists when:
- Purchaser name missing
OR
- Selling price missing

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Open title requires:
- Bill of sale
- County stamp
- Circled deficiency

==================================================
ODOMETER OMISSION RULE
==================================================

Missing odometer disclosure:
NOT automatically open title.

Refer to TL-09.

==================================================
CONSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

If dealership returns title after failed consignment:
Dealer affidavit required.

Must state:
Dealer never took ownership.

==================================================
WRONG SIGNATURE SECTION RULE
==================================================

If signed wrong section:
Affidavit explaining mistake required.

==================================================
ALTERED PURCHASER NAME RULE
==================================================

If purchaser name altered/lined through:
Requires:
- Seller affidavit
OR
- Duplicate title
OR
- Court order

==================================================
ALTERED PURCHASE PRICE RULE
==================================================

Altered purchase price:
Requires signed bill of sale.

==================================================
INSURANCE COMPANY VESSEL RULE
==================================================

Insurance company acquiring vessel:
Must title vessel in insurance company name first.

==================================================
ELECTRONIC TITLE PRINT RULE
==================================================

Paper title can be requested anytime
from electronic record.

==================================================
DONATION RULE
==================================================

Non-profit without dealer license:
Must title vehicle in organization name
before resale.

==================================================
NOTICE OF SALE RULE
==================================================

Seller should notify FLHSMV within 30 days.

Uses:
Notice of Sale section
OR
HSMV 82050.

==================================================
TRUST TITLE RULE
==================================================

Trust-owned title transfer:
Requires:
- Trust agreement
OR
- Certification of trust

==================================================
LEASE BRAND RULE
==================================================

Lease-branded vehicles:
Carry brand forward
while used as lease vehicles.

==================================================
LEASE BRAND REMOVAL RULE
==================================================

Lease brand removal requires:
- Affidavit
- Private use designation
- Title fees

Dealer cannot remove lease brand.

==================================================
DEALERSHIP HIGH-RISK ERROR AREAS
==================================================

MOST COMMON ERRORS:
- Open titles
- Missing selling price
- Missing purchaser name
- Wrong reassignment sequence
- Missing odometer disclosure
- Wrong electronic title process
- Missing affidavits
- Incorrect lien transfer
- Incorrect Transfer of Equity
- Altered purchaser names
- Missing lienholder permission
- Improper casual sale paperwork
- Incorrect exempt handling

==================================================
WORKFLOW BEST PRACTICE
==================================================

Before processing transfer:

1. Verify title type
2. Verify reassignment sequence
3. Verify purchaser name present
4. Verify selling price
5. Verify odometer completion
6. Verify lien status
7. Verify signatures
8. Verify electronic/paper status
9. Verify supporting affidavits
10. Verify taxes and registration
