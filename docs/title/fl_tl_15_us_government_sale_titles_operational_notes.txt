FLORIDA TITLE & LIEN PROCEDURE
U.S. GOVERNMENT SALE TITLE APPLICATIONS
SOURCE: TL-15
REVISION DATE: 06/26/2023

==================================================
PURPOSE
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Vehicles purchased at U.S. Government sales
- Seized vehicle title processing
- IRS seizure vehicle title processing
- Standard Form 97 processing
- Government forfeiture transactions

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Standard Form 97:
Acts like title or MCO.

==================================================
STANDARD FORM 97 RULE
==================================================

Standard Form 97:
U.S. Government Certificate to Obtain Title.

Required for government sale vehicles.

==================================================
CORE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required:
- Standard Form 97
- HSMV 82040
- VIN verification if required
- Tax information
- Plate/non-use affidavit
- Fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Government sale:
Does NOT require lien satisfaction.

Unless IRS seizure situation.

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state or untitled vehicles:
Require HSMV 82042
or VIN verification section.

==================================================
VERIFICATION EXEMPTIONS
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required for:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel RV trailers

==================================================
DEALER RULE
==================================================

Licensed dealer:
May reassign Standard Form 97
without titling into dealership name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Individual purchaser:
MUST title vehicle in own name
before resale.

==================================================
SEIZED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Law enforcement seizure requires:
- Federal Court Order of Forfeiture
OR
- Declaration of Forfeiture

==================================================
IRS SEIZURE RULE
==================================================

IRS seizure requires:
Form 2435
Certificate of Sale of Seized Property.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

IRS seizure:
Does NOT automatically clear purchase money liens.

==================================================
LIEN RULE
==================================================

IRS seizure transactions:
Require lien satisfactions
when applicable.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Government seizure by law enforcement:
Does NOT require lien satisfaction.

==================================================
ODOMETER RULE
==================================================

Standard Form 97 odometer section:
Must show government agency name,
not individual seller name.

==================================================
NON-USE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicles:
May use non-use affidavit.

Mobile homes:
Cannot use non-use affidavit.

==================================================
NEW 82040 FORM RULE
==================================================

Updated forms:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 MH
- HSMV 82040 VS

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RULE
==================================================

Nongame Wildlife Fee:
NOT required
with Standard Form 97.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE RECORD RULE
==================================================

If Florida title record already exists:
Same title number must be reused.

==================================================
DEALERSHIP HIGH-RISK ERROR AREAS
==================================================

MOST COMMON ERRORS:
- Missing Standard Form 97
- Missing forfeiture order
- Missing Form 2435
- Incorrect lien handling
- Improper dealer reassignment
- Missing VIN verification
- Wrong tax collection
- Using non-use affidavit for mobile home
- Treating IRS seizure like normal seizure
- Missing title in individual purchaser name
