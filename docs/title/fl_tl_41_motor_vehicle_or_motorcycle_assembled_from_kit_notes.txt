FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — MOTOR VEHICLE OR MOTORCYCLE ASSEMBLED FROM A KIT
PROCEDURE: TL-41
CATEGORY: KIT CAR / REPLICA / ASSEMBLED VEHICLE TITLE PROCESSING

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

- Kit car title applications
- Motorcycle kit title applications
- Replica vehicle kits

- Homemade frame kit vehicles
- Donor vehicle kit assembly
- Custom vehicle titling
- Street rod titling
- FLA VIN assignments
- Regional Office inspections
- Replica branding
- Kit Car branding

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Motorcycles
- Replica kits
- Custom vehicles
- Street rods
- Builders
- Applicants
- Tax collectors
- Regional Offices
- DMS Compliance Examiners

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LEGAL RULE
==================================================

NO PERSON may:
- Sell
- Offer for sale
- Exchange

A rebuilt or assembled kit vehicle:
UNTIL:
- Proper title application submitted
AND
- Physical inspection completed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT KIT CAR DEFINITION
==================================================

Kit Car:
Motor vehicle assembled from:
- Manufacturer-supplied kit

Using:
- Major components from donor vehicle(s).

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPLETE VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Motor vehicle/motorcycle MUST be:
- Fully completed

BEFORE:
- Applying for title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGIONAL OFFICE RULE
==================================================

“Assembled From Kit” applications:
MUST be submitted to:
- Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DOCUMENT PACKAGE RULE
==================================================

Applicant should provide:
- Original documents
AND
- One photocopy set

Inside:
- 5x7 or 8x10 envelope.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REJECTION RULE
==================================================

If application rejected:
Regional Office:
- Holds paperwork
UNTIL:
- Deficiency corrected.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSPECTION FEE RULE
==================================================

Initial inspection:
- Fee required.

Additional inspections:
- Additional fees apply.

==================================================
KIT VEHICLE TITLE RULES
==================================================

YEAR:
- Year physically assembled
UNLESS:
- Replica 25+ years old.

MAKE:
- Make shown on MCO or manufacturer invoice.

VIN:
- VIN assigned by manufacturer
OR
- FLA number assigned by Compliance Examiner.

BODY:
- Body type shown on MCO or invoice.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLICA YEAR RULE
==================================================

If replica resembles vehicle:
- 25 years old or older

THEN:
Title year:
- Year vehicle built to resemble.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MODERN REPLICA RULE
==================================================

If replica resembles vehicle:
- Less than 25 years old

THEN:
Title year:
- Actual assembly year.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE BRAND RULE
==================================================

All applicable titles branded:
- KIT CAR

If replica:
- REPLICA also added.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BRAND CARRY-FORWARD RULE
==================================================

KIT CAR and REPLICA brands:
Carry forward:
- For life of vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMMENT FIELD RULE
==================================================

If replica resembles vehicle:
- 25 years old or older

Actual assembly year:
MUST be entered:
- Comment field.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

If manufacturer VIN appears invalid/model number:
Agency should:
- Verify with manufacturer.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA NUMBER RULE
==================================================

If manufacturer did NOT assign VIN:
Florida Compliance Examiner:
- Assigns FLA number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ORIGINAL USED RULE
==================================================

Kit vehicles processed as:
- Original Used vehicles.

==================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS — FLORIDA DONOR FRAME
==================================================

Need:
1. Florida title for donor frame
2. MCO or manufacturer invoice
3. HSMV 82040
4. HSMV 84490
5. Bills of sale/receipts for parts
6. Replica affidavit if applicable
7. Sales tax documentation
8. Certified weight slip
9. Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DONOR TITLE RULE
==================================================

Donor vehicle title:
MUST be:
- Cancelled

AND comment field updated with:
- New kit title number
- New VIN number
- Note donor used for kit vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MCO RULE
==================================================

If manufacturer does not issue MCO:
Need:
- Manufacturer invoice/bill of sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MCO CONTENT RULE
==================================================

MCO/invoice should show:
- Year
- Make
- Manufacturer name/address
- VIN if assigned

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 84490 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 84490:
Must be completed by:
- Applicant
AND
- Compliance Examiner.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

Original receipts required for:
- All major component parts
NOT included in:
- Title
OR
- MCO.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECEIPT CONTENT RULE
==================================================

Receipts must show:
- Seller name
- Seller address
- Seller signature
- VIN or identifying number
- Component identification

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-1955 ENGINE RULE
==================================================

For vehicles prior to 1955:
Engine receipt must show:
- Engine number
- Engine year.

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS — MOTOR VEHICLES
==================================================

Includes:
- Fender
- Hood
- Bumper
- Cowl assembly
- Rear quarter panel
- Trunk lid
- Door
- Decklid
- Floor pan
- Engine
- Frame
- Transmission
- Catalytic converter
- Airbag

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS — TRUCKS
==================================================

Includes standard parts PLUS:
- Dump bed
- Wrecker bed
- Crane body
- Mixer body
- Cargo box

==================================================
MAJOR COMPONENT PARTS — MOTORCYCLES
==================================================

Includes:
- Frame
- Fenders
- Gas tanks
- Engine
- Transmission
- Front fork assembly
- Wheels

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HARLEY RULE
==================================================

Harley Davidson frames before 1970:
- Bills of sale acceptable without VIN.

Other makes:
- NOT acceptable.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLICA AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Replica vehicles require affidavit stating:
- Vehicle meets safety/equipment laws
for model year represented

AND:
- Vehicle NOT for daily transportation.

==================================================
REPLICA PERMITTED USES
==================================================

Replica may be maintained for:
- Occasional transportation
- Exhibitions
- Club activities
- Parades
- Tours
- Public interest functions

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax due on:
- Vehicle purchase price
AND
- Component parts
where tax not previously paid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WEIGHT SLIP RULE
==================================================

Certified weight slip required:
- Except motorcycles.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SEALED ENVELOPE RULE
==================================================

Regional Office may issue:
- Sealed document envelope

If seal broken:
- DO NOT accept.

Customer must:
- Return to Regional Office.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL COUNT RULE
==================================================

Bills of sale count:
MUST match:
- HSMV 84490 count.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE FRAME RULE
==================================================

If donor frame titled out-of-state:
Need:
- Out-of-state ownership proof
PLUS:
- All remaining required documents.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE VIN RULE
==================================================

VIN verification NOT required:
If titled using:
- Kit manufacturer VIN.

==================================================
HOMEMADE FRAME RULE
==================================================

If homemade frame:
Need:
- Affidavit describing construction
OR
- Material receipts.

==================================================
FRAME-INCLUDED RULE
==================================================

If frame included with kit:
Need manufacturer proof:
- Frame included in purchase.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALVAGE RULE
==================================================

Florida title CANNOT issue if ownership is:
- Salvage Unrebuildable
OR
- Certificate of Destruction.

These vehicles:
- Parts/scrap only.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SCRATCH-BUILT RULE
==================================================

Vehicle built from scratch WITHOUT kit:
Classified:
- Assembled From Parts

See:
- TL-43.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ODOMETER RULE
==================================================

Odometer reading generally:
- NOT required.

If system prompts:
May use:
- All 9s
AND
- Not Actual Mileage.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INITIAL REGISTRATION RULE
==================================================

If titled as:
- 30 years old or older

Initial registration fee:
- NOT due.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTRATION EXAMPLE RULE
==================================================

Built as 2010 replica:
- Initial registration fee due.

Built as 1975 replica:
- Initial registration fee NOT due.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COMPLETE REPLICA RULE
==================================================

Complete turnkey replica vehicles:
Handled under:
- TL-48

NOT:
- TL-41.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORMS UPDATE RULE
==================================================

As of July 1, 2023:
HSMV 82040 split into:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82040 MH

==================================================
VEHICLE TYPES FOR HSMV 82040 MV
==================================================

Used for:
- AU Auto
- BS Bus
- MC Motorcycle
- OH Off-Highway
- TO Tools
- TR Truck
- TT Travel Trailer
- VT Vehicle Trailer

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VESSEL RULE
==================================================

As of January 1, 2024:
Motor vehicles/mobile homes:
MUST use:
- HSMV 82040 MV
OR
- HSMV 82040 MH.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Is vehicle fully assembled?
2. Is this a kit or complete replica?
3. Is donor frame Florida or out-of-state?
4. Is homemade frame involved?
5. Is frame included with kit?
6. Is VIN manufacturer-issued?
7. Is FLA assignment needed?
8. Are all component receipts present?
9. Is replica affidavit required?
10. Is certified weight slip required?
11. Is title branding required?
12. Is donor title cancellation required?
13. Is odometer exemption applicable?
14. Is initial registration fee due?
15. Is salvage unrebuildable ownership prohibited?
