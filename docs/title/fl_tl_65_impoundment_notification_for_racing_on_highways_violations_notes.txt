GENERAL INFORMATION
==================================================

FLHSMV must be notified:
Of any impoundment:
Related to:
316.191 violations.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DRAG RACE RULE
==================================================

Drag race:
Operation of:
Two or more vehicles:
Accelerating competitively.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RACE RULE
==================================================

Race:
Competition:
To demonstrate:
Vehicle or driver superiority.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STREET TAKEOVER RULE
==================================================

Street takeover:
Blocking or impeding roadway:
To perform:
- Race
- Burnout
- Doughnut
- Drifting
- Wheelie
- Stunt driving

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COORDINATED TAKEOVER RULE
==================================================

Coordinated street takeover:
10 or more vehicles:
Operating in organized manner.

==================================================
PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES RULE
==================================================

Illegal activities include:
- Racing
- Drag racing
- Street takeovers
- Stunt driving
- Speed contests
- Exhibitions of speed
- Acceleration contests

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SOCIAL MEDIA RULE
==================================================

Illegal to:
Coordinate or facilitate:
Street racing:
Through social media or otherwise.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PASSENGER RULE
==================================================

Knowingly riding as passenger:
In prohibited racing activity:
Is illegal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRAFFIC IMPEDIMENT RULE
==================================================

Illegal to:
Slow or stop traffic:
To support racing activity.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FILMING RULE
==================================================

Illegal to:
Operate vehicle:
For purpose of filming racing activity.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MEDIA EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Filming restriction:
Does NOT apply:
To bona fide news media.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FUEL SUPPORT RULE
==================================================

Illegal to:
Transport fuel:
To support racing activity.

==================================================
CRIMINAL PENALTY RULE
==================================================

Violation of:
316.191(2):
First-degree misdemeanor.

==================================================
SPECTATOR RULE
==================================================

Spectators:
At race or street takeover:
Commit:
Noncriminal traffic infraction.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ARREST RULE
==================================================

Law enforcement may:
Arrest violator:
Without warrant:
If probable cause exists.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IMPOUNDMENT RULE
==================================================

Court may order:
Impoundment or immobilization:
As condition of:
- Incarceration
- Probation

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER NOTICE RULE
==================================================

Within:
7 business days:
Court clerk must send:
Certified mail notice:
To:
- Registered owner
- Recorded lienholders

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NON-OWNER RULE
==================================================

Notice required:
If registered owner:
Different from defendant.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RELEASE RULE
==================================================

Impounding agency:
May release vehicle:
Under statutory release conditions.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSE RULE
==================================================

Owner or agent:
Must present:
Valid driver license:
To recover vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COST RULE
==================================================

Owner responsible for:
- Impound fees
- Notification costs
- Immobilization costs

Unless:
Order dismissed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IMMEDIATE IMPOUND RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may be:
Immediately impounded:
If:
- Driver arrested
AND
- Driver is registered owner or co-owner.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 30-BUSINESS-DAY RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may be impounded:
For:
30 business days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLHSMV NOTIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Law enforcement must notify:
FLHSMV:
Of impoundment.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPEAT OFFENDER RULE
==================================================

Second or subsequent offense:
Within 5 years:
May result in:
Vehicle seizure and forfeiture.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER REQUIREMENT RULE
==================================================

Forfeiture applies:
ONLY if:
Vehicle owner:
Committed offense.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EMERGENCY VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may be seized:
If racing activity:
Impedes authorized emergency vehicle:
Responding to unrelated emergency.

==================================================
LAW ENFORCEMENT NOTIFICATION RULES
==================================================

Law enforcement must:
Email notice:
To FLHSMV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EMAIL RULE
==================================================

Required email:
AdminStop@flhsmv.gov

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LETTERHEAD RULE
==================================================

Notice must be:
On agency letterhead.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REQUIRED NOTICE INFO RULE
==================================================

Notice must include:
- Driver name
- Driver license number
- VIN
- License plate number

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ADMINISTRATIVE STOP RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV places:
Administrative stop:
Upon receiving notice.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STOP DURATION RULE
==================================================

Administrative stop remains:
Until further notification:
By:
- Court
OR
- Reporting agency

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RELATED PROCEDURE RULE
==================================================

Administrative stop handling:
See:
TL-24.

IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

DRAG RACE:
Competitive acceleration between vehicles.

STREET TAKEOVER:
Blocking roadway for stunt or racing activity.

COORDINATED STREET TAKEOVER:
Organized takeover involving 10 or more vehicles.

ADMINISTRATIVE STOP:
FLHSMV system restriction preventing processing activity.
