==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Vehicle titles in trust names
- Transfers into trusts
- Transfers out of trusts
- Duplicate titles involving trusts
- Trustee signing authority
- Successor trustee authority

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes
- Vessels

A trust is treated as a separate legal entity.

For trust ownership to legally control:
- Vehicle must actually be titled in trust name.

==================================================
CORE LEGAL PRINCIPLE
==================================================

A trust is a separate legal entity.

Title may be:
- Issued INTO trust
- Issued OUT OF trust
- Transferred BY trustee
- Held by trustee for trust

Trust authority must be verified before processing.

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE TRUST DOCUMENTATION
==================================================

ONE OF THE FOLLOWING REQUIRED:

1. Copy of trust agreement
OR
2. Certification of Trust

==================================================
MINIMUM TRUST AGREEMENT PAGES REQUIRED
==================================================

ONLY THESE PAGES REQUIRED:

- First page
- Page naming trustee/successor trustee
- Signature page of creator/settlor

==================================================
CERTIFICATION OF TRUST REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Certification of Trust must contain:

- Trust exists
- Date trust executed
- Identity of settlor
- Identity/address of trustee
- Trustee powers
- Revocable vs irrevocable status
- Revocation authority
- Co-trustee authority
- Title-taking instructions
- Statement trust not revoked/amended

Must be:
- Signed by trustee
- Notarized

OR

Contain penalty of perjury clause.

==================================================
TRUST TITLE NAMING RULE
==================================================

IMPORTANT:

Trust name MUST appear on title.

Even if customer only wants trustee shown.

EXAMPLES:
- Smith Family Trust
- John Smith as Trustee for Smith Family Trust
- Friendly Bank U/D/T

==================================================
TRUSTEE SIGNATURE RULES
==================================================

IF trustee already named on title:

EXAMPLE:
"John Smith, Trustee"

THEN:
John Smith signs as trustee.

==================================================
UNIDENTIFIED TRUSTEE RULE
==================================================

IF title only shows trust name:

EXAMPLE:
"Smith Family Trust"

THEN:
Trustee signs:
"John Smith, Trustee for Smith Family Trust"

==================================================
CO-TRUSTEE SIGNATURE RULES
==================================================

IF trustees joined by:
- "AND"
THEN:
Both must sign.

IF joined by:
- "OR"
THEN:
Either may sign.

IF trust agreement allows independent action:
- Either trustee may sign.

==================================================
TRANSFER INTO TRUST
==================================================

REQUIRED:
- Title assigned into trust
- HSMV title application
- Trust agreement OR certification
- VIN verification if required
- Sales tax information
- Registration/decal information
- Title fees

==================================================
TRANSFER OUT OF TRUST
==================================================

REQUIRED:
- Title assigned by trustee
- Purchaser title application
- Lien satisfactions if applicable
- Sales tax information
- Title fees

IMPORTANT:
If title only shows trust name:
- Trust agreement/certification required again.

==================================================
DUPLICATE TITLE PROCEDURES
==================================================

APPLICATION FOR DUPLICATE:
- HSMV 82101 completed by trustee

DUPLICATE WITH TRANSFER:
- HSMV 82101 completed by trustee and purchaser
- Tax information
- Registration/decal information
- Fees

==================================================
DECEASED TRUSTEE RULES
==================================================

IF trustee deceased:
- Follow TL-18 deceased procedures

IMPORTANT:
Duplicate with transfer NOT allowed when:
- Title in trust name
AND
- Trustee deceased

==================================================
SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE RULES
==================================================

Successor trustee assumes authority:
- Upon death of trustee

May require:
- Death certificate
- Trust documentation

==================================================
POWER OF ATTORNEY RULES
==================================================

Only trustee may sign title work UNLESS:
- Valid power of attorney exists

If business signs:
- Affidavit required
- Must state acting for trustee

POA must contain:
- Complete vehicle description

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Out-of-state titles require:
- Ownership proof
- VIN verification if applicable
- HSMV application
- Trust documentation
- Fees and taxes

==================================================
TRUST VS WILL LOGIC
==================================================

IMPORTANT:

Ownership controls procedure.

IF vehicle owned by:
- Estate → follow deceased owner procedures
- Trust → follow trust procedures

IF will leaves vehicle to trust:
- Title must first go into trust before sale.

==================================================
SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE AUTHORITY
==================================================

Successor trustee may:
- Transfer ownership
- Sell vehicle
- Act for trust

WITHOUT first titling into own name,
IF trust ownership already established.

==================================================
COMMON DEALERSHIP ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing trust documentation
- Accepting trustee signature without authority proof
- Forgetting trust name must appear on title
- Not checking AND vs OR trustee wording
- Processing duplicate with transfer after trustee death
- Treating estate ownership same as trust ownership
- Missing successor trustee documentation
- Accepting unsigned certification of trust
- Missing notarization/perjury statement

==================================================
AI RESPONSE GUIDANCE
==================================================

AI should:
1. Determine if ownership is trust or estate
2. Verify trust documentation
3. Verify trustee authority
4. Verify successor trustee status
5. Determine whether co-trustees must both sign
6. Verify duplicate title eligibility
7. Verify transfer authority
8. Verify tax/registration requirements
9. Verify VIN requirements
10. Confirm title naming format

==================================================
TRUST DEFINITIONS
==================================================

LIVING TRUST:
Trust effective during settlor lifetime.

REVOCABLE TRUST:
Trust may be changed/revoked.

IRREVOCABLE TRUST:
Trust cannot be changed/revoked.

SETTLOR:
Person creating trust.

TRUSTEE:
Person managing trust.

SUCCESSOR TRUSTEE:
Replacement trustee after trustee death/incapacity.

UNDER DECLARATION OF TRUST (U/D/T):
Formal trust declaration structure.
