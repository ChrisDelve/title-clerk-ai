FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — TRANSFER OF CERTIFICATE OF TITLE INVOLVING GUARDIANSHIP
PROCEDURE: TL-20
CATEGORY: TITLE TRANSFERS / GUARDIANSHIP / COURT ORDER
RELATED STATUTES:
- Chapter 744 Florida Statutes

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs title transactions involving motor vehicles or mobile homes owned by a person under guardianship.

A guardian may NOT:
- Purchase
- Sell
- Transfer
- Record liens

against a vehicle or mobile home UNLESS authorized by court order or guardianship authority.

The procedure establishes requirements for:
- Florida title transfers
- Out-of-state title transfers
- Duplicate titles
- Court-authorized transfers
- Guardian authority verification

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Mobile homes

==================================================
IMPORTANT LEGAL RULE
==================================================

Guardians may NOT:
- Purchase
- Sell
- Transfer
- Record liens

WITHOUT court authorization.

Guardian authority must be verified before processing transaction.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE AVAILABLE
==================================================

IF FLORIDA TITLE IS AVAILABLE:

REQUIRED:
- Florida certificate of title completed for transfer
- Guardian documentation
- Court authorization
- Sales tax information
- Registration/decal information
- Title fees

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE GUARDIAN DOCUMENTS
==================================================

ONE OF THE FOLLOWING REQUIRED:

- Letters of guardianship
- Court order appointing guardian ad litem
- Order appointing successor guardian
- Conservator appointment for estate

==================================================
COURT ORDER REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

A court order authorizing the following is required:

- Purchase
- Sale
- Transfer
- Recording of lien

IF the court order includes:
- Guardian name
AND
- Authorization details

THEN separate guardian appointment paperwork may not be required.

==================================================
PLENARY GUARDIANSHIP RULE
==================================================

IMPORTANT:

Plenary guardianship usually grants:
- Full legal authority
- Complete powers over property
- Authority to transfer or sell vehicles

IF plenary authority clearly exists:
- Separate court order may NOT be required.

==================================================
TEMPORARY GUARDIANSHIP RULE
==================================================

IMPORTANT:

Temporary guardianship ALONE does NOT authorize:
- Purchase
- Sale
- Transfer
- Lien recording

WITHOUT specific court order.

==================================================
REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

MOTOR VEHICLES:
- Florida plate transfer/purchase
OR
- Non-use affidavit

MOBILE HOMES:
- Mobile home decal
OR
- RP decal

IMPORTANT:
Non-use affidavit NOT acceptable for mobile homes.

==================================================
DUPLICATE TITLE PROCEDURES
==================================================

IF FLORIDA TITLE NOT AVAILABLE:

REQUIRED:
- HSMV 82101
- Guardian signs application
- Application completed in owner name
- Guardian address used for mailing
- Guardian proof of identification
- Court documentation

IMPORTANT:
Duplicate title should be mailed to guardian — not owner.

Court order serves as address verification.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE TITLE PROCEDURES
==================================================

REQUIRED:
- Out-of-state title or registration
OR
- Ownership/lien verification
- HSMV title application
- VIN verification if required
- Guardian documentation
- Court authorization
- Tax information
- Registration/decal information
- Title fees

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE OWNERSHIP VERIFICATION
==================================================

IF OUT-OF-STATE TITLE NOT AVAILABLE:

ACCEPTABLE:
- State computer printout
- State letterhead verification
- Teletype verification
- Photocopy of out-of-state title

Verification must show:
- Ownership
- Lien status
- State source

==================================================
VIN VERIFICATION RULES
==================================================

VIN VERIFICATION REQUIRED FOR:
- Most out-of-state motor vehicles

VIN VERIFICATION NOT REQUIRED FOR:
- Mobile homes
- Trailers under 2,000 lbs
- Travel trailers
- Camping trailers
- Truck campers
- Fifth-wheel recreation trailers

==================================================
ACCEPTABLE VIN VERIFICATION METHODS
==================================================

ACCEPTABLE:
- HSMV 82042
- Section 8 of HSMV 82040
- Affidavit verifying VIN

==================================================
TITLE APPLICATION FORMS
==================================================

MOTOR VEHICLES:
- HSMV 82040 MV

VESSELS:
- HSMV 82040 VS

MOBILE HOMES:
- HSMV 82040 MH

==================================================
COMMON DEALERSHIP ERRORS
==================================================

- Accepting guardian signature without court authority
- Assuming temporary guardianship gives full authority
- Missing court order
- Missing letters of guardianship
- Mailing duplicate title to owner instead of guardian
- Missing VIN verification
- Processing transfer without proof of guardian appointment
- Using non-use affidavit for mobile home
- Not verifying plenary vs limited powers

==================================================
DEFINITIONAL LOGIC
==================================================

GUARDIAN:
Court-appointed person acting on behalf of ward.

WARD:
Person under guardianship.

PLENARY GUARDIAN:
Guardian with full legal authority.

LIMITED GUARDIAN:
Guardian with only specifically granted powers.

GUARDIAN AD LITEM:
Guardian appointed for legal proceeding only.

SUCCESSOR GUARDIAN:
Replacement guardian assuming duties.

CONSERVATOR:
Person protecting incompetent person's estate/property.

IMPORTANT OPERATIONAL NOTES
