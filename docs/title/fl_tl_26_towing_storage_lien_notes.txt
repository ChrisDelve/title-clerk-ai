==================================================

- Towing liens
- Storage liens
- Public tow sales
- Certificate of Title issuance after tow sale
- Certificate of Destruction issuance
- Mobile home towing lien procedures
- Tow company notice requirements
- NMVTIS requirements
- Third-party service provider requirements

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Vessels
- Mobile homes

==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

MOTOR VEHICLE:
Any mobile item mounted on wheels.
Includes:
- Off-highway vehicles

VESSEL:
Watercraft capable of transportation on water.

WRECKER:
Vehicle used to tow or transport vehicles/vessels.

NEWER MODEL:
3 model years old or less.

OLDER MODEL:
More than 3 model years old.

GOOD FAITH EFFORT:
Series of ownership checks required under statute.

STORAGE:
Begins the day AFTER tow date.

==================================================
WHO MAY AUTHORIZE TOW
==================================================

Authorized parties:
- Vehicle owner
- Property owner
- Authorized property representative
- Landlord
- Law enforcement

PRIVATE PROPERTY TOW RULE:
Tow company must notify law enforcement:
- Within 30 minutes of vehicle arriving at storage facility

Tow company must document:
- Officer name
- Badge number
- Date/time notified

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT STORAGE FEE RULE
==================================================

No storage fee:
- If stored less than 6 hours

==================================================
THIRD-PARTY SERVICE PROVIDERS
==================================================

Tow companies MUST use approved third-party providers.

APPROVED PROVIDERS:
- Auto Data Direct (ADD123.com)
- Beacon Software (Towlien.com)

Third-party providers handle:
- Notice processing
- Mailing
- Tracking
- Electronic reporting
- NMVTIS integration

==================================================
NOTICE OF LIEN REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Notice of Lien MUST include:
- Fact of possession
- Who authorized tow
- Lien claim statement
- Itemized charges
- Hearing rights
- Sale rights
- Storage location
- Administrative fee disclosure

==================================================
ADMINISTRATIVE FEE RULE
==================================================

Administrative fee:
- Maximum $250

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

If Notice returned undeliverable:
- Last 8 VIN/HIN digits must appear:
  - In address box
  - On outside envelope

Envelope must also show:
- Tow company name
- Physical address
- Telephone number

==================================================
CO-OWNER / LIENHOLDER RULE
==================================================

If multiple owners:
- ALL must be notified

If lienholder has multiple addresses:
- Notice must go to ALL addresses

==================================================
INSURANCE COMPANY NOTICE RULE
==================================================

Insurance notice NOT required if:
- Owner requested tow
- Code enforcement tow
- Insurance cancelled before tow
- Out-of-state title state does not release insurance info

==================================================
LAW ENFORCEMENT BLOCK RULE
==================================================

If FLHSMV shows:
- "Law Enforcement Block"

Tow company must send certified notice to:

FLHSMV
ATTN: Law Enforcement Block
2900 Apalachee Parkway
MS #57 – B231
Tallahassee FL 32399

==================================================
PRIVACY ACT BLOCK RULE
==================================================

If Privacy Act Block exists:
- Tax collector/license plate office may release info

==================================================
CRITICAL TIMING RULES
==================================================

FOR TOWS ON OR BEFORE 06/30/2024:

Notice of Lien:
- Within 7 business days after tow

Older models:
- Sale after 35 days

Newer models:
- Sale after 50 days

Notice of Sale:
- At least 30 days before sale

Newspaper ad:
- 10 calendar days before sale

==================================================
UPDATED 07/01/2024 RULES
==================================================

FOR TOWS ON OR AFTER 07/01/2024:

Notice of Lien:
- Within 5 business days after tow

Older models:
- Sale after 35 days

Newer models:
- Sale after 57 days

Notice of Sale:
- 30 days before sale for older model
- 52 days before sale for newer model

Public sale ad:
- Must post online
- 20 days before sale

==================================================
ONLINE PUBLIC SALE REQUIREMENT
==================================================

Approved public sale sites:
- CarLocate.com
- findmycar.us

Ad must include:
- Tow company info
- Vehicle/Vessel info
- Sale location/date/time
- Amount due breakdown

==================================================
GOOD FAITH NOTICE
==================================================

If owner/lienholder cannot be located:
- Tow company must notify local law enforcement

Deadline:
- Within 5 business days after tow
  (new law)
- Previously 7 business days

Requires:
- Certified mail
OR
- Receipt-acknowledged electronic delivery

==================================================
GOOD FAITH SEARCH REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Tow company must:
- Conduct physical search
- Run ownership checks
- Request 50-state search

If no state found:
- Signed Good Faith Notice replaces Notice of Lien

If state found:
- Tow company must contact title state
- Must mail notices to returned addresses

==================================================
STORAGE FEE PENALTIES
==================================================

If notices late:
- Storage fees restricted or eliminated

If owner only:
- Late notice = no storage fees

If lienholder exists:
- Only limited storage fees allowed

==================================================
LAW ENFORCEMENT STORAGE RULE
==================================================

If law enforcement requests hold:
- Up to 5 days:
  Owner/lienholder pays

- Beyond 5 days:
  Law enforcement pays storage after day 5

==================================================
PUBLIC SALE RULES
==================================================

Public sale:
- Must occur after statutory waiting period
- Must occur in proper county

Older models:
- 35-day minimum

Newer models:
- 57-day minimum (new law)

==================================================
AGE DETERMINATION RULE
==================================================

Model year counts as Year 1.

Example:
2023 vehicle year calculations:
- 2021 model = 3 years old
- 2020 model = 4 years old

==================================================
REQUIRED TITLE DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Required:
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82040 VS
- HSMV 82040 MH
- HSMV 82012 (COD)
- Notice of Lien
- Notice of Sale if required
- Tow ticket/trip sheet
- PS 3877 certified mailing proof
- NMVTIS proof
- Public sale proof
- Mailing proof
- Owner/lienholder record proof

==================================================
TOW TICKET REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Tow ticket must contain:
- Tow company name/address/phone
- Vehicle description
- VIN/HIN
- License plate
- Tow location
- Storage location
- Date/time tow
- Authorizing party
- Tow fees
- Mileage
- Storage fee
- Recovery fees
- Administrative fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LAW ENFORCEMENT NAME RULE
==================================================

NO abbreviations allowed for:
- Law enforcement agency names

==================================================
NON-CONSENSUAL TOW DISTANCE RULES
==================================================

COUNTIES 500,000+:
- Storage within 10 miles
- Up to 20 if no closer facility

COUNTIES UNDER 500,000:
- Storage within 15 miles
- Up to 30 if no closer facility

==================================================
PUBLICATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

PRE-07/01/2024:
- Newspaper publication required

POST-07/01/2024:
- Online public sale posting required

==================================================
CERTIFICATE OF DESTRUCTION RULES
==================================================

COD application:
- HSMV 82012

Must include:
- NMVTIS reporting proof
- Proof vehicle destroyed/scrapped/etc.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COD RESTRICTIONS
==================================================

Certificate of Destruction cannot issue if:
- FCIC stolen stop
- Pending title
- Administrative stop
- Cancelled/voided record
- Correspondence stop

==================================================
OFF-HIGHWAY VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Certificate of Destruction:
- CANNOT issue for off-highway vehicle

==================================================
JUNK VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If FLHSMV record shows:
- "Junk"

THEN:
- Certificate of Destruction required
- No title issued

==================================================
SALVAGE TITLE OPTION
==================================================

Tow company or purchaser may use:
- HSMV 82363

Possible result:
- Salvage Rebuildable Title

==================================================
TITLE IN DEALER NAME RULE
==================================================

Dealer purchasing at tow sale:
- MUST title in dealer name first
- BEFORE resale

==================================================
UNSOLD VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If unsold:
- Tow company applies for title/COD in own name

==================================================
SALES TAX EXEMPTION WORDING
==================================================

Required wording:
"Obtained per Florida Statutes 713.78 or 713.785, for towing and storage charges."

==================================================
LIENS ON NEW TITLE
==================================================

All prior liens:
- Removed from title
- Unless court order states otherwise

==================================================
CORRECTION / VIN ERROR RULE
==================================================

If incorrect VIN/HIN used:
- Title cancelled
- Owner/lienholder must be re-notified
- Advertisement republished
- Confidential VIN inspection required

==================================================
CERTIFICATE OF DESTRUCTION LIFECYCLE RULE
==================================================

Once COD issued:
- No title may ever issue again

COD:
- Reassignable maximum 2 times

Final dismantler:
- Must keep COD for 3 years

==================================================
IMPORTANT FLHSMV SYSTEM RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV system displays:
- Notice of Lien
- Notice of Sale

If displayed info:
- Does NOT match paperwork
→ Reject transaction

==================================================
SHOW CAUSE LETTER RULE
==================================================

FLHSMV:
- No longer issues Show Cause letters

==================================================
LIEN ONLY IN-STATE LIEN STOP
==================================================

If stop shows:
- "LIEN ONLY – IN STATE LIEN"

Tow company must:
- Obtain lienholder info
- Send notices to lienholder

==================================================
LICENSE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Owner may retrieve:
- Valid OR expired plate

If unclaimed:
- Tow company should surrender plate to tax collector

==================================================
SALVAGE BRAND LIFETIME RULE
==================================================

Once salvage title issued:
- Salvage designation remains forever

==================================================
WRECKER OPERATOR LIEN
==================================================

Tow company may submit:
- Wrecker Operator Lien

Maximum:
- 7 days of charges

See:
- RS-50

==================================================
IMPORTANT OPERATIONAL NOTES
==================================================

This is one of the most timing-sensitive title procedures.

Small errors can:
- Void sale
- Void storage charges
- Require re-mailing
- Require re-publication
- Require restart of process

Tow lien title work requires:
- Exact statutory timing
- Proper mailing proof
- NMVTIS verification
- Complete documentation
- Correct model-year calculations
