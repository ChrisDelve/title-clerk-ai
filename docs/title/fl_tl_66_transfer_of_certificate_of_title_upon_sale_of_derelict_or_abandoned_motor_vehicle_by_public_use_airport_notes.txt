FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — TRANSFER OF CERTIFICATE OF TITLE UPON SALE OF DERELICT OR ABANDONED MOTOR VEHICLE BY A PUBLIC-USE AIRPORT
PROCEDURE: TL-66
CATEGORY: ABANDONED VEHICLES / DERELICT VEHICLES / AIRPORT SALES / OPERATION OF LAW

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Abandoned vehicles at airports
- Derelict vehicles at airports
- Public-use airport towing liens
- Airport vehicle auctions
- Airport operation-of-law title transfers
- Claim of lien filings
- Airport public sales
- Certified notice requirements
- Airport storage liens
- Airport title issuance

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Public-use airports
- Airport directors
- Tax collectors
- License plate agencies
- Purchasers at airport auctions

DOES NOT APPLY TO:
- Vessels
- Mobile homes

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ABANDONED VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

Abandoned motor vehicle:
Vehicle:
- Wrecked
- Inoperative
- Partially dismantled
OR
Idle on airport premises:
For 45 consecutive calendar days.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DERELICT RULE
==================================================

Derelict motor vehicle:
Vehicle:
Not in drivable condition.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AIRPORT POSSESSION RULE
==================================================

Airport director or designee:
May take possession:
Of abandoned or derelict vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT RECORD RULE
==================================================

Airport must record:
Date vehicle found.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WRECKER RULE
==================================================

Airport may use:
- Airport wrecker
OR
- Licensed independent wrecker company.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT WRECKER EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

If independent wrecker used:
Wrecker must comply with:
713.78 FS.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OWNER SEARCH RULE
==================================================

Airport must contact:
FLHSMV:
To determine:
- Owner
- Insurance company
- Lienholders

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 7-BUSINESS-DAY RULE
==================================================

Within:
7 business days:
Airport must send:
Certified notice:
To:
- Owner
- Insurance company
- Lienholders

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CERTIFIED MAIL RULE
==================================================

Notice requires:
Certified mail:
Return receipt requested.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE CONTENT RULE
==================================================

Notice must state:
- Vehicle possession
- Towing/storage/parking charges
- Lien claim
- Hearing rights
- Potential sale/disposal rights

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 30-DAY RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may be disposed:
After:
30 calendar days:
If not claimed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LIEN AGE RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may be sold free of liens:
After:
35 days:
If liens older than 5 years.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT 50-DAY RULE
==================================================

Vehicle may be sold free of liens:
After:
50 days:
If liens 5 years old or less.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FAILED NOTICE RULE
==================================================

If notice attempts unsuccessful:
Notice requirement considered satisfied.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COURT COMPLAINT RULE
==================================================

Owner/lienholder may:
File complaint:
Within:
10 calendar days:
After learning vehicle location.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BOND RULE
==================================================

Owner/lienholder may recover vehicle:
By posting:
Cash bond or security.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AUCTION RULE
==================================================

Airport may sell vehicle:
At:
- Internet auction
OR
- Physical auction

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE NOTICE RULE
==================================================

If sale date not included in original notice:
Separate sale notice required.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE NOTICE TIMING RULE
==================================================

Sale notice must be mailed:
At least:
10 calendar days:
Before sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NEWSPAPER RULE
==================================================

Public auction notice:
Must be published:
One time:
At least 10 calendar days before sale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NEWSPAPER REQUIREMENT RULE
==================================================

Newspaper must:
- Be general circulation
- In county where sale held

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXCESS PROCEEDS RULE
==================================================

Airport retains:
Excess proceeds:
After towing/storage/sale costs paid.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CLAIM OF LIEN RULE
==================================================

Airport must record:
Claim of lien:
With clerk of court.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT GOOD FAITH PURCHASER RULE
==================================================

Good faith purchaser:
Takes vehicle:
Free of prior interests.

==================================================
GENERAL INFORMATION
==================================================

Procedure does NOT apply to:
- Vessels
- Mobile homes

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE PENALTY RULE
==================================================

If title application:
Not submitted within:
30 days of auction:
Penalty fee due.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MISSING VIN RULE
==================================================

If visible VIN missing:
Contact:
- Regional office
OR
- Auto theft law enforcement.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REPLACEMENT VIN RULE
==================================================

Replacement VIN decal:
Must be affixed:
Before vehicle sold.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AIRPORT PROCEEDS RULE
==================================================

Airport retains:
Sale proceeds exceeding costs.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT DEALER TITLE RULE
==================================================

Dealer or individual:
Purchasing through operation of law:
Must title vehicle:
In own name:
Before resale.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE DISCLOSURE RULE
==================================================

Tax collectors and LPAs:
May disclose insurance info:
To towing companies:
Under:
705.184 FS.

==================================================
DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

Required:
1. Notice of Abandoned/Derelict Vehicle
2. Certified mail receipts/unclaimed letters
3. Notice of Sale
4. Newspaper advertisement
5. Claim of Lien
6. Bill of Sale if sold
7. HSMV 82040 MV
8. Sales tax documentation
9. Plate or non-use affidavit
10. Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REGISTERED OWNER RULE
==================================================

If registered owner differs from titled owner:
Both must receive notice.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CO-OWNER RULE
==================================================

If co-owners have different addresses:
Each owner must receive notice.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INSURANCE EXCEPTION RULE
==================================================

Insurance company notice:
NOT required:
If FRVIS shows policy cancelled:
Before vehicle found/towed.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT USPS RULE
==================================================

Acceptable proof:
- Signed return receipt
- Unclaimed certified letter
- Electronic return receipt

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FORWARDING ORDER RULE
==================================================

Letters marked:
"Forwarding Order Expired"
Are acceptable.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UNCLAIMED LETTER RULE
==================================================

Tax collector staff:
Must open:
Returned unclaimed letters.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT INITIALS RULE
==================================================

Staff must place:
Initials and date:
On opened envelope.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TRACK AND CONFIRM RULE
==================================================

USPS "Track & Confirm":
NOT acceptable:
Instead of electronic return receipt.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REQUIRED NOTICE ITEMS RULE
==================================================

Notice must include:
- Airport name/address
- Year/make/VIN
- Possession statement
- Lien claim
- Charges owed
- Hearing rights
- Sale/disposal rights

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTICE OF SALE RULE
==================================================

Sale notice must include:
- Date of sale
- Time of sale
- Location of sale
- Vehicle location
- Vehicle description
- Airport information

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALE DATE RULE
==================================================

Sale date in newspaper:
Must match:
Sale notice date.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NEWSPAPER TIMING RULE
==================================================

10-day publication period:
Does NOT include:
- Publication date
- Sale date

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT COUNTY RULE
==================================================

Newspaper notice:
Must specify:
County circulated.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CLAIM SERVICE RULE
==================================================

Claim of lien:
Must be served:
Before recording:
With clerk of court.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT METHOD RULE
==================================================

Method of service:
Must be specified:
On HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT BILL OF SALE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle sold:
Need original bill of sale:
Showing complete description.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT UNSOLD VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If vehicle not sold:
Airport MUST title vehicle:
In airport name.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Sales tax or exemption:
Must appear on:
HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AIRPORT TITLE STATEMENT RULE
==================================================

If airport titling vehicle:
82040 MV must state:
"obtained per Florida statutes, s. 705.184, for towing, storage and parking fees only."

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT LICENSE PLATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT AIRPORT EXEMPTION RULE
==================================================

Airport titling in own name:
Does NOT require:
Plate/non-use affidavit.

10. Was newspaper publication timely?
11. Are lien age timelines satisfied?
12. Is vehicle eligible for free-and-clear sale?
13. Was claim of lien recorded?
14. Was lien served before recording?
15. Was VIN missing?
16. Was replacement VIN assigned?
17. Is airport titling vehicle itself?
18. Was vehicle sold at proper auction?
19. Was title application timely?
20. Does penalty fee apply?
21. Is operation-of-law titling required?
22. Must dealer title before resale?
23. Are excess proceeds retained properly?
24. Is HSMV 82040 MV completed correctly?

COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Missing insurance company notice
- Missing co-owner notices
- Incorrect certified mail timing
- Using Track & Confirm only
- Missing newspaper publication
- Wrong auction dates
- Missing claim of lien recording
- Failure to open unclaimed letters
- Missing initials/date on envelopes
- Missing operation-of-law title transfer
- Dealer resale without titling
- Missing replacement VIN
- Incorrect lien age calculations
- Missing airport statutory statement
- Late title application penalty oversight

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE CONCEPT
==================================================

Public-use airports:
May dispose of abandoned or derelict vehicles:
Through operation-of-law auction procedures:
After strict notice,
lien,
and publication compliance.
