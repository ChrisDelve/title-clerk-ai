FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — CHANGE OF MOTOR ON VEHICLES MANUFACTURED PRIOR TO 1955
PROCEDURE: TL-50
CATEGORY: PRE-1955 VEHICLES / MOTOR NUMBER TITLING / ENGINE REPLACEMENT

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Change of motor on pre-1955 vehicles
- Motor number title updates
- Engine replacement title processing
- FLA VIN assignment after engine changes
- Out-of-state pre-1955 title conversions
- Verification of motor numbers
- Ownership transfers involving changed motors

Applies to:
- Vehicles manufactured before 1955
- Antique vehicles
- Classic vehicles
- Motor-number titled vehicles

LEGAL AUTHORITY
==================================================

Governed by:
- 319.23 FS

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-1955 RULE
==================================================

Vehicles manufactured prior to:
- 1955

Are titled by:
- Motor number.

NOT:
- VIN.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN RULE
==================================================

If out-of-state title:
Already uses VIN:
Florida title:
Will continue using:
- VIN.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT OUT-OF-STATE VIN RULE
==================================================

If out-of-state title:
Shows VIN instead of motor number:
Refer to:
- TL-10.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE / SAME OWNER MOTOR CHANGE
==================================================

If:
- Florida titled
AND
- Same owner replaced motor

Need:
- Florida title
- HSMV 82103
- Bill of sale/receipt for motor
- Lien satisfactions
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82103 RULE
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82103
Affidavit for Change of Motor.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT MOTOR RECEIPT RULE
==================================================

Motor bill of sale/receipt:
Must show:
- Year
- Make
- Motor number
- Seller name
- Seller address
- Seller signature

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NOTARIZATION RULE
==================================================

Motor bill of sale:
Does NOT require:
- Notarization.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-1955 REPLACEMENT MOTOR RULE
==================================================

If replacement motor:
Manufactured BEFORE 1955:

Title:
MUST display:
- Replacement motor number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT POST-1955 MOTOR RULE
==================================================

If replacement motor:
Manufactured 1955 or newer:

Florida:
Assigns:
- FLA VIN decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA DECAL RULE
==================================================

If FLA assigned:
Certificate of title:
Will show:
- Assigned FLA number
AS:
- Vehicle identification number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT YEAR/MAKE RULE
==================================================

When FLA assigned:
- Year DOES NOT change
- Make DOES NOT change.

==================================================
FLORIDA TITLE / NEW PURCHASER RULE
==================================================

If:
- Vehicle sold
AND
- Purchaser replaced motor

Need:
- Assigned Florida title
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82103
- Motor bill of sale
- Lien satisfactions
- Sales tax
- Plate/non-use affidavit
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida sales tax
OR
- Sales tax exemption information.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PLATE RULE
==================================================

Need:
- Florida plate
OR
- Non-use affidavit.

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE MOTOR NUMBER TITLE RULE
==================================================

If out-of-state title:
Uses motor number
AND
Motor replaced:

Need:
- Out-of-state ownership proof
OR
- Title/lien verification
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82042 or equivalent VIN verification
- HSMV 82103
- Motor receipt
- Lien satisfactions
- Sales tax
- Plate/non-use affidavit
- Fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE/LIEN VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Out-of-state verification may be:
- Computer printout
- DMV letter
- Photocopy of title
- Teletype/law enforcement verification

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT HSMV 82042 RULE
==================================================

Need:
- HSMV 82042
OR
- VIN section on HSMV 82040 MV.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT VIN/MOTOR VERIFICATION RULE
==================================================

Verification must confirm:
- VIN
AND
- Motor number.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT ALTERNATE AFFIDAVIT RULE
==================================================

Instead of HSMV 82042:
Can use:
- Affidavit from seller and purchaser

Verifying:
- VIN and motor numbers match vehicle.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SAME VEHICLE RULE
==================================================

If out-of-state title:
Used motor number
AND
Motor replaced:

Must determine:
Vehicle remains:
- Same vehicle shown on title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FLA ASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

If replacement motor:
1955 or newer:
- Assign FLA VIN decal.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE DISPLAY RULE
==================================================

After FLA assignment:
Title shows:
- FLA VIN

NOT:
- New motor number.

==================================================
MISCELLANEOUS RULES
==================================================

If out-of-state title:
Already uses VIN:
Continue using:
- VIN title process.

==================================================
EXHIBIT A CHECKLIST ITEMS
==================================================

Florida record requires:
- Florida title
- HSMV 82040 MV
- HSMV 82103
- HSMV 82042 or VIN verification
- Motor bill of sale
- Lien satisfactions
- Sales tax
- Plate/non-use affidavit
- Title fees

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PRE-1955 IDENTIFICATION CONCEPT
==================================================

Before 1955:
Vehicle identification commonly based on:
- Engine/motor number

NOT:
- Chassis VIN.

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Was vehicle manufactured before 1955?
2. Does title use motor number?
3. Is vehicle Florida titled?
4. Is ownership changing?
5. Was motor replaced?
6. Is replacement motor pre-1955?
7. Is replacement motor 1955 or newer?
8. Is FLA VIN assignment required?
9. Is out-of-state title involved?
10. Is VIN verification required?
11. Are liens satisfied?
12. Is motor bill of sale complete?
13. Is HSMV 82103 completed?
14. Is vehicle still identifiable as same vehicle?
15. Is sales tax documented?
16. Is plate/non-use affidavit included?

==================================================
COMMON TITLE CLERK ERRORS
==================================================

- Confusing motor number with VIN
- Forgetting FLA assignment for newer motors
- Changing year/make incorrectly
- Missing HSMV 82103
- Accepting incomplete engine receipt
- Missing VIN verification
- Failing to verify same vehicle identity
- Improper out-of-state processing
- Forgetting sales tax documentation
- Using wrong identification number on title

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT IDENTIFICATION TRANSITION RULE
==================================================

If modern engine installed:
Florida transitions identification from:
- Motor number

TO:
- Assigned FLA VIN decal.
