PROCEDURE: TL-71
CATEGORY: TITLE ELIGIBILITY / TITLE DETERMINATION / VEHICLE CLASSIFICATION

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure governs:
- Vehicle title eligibility determination
- Florida title qualification review
- Vehicle classification analysis
- Correct title branding procedures
- Determination flow process
- Salvage and rebuilt determination
- Replica and assembled-from-parts analysis
- Manufacturer title review
- Kit car title determination

Applies to:
- Motor vehicles
- Rebuilt vehicles
- Salvage vehicles
- Replica vehicles
- Kit cars
- Assembled-from-parts vehicles
- Tax collectors
- License plate agencies
- FLHSMV personnel

==================================================
IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS
==================================================

MOTOR VEHICLE:
Self-propelled vehicle:
Used to transport persons or property.

KIT CAR:
Motor vehicle assembled:
Using manufacturer supplied kit:
To rebuild wrecked or outdated vehicle.

==================================================
DESCRIPTION AND USE
==================================================

Procedure provides:
Instructions and flow process:
To determine:
- Whether vehicle may be legally titled
- Proper title brand designation

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT CORE RULE
==================================================

Flow chart:
Must be used:
To determine:
If vehicle can legally receive Florida title.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT PROCEDURE RULE
==================================================

If flow process directs:
Specific title procedure:
That procedure is:
ONLY authorized method.

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NO TITLE RULE
==================================================

If flow chart outcome:
"Vehicle cannot be titled"
NO Florida title:
May be issued or transferred.
