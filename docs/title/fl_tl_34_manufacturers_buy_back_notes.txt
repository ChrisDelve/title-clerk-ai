FLORIDA TITLE PROCEDURE — MANUFACTURER’S BUY BACK
PROCEDURE: TL-34
CATEGORY: LEMON LAW / BRANDED TITLES / MANUFACTURER BUY BACK

==================================================
PROCEDURE SUMMARY
==================================================

This procedure explains:
- Florida Lemon Law buy backs
- Manufacturer repurchased vehicles
- "Manufacturer’s Buy Back" title branding
- Required documents
- Resale disclosure requirements
- Brand removal limitations

Applies when:
- Manufacturer repurchases vehicle
due to:
- Defects
- Nonconformities
- Lemon Law settlements
- Arbitration decisions
- Court actions

==================================================
LEGAL PURPOSE
==================================================

Florida law protects consumers by:
- Requiring disclosure
of:
- Defective vehicles
- Lemon law repurchases

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT TITLE BRAND RULE
==================================================

If manufacturer repurchases vehicle:
Title MUST be branded:
- Manufacturer’s Buy Back

System shorthand:
- Manuf Buy Bk

==================================================
WHEN BRAND REQUIRED
==================================================

Brand required if:
Vehicle repurchased due to:
- Settlement
- Arbitration
- Attorney General action
- Court decision
- Lemon Law determination

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT EXCEPTION
==================================================

If manufacturer states:
Settlement occurred BEFORE:
- Formal claim filed

Then:
Brand may NOT be required.

==================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
==================================================

Need:
1. Current title assigned to manufacturer
2. HSMV 82040
3. Certified Resale Disclosure of Nonconformity
4. Sales tax exemption wording
5. Plate transfer or non-use affidavit
6. Title fee

==================================================
TITLE ASSIGNMENT RULE
==================================================

Original owner must complete:
- Transfer of Title by Seller section

Vehicle transfers:
- Into manufacturer name

==================================================
HSMV 82040 RULE
==================================================

HSMV 82040 must:
- Be completed in manufacturer name

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT SALES TAX WORDING
==================================================

The OTHER section must state EXACTLY:

"OBTAINED PURSUANT TO CHAPTER 681, FLORIDA STATUTES"

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT REJECTION RULE
==================================================

If exact Chapter 681 wording missing:
Application:
- MUST be rejected

==================================================
PLATE REQUIREMENT
==================================================

Need:
- Plate transfer
OR
- Non-use affidavit

==================================================
TITLE BRAND RESULT
==================================================

New title issued to manufacturer will display:
- Manuf Buy Bk

==================================================
OUT-OF-STATE BRAND RULE
==================================================

Florida CANNOT remove:
- Manufacturer Buy Back brand
added by:
- Another state

==================================================
BRAND REMOVAL RULE
==================================================

To request removal:
Need:
- Current title
- Affidavit requesting removal
- Current odometer reading
- Proper title fee

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT NMVTIS RULE
==================================================

Even if Florida removes:
- Manuf Buy Bk

NMVTIS brand:
- REMAINS

==================================================
VERY IMPORTANT FIELD SUPPORT RULE
==================================================

Field Support Helpdesk must assist with:
- Brand removal processing

==================================================
RESALE DISCLOSURE OF NONCONFORMITY
==================================================

Certified copy required.

Disclosure explains:
- Vehicle repurchased due to defects
- Nonconformities not repaired
within reasonable attempts

==================================================
EXHIBIT A PURPOSE
==================================================

Exhibit A documents:
- Defect disclosure
- Settlement/arbitration source
- Manufacturer acknowledgment
- Consumer acknowledgment

==================================================
EXHIBIT A IMPORTANT DATA
==================================================

Disclosure includes:
- VIN
- Odometer
- Vehicle make/model
- Defect description
- Settlement details
- Arbitration details
- Consumer signatures

==================================================
AI DECISION LOGIC
==================================================

AI should determine:
1. Was vehicle repurchased by manufacturer?
2. Was Lemon Law involved?
3. Is title branding required?
4. Is certified disclosure included?
5. Is exact Chapter 681 wording present?
6. Is vehicle transferring into manufacturer name?
7. Is plate/non-use affidavit included?
8. Is existing brand from another state?
9. Is customer requesting brand removal?
10. Is Field Support involvement required?

- Forgetting title brand
- Incorrect manufacturer transfer
- Attempting to remove out-of-state brand
- Missing title fee
- Missing odometer reading for removal
- Accepting incomplete disclosure forms
